# How can I compare a group of jobs that happen to have different phase masks?

Answer: You can "map" your different phase masks to a common grouping using the Alternate Phase field. The alternate phase option is available in [Job Phases](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/jobs/job-properties/job-phases). This is an optional field that may be used as a way to compare specific tasks across jobs with different phase structures recorded for different jobs. You can also use alternate phase code in the Project Setup history download file.
The Job Comparison Report may be printed to obtain a comparison of the costs for up to five selected jobs. Actual figures for the phases/cost types are printed so that any correlation between similar jobs may be seen. There are no special calculations for this report. All figures are printed directly from Job Phases.
