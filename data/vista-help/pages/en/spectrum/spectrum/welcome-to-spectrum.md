# Welcome to Spectrum

Spectrum® is a comprehensive, cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution designed specifically for construction firms to connect the office, field, and entire project life cycle.
As a core component of the Trimble Construction One suite, Spectrum provides a unified, real-time view of your business operations. This solution connects key back-office workflows - from initial financial planning and detailed job cost management to payroll, HR, and final regulatory reporting - ensuring every member of your team works with accurate, timely data to improve decision-making and project profitability.

## Spectrum's Core Value

Spectrum is built to help contractors streamline their operations, mitigate risk, and realize stronger margins by integrating data across critical functional areas:

- Financial Management: Gain real-time visibility into costs, contracts, and general ledger data with powerful, automated accounting tools.

- Job Costing & Project Management: Track costs, manage project progress, and monitor productivity from the 30,000-foot view down to individual invoices and cost codes.

- HR & Payroll: Simplify complex construction payroll, including support for union rules, certified payroll, and complete employee management.

- Equipment & Inventory: Monitor and track equipment usage, maintenance schedules, and materials inventory to optimize resource allocation.

- Field-to-Office Connection: Utilize integrated web and mobile applications to ensure field teams and project managers are always working with the same, up-to-date information as the back-office.
