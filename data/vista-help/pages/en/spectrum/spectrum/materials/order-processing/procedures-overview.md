# Procedures Overview

The typical order of operations and suggestions for storing
 reports are contained in this Help section.
If these procedures are used as a guideline, there will be valuable printed documents on
 file for the company that may be used in case of hardware problems. If maintained properly,
 the suggested printed documents could save many hours of retrieval time in case of hardware
 problems.
The frequency with which these procedures are followed depends in large measure on the circumstances of the individual company.
