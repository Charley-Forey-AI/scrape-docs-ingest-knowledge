# Service Tech

Use the Service Tech Mobile app to review your work orders, enter and edit time, and add notes and images.
Trimble Viewpoint products are upgraded to always take advantage of the latest features that come with Operating System updates.
To ensure the best performance and full compatibility with all features, we recommend that your device is able to run the minimum version of the OS below.
PlatformRecommended OS VersionNotes
Android14/15[Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.viewpoint.servicetech)
iOS17/18[App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/spectrum-service-tech/id1451768891)

## Built For Your Phone

The Service Tech mobile app is optimized for use on a phone, but works on tablets as well.

## Content Forward Design

This app eases work order management with unique content forward design. With a brief view at the top levels of the work order, technicians can quickly see and understand the current status - without having to drill down into the work order. For example, the Labor button gives a summary of the labor hours on the work order, and then the labor page provides a management view of hours by tech.

## Enhanced Offline Capability

With a built-in database, the app is designed for offline work. Techs logging in for the first time may need to stay connected to the internet until all data is synced.
