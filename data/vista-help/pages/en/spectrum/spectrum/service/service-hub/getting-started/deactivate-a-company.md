# Deactivate a Company

You must be an ERP Admin or Company Admin to complete these steps.

1. In the ERP Administration page, select the
 ellipses icon  next to the
 appropriate company from the list.

1. Select Deactivate Company.A notification displays to confirm your intent.

1. Select Deactivate Company again to confirm.

The company is deactivated. You can reactivate it again
 at any time.
