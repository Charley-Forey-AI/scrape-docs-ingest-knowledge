# Change Company Settings

You can easily enable different Service Hub features from the ERP
 Administrator page.
You must be an ERP Admin or Company Admin to complete these steps.

1. In the ERP Administrator page, select the company
 whose settings you want to change from the list of Activated Companies.The Company Settings page opens.

1. Choose one of the following options to proceed:

- Select the pencil icon  next to Service Request
 Settings to toggle service request emails, change the email to receive
 service requests, or add custom Service Request form fields.

- Select the pencil icon  next to Payment Settings to
 toggle Stripe payments, change your Stripe Account ID, unlink your Stripe
 Account, or change your email to receive payment notifications.

1. Select Update.

1. If you changed the email to receive service requests or payment notifications,
 verify the new email address.

Your business settings are updated.
