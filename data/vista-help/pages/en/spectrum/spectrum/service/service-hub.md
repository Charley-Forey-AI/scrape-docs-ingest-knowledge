# Service Hub

Service Hub streamlines the interaction between service providers in Spectrum and their service customers by providing a central hub where a service customer can create service requests, manage work orders, and pay invoices.

## Getting Started

Service Hub is available to all Service Tech customers. If you would like to get started with Service Hub, contact your Trimble representative.
Once you have been added as a Service Hub user, you will receive an email notification and can then sign in.

- See [Sign in to Service Hub](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-hub/getting-started/sign-in-to-service-hub) for initial sign in steps.

- See [Set up Service Hub](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-hub/getting-started/set-up-service-hub) for steps on adding and configuring a company to use Service Hub.

## Overview

The following sections explain how service customers can add users, manage work orders, and handle invoices.

- See [Manage Users](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-hub/manage-users) to learn how to invite users to a company in Service Hub and assign a role. To review the permissions for each role, see [User Roles](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-hub/manage-users/user-roles).

- See [Work Orders](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-hub/work-orders) to learn how to create service requests and manage work orders.

- See [Invoices](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-hub/invoices) to learn how to review and pay invoices.

## Improve Service Hub

Have a suggestion to improve the Service Hub experience? Select the lightbulb icon  at the top right of the ERP Administrator context to add your idea to the suggestion box.
