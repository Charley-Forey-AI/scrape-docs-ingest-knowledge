# Service Contracts

The Service Contracts screen is used
 to create and manage service contracts in Spectrum. Information recorded on this screen will
 be updated to the new work order, based on the settings from the Service Contract
 Installation screen.
From the Site
 Map, select Maintenance > Service Contracts.
Use the New
 and Edit buttons to record
 contract specifications. Exceptions to the contract, for example, a certain air
 conditioning unit or off-hours service type, can be recorded in the Exception
 Notes window and will then print in the Exceptions section of the service
 contract form.
Click the Status button in the upper right of the screen to filter or view service
 contracts based on their status: Proposal, Active,
 Inactive, or Expired Proposal.
Begin by clicking the [New Service Contract](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/maintenance-overview/service-contracts/new-service-contract) button or by entering a service contract code in the search
 field. If you leave the Search field blank and click the Go button,
 the screen will return a list of valid service contract codes.
Note: The Service Contracts screen has two
 heading formats, depending upon whether it is accessed in as a general purpose list or for
 a specific site. This screen serves two purposes: in addition to showing service contracts
 across all sites, it is accessible in context to a particular site, and available from the
 Site [Info Bar](/en/spectrum/spectrum/getting-started/spectrum-interface-faqs/info-bar/info-bar-faqs), for convenient inquiry.
