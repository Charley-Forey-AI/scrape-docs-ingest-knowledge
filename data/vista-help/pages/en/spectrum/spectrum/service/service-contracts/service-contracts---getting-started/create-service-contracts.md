# Create Service Contracts

Create service contracts to manage and control contract
 revenues and costs. After you set up Service Contract Properties, use the Info Bar links to proceed through contract
 setup.

1. Select Service Contracts > Maintenance > Service Contracts.Note: For field descriptions, see [Service Contracts](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/service-contracts).

1. Click New.

1. Select a Site.

1. Enter a Contract #, or leave this field blank if you want to system to assign
 the next available contract number.

1. Select a Contract Description, for example, Maintenance Contract.

1. Choose a Contract Type, for example, Full Maintenance.

1. Select a Work Order Type, for example, Maintenance Contracts.

1. Accept the default Customer, or enter a different
 customer.

1. Click OK.

1. On the Service Contract Main Properties tab, enter information regarding the
 service contract salesperson, start and end date for the contract, and labor and
 material levels. If this is a proposal, mark the Proposed Service Contract
 checkbox.

- Note the information shown in the Profit summary section, and
 click the available links for more details:

- [Earned Revenue Processing](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/data-entry-overview/earned-revenue-processing): View a list of all earned revenue for the
 selected period. Earned revenue is the portion of the total contract amount
 that has been booked as revenue in the General Ledger.

- [Service Contract Work Order Revenue](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/service-contracts/service-contract-work-order-revenue): View a breakdown of posted revenue for work
 orders that reference the selected contract.

- Total revenue: Shows the total of all earned and work
 order revenue.

- [Total Cost](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/work-order/spectrum-menus-overview/inquiries-overview/work-order-cost-history): View past cost information for work orders.

- Gross profit: Shows the difference between total
 revenue and total cost.

1. Continue through the Info Bar links to set up the
 following service contract options:

- Select Covered Equipment to select site equipment that this
 service contract covers.

- Select [Labor Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/work-order/spectrum-menus-overview/maintenance-overview/labor-billing-rates-maintenance/contract-labor-billing-rates-maintenance) to set up contract-specific
 rates .

1. Select Visits on the Info
 Bar to schedule [Service Contract Visits](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/service-contracts/service-contract-visits).
