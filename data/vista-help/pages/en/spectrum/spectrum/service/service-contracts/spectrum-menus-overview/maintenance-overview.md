# Maintenance Overview

Use the Service Contracts >  Maintenance menu to create and manage service contracts.
In order to create a new service contract, you must first set up
 maintenance codes for contract types (for example, full maintenance, inspection, and
 warranty), covered equipment, and labor billing rates. Typically, codes for equipment and
 labor billing rates are set up in [Work
 Order Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/work-order/spectrum-menus-overview/maintenance-overview) or other modules.
 On the Site Map
 , click Service
 Contracts, and then click Maintenance.
