# Billing Work Orders

Use this procedure to transfer one or several work
 orders.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 System Administration > Installation > Work Order.

1. Click the Printing tab and
 select the Transfer invoices as 'Printed' checkbox and click
 OK to return to the main menu.

Work Oder is set to transfer work orders without
 printing their invoices.Note: If you want to print hard
 copies of the invoices, leave the Transfer invoices as 'Printed'
 checkbox clear. However, be advised that the Invoice Entry screen
 will not allow you to update to the Sales Journal/Update until the invoices are
 printed.
The following procedures assume that you have
 already entered your work order information. You can then transfer work orders
 individually or in groups.
Option A: Transferring a Single
 Work Order

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Data Entry > Work Order Entry.

1. Enter the work order number that you would like to transfer and
 press Enter.

1. Click the Financials tab and click the Bill Now button.

1. Review the details on the Create A/R invoice window.

1. Click Continue to start the
 transfer.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Accounts Receivable > Data Entry > Invoice/Credit Memo Entry.

1. Click the Print button.

1. Select the Work Order form type.

1. Click the Print button to print the Work
 Order invoice.

1. Next, click the Update button. The Sales Journal / Update
 screen displays.

1. In the Customer number field, enter the
 customer on the work order.

1. Enter the Job code and From and To G/L dates, if necessary.
 (The Batch code will default automatically.)

1. Preview or Print the report.

1. When the Sales Journal / Update screen
 displays, select Continue to update the invoice information
 to the customer file, contract file, General Ledger, Job Cost, and Equipment
 Control.

Option B: Transferring a Group of Work Orders

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Data Entry  >  Update Work Orders to Invoice
 Entry.

1. Complete the Build list of Work Orders to Bill
 selections window with the work order information that you want to
 update to Accounts Receivable.

1. The list of work orders available for transfer displays in the
 paging portion of the screen. Select the work orders you wish to transfer from the
 Update column and click the Continue button.

1. When the Print Report dialog box
 displays, select to Preview or Print and review the list of work orders being
 transferred. Keep this report handy.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Accounts Receivable > Data Entry > Invoice/Credit Memo Entry.

1. Click the Print button.

1. Select the Work Order form type.

1. Click the Print button to print out the
 Work Order Invoice.

1. Click the Update button. The
 Sales Journal / Update screen displays.

1. In the Customer number field, enter the
 customer on the work order.

1. Enter the Job code and From and To G/L dates, if necessary.
 (The Batch code will default automatically.)

1. Preview or Print the report.

1. When the Sales Journal / Update screen displays, select
 Continue to update the invoice information to the customer file, contract file,
 General Ledger, Job Cost and Equipment Control.
