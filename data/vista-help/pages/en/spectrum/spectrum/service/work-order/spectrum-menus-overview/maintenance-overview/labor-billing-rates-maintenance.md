# Labor Billing Rates Maintenance

Use the Labor Billing Rates Maintenance screen to manage
 labor billing codes and pay rate levels.
These billing codes are used in Work Order Entry and on the Labor Entry
 screen when actual labor hours are entered to determine the hourly rate to charge the
 customer. They are also used in determining the labor portion of Flat Rate Tasks.
