# Other Charges Maintenance - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Field
Description

Other charge item
Enter a unique code for identifying
 this other charge item.
Description
Enter a description of the other charge
 item.
Rate type
Click to mark the appropriate type of
 rate calculation for this code: Fixed amount, Hourly, Percent of bill, or Percent of cost. Work order 'other charge' calculations include
 non-percent of billing other charges in the total work order billing amount
 when computing percent of billing other charges. First, the billing and cost
 amounts for other charge items computed from Fixed and Hourly rates are
 computed. Second, the billing and cost amounts for other charge items
 computed as a Percent of cost are computed based on labor costs, material
 costs, and costs associated with fixed and hourly other charge items.
 Lastly, the billing and cost amounts for other cost items computed as a
 Percent of bill are computed based on flat rate items, labor, and materials
 billed plus the fixed, hourly, and percent of cost billing amounts
 associated with other charge items.

Taxable
Select Yes if this cost is a taxable
 item, No if this is
 not a taxable item, or By work
 order if the item is conditionally taxable and the decision
 needs to be made on a case-by-case basis.
Billing rate
Enter the billing rate for this other
 charge. Entry will depend on the rate type specified above. For fixed amount
 and hourly, enter the appropriate dollar amount. For percent of bill/cost,
 enter the percentage.
Cost rate
Enter the cost rate for this other cost
 item. Like the billing rate, enter an amount if the rate type is Fixed or
 Hourly. Enter a percentage if the rate type is Percent of bill or Percent of
 cost.
Notes
Enter notes, if desired. These notes
 are for internal purposes and do not print on the work order.
