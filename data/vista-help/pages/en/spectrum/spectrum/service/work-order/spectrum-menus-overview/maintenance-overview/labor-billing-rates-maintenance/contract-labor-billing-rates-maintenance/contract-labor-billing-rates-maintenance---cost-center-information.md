# Contract Labor Billing Rates Maintenance - Cost Center Information

When the Enterprise Installation option for Cost centers is set to Yes for this company, the Contract Labor Billing Rates Maintenance screen will only permit the operator to add or view contract labor billing rates they have permission to access.
In the New Contract Labor Billing Rates window, Spectrum verifies that the operator is permitted to access the Contract code entered. The software compares the cost center assigned to the contract with cost centers in the operator's assigned cost center scheme; if the cost center is not included, then labor billing rates cannot be included.
