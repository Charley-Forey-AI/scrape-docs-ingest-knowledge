# Other Charges Maintenance

The Other Charges Maintenance screen is used to manage
 other charge codes for miscellaneous billing items not generally included under labor and
 material umbrellas.
For example, other charges codes might be established for overhead or travel costs. The
 selling price for these other charges can be computed several ways: a flat fee, an hourly
 rate, a percentage of the billed amount, or a percentage of the cost.
