# Mobile Overview

Information entered using the Service Tech Mobile app is synced to the Spectrum application, even though you don't need an application license to use the mobile app.
Service Tech Mobile offers these functions, and others.

- Clock in to your assigned work orders

- Manage your timesheets

- Enter work order notes

- Attach documents and photos

- Record customer signatures
