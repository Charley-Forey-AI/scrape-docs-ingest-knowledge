# Application and Operating System Upgrade FAQ

Information regarding the planned ERP and Operating System upgrades as part of our technology modernization.

## What is changing?

As part of our ongoing platform improvements, we are performing two planned upgrades:

- Spectrum Framework - updating the framework that powers your ERP application, from PRO-IV Version 8 to Version 10.

- Operating System - upgrading SQL Server from 2016 to 2022 to ensure your continued compatibility and long-term support, as SQL Server 2016 has reached the end of its support lifecycle.

## Is any action required from me?

For the upgrade itself, no action is required from you; all upgrade activities will be handled by the support team.
Important: The only requirement is that each user clear their browser cache after they receive the upgrade completion confirmation email (described below) and before signing in to Spectrum.

## Why is this update required?

This upgrade provides you with continued vendor support, improved performance and stability, enhanced security, and compatibility with modern infrastructure.

## Will there be changes to my business processes?

No. Your existing workflows, screens, and reports will continue to function as they do today.

## Will I need training?

No training is expected for you; your application experience remains the same.

## What happens to my existing data?

All of your data will be fully retained, migrated, and validated with no data loss expected.

## Will there be downtime for the ERP update?

Yes. A planned maintenance window will be required, and we will communicate those details to you in advance.

## What is the rollback plan for the ERP update?

In case of critical issues, a full system snapshot will be taken before the update so your system can be rolled back to the previous stable state, ensuring your business continuity.

## What is changing with the Windows Server upgrade?

Your operating system will be upgraded from Windows Server 2016 to Windows Server 2022.

## Why is this OS upgrade needed?

This upgrade addresses the end of support for Windows Server 2016 and provides you with improved security, long-term stability, and support for the next generation of hardware.

## Will this impact my ERP application?

No functional impact is expected for you, and the ERP system will be fully validated post-upgrade.

## Will there be downtime for the OS upgrade?

Yes. A separate planned maintenance window will be scheduled and communicated to you.

## What is the rollback plan for the OS upgrade?

A pre-upgrade snapshot will be taken so that, if required, your system can be restored to the previous Windows Server 2016 state.

## How will I receive notifications for these changes?

You will receive all notifications via in-app alerts, including schedules, downtime notifications, and completion confirmations.

## Can we request a different migration date?

Yes. You may request to reschedule your migration to a preferred date, subject to availability.

## How can we change or reschedule the migration date?

You can request a date change at any time by emailing spectrum-migration-support@trimble.com.

## Are there any licensing changes?

No. There are no changes to your existing licenses as part of either upgrade.

## What go-live support will be provided?

After each upgrade, you will be provided with a hypercare support period of 48–72 hours to promptly address any issues.

## What are the support hours during the upgrade window?

During the upgrade and hypercare period, support will be available as per your standard SLA with extended monitoring in place.

## Who should I contact for support?

For any questions, concerns, or rescheduling requests, you should contact spectrum-migration-support@trimble.com.
