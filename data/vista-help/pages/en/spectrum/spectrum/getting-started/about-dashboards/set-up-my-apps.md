# Set Up 'My' Apps

You can customize many apps to display information specific to you. Any dashboard app with 'My' in the title displays information unique to you.
To enable My apps, perform these steps:

1. Set up the contact.

1. Go to System
 Administration > Contacts.

1. Select New.

1. In the Contact name field, add a new
 contact for your operator.

1. On the Jobs tab, add some jobs to
 the contact.

1. Select OK to save.

1. Link the contact with the operator.

1. Go to System
 Administration > Security > Operator
 Maintenance.

1. In the Contact field, add the
 newly-created contact to your operator.

1. Set up the Dashboard.

1. On the Dashboard, select the Settings button and then add
 the My Jobs app to
 your dashboard. The jobs for which your operator is an associated contact will
 display.
