# Employee Earnings History Report

The Employee Earnings History Report lists history of all
 employee earnings (regular and overtime), federal income tax, FICA, and other taxes.
This report can be printed in summary for each employee, or in detail on every check stub
 printed for each employee. It may be generated from any pay period through any pay period.
 The report is printed in alphabetical sequence by employee code, and is available in three
 formats.
Any [related
 taxes](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/maintenance-overview/tax-table-maintenance/newedit-income-tax-details---properties-tab/newedit-tax-table---related-taxes-tab) set up for the employee code will be included in the state, county, and
 local amounts.
