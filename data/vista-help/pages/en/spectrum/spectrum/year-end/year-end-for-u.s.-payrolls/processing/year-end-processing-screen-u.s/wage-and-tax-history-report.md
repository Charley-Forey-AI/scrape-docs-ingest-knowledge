# Wage and Tax History Report

Use the Wage and Tax History Report to print wage and
 tax information for a given date range.
The Wage and Tax History Report provides income tax, workers' compensation,
 SDI/FICA, and unemployment figures. It is available both in summary and detail, and it may be
 sorted both by employee and by tax code.
It details earnings subject to each tax and the tax
 amounts at any level:

- state, for single-state operations

- federal

- county

- local municipalities

CPP Tax Exception Report
Use this modified version to identify employees who have over-accrued Tier 1 tax. Correct those amounts during the payroll cycle using the Cheque Adjustment screen.
Access this version using the My Reports button.

See also [Subject-to-Tax Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/year-end/year-end-for-u.s.-payrolls/processing/year-end-processing-screen-u.s./subject-to-tax-report).
