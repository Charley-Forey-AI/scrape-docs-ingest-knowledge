# Release Notes

Enhancements to the Payroll Time Entry (PTE) mobile app
 and web portal application.

## December 2022

Employees can now be removed from a particular job from within the PTE mobile app, in the Manage Job screen. Just as when *adding* an employee to a job, removing one affects the current job only.

Note: Check for and delete Timecards first.If the employee you delete has un-synced Timecard records, they will still sync to the portal the next time an app sync is performed.

## April 2022

- Non-admins (Co. Admins, Dept Mgrs, and PMs) can now bring
 new jobs, new equipment, and new employees from Spectrum into the portal without
 requiring a Full Sync by an admin, or even access to
 Administration.

- Manual updates are no longer required in the portal to
 remove inactive employees and equipment. Setting employee or equipment status to
 'Inactive' in Spectrum automatically removes them from the job(s) in the portal
 and the mobile app.Note: If a former employee is
 re-hired (made active again), the projects and jobs which were assigned to
 them - at the time they were made inactive - are still assigned. You can
 make any needed changes in the portal.

- If your organization has the option to exceed your
 contracted license count, the Project Managers tab now provides an alert message
 in red at any time that the current number of active managers exceeds that
 number. The alert calls your attention to the overage in case you want to change
 the status on any other licenses, and makes it clearer when you should
 anticipate receiving an invoice to cover the additional license
 usage.

- As a usability improvement to help prevent duplicate phases
 on a job, we've provided a visual indicator to signal that the process of
 updating phases has already been initiated. Once a user clicks the Update Phases button to sync
 changes made in Spectrum, the button is greyed out and disabled.

- A new option in the Company Settings screen prevents
 timecards from being edited or deleted if they have already been batched and
 posted to Spectrum. By default, and to match current functionality, the new
 Disable Edit Timecard When
 Batched checkbox is not selected.

- The [online Help for Payroll Time Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry) is now available on
 the Viewpoint Help website, help.viewpoint.com.

## February 2021

- The Payroll Time Entry mobile app is enhanced to restrict
 [entering time](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry/mobile-app-overview/about-entering-time) to the
 current pay period. This applies to labor, quantities, job clock, equipment
 time, and add-ons.

- The [Administration](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry/web-portal-overview/administration) screen in the web portal is enhanced with new
 options to limit time card entry to the current pay period only, and prevent
 copying the wage code and pay level when copying timecards from one employee to
 another. You can override the default setting for each job on the [Job Setup](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry/web-portal-overview/job-setup) screen.

- When new jobs are added in Payroll Time Entry, phases are
 synced automatically from Spectrum. A warning message appears in the [Job Setup](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry/web-portal-overview/job-setup) tab in the web
 portal to click the Update
 Phases button only when phases have changed in Spectrum.

## November 2020

- The [Enter Time](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry/mobile-app-overview/about-entering-time) page in the mobile app is enhanced to display
 Job-to-Date quantity.

- The [Signature Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry/mobile-app-overview/signature-entry) page in the mobile app is enhanced to display the
 Signature Agreement text entered on the [Administration](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/payroll-time-entry/web-portal-overview/administration) screen.

## March 2019

- The Payroll Time Entry web portal application is enhanced
 with a new Cloud Sync Configuration screen for PTE Admins. The Cloud Sync
 Configuration screen contains tabs for selecting companies for your tenant,
 appropriate equipment statuses, cost categories, and add-ons for your companies,
 and then syncs these selections from Spectrum to the PTE server.
