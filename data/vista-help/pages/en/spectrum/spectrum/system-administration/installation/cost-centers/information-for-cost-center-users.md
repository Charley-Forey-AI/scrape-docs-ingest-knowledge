# Information for Cost Center Users

For users who have the Enterprise Management module installed, the optional cost center feature allows companies with multiple divisions/locations to share certain company records with other divisions/locations without sharing all of their location-specific records. For example, while each data entry transaction belongs to a certain cost center, it may be viewable across selected cost centers, depending on the operator's cost center security.
Cost center information is provided throughout the Help, where applicable.
