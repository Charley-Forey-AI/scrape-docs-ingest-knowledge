# Spectrum BI and Team Reporting to Trimble Analytics Upgrade

As of March 31, 2025, the migration from Spectrum BI and
 Team Reporting to Trimble Analytics is
 officially complete. As of this release, Spectrum BI and Team Reporting have been disabled.

## What is this upgrade?

We have upgraded from Spectrum BI (powered by
 Hitachi Pentaho) and Team Reporting (powered by Hitachi Pentaho) to
 Trimble Analytics (powered by Microsoft
 Power BI).

## Why is this happening? What are the benefits?

Microsoft Power BI is a widely used technology. By powering
 our Analytics platform with Power BI, we can provide new capabilities that are not
 possible on the current platform. These new capabilities include:

- Use multiple data sets or "cubes" in a single
 report

- Control access to individual reports

- Create paginated (aka SSRS) reports

- A faster and more intuitive report building
 experience

- A more modern way to visualize your data

- A more attractive, modern interface

You will benefit from Microsoft's active development of Power BI and an existing, robust user community.
Moving forward, Trimble will focus development efforts and resources on one reporting platform: Trimble Analytics. This focus will improve the speed of product development and new features to better serve you.

## Is there a cost for this upgrade?

No, this upgrade does not have an additional cost. We are excited to offer you better technology, faster reporting, and more capabilities than before, with no extra cost.

## Is all of my data in Spectrum BI and Team
 Reporting available in Trimble Analytics?

Yes.

## Can I move reports over automatically?

No. Unfortunately, there is not a way to convert existing reports to Trimble Analytics automatically; however, creating reports is much faster in Power BI.

## Can I move custom measures to Trimble Analytics?

No. Custom measures are not currently available in Analytics. This
 functionality will be added at a later date. We will keep you updated when they are
 available. In the meantime, we recommend exporting your reports to Excel. This
 creates a handy guide for recreating the report, and also saves the formula for any
 custom measures so they can be added later.

## Is Trimble Analytics very different in look and in the way it's used,
 compared to Spectrum BI and Team
 Reporting? Will I need to learn how to use Trimble Analytics?

Trimble Analytics has an
 improved UI compared to Spectrum BI and Team Reporting. Like any new
 platform, users will have to learn it, but we provide resources to help
 you.
The [online Help](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:Analytics_000004:Analytics:Analytics) has more information.

## Is there training available?

Yes. The Trimble Construction Academy has courses prepared for you.

- For a comprehensive learning course including granting access and an
 overview of all of the tools available, see [Trimble Analytics](https://academy.viewpoint.com/learn/courses/4714/trimble-construction-one-analytics).

- For a course showing how to build a Power BI report using Spectrum BI data, see [Custom Power BI Reports through
