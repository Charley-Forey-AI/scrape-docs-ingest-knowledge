# Frequently Asked Questions

Read the answers to some of the most common questions about
 this module.

## Assign a Job's Resources

Follow these steps to assign resources to a job. Employees and pieces
 of equipment are considered resources; both or either resource type can be assigned to
 jobs.

- Navigate to Resource Scheduling  >  Data Entry  >  Schedule Employees and
 Equipment.

- Select a Resource
 type: Employees or Equipment.

- On the Resource listbox, select the employee or equipment code you wish to
 assign.

- On the Jobs listbox, select the desired job you wish to assign the resource
 to.

- Specify an assignment start date and time and end date that the
 resource will be assigned to the job.

- Click the Assign button.

- Once a resource is assigned to a job, the 'Assigned' column will
 reflect the change in the assignment. Click the Details button to view all
 resources assigned to this job and edit any assignments.

- When all resources have been assigned to the job, save and close
 this screen.

## Schedule Equipment Moves

Follow these steps to schedule equipment moves using the Build
 feature on the Schedule Equipment Moves screen.

- Navigate to Resource Scheduling  >  Data Entry  >  Schedule Equipment
 Moves.

- Click the Build
 button. The Build
 Unassigned Equipment Moves window displays.

- Complete the fields in the Selections section as needed for
 the move and then click Continue.

- Select one or more items from the Unassigned moves listbox on
 the left and a mover from the Movers listbox on the right.

- Specify a start date and time.

- Click the Assign
 button.

- When all moves are assigned, click the Report button.

- Preview the listing to confirm the moves.

## Unassign a Job's Resources

Follow these steps to unassign all of a job's resources (employee and
 equipment) for the selected date or date range.

- Navigate to Resource Scheduling  >  Data Entry  >  Schedule Employees and
 Equipment.

- On the Jobs listbox, select the desired job you wish to change. Click the
 Details button to view
 all resources assigned to the job.

- Specify a date range for the selected job.

- Click the Unassign button to remove all resources from the job for the
 specified date range.
