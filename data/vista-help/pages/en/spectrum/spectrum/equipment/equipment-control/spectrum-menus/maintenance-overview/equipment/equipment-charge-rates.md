# Equipment Charge Rates

Use this screen to add or change the job rates for a
 specific piece of equipment.
The current job assignment displays if the Resource Scheduling module is
 present, and an inquiry button (adjacent to the Currently scheduled field) will open the Resource Scheduling > Equipment Schedule History Inquiry window.
