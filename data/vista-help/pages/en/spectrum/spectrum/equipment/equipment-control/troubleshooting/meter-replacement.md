# Meter Replacement

Below is a frequently asked question regarding meter
 replacement.
Question:
When a meter is replaced on a piece of equipment due to damage or other causes, how do I keep the proper amount of hours/miles being accumulated in Preventive Maintenance and Equipment Control?
Answer:
Use the [Replace Meter](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/meter-reading-transaction-entry/replace-meter) window accessible from the Meter Transaction Entry screen to
 track equipment usage without interruption when a meter needs to be replaced. The window
 allows you to record information for the new (replacement) meter and the old (broken) meter
 separately.
