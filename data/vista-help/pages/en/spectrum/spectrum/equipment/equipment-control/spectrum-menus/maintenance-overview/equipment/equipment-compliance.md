# Equipment Compliance

Use this screen to add and maintain a list of equipment
 compliance codes and to view all compliance records, across equipment at-a-glance.
This allows you to easily maintain multiple compliance codes (such as licenses and
 renewals) in the current company, including codes that are expired or closed, even if you
 don't have security access to the main Equipment screen.

- Click New to display the [New Equipment Compliance Code](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-compliance/new-equipment-compliance-code) window and create a new code.

- Click Status to determine which types of records will display in the list box.

- Click Renewal to display the [Renew Equipment License](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-compliance/renew-equipment-license) window.
