# Fuel & Oil Report

The Fuel & Oil Report provides information based on quantity entries made in the Transaction Entry screen.
Use this report to analyze fuel and oil usage for any relevant date range, including quarterly reporting and multi-year date ranges. This report provides a convenient way to review equipment burn rate statistics based on equipment usage and fuel consumption over time. The report may be sorted by equipment codes (numerically, then alphabetically), or by equipment type.
Note: The unit of measure types (for example, Gallons vs. Liters) on
 this report depends on the lexicon specified in the Company Installation
 screen.
