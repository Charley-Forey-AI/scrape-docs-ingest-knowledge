# Equipment Revenue History Report

The Equipment Revenue History Report screen prints a
 transaction record of usage revenue sorted by piece of equipment.
It provides information about the jobs and phases on which the equipment
 was used, time used, and revenue from usage. Revenue from rental activity is also provided.
 The figures on this report provide the detail for information found on the Equipment
 Revenue Report. The Equipment Revenue Report provides revenue-to-date information through a
 specified period, but this report may be printed for any desired date range. Remarks added
 during revenue entry will be included on the report underneath the transaction.
