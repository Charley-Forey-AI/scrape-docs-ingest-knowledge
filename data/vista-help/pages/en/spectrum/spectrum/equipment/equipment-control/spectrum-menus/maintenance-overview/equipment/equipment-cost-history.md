# Equipment Cost History

The Equipment Cost History screen displays a record of all
 of the costs incurred and shows total revenue and total hours.
Additionally, it provides information on which employees worked on
 repairs, what parts might have been purchased through Accounts Payable, and any equipment
 control transaction entries. No changes are allowed.
This information is also available on the [Equipment Cost History Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-cost-history-report)

- Click Selections to display the Advanced Selections window where you can
 narrow your inquiry results by selecting a cost category, source, and transaction
 date range.

- Click Time
 Card to display the Payroll Time Card History window.

- Click Invoice to display the Accounts Payable Invoice History window.
