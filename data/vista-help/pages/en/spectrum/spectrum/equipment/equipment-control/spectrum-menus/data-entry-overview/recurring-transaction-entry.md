# Recurring Transaction Entry

The Recurring Transaction Entry screen is a speed-type feature used to enter recurring transactions for Equipment Usage (EU) and Equipment Standby (ES).
After completing the header portion of the screen, you will need to choose to enter recurring transaction information by Equipment or by Job.
After completing the Batch Frequency and Recurring # fields, click one of the available tabs (EU - Usage or ES- Standby).
