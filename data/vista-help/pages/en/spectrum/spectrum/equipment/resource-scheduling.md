# Resource Scheduling

The primary function of Resource Scheduling is to assist in
 the scheduling of equipment and employees.
Proper use of resources is key to overall company profitability. Without appropriate
 resources, jobs can be delayed which can delay payment from customers. Improper use of
 equipment can lead to events like having to rent a certain piece of equipment even when the
 company already owns one.
Here are just a few of the ways Resource Scheduling can help to simplify how you manage your employees and equipment.

- Allows you to schedule employees and equipment to crews and jobs to
 ensure all necessary resources are available.

- Allows you to schedule equipment moves from one job site to another

- Allows you to track the current and historical whereabouts of
 employees and equipment (using the provided reports)

The equipment, employee, vendor and job information used in Resource Scheduling originates in the Accounts Payable, Job Cost, and Equipment Control.
