# Set Up the Hilti ON!Track Integration in Spectrum

Setup for this integration requires enabling the Equipment
 Tracking module with Hilti ON!Track and creating
 Hilti-specific equipment type codes in Spectrum.
Important: Verify that you meet
 the following requirements for the integration:

- Trimble Construction One
 subscription to Spectrum

- Spectrum version 2023 R1 or higher

- Enterprise ID for your company on the Trimble Platform

- Spectrum Equipment Control and Equipment Tracking
 modules configured

- Access to Hilti ON!Track Pro Plus

Complete the following setup tasks:

1. [Enable Hilti ON!Track in Equipment Tracking Installation](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/hilti-ontrack-integration-with-spectrum/set-up-the-hilti-ontrack-integration-in-spectrum/enable-hilti-ontrack-in-equipment-tracking-installation)

1. [Create a Hilti Equipment Type](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/hilti-ontrack-integration-with-spectrum/set-up-the-hilti-ontrack-integration-in-spectrum/create-a-hilti-equipment-type)
