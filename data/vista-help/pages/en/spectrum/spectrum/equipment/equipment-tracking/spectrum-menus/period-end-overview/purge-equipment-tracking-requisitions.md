# Purge Equipment Tracking Requisitions

The purge equipment tracking requisitions screen purges
 Equipment Tracking transactions from the history file by deleting transactions for a selected
 job.

1. At the Purge through requisition date field enter the date through which transaction records are to be purged.

1. Click Continue. A confirmation window displays when the purge is complete.
