# Importing Equipment Requisitions into Equipment Tracking Requisition

Learn how to create requisitions (Issue, Return) for one
 job at a time.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Equipment Tracking  >  Data Entry  >  Equipment Tracking.

1. At the Requisition field, press Enter.

1. Complete the remaining header fields.

1. Click the Import button.

1. Browse to locate the import file and click the Upload button. If there are any errors in the import, the Exception Listing window or the Equipment Tracking Exception Report will display.
