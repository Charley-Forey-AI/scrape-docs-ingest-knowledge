# Adding a New Trigger

Create software triggers to automate specific actions in
 Human Resources, such as warnings, email notifications, and updates to data
 fields.
Spectrum's Add Trigger Wizard window
 guides you through the process of setting up the trigger and its parameters. See [Creating Triggers](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/hr-maintenance-overview/triggers-maintenance/creating-triggers) for details on trigger types,
 actions, and examples.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources  >  Maintenance  >  Triggers.For field descriptions, see [Triggers Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/hr-maintenance-overview/triggers-maintenance).

1. On the Triggers Maintenance
 screen, click the New button.The system will open the New Trigger window and will
 automatically begin the wizard by displaying the Tracking Item
 window.

1. Highlight the item you want to track (in this example,
 Driver's license) and click OK.

1. In the Values section of the
 screen, the software will display your selection. In our example, the software
 displays the What item would you like to track prompt. The prompts that display in
 this section will vary depending on the type of trigger you have selected. Press
 Enter to accept your selection.The software will display the Select Trigger window.

1. Highlight the trigger you want and click
 OK.

1. In the Values section of the
 screen, the software will display your selection in the What event should activate
 this trigger field. Press Enter to accept your
 selection.The software will display the Select Action window.

1. Highlight the action you want and click
 OK.

1. In the Values section of the
 screen, the software will display your selection as a field, for example, What action
 should take place on trigger. Press Enter to accept your
 selection.Note: More than one field may display depending on the trigger
 you selected in step 4.

The system will display the What value field.

1. Type the trigger's value, and press
 Enter.Note: The value you enter will display in the
 Description field once you press
 Enter.

1. In the Values section of the
 screen, select whether the trigger applies to All employees or
 Employees of a specific supervisor, and press
 Enter. Your selection will display in the
 Description field.Note: If you select the latter option, you must enter a
 supervisor code in the Supervisor field that displays.
 Press F4 to search for a supervisor code, if
 necessary.

1. If you want this trigger to expire at a certain point
 in time, in the Values section of the screen, select the
 Discontinue use of this trigger on a specified date
 checkbox. If you do not want to apply an expiration date to this trigger, leave this
 checkbox clear. Press Enter.

1. If you select to apply an expiration date, enter this
 date in the Expiration date field.Note: The expiration date you enter will display in the
 Description field.

1. At the Warning message field, type the warning message you want to display when the trigger is activated, for example, "Driver's License has expired" and press Enter.

1. The system will display the
 Options window prompting you to Save and
 exit, Save and add next, Edit
 current, or Cancel. Make your selection to
 complete the Add Triggers Wizard. Your new trigger will
 display in the Triggers Maintenance screen.
