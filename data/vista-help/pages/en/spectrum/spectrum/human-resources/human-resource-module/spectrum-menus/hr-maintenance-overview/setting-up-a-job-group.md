# Setting Up a Job Group

Create Human Resources job group codes and descriptions
 that are used to help categorize Job Title Maintenance.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources >  Maintenance >  Job Groups.

1. Click the New button.Note: For field descriptions, see [Job Group Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/hr-maintenance-overview/job-group-maintenance).

1. Complete the New Job Group
 window and click OK.

1. To print a listing of any or all job groups in the
 software, click the Listing button.
