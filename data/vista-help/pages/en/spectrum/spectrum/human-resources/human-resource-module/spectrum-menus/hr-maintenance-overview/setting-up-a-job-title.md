# Setting Up a Job Title

Find how to set up a job title.
Before setting up job titles, be sure that you have
 established all of your [Job Group Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/hr-maintenance-overview/job-group-maintenance). Once you have a set up job titles in the software, job titles
 can be grouped together using Job Group Maintenance.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources >  Maintenance >  Job Titles.

1. Click the New button.Note: For field descriptions, see [Job Title Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/hr-maintenance-overview/job-title-maintenance).

1. Complete the Properties,
 Qualifications, and Salary tab and
 click OK.

1. Repeat step 3 for each additional job title you are
 adding to the software.

1. To print a listing of any or all job titles in the
 software, click the Listing button.
