# Accounts Payable

Accounts Payable provides control over all cash payments. The module provides immediate access to vendor and subcontract status and includes control over subcontract change orders.
A few of its capabilities are:

- Retention

- Joint checks

- Multiple bank accounts

- Automatic invoice reversal

- Automatic Invoice entry (for Trimble Construction One organizations only)

Accounts Payable is integrated with Document Imaging to scan and retrieve vendor documents, tracking, and invoices.
