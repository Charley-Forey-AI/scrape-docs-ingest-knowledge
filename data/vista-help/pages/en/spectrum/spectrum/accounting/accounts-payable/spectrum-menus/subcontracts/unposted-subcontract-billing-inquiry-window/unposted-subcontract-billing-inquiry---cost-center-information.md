# Unposted Subcontract Billing Inquiry - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, all invoices associated with the designated subcontract will display in this window and will be included in the financial calculations, even if the cost center assigned to the invoice is not allowed for the operator.
