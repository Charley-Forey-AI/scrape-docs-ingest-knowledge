# Miscellaneous Questions

## When does an A/P subcontract change order become part of committed costs?

The date entered in the Proposal date field will determine when the change order becomes part of open commitments.

## Which date controls when the change order becomes part of the contract?

The Date issued field determines the date when the change order becomes part of the contract.

## Which date controls when an executed change request (action types 1 or 2) become part of the contract?

The Approved date determines the date when the change order becomes part of the contract. Of course, the change request must have a status with action types 1 or 2.
