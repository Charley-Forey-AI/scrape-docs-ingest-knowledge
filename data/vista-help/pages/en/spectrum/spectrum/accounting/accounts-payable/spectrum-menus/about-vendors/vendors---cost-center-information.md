# Vendors - Cost Center Information

When a new vendor is added, you may assign any active cost centers to the vendor, including one that your operator will not be authorized for future access.
When editing, viewing, or deleting a vendor, Spectrum compares the vendor's list of shared cost centers with the list of cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme. If there are no common cost centers, then the vendor code does not display.
