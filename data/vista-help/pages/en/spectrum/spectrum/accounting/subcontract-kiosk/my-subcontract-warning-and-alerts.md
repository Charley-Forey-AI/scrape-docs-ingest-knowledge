# My Subcontract Warning and Alerts

The My Subcontract Warning and Alerts app displays open
 compliance items among subcontracts for the Subcontract Kiosk user.
Click on any of the items to open the [Submit Documentation](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/subcontract-kiosk/my-subcontract-warning-and-alerts/submit-documentation) screen in context. Hover over
 an item to show the job number associated with the subcontract and the due date of the
 compliance item.
