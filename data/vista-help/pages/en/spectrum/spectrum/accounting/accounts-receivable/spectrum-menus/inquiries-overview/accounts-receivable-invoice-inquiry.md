# Accounts Receivable Invoice Inquiry

Use this window to view general financial information about the invoice (including current outstanding balances), distribution information, customer information, shipping information, invoice terms information, and any user-defined information for the selected invoice.
Note: No changes are allowed in this window, except to add
 internal notes or attach images.
