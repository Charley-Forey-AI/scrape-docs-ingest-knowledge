# Warning - Change Orders Later Than 'Period To' Date Are Not Shown.

This warning informs you that there are change orders with executed dates past the current draw request's period to date.
For example, assume that the current draw has a period to date of 6/23/23 and a change order with an executed date of 6/30/23. Because the date on the change order is later than the draw's date, this message displays and the date doesn't appear on this screen. If the period to date is changed to 6/30/23, the error goes away and the change order displays on the list.
The application date is used as the invoice date for the draw's invoice. The period to date is used as the invoice's G/L date.
