# Time + Material - Assign Labor Billing Codes to Employees

The system automatically applies these rates when time is
 entered against a T+M job.
If you [Time + Material - Set Up T+M Labor Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/time--material---getting-started/time--material---set-up-tm-labor-billing-rates),
 assign these special billing codes to employees who work on Time + Material jobs. If you
 use special labor billing rates and do not assign them to employees, you will have to enter
 T+M rate information manually on each relevant time card.

1. Select Payroll >  Employees >  Employees.

1. Select an Employee.

1. In the Info Bar, select Properties >  Pay Rates.For field descriptions, see [Employee Pay Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/employees/employee-properties/employee-pay-rates).

1. Click Edit.

1. In Billing code, select the labor billing code to
 assign to this employee. For example, L1.

1. Click OK.

1. Click Save.
