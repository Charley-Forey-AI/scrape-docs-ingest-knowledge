# Time + Material - Update Time + Material Billings

Use Update Time + Material billing to transfer billing data
 to Accounts Receivable.
The system creates sequential invoice numbers based on the
 next available invoice number in the Accounts Receivable module. Selected bills are removed
 from the Detail file and stored in the Time + Material History file.

1. Select Time + Material > Reports > Update T+M Billings.

1. Select a job, or enter All for all jobs.

1. Enter an A/R invoice batch number.For field descriptions, see [Update T+M Billings](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/update-tm-billings).

1. Click OK. The grid displays
 billing details, including job and customer name, billing number and total, invoice
 date, and G/L date.

1. For each transaction, select the
 Update? checkbox to transfer that billing to Accounts
 Receivable.

1. Click Continue.

- The system creates and displays an A/R invoice for the
 billing amount.

- The Sales Journal/Update tab opens automatically.

1. On the Sales Journal / Update
 tab, click Preview.

1. Review report details to ensure all entries are
 correct as shown.

1. Click Continue to post
 invoice entries to Accounts Receivable.

1. For a billing report history, select Time + Material > Reports > Cost Type Billing History or Detail Billing History.
