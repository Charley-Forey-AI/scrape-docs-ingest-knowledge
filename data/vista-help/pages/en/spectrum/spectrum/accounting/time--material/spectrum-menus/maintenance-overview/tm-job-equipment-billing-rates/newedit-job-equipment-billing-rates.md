# New/Edit Job Equipment Billing Rates

Use this window to add or edit job equipment Billing codes
 and up to five pay rate levels.
Fields
Descriptions

Job
Enter a job number. The job description will display, as
 well as all fields linked to the job.

Customer
Based on the job number entered, the Customer code and
 name display.

Billing code
Enter or search for the equipment Billing code. The
 billing code can be up to 10 characters.

Description
Enter the Time + Material equipment Billing rate
 description. The default Billing Rate description will display from
 Standard Equipment Billing Rates Maintenance.

Rates

Level
Enter the job equipment billing rate for levels 1 through
 5 in each of the three rate type columns: Regular, Overtime and Double time.
 The level number refers to the equipment billing rates entered in the
 Equipment Billing Rates Maintenance screen.

Stand-by rates
Enter stand-by job equipment billing rates for Hourly, Daily, Weekly and Monthly periods. These
 rates will only be posted to Equipment Control > Revenue Entry.
