# Rebuild a T+M Job

If a job's billing type was originally set up incorrectly
 in the Job Cost > Job File Maintenance > Properties window and the error is discovered after a portion of the work is complete and
 billed for, it may be necessary to rebuild the job based on new default billing
 settings.
If you need further information, you can contact Viewpoint Customer
 Support by visiting the [Viewpoint Customer Portal](https://support.viewpoint.com/s/).
