# Job Billing/Cost Analysis Report

Use this report to analyze billing versus cost values on a
 phase-by-phase basis. The report breaks billing values down into three groups: Pre-billing,
 Billing in Progress, and Billing History. Then it compares the billing totals with actual Job
 Cost history on a phase-by-phase basis, and calculates the variance and percent variance
 between the billing and cost figures.
In the Variance filter section, you can select to show only specific job
 phases based on a designated variance amount or percent. This allows you to easily sort
 phases that are not currently being billed, or those with too little markup. The report
 also allows you to conveniently identify phases with the highest profitability.
