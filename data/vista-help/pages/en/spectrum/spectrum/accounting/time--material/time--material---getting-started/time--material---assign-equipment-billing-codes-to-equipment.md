# Time + Material - Assign Equipment Billing Codes to
 Equipment

The system automatically applies these rates when equipment
 usage hours are entered against a T+M job.
If you [Time + Material - Set Up T+M Equipment Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/time--material---getting-started/time--material---set-up-tm-equipment-billing-rates),
 assign these special rate codes to equipment in the Equipment Control module.

1. Select Equipment Control >  Equipment >  Equipment.

1. Select an Equipment code.

1. Click Edit.For field descriptions, see [Equipment Main Properties](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-main-properties).

1. In the Current defaults area, select the Billing code to assign to this piece of equipment.

1. Click OK.

1. Click Save.
