# Job Equipment Billing Rates Listing

You can use the Job Equipment Billing Rates Listing report
 to view special billing rates.
Use this report to view special billing rates set up for specific jobs
 in [T+M Job Equipment Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/job-overview/tm-job-equipment-billing-rates). The listing
 prints in job number, then billing code sequence.
