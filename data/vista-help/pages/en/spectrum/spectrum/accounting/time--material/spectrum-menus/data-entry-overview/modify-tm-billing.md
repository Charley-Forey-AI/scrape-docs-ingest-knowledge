# Modify T+M Billing

Use this screen to inquire about or modify billing detail. The screen shows the complete detail of a bill based on the selection criteria entered. You are able to modify these line items. Sales tax and retention is recalculated for each billing accessed in this screen, based on current percentages and billing amounts.
