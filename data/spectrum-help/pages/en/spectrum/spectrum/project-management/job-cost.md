# Job Cost

Use the Job Cost module to track costs, wages, time expenditures, and other variables that need to be attributed to a job in progress. It also provides the project manager all of the information needed to keep jobs on track, spot potential overruns, and provide historical cost information by creating a Date sensitive, work-in-process schedule as needed.
Job Cost is integrated with Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Payroll, Work Order, Inventory Control, Equipment Control, Project Setup, General Ledger, Purchase Order, Time + Material, Materials Management, Equipment Tracking, Confidential Payroll, Cash Management, Project Management, and Small Tools. In addition, Job Cost is integrated with Document Imaging to scan and retrieve job documents.
