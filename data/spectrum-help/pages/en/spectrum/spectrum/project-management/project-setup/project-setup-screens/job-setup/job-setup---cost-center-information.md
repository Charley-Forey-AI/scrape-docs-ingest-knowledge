# Job Setup - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, you may assign phase level
 cost centers in the Job Setup entry screen or during the Project Setup import update.
You may also import cost centers for billing detail entries and edit
 cost centers in the Billing Detail Setup screen and then these
 cost centers will be updated to Job Cost > Phase and Accounts Receivable > Contract Detail. The Update Project Setup also allows you to set
 the contract cost center equal to the job's cost center.
