# Report Calculations - Job Productivity Report (Projected Cost = Yes)

This report prints the projected hours. If no projected hours are entered, then the report will calculate the hours as it normally does.
Field
Description

Actual Cost
The actual job cost amount for the selected period, as well as the job-to-date amount, through the year and period displays.

Units to Complete
The estimated number of units necessary to complete the job display. Units are dollars, labor hours, and square feet. Units to complete are calculated as the difference between the "projected" and "actual job-to-date" figures ("projected" is the next column, "job-to-date" is the previous column).

Projected
The total cost projected at completion displays from Job Phases.

Estimate
The current estimated amount displays from Job Phases.

Variance
Variance is calculated as the difference between the two preceding columns; that is, the projected amount less the estimated amount.

% Variance
Percent variance is calculated as the quotient of the two preceding columns; that is, the variance amount divided by the estimate amount, multiplied by 100.

% Complete
Percent complete is calculated as the actual job-to-date amount divided by the Projected cost, multiplied by 100.
