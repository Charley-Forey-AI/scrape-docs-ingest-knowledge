# Projected Cost Worksheet - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when printing the Projected Cost Worksheet Spectrum includes the job only if you have permission to access the job information. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then entries for that job will not be shown. phases for authorized jobs display on the worksheet.
When your scheme includes override settings for job entries in Cost Center Scheme Maintenance, the Projected Cost Worksheet validates the cost center assigned to jobs based on these overrides. The override cost center(s) supersede the cost center list defined for the scheme in general. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned in the entry screen with job override cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, the job will not display.
