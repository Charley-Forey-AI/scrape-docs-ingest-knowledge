# Report Calculations - Job Profitability Report (Projected Cost = No)

The following table describes how each column is calculated on this report.
Field
Description

Billed To Date
The total job-to-date billed amount through the specified year and period displays.

% Complete
Percent complete is calculated as job-to-date actual cost divided by the estimated cost.

Actual Cost To Date
The total job-to date actual cost amount through the specified year and period displays.

Estimated Cost
The revised estimate displays from Jobs.

Gross Profit
The gross profit is calculated as the difference between the billed-to-date amount and the actual cost-to-date amount.

% Profit
This percent profit column is calculated as the gross profit divided by the billed-to-date amount, multiplied by 100.

Estimated Profit
Estimated profit is calculated as the difference between the contract amount and the estimated cost.

% Profit
This percent profit column is calculated as the estimated profit divided by the contract amount, multiplied by 100.

Variance
Variance is calculated as the difference between the gross profit and the estimated profit.
