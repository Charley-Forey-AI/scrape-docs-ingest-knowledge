# Job Overview Report

You can preview the Job Overview Report either with or without projected costs. The Crystal-Link Cost Variance Reports print each job on a separate screen; the information is listed in sequence by Cost type group (Materials, Labor, Subcontract, Equipment and two other user-defined groups). Information is listed by phase within each group. The Hours Variance report shows only the Labor and Equipment cost type groups.
