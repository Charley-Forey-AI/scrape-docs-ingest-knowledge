# Set Projected Contract Quantities

Use this utility to reset the Update projected contract quantity  checkbox in Job Phases for selected unit price jobs and specified cost types. The projected contract amount will be used for these unit price jobs on all the Contract Status Reports.
The projected contract amount is often the more accurate contract amount that will be billed for the job because the original contract amount rarely reflects the true quantities that are being installed in the job.
