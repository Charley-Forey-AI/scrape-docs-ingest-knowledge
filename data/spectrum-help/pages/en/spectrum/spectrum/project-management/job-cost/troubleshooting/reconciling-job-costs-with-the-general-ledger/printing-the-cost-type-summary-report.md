# Printing the Cost Type Summary Report

You can print the Cost Type Summary Report.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Job Cost  >  Period End  >  Cost  > Type Summary Report.

1. In the Selections portion of the screen, enter the necessary information. You can press Enter at any field to accept the system default of ALL.

1. In the Report type section of the screen, select Summary report.

1. In the Totals portion of the screen, selectYear-to-date.

1. Click Print.
