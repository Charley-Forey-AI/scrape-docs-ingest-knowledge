# Job Billing History Report - Cost Center Information

If your company uses cost centers, when printing the Billing History Report Spectrum will include activity only if you have permission to access the job. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the job will not display.
