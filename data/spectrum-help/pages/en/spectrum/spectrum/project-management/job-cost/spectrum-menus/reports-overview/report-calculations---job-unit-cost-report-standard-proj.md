# Report Calculations - Job Unit Cost Report (Standard, Proj. Cost = Yes)

The following tables describes how each column is calculated on this report.

## Field Descriptions

Field
Description

Qty
The period-to-date, projected, and estimated quantities display from Job Phases. Variance is calculated as the difference between projected and actual. Percent complete is calculated as period-to-date divided by projected, multiplied by 100.

UM
The unit of measure UM displays for the phase.

## Unit Costs

Field
Description

Job To Date
The job to date unit costs are calculated as total job to date costs divided by job to date quantity and display for Labor, Materials, Subcontracts, Equipment, and Other cost types.

Projected
The projected unit costs are calculated as total projected costs divided by projected quantity and display for Labor, Materials, Subcontracts, Equipment, and Other cost types.

Estimate
The estimated unit costs are calculated as total estimated costs divided by estimated quantity and display for Labor, Materials, Subcontracts, Equipment, and Other cost types.

## Total Costs

Field
Description

Job To Date
The total job to date costs display for Labor, Materials, Subcontracts, Equipment, and Other cost types.

Projected
The projected costs display for Labor, Materials, Subcontracts, Equipment, and Other cost types.

Estimate
The estimated costs display from Job Phases for Labor, Materials, Subcontracts, Equipment, and Other cost types.

Unit price jobs
Unit price jobs are calculated as the job-to-date quantity multiplied by variance unit cost.

## Variance

Field
Description

Unit Costs
Variance in unit cost for the five listed cost types is calculated as the difference between the job-to-date unit cost and job-to-date projected cost.

Total Costs
Variance in total cost for the five listed cost types is calculated as the difference between projected total costs and job-to-date total costs.
