# Adding Non-Payroll Overhead Costs to a Job

Overhead is an optional, additional allocation that may be
 applied to a job.
The job overhead allocation batch code is used in
 conjunction with the Overhead Allocation
 Maintenance screen and update to post overhead by batch code. You can assign
 a batch code to a job in the Job Cost > Maintenance > Job > Properties > Overhead window.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Job Cost  >  Maintenance  >  Jobs.

1. In the Job field, enter the job number, or press F4 or double-click on this field to
 select from a list of available job numbers.

1. On the InfoBar, click the Job Setup link.

1. In the Job overhead batch field, enter the batch code, or press F4 or double-click on this field to
 select from a list of available batch codes and then click Save.

1. Click Job Cost  >  Maintenance  >  Overhead >  Allocation to open the Overhead
 Allocation Maintenance screen to enter the job allocation batch.

1. Click New and enter the Batch
 code field, enter the batch code used in the Job Setup screen.

1. Enter a reference number and description for the
 overhead cost.

1. In the Type field, enter P for Percent or R for Rate.

1. In the Basis field (if you entered P in the Type field), enter C for costs (from the Accounts
 Payable, Payroll, or Inventory Control modules) or B for billings (from the Accounts
 Receivable or Time + Material modules). --OR-- In the Basis field (if you entered R in the Type field), enter R for Regular, O for Overtime, D for Double time, V for Vacation, S for Sick, H for Holiday, or A for All.

1. If you entered C in the Basis field, enter up to ten cost
 types in the Cost types
 field.

1. Complete the Rate, Phase (if applicable), Ct (where posting the costs to),
 G/L Debit (must be a
 direct cost account), and G/L credit
 (cannot be a direct cost account), and Basis phase fields.

1. Click OK until you return to the Site Map screen.

1. Click Job Cost  >  Data Entry  >  Overhead  > Allocation.

1. In the Transaction date field, enter the date that the overhead allocation
 will post to the General Ledger.

1. Complete the Batchcode, Job number, Job status, and Division fields.

1. The From
 date and To
 date fields default to the first day of the current month and the
 current date.

1. Select the appropriate job status and then click
 Continue.

1. On the Site Mapscreen, click Job Cost  >  Data Entry  >  Job Cost  > Transaction.

1. Click Update Job and make the appropriate selections for dates, sorting,
 and transaction type.

1. Click Preview and then download, archive, print or email the report and
 click Continue, following
 the same steps for updating the general ledger.Once you run the update, overhead costs for the selected dates
 are flagged as updated in Spectrum.
