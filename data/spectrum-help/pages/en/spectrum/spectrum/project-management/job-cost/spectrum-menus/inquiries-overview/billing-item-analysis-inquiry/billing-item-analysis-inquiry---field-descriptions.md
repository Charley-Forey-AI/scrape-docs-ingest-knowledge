# Billing Item Analysis Inquiry - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Customer code Job code
The customer and job codes and descriptions display.

Fiscal year
The fiscal year corresponding to the entered date displays.

Period
The fiscal period corresponding to the entered date displays.

Job-to-date and Projected summary totals

Revenue
Job-to-date and projected total revenue for the job display.

Cost
Job-to-date and projected total cost for the job display.

Gain (loss)
The difference between revenue and cost displays for both job-to-date and projected dollar values.

JTD Revenue
Click to view both job-to-date and projected total revenue for the selected billing line item.

JTD Costs
This amount includes unposted costs. Click to view phase detail information for the selected billing item line item, including a phase description and cost.

List box

Billing item
Description
The billing item number and description display. The list box is primarily sorted by billing code.

Um
Contract quantity
Contract unit $
Contract amt
The unit of measure for the billing item, contract quantity, unit price, and amount display.

JTD quantity
The billing item quantity for the job-to-date displays.

JTD revenue
The billing item revenue amount for the job-to-date displays.

JTD cost
The billing item cost amount for the job-to-date displays (includes unposted costs).

JTD gain/loss
The job-to-date gain or loss amount displays.

Proj quantity
The projected billing item quantity displays.

Proj revenue
 The projected revenue is a great management tool for estimating the revenue from change orders that are approved, or even proposed.

Proj cost
The projected cost amount displays.

Proj gain/loss
The projected amount of gain or loss displays.
