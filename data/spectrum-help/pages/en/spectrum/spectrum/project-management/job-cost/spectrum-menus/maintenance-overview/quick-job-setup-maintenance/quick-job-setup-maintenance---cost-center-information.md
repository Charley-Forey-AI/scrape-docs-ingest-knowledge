# Quick Job Setup Maintenance - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, you can also select cost centers assigned to job files. When adding or editing a Quick Job Setup code, Spectrum permits you to assign a 'copy from job' only if you have permission to access the job. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme. If the cost center is not included, then that job cannot be assigned as the source job in the Quick Job Setup screen. If you attempt to access a job group that is assigned to a disallowed job, an error displays.
