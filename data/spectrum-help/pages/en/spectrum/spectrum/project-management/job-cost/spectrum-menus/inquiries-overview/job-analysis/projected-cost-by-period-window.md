# Projected Cost by Period window

Use this window to view the projected cost by period for this contract. The projected cost history displays based on the maximum date entered in the J/C Processing Date Change screen; the current maximum processing date displays on the first line. This window only displays the 12 periods of projected history and therefore may not be the same as the number on the main screen. If the selected job is a Master job, master and sub-job totals will be included in projected totals.
Entry in this window is not allowed. Once you have finished viewing the necessary information, click OK to return to the previous window.
Field
Description

Year
The year for this period displays.

Period
The period number displays.

Period name
The period month displays.

Projected change
The total projected change in revenue for each phase displays.

Total project cost
The total of the projected cost for this contract displays.
The amount in this field may not be equal to the total for the projected contract under the estimated costs in the Contract Profit Summary window; this is due to the Projected Cost by Period window displaying information up to the Job Cost maximum processing date while the estimated costs are on a job-to-date basis.

History
Click this button to open the [Projected Cost History Detail](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/inquiries-overview/job-analysis/projected-cost-history-detail) window.
