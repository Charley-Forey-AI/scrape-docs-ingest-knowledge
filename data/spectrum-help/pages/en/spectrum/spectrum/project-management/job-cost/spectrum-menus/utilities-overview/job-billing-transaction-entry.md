# Job Billing Transaction Entry

Use the Billing Transaction Entry screen to enter customer billing information into
 Job Cost. This screen is typically only used for conversion.
For companies that do not have Accounts Receivable installed, this screen is used to record customer invoices, allowing billing amounts to be added to the job. Billing amounts recorded in this screen are included in the billed-to-date total appearing on the [Contract Status Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/reports-overview/contract-status-report), and is used to compute the over/under billing value.
After invoices have been sent to the customer, this entry screen allows distribution of the invoice amount to the correct job for month-to-date billing information. This information is required for the Contract Status Report in order to calculate the over/under billed for the period.
Important: The billing information entered in this screen
 does not update to Accounts Receivable or General Ledger.
If customer invoices are generated through A/R, all corrections and
 adjustments should be recorded in Accounts Receivable > Invoice Entry or General Ledger > Journal Entry. This screen should only be used during conversion and under the direction
 of the Viewpoint Support department.
