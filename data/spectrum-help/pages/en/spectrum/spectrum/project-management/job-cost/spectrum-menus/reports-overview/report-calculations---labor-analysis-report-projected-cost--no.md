# Report Calculations - Labor Analysis Report (Projected Cost = No)

Field
Description

Hours Estimated
The current estimated hours display from Job Phases.

Hours JTD
The actual job-to-date labor hours through the year and period display.

Hours Variance
The hours variance is calculated as the difference between the two preceding columns; that is, job-to-date hours less estimated hours.

Labor Estimate
The current estimated labor costs display from Job Phases.

Labor Job-to-Date
The actual job-to-date labor costs through the year and period display.

Cost Variance
Cost variance of labor is calculated as the difference between the two preceding columns; that is, actual labor to-date less the estimated labor cost.

Est $/HR
Estimated dollars per hour are calculated as the quotient of the first and fourth columns; that is, the labor estimated cost divided by the hours estimate.

JTD $/HR
Job-to-date rate per hour is calculated as the quotient of the second and fifth columns; that is, the actual labor cost divided by the actual hours.

$/HR Variance
Dollars per hour variance is calculated as the difference of the two preceding columns; that is, the job-to-date cost per hour less the estimated cost per hour.
