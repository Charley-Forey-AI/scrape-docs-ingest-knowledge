# Open Commitments window

The Open Commitments window displays detailed information
 for any open commitments related to the selected cost type.
These are costs that have been committed to, but not yet incurred. Examples include
 purchase orders that have been placed but not yet received, subcontracts that have been
 signed but not yet completely invoiced, or inventory job requisitions that have been
 entered but not updated.
The purpose of committed costs is to let the project manager know as early in the project
 as possible what costs are fixed (for example, what money has already been spent even
 though the invoices have not yet been received). If the selected job is a Master job,
 master and sub-job totals are included in commitment totals.
Note: This window is available only if data displays in the Committed cost field in Job Analysis.

Purchase orderThe purchase order number displays for transactions that include a purchase order.
Select the drop-down arrow to view the Purchase Order Commitments window and review purchase order numbers, vendor codes, received amounts, and more.
The information shown in this window matches the data in the Purchase Order Status Report. The Purchase Order Status Report doesn't include 'use tax' in the 'Remaining Amount' column.

SubcontractsThe Ordered, Received, and Open amounts for this phase's subcontracts display. The Received amount will be negative if an over-shipment was received.
Select the drop-down arrow to display the Subcontract Commitments window and review subcontract numbers, original contract and change order amounts, billed-to-date amounts, and more.

Job requisitionsThe Ordered, Received, and Open amounts for this phase's job requisitions display. The Received amount will be negative if an over-shipment was received.
Select the drop-down arrow to display the Job Requisitions Commitments window and review requisition numbers, item codes and descriptions, warehouses, open commitments, and more.

Total commitmentThe ordered, received, and open totals for this job's purchase order(s), subcontracts, and job requisitions display.
