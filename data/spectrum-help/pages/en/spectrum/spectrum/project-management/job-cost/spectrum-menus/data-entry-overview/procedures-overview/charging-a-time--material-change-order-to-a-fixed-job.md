# Charging a Time + Material Change Order to a Fixed Job

If you need to record a Time + Materials change order to a
 fixed price job, complete the following steps.
The following procedure assumes that you are creating
 additional phases to track this Time + Material activity.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Job Cost >  Maintenance >  Phases.

1. Complete the Job code field and the
 Job Phases screen displays.

1. Click New. Complete the fields.

1. In the Info Bar, select Properties > T+M Billing Setup.

1. Complete the relevant fields in the Properties and Billing details portions of the screen.

1. In the Markups window, complete the Ct, Description, Type, and Amount fields for each markup (if applicable) and then click OK twice.

1. In the Add-ons window, complete the Add-on, Description, Rate, and Costtype fields for each add-on (if applicable) and then click OK twice.

1. In the Fees window, complete the Description, Type, Base on, Cost type, and Taxable fields (if applicable) and then click OK until you return to the Site Map screen.

1. The following steps are optional. Complete them only
 if you want to record that there were estimates and a change order.

1. On the Site Map
 screen, click Accounts
 Receivable >  Data Entry > Change Order Entry.

1. Complete the Job,
 Customer, and
 Change order
 fields.

1. Click the Add CR
 button.

1. In the Add Change Requests to
 Change Order window, select the change request(s) you want to
 include on this change order.

1. Click OK until you
 return to the Site
 Map screen.
