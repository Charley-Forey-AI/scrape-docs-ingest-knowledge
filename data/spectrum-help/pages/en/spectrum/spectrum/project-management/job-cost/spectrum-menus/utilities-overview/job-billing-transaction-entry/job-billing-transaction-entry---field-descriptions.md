# Job Billing Transaction Entry - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing fields on this screen.
Fields
Descriptions

Job
Enter a valid job number. The job number must have been previously set up
 under Jobs. The job name will also display.

Invoice number
Enter the invoice number. This is for informational purposes and is printed on the [Job Billing History Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/reports-overview/job-billing-history-report). The invoice number is also used as a selection criterion on the selection screen for the Job Billing History Report.

Description
Enter a billing description. This is for informational purposes and is printed on the Job Billing History Report.

Invoice date
Enter the invoice date, use date shortcuts, or click the drop-down arrow to search calendar dates. This is the actual invoice date and is for informational purposes. The invoice date is available for selection on the billing history selection screen. This date may be different from the G/L period date due to month-end cutoff procedures.

G/L period-end date
Enter the date for the appropriate General Ledger period, use date shortcuts, or click the drop-down arrow to search calendar dates. This is the date used for the Billing Transaction Report and corresponding update screen; this entry will not update to the General Ledger, regardless of the date entered here.

Invoice amount
Enter the invoice amount. This is the actual amount of the invoice that is to be distributed to this job. This amount will update the month-, year-, and job-to-date billing amounts in the job file.
