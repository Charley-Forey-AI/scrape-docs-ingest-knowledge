# Earned Revenue Update

Learn about the Earned Revenue update.
Once you have previewed the update and it is accurate, click Continue to proceed. This feature updates the billing balance and revenue history files with earned revenues entered in the [Earned Revenue](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/earned-revenue) screen.
Important: Do not continue until the Earned Revenue Update
 has printed correctly.
