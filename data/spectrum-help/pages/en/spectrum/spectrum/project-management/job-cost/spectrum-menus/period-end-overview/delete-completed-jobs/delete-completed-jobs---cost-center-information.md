# Delete Completed Jobs - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when purging jobs Spectrum will delete only if you have permission to access the job's information. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the job cannot be added to the deletion list.
