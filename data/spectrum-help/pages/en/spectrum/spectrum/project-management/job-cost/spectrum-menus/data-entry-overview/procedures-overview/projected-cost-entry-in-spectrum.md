# Projected Cost Entry in Spectrum

The Projected Cost Entry in Spectrum is a process within the Job Cost module that allows project managers to review the Job-to-date costs, current and original estimated costs, percent complete, projected cost and projected cost to complete on a phase-by-phase basis.
The Projected Cost Entry process is currently a "batch" process, beginning with the [Building Projected Costs](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/procedures-overview/building-projected-costs).
