# Sample Reports Overview

Spectrum offers a wide variety of Job Cost reports in both Crystal and standard formats. Click the link corresponding to the sample report that you want to view.
Note: Occasionally, some Crystal reports that rely on sub-reports (for example, Job Cost
 Overview Report) will not export correctly unless you navigate to the last screen of the
 report before exporting.
