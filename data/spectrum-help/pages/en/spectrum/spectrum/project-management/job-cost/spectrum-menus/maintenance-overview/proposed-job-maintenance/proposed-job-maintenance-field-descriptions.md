# Proposed Job Maintenance Field Descriptions

View the Proposed Job Maintenance field
 descriptions when completing fields on this screen.
FieldsDescriptions
Expected revenueThe expected revenue is calculated based on the sum
 of the estimated Contract amount fields (for
 all proposed jobs) entered in the [New/Edit Proposed Job](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/proposed-job-maintenance/newedit-proposed-job).
Expected costThe expected cost is calculated based on the sum of
 the estimated Cost field amounts (for all
 proposed jobs) entered in the New/Edit Proposed job
 window.
Expected profitThe expected profit for all the
 proposed jobs combined is equal to the Expected revenue minus the
 Expected cost fields.
