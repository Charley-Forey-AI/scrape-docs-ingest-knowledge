# Job Phases - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the Cost center field displays in the Properties window.
At the Job field, Spectrum verifies that the user has authorization for the job. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme. If the cost center is not included, then phase details for that job are not shown.
Cost types assigned to a phase that you are not permitted to access do not display on the Phase File Maintenance screen. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to each cost type for the phase with cost centers in your assigned scheme. If the cost center is not included, then that phase is not shown. The phase totals display the sum of all cost types, regardless of cost center security.
