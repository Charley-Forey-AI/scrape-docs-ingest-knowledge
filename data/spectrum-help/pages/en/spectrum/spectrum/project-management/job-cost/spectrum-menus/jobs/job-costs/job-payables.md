# Job Payables

Use this screen to review all vendor and subcontractor invoices (including unapproved, unposted, and open items), for the job currently selected.

- If the job is currently authorized for pay-when-paid processing, the pay-when-paid policy displays at the top of the page. Provided you have edit security to the Job Setup page, you will also see the Pay-When-Paid Policy button--click it to modify the settings.

- Project Managers can use this screen to review and release vendor invoices that are assigned to pay-when-paid by customer. Multi-tiered security is incorporated into this screen to permit senior operators to establish the pay-when-paid policy, while allowing mid-level staff to 'release' items and make other payment status changes at the invoice level.

- Job personnel can use this screen to see valuable information even when pay-when-paid is not authorized for the particular job--presenting a handy list of all open payables, including ones currently being routed for approval.

- This screen is accessible from the Costs section on the Job Info Bar, and users with the 'Vendor Pay-When-Paid Invoices' or the 'My Jobs Pay-When-Paid Review' dashboard apps will open this screen when they click on a record to drill-down for more information.
Warning: You can set an invoice to any status, regardless of
 whether or not cash has been received from the customer.
