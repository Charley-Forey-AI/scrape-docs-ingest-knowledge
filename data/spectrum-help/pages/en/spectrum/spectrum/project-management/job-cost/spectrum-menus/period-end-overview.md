# Period End Overview

On the Site
 Map screen, click on the Job
 Cost folder, and then click the Period End folder to access the Period End menu selections discussed below.
The Period End
 menu is used on occasion to delete historical information that is no longer required.
 Consult the Procedures chapter for detailed information about weekly, monthly, and yearly
 procedures.
CAUTION: Extreme caution should be exercised when
 performing purges.
