# Job Listing - Cost Center Information

If your company is using cost centers, Spectrum will include jobs on the Job Listing if you have permission to access the job information.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme. The job will not display on the listing if the cost center is not included in your assigned scheme.
The Validate job division with G/L department checkbox displays on the Job Cost Installation screen. If this checkbox is selected, a window is available to select G/L department codes.
