# Job Quantity Complete Entry

The Job Quantity Complete Entry screen is used to facilitate entry of job cost information. This feature is convenient for companies who have unit priced jobs and whose quantity-complete information flow from the field corresponds with the Payroll process.
Once the Quantity Complete Update is run, pre-time card entries will be posted to Job Quantity History as part of the Payroll Update.
