# Billing Item Analysis Inquiry - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when requesting a job in Billing Item Analysis Inquiry Spectrum will display the job only if you have permission to access the Accounts Receivable contract. As you request each job, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the contract with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then inquiry on that ob will not be allowed.
Spectrum does not compare the cost center assigned to each billing item or phase, if applicable, with cost centers in your assigned scheme to compute totals. Instead, activity for all billing items and phases of authorized contracts are included in the inquiry display.
