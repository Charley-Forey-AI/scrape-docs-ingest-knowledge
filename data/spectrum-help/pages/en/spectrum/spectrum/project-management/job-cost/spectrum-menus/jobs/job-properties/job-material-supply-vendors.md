# Job Material Supply Vendors

Use this screen to manage all suppliers (that is, non-subcontract vendors) that have compliance issues to track. As vendors may have Document Tracking items that are not job specific, adding a vendor here will include their non-job specific tracking items to the Job Compliance Tracking Log. Vendors do not need to be set up here to log lien releases; liens will write to the Compliance Log automatically.

- Click the Add Vendor button to add vendors to the selected job. The search window will only include vendors the Operator is allowed to access.

- Click the Remove button to remove selected vendor from the job.
Note: Do not add subcontractors to this list. If one is added
 here, it is possible that certain tracking items might appear twice, since they would
 be compiled from here as well as subcontracts.
