# Phase Listing

The Phase Listing prints information from the Phase file. Phase descriptions and current estimated quantities, hours, and costs are printed. The total estimated cost should match the total Estimate in Jobs.
The listing can be limited to a range of job numbers, phases, or cost types by entering the appropriate information on this screen.
