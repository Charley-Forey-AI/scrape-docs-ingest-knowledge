# Earned Revenue

The Earned Revenue screen is typically used by operations
 that have multi-year jobs.
You can record amounts earned at a particular point in time; the data is
 used for the [Contract Status Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/reports-overview/contract-status-report).
This feature may be used as frequently as needed when entering earned revenue for a job, but is particularly useful at year end. Entry of earned revenue has no effect on the General Ledger.
