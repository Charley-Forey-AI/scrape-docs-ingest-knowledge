# Inquiries Overview

On the Site Map screen, click the Job Costbutton, and then click the Inquiries button to access the Inquiries menu selections discussed below.
The Inquiries menu is used to view information about jobs, including analysis, productivity, historical detail and summarized overview. It also includes subcontract information, as needed.
