# Proposed Job Maintenance - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the cost center code assigned to the proposed job will display in the Cost center column. If the Operator is not authorized to access a cost center, proposed jobs that are assigned this cost center will be hidden. If the Advanced Search Options is used to make a partial selection of proposed jobs, disallowed jobs will be hidden. Hidden jobs will not be included in the Totals section of this screen. The Operator will not be allowed to delete any disallowed jobs.
