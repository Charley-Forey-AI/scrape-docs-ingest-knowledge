# Job Analysis - Cost Center Information

 If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, Spectrum will display the job only if the in you have permission to access the job's information. As you request each job, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the inquiry on that job will not be permitted.
Spectrum does not compare the cost center assigned to each phase, if applicable, with cost centers in your assigned scheme to compute job and phase totals. Instead, activity for all authorized jobs' phases are included in the job totals and the inquiry screen's group and phase drill-down windows.
