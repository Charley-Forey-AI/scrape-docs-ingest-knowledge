# Job Billing Transaction Entry - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when entering a job Spectrum will allow billing transaction entry for the job only if you have permission to access the job information. As you request each job, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in you assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then entries for that job will not be allowed.
When your scheme includes override settings for job entries in Cost Center Scheme Maintenance, the Billing Transaction Entry screen will validate the cost center assigned to transaction detail lines based on these overrides. The override cost center(s) supersede the cost center list defined for the scheme in general. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned in the entry screen detail with job override cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the transaction entry line is not allowed.
