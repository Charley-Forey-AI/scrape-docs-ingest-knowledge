# Earned Revenue - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Description

- Save

- Cancel

Click to save entries as unposted transactions. These are ready for [Earned Revenue Journal](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/earned-revenue/earned-revenue-journal)
Click cancel to exit the screen without saving changes.

Update
Click to display the [Earned Revenue Journal](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/earned-revenue/earned-revenue-journal) screen.

New/Edit/Delete
Click to add, edit, or delete selected transactions.

Delete All
Click to delete all transactions.

Grid

Date
You can enter and view multiple dates for earned revenue transactions. This date will be attached to the revenue entered.
The same date entered in the previous line defaults.

- Job

- Description

When you enter a valid job number, the job description displays.

Amount
Enter the incremental dollar amount of revenue earned during the period. This is the additional amount of earned revenue.
