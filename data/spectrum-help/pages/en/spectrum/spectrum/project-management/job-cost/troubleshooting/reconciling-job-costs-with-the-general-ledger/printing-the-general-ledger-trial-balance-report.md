# Printing the General Ledger Trial Balance Report

You can printi the General Ledger Trial Balance
 Report.

1. On the Site Map screen, click General Ledger  >  Reports  >  Trial  > Balance.

1. In the Selections portion of the screen, enter the necessary information. You can press Enter at any field to accept the system default of ALL.

1. In the Report format (non-Crystal only) section of the screen, select Summary.

1. In the Report to includesection of the screen, select All accounts.

1. Click Print.
