# Billing Item Analysis Inquiry

Use this screen to display contract cost and revenue by
 billing item on contracts. This is particularly useful for unit price jobs. This screen
 details total revenue and costs by billing item; this information is generated from the
 A/R Contracts screen (which stores revenue to date by billing item)
 for revenue and projected revenue, and from the Job Phases screen for
 costs (each phase and cost type has a field for referencing the billing item).
The screen provides information on the status of billings vs. costs by contract item. This enables you to identify possible billing problems before they affect cash flow.
This screen also compares revenue and cost by billing item. The revenue
 and costs reflected in this screen are normally those that have been posted to Job Cost/
 A/R. It also allows analysis to be done on costs that have not yet reached job cost, but
 have been entered. For example, this screen allows the user to include pre-time cards. This
 process allows for selecting specific (or all) Payroll > Pre-Time Card Entry batches to be include as costs for a job – before the pay cycle has been
 posted to job cost. This is a useful tool for up-to-the-minute analysis.
In addition, this screen can be displayed to include Draw Request Pre-Billing Quantity Entry information (billing entries and the corresponding billing extensions) that have not been posted.
Click Switch to view a different customer record, or click [Billing Item Analysis - Selections window](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/inquiries-overview/billing-item-analysis-inquiry/billing-item-analysis---selections-window) to change your settings for this screen.
