# Phase Copy - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, Spectrum permits you to copy from a job only if you have permission to access the job. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then entry of that job is not permitted. Spectrum also performs this validation at the New job field. During the phase copy update, even if the operator is not authorized for certain phases, all phases of the source job are copied to the new job.When copying phases to a job in another company and when Enterprise Installation option for Cost centers is set to Yes in the destination company, when running the Phase Copy, Spectrum will permit you to copy to the new job only if you have permission to access that job in the destination company. If the cost center is not included, then the phase is not copied.
