# Projected Cost Entry by Group - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when entering projected cost transactions Spectrum allows entries for the job only if you have permission to access the job. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then entries for that job will not display. In addition, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to each phase, if any, with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then that phase cannot be changed or deleted. Spectrum also does not allow you to add transaction lines for disallowed phases. All phases for authorized jobs appear in the entry screen.When your scheme includes override settings for job entries in Cost Center Scheme Maintenance, the Projected Cost Entry screen will validate the cost center assigned to transaction detail lines based on these overrides. The override cost center(s) supersede the cost center list defined for the scheme in general. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned in the entry screen detail with job override cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the transaction entry line is not allowed.
