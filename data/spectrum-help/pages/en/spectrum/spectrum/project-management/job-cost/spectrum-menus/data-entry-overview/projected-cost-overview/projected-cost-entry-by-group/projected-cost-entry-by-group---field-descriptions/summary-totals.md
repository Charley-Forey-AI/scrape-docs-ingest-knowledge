# Summary Totals

Use this window to view job revenue and job cost totals. If you are changing financial information, the original estimated cost will not be recalculated unless you enter a new unit cost, a new original quantity, or a new original estimated cost. If new figures are not entered for these values, then the current phase numbers will not be recalculated.
Fields
Descriptions

Projected complete date
Enter the projected date of completion for the current job or click the
 drop-down arrow to open the Search Calendar Dates window. After running the
 [Projected Cost Update](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/projected-cost-overview/projected-cost-update), the date entered here will update to the [Jobs](/en/spectrum/spectrum/project-management/job-cost/spectrum-menus/jobs) screen.

Job revenue totals

Revised contract/Projected Contract
The revised contract amount for the job displays.
The projected contract amount displays for unit cost jobs.

Projected profit
The job's projected profit amount displays. Projected profit is defined as Revised contract (or Projected contract for unit price jobs) less Projected costs.

Estimated profit
The job's estimated profit amount displays. Spectrum calculates this amount as the contract amount less the current estimate amount.

Job cost totals

- Estimate

- Estimated variance

- Last projection

- Projected variance

- Week-/Year-/Month-to-date cost

- Job-to-date

- Percent complete

- Cost to complete

- Projected at completion

The column totals for dollar amounts that appear in the line transactions in the Projected Cost Entry screen display. As changes to the detail lines are saved, the total amount that displays here will automatically update.
