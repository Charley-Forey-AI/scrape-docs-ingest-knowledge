# Purge Job Cost Quantity History - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing fields on this screen.
Field
Description

Job
Enter the job you want to include on this report.

From transaction date
Enter the first date to be included on this report, or press Enter to begin with the earliest quantity history date.

To transaction date
Enter the last date to be included on this report, or press Enter to end with the current Job Cost processing date.
