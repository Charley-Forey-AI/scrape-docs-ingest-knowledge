# Earned Revenue History Report

Use this report to review incremental adjustments to earned revenue by job. Earned revenue is recorded at a point in time by using the Prior Period Earned Revenue Entry screen. It is also recorded when the Contract Status with YTD and MTD are run with the 'Link to option to create earned revenue entries' option is selected. Earned revenue by year is used on the Contract Status with YTD to determine prior year earnings. The same is true for revenue by month using the Contract Status with MTD.
