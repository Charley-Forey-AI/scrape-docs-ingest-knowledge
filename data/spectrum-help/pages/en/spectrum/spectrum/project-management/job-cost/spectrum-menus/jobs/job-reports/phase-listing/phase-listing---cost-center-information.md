# Phase Listing - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, Spectrum includes the job on the listing only if you have permission to access the job. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then that job is not shown on the listing. In addition, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to each phase, if any, with cost centers in your assigned scheme. If the cost center is not included, then that phase does not display on the listing.
