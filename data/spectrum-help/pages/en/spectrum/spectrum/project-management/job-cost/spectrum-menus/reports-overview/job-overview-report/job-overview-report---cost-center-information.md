# Job Overview Report - Cost Center Information

Job Overview Report - Cost Center Information If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when printing the Job overview Reports Spectrum will include activity only if you have permission to access the job's information. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the job will not display.Activity for all phases of authorized jobs is included on the report.
This report includes a 'Cost group' selection, in order to filter reporting by 'cost center'. The software will read for the cost center assigned to the job to determine whether to include that particular job on the report.
