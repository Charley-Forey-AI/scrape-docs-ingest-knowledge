# Reports Overview

On the Site Map screen, click the Job Cost button, and then click the Reports button to access the Reports menu selections discussed below.
The Reports menu is used to print a variety of reports pertaining to Job Cost records. Included in the discussion of each report is an explanation of how all figures on the report are calculated. We encouraged you to examine these examples to determine which reports will best meet the needs of your company.
