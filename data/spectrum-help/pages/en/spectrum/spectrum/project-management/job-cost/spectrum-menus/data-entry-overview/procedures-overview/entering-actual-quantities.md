# Entering Actual Quantities

Once the Quantity Complete file has been built, the jobs included in the build are now accessible.
When calculating projected costs, two different unit cost methods are allowed:

- Estimated – this method might be used if the per unit cost is staying the same, but the projected quantities are increasing. This might be a scope change.

- Actual – the projected quantities may or may not change, and the projected cost is updated to reflect a change in the actual costs from estimated costs.
