# Payroll Hours Analysis Report - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when printing the Payroll Hours Analysis Report Spectrum will include activity only in you have permission to access the job's information. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the job will not display.Additionally, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to each phase, if applicable, with cost centers in your assigned scheme. If the cost center is not included, then that phase's activity does not display. A note will display next to the job total if some records are not included due to cost center security.
