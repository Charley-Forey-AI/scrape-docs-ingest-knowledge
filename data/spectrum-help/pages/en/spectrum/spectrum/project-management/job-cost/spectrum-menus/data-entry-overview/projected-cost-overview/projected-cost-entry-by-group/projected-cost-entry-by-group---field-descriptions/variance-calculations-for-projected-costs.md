# Variance Calculations for Projected Costs

## Fixed Price Variance

Estimated Variance = At Completion Dollars less Current Estimate Dollars
Projected Variance = At Completion Dollars less Last Projection Dollars

## Unit Price Variance

Est Variance = At Completion Quantity * (At Completion Unit Cost – Estimated Unit Cost)
Proj Variance = At Completion Quantity * (At Completion Unit Cost – Last Proj Unit Cost)
