# Job Summary Cost Report - Cost Center Information

If your company is using cost centers, when previewing the Job Summary Cost Report Spectrum will include activity only if you have permission to access the job. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned scheme; if the cost center is not included, then the job will display. Activity for all phases of authorized jobs is included on the report.
