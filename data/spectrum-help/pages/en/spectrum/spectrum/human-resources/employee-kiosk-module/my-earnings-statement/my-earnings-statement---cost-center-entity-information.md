# My Earnings Statement - Cost Center Entity Information

If cost centers and entity tracking is enabled in Enterprise Installation, the entity name will display (from the Entity Master Table) in place of the 'Company name' at the top of the page when an 'Entity' code is stored in the Earning History Table for the selected check.
