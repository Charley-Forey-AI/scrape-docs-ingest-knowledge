# Setting up 'My' Apps

Many apps may be customized to display user-specific
 information. Any dashboard app with 'My' in the title displays information unique to the
 operator who is logged in.
Example: My Job App
To enable My apps, perform the following steps:

## Part 1 - Contact Setup

1. On the Site Map, click System Administration
 Contacts.

1. Click New and in the Contact name
 field, add a new contact for your operator.

1. On the Jobs tab, add some jobs to the contact.

1. Click OK to save.


## Part 2 - Operator Maintenance

1. On the Site Map, click
 System Administration  >  Security  >  Operator
 Maintenance.

1. In the Contact field,
 add the newly-created contact to your operator.

## Part 3 - Dashboard Setup

1. On the Dashboard, click the Settings button and then add the My Jobs app to your
 dashboard. The jobs for which your operator is an associated contact will
 display.
