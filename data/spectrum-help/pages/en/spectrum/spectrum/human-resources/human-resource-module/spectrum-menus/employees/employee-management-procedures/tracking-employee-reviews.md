# Tracking Employee Reviews

Learn how to add, edit, or delete employee review
 information.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources  >  Employees  >  Reviews.

1. Enter an Employee code.

1. To add a new review, click the New button.

1. To edit an existing review, highlight the form in the list box and click the
 Edit button.

1. To delete an existing review, highlight the form in the list box and click
 the Delete button
