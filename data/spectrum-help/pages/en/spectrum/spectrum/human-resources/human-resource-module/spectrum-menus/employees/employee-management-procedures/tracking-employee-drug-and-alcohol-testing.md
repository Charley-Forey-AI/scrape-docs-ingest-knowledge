# Tracking Employee Drug and Alcohol Testing

Learn how to add or edit employee drug and alcohol test
 scheduling and results.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources >  Employees >  Drug / Alcohol Testing.

1. Enter the Employee code for whom you want to add drug or alcohol information.

1. Click the New button to enter a new test, or click Edit to view details of selected tests.

1. At the Status field, the test's
 status displays based on the test type you selected in the Drug and
 Alcohol Tests for window. No entry is allowed.

1. At the Reason field, use the
 drop-down menu to select a reason for the drug or alcohol test.Note: If you select Reasonable Suspicion from the
 drop-down menu, then the Statement button will
 activate.

1. Once you have entered all of the necessary information
 in this window, click the Statement button to enter
 information surrounding the reason for the reasonable suspicion.

1. At the remaining fields, enter drug and alcohol scheduling and administration information.

1. Once you have completed the window, click
 OK. The testing information you entered will now appear in the main screen.
