# FMLA Weeks Used Update

This update recalculates the FMLA weeks used as well as the
 remaining FMLA time available for selected FMLA-eligible employees, based on the calculation
 method chosen in the Human Resources Installation screen. According to
 the U.S. Department of Labor, this method should not be changed without giving employees prior
 notice. Please contact the U.S. Department of Labor for further details about employee
 notification requirements and timelines. All employees who are not eligible for FMLA are
 always excluded from this update.
This update should be run at regular intervals throughout the year to
 ensure that the FMLA weeks used by employees data are up-to-date.
Note:
 If an Employees is eligible for family and medical leave under FMLA,
 and the checkbox is clear in the Human Resources Installation > FMLA/OSHA screen, then the following message will display: **ERROR – THE FMLA
 PERIOD OPTION IS NOT SET. PLEASE COMPLETE DATA IN HR INSTALLATION SCREEN**
 To initiate use of FMLA features, you must adjust settings on the Human Resources Installation > FMLA/OSHA screen.
