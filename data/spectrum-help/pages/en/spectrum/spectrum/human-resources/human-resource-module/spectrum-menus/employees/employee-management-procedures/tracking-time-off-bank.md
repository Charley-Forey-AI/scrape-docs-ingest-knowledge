# Tracking Time Off Bank

Learn how to track an employee's banked vacation, holiday,
 and sick hours.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources  >  Employees  >  Employees.

1. Enter an employee code, and then select the Time Off Bank on the Info Bar.

1. The employee's banked vacation, holiday, and sick hours display. To make any adjustments, click the Edit button.
