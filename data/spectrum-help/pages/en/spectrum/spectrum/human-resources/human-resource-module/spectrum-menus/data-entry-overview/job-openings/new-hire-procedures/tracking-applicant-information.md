# Tracking Applicant Information

Use this procedure when tracking applicant information such
 as references and qualifications.

1. On the Site Map screen, select
 Human Resources >  Applicants >  Applicants.

1. In the screen's list box, locate the applicant and
 click the Edit button to
 open the Applicant Main Properties screen.

1. Use this screen to enter details about applicants who
 are applying for positions within your company.
