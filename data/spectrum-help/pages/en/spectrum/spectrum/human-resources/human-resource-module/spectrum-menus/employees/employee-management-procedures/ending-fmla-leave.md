# Ending FMLA Leave

Learn how to end FMLA leave.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources  >  Employees  >  Employees.

1. On the Employees screen, click to highlight the employee information you are editing and click the Edit button.

1. Click the FMLA link in the Human Resources section of the Info Bar.

1. Select the End current FMLA leave checkbox.

1. In the Actual end date field, enter the actual end date and click OK.
