# Update Pay Rates

Use the Update Pay Rates screen to print a listing of rates. Rates can be sorted in the following ways: Employee code, Employee name, Rate/pay period, Percentage Amount or New Rate.
Once you have previewed the listing, the Update Pay Rates confirmation screen will appear so you can complete the rate update process.
