# Tracking Employee Contact Information

Learn how to enter contact information for a selected
 employee.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources  >  Employees.

1. Enter the Employee code and select the Personal Info link on the Info Bar.

1. Click the Edit button.

1. Complete the Contact, Emergency, Mailing address, and Domicile address portions of the screen and click Save to save your changes.
