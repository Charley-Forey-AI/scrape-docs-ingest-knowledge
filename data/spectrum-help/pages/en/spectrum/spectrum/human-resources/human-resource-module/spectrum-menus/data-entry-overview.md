# Data Entry Overview

The Data Entry menu is used to manage and track the benefits, activities, and classifications provided to employees. For example, track and authorize benefits, such as 401(k); make global pay rate changes; or manage job openings at your company. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources, and then click Data Entry.
