# Triggers - Trigger Actions

The following list illustrates the types of trigger actions available in Spectrum.

- Warn in Applicants

- Warn in Employees

- Warn in Time Card Entry

- Send an email during Payroll Update

- Prevent Entry in Applicants

- Prevent Entry in Employee Management (prevents entry in Human Resources > Employees and in Payroll > Maintenance > Employee Maintenance)

- Prevent Time Card Entry for Employee

- Print Warning on Time Card Edit List

- Print Warning during Check Calculation

- Print Warning during Payroll Update

- Change a field during Payroll Update
