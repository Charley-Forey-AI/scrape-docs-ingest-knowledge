# Job Group Maintenance

Use the Job Group Maintenance screen to set up job group codes and
 descriptions. These job groups are used to define job titles in [Job Title Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/hr-maintenance-overview/job-title-maintenance)
