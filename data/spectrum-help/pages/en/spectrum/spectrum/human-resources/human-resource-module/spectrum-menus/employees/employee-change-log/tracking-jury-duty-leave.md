# Tracking Jury Duty Leave

Learn how to track jury duty leave for employees.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources >  Employees >  Civil / Legal / Other.

1. Enter an Employee code.

1. In the Date field, enter the
 date on which the jury duty will begin, or press F4 to select
 a date from the Date Change window.

1. In the Activity code field,
 enter the code for jury duty leave, or press F4 to search for
 the jury duty code.Note: The codes used in this field are added to the software
 using Civil/Legal/Other Activities Maintenance.

1. In the Hours field, enter the
 number of total hours used by the employee for jury duty leave.

1. In the Total cost field, enter
 the total cost of the employee's jury duty leave for the company.

1. In the Notes field, enter any
 notes pertaining to the leave.

1. Repeat steps 2-7 for each additional instance of jury
 duty leave.

1. Click Save to save your
 changes.
