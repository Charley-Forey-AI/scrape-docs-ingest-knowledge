# Returning an Issued Item to the Company

Learn how to return a company-owned item from an employee
 to the company.

1. On the Site Map screen, select Human Resources  >  Data Entry  >  Item Tracking.

1. At the list box, highlight the item you want to issue
 and then click the Return Item button to display the Return Item window.

1. At the Location field, enter the location to which the
 item is being returned or press F4 to select from a list of available
 locations.

1. At the Return date field, enter the date on which the
 item was returned or press F4 to select a date from the Date Change window.

1. At the Notes field, enter any notes pertaining to the
 item's return. For example, you might make note of the condition of the item upon
 return.

1. The item can now be issued to a different employee or
 retired from the software.
