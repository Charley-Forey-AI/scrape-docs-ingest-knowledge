# Managing Benefits Using the Benefit Management Screen

Learn how to manage employee benefits using the
 Benefit Management screen.

1. On the Site Map screen, select
 Human Resources >  Data Entry >  Benefit Management .

1. In the Build Employee List for Benefit Management window, specify an
 Employee code, Department code,
 Benefit code and/or Benefit status, or press Enter to include ALL employees in
 the Benefit Management listbox.

1. After entering the benefit selection criteria, all
 employees that meet your selection criteria and with a status of
 Pending, review again next time will display. Additionally,
 those codes that match codes specified in the Recurring Benefits File
 Maintenance, screen will display, based on the same eligibility
 requirements associated with the benefit code(s) selected.

1. At the Status field, use the
 drop-down menu to select a status for the corresponding benefit.Note: All employees who are set up for recurring benefits for
 which they do not qualify will display in red.

1. Click the Update button to commit changes.
