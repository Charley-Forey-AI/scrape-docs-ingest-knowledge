# Utilities Overview

On the Site Map screen, click the Human Resources button, and then click the Utilities button to access the Utilities menu selections discussed below. The Utilities menu is used to track employee FMLA eligibility, update the amount of FMLA used by employees, and build job titles.
Important: Screens accessed from the Utilities menu are
 normally reserved for use by supervisory and administrative personnel only.

- [Build Job Titles](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/utilities-overview/build-job-titles)

- [ FMLA Eligibility](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/utilities-overview/fmla-eligibility)

- [FMLA Weeks Used Update](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/utilities-overview/fmla-weeks-used-update)

- [Set Veteran Status](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/utilities-overview/set-veteran-status)
