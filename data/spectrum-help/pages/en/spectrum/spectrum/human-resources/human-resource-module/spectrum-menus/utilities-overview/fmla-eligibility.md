#  FMLA Eligibility

This update designates FMLA eligibility for the specified employee, based on the employee's length of employment and the number of hours worked over the past year. The hours worked are based on the time card history file, excluding specified pay types.
Employees without a hire date in Employees and employees who are currently on FMLA leave are not included in this update.
The Minimum requirements section is available so you can establish the minimum length of employment requirement an employee must meet in order to become eligible for FMLA leave.
If the Employees are eligible for family and medical leave under FMLA
 checkbox is clear in the Human Resources Installation > FMLA/OSHA screen, then the following message will display:
**ERROR – THE FMLA PERIOD OPTION IS NOT SET. PLEASE COMPLETE DATA IN HR INSTALLATION SCREEN
To initiate use of FMLA features in Spectrum, you must adjust settings
 on the Human Resources Installation > FMLA/OSHA screen.
