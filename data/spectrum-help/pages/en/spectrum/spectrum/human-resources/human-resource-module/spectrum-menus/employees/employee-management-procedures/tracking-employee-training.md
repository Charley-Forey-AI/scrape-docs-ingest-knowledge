# Tracking Employee Training

Learn how to add, edit, or delete employee training
 information.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources  >  Employees  >  Training / Certifications.

1. Enter an Employee code. A list of training events
 entered to the software for the employee will display, if any exist.

1. To add a training event, click the New button and complete the New Employee
 Training / Certification window.

1. To edit a training event, highlight the training event in the list box and
 click the Edit button.

1. To delete a training event, highlight the training event and click the Delete
 button.
