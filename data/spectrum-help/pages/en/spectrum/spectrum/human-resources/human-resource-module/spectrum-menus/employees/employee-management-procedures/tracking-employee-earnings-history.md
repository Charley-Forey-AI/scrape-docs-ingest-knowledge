# Tracking Employee Earnings History

Learn how to track earnings history for a selected
 employee.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources  >  Employees  >  Employees.

1. Select the Earnings History link on the Info
 Bar.

1. Enter an Employee code.

1. A list of employee earnings to date displays. Click
 the Check button to view
 the Payroll Check History
 Inquiry, or click the Time Card button to view the Time Card History Inquiry.
