# Managing a Job Opening

Use this procedure when adding, editing, or deleting a job
 opening.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources  >  Data Entry  >  Job Openings.

1. In the Search field, press
 Enter to display ALL job openings.Note: To change the type of job opening status you want to
 display, from the Job title field arrow up one time so that
 your cursor is in the Status field, and then use the
 drop-down menu to select a specific job status type.

1. All job openings will display in the list box. From
 here you can choose to edit a job opening, add a job opening, or delete a job
 opening.

- To add a new job opening to the software, click the
 New button and complete the New Job
 Opening window.

- To edit a job opening, highlight the opening in the list box
 and click the Edit button to open the Edit Job Opening window.

- To delete an existing job opening, highlight the opening in the
 list box, click the Delete button, and then click
 OK in the confirmation window.
