# Hiring an Applicant

Use this procedure when hiring an applicant.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources >  Applicants >  Applicants.

1. In the Search field, enter the
 code for the applicant you are hiring or press F4 to select an
 applicant code.

1. Select the applicant in the listbox and click the
 Hire Now button.

1. Enter in New hire information and Pay rate and taxes for the
 applicant.

1. Click OK.
