# Human Resources Installation - Reporting

Use the Reporting tab to set FMLA/OSHA-related defaults that will be used throughout the Human Resources module.
Field/ButtonDescription
Employees are eligible for family and medical leave under FMLA Select this checkbox is employees in your company are eligible for FMLA leave; otherwise, leave this checkbox clear.
Weeks available per 12-month periodEnter the maximum number of weeks of FMLA leave each employee may take during a 12-month period.
12 month FMLA period is based onSelect the time period to use for calculating employee FMLA eligibility and usage from the following options.

- Calendar year

- A fixed 12-month period such as fiscal year

- A 12-month period based on each employee's hire date

- The 12-month period forward from the date FMLA begins

- A rolling 12-month period measured backward from FMLA leave

The option selected appears as a display-only field on the FMLA Weeks Used Update screen.
OSHA
Industry descriptionEnter the description of the industry with which the company is affiliated. This description will be used with OSHA claims.
NAICS codeEnter the North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for your company's industry. If you do not know your company's code, you can look it up at www.osha.gov.
Average number of employeesEnter the average number of people that the company employs during the year. Select the Calculate button to calculate the average number of employees based on pay period year.
See [OSHA Forms](/en/spectrum/spectrum/human-resources/human-resource-module/spectrum-menus/reports-overview/osha-forms) for more details.
If an OSHA establishment is defined, Spectrum will identify all locations assigned to the given establishment and read the Payroll history for all employees assigned to those locations, calculating establishment-specific values. Spectrum will then open a confirmation window displaying the breakdown.

Total hours worked last year Enter the average number of total hours worked by all employees in a year. Select the Calculate button to automatically calculate the average number of hours worked based on a specified pay period year.
If an OSHA establishment is defined, Spectrum will identify all locations assigned to the given establishment and read the Payroll history for all employees assigned to those locations, calculating establishment-specific values.Spectrum will then open a confirmation window displaying the breakdown.
Important: When calculating the average number of hours worked, the following pay types are NOT included: Vacation, Holiday, Sick, Equipment Usage, and JX.
