# Issuing a Tracking Item to an Employee

Learn how to issue a company-owned item to an
 employee.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Human Resources >  Data Entry >  Item Tracking to display the Employee Item Tracking
 screen.

1. Highlight the item you want to issue in the listbox
 and then click the Status button to display the
 Item Status window.

1. Select the item you want to issue and click the
 Issue Item button to display the Issue
 Item window.Note: If the Issue Item button is deactivated, this means the selected item has
 a status of inactive or is currently issued to an employee and has not yet been
 returned.

1. Enter the Employee to whom the
 item is being issued, as well as Location,
 Requisition date, and Due date
 information for the issue.

1. Click OK.
