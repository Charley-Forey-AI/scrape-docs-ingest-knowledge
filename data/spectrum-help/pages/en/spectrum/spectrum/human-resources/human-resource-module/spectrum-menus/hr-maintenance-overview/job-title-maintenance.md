# Job Title Maintenance

Use the Job Titles Maintenance screen to add new job titles
 to the software, edit existing job titles, and delete a selected job title. This feature is
 similar to the Employee Titles feature in the Payroll module, except that Human Resources
 stores qualifications, salary ranges, and other similar information. Information entered here
 will default in the following screens: Job Openings, Applicants, and Employees.
