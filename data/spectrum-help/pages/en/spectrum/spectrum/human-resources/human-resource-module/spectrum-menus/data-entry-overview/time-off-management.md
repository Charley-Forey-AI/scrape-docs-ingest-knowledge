# Time Off Management

Use the Time Off Management screen to reset employee
 vacation, holiday and sick balances globally in Spectrum.
For example, if your company prefers to set vacation balances at the
 beginning of the year rather than accruing them throughout the year, this screen can be
 used to set employee vacation hours to "40" simultaneously. Further, this screen can be
 used to set different vacation rates globally for employees with different lengths of
 employment.
When this screen is first accessed, specify employee selection criteria
 in the Build Employee List for Time Off
 Management window. After entering the benefit selection criteria, all
 employees that meet your selection criteria will display. Current hours will be calculated
 from the Payroll Time Off Bank Log Table. Any changes made in this screen will be committed
 once the operator clicks the Update button.
