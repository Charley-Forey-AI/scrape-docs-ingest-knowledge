# Period End Overview

On the Site Map screen, click the
 Human Resources button, and then click the Period
 End button to access the Period End menu selections
 discussed below.
The Period End menu is used at the end of every
 payroll period (month/quarter/year). This menu normally would not be accessed until after
 all required reports are obtained through the Reports menu.
Important: Use extreme caution when performing
 purges!
