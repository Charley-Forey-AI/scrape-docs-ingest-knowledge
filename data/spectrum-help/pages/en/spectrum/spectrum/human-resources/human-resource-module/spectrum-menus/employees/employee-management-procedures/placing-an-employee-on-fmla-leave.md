# Placing an Employee on FMLA Leave

Learn how to place an employee on FMLA leave.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Human Resources  >  Employees  >  Employees.

1. On the Employees screen, click to highlight the employee information you are editing and click the Edit button.

1. Click the FMLA link in the Human Resources section of the Info Bar.

1. In the Start FMLA leave section, select the Currently on FMLA leave checkbox.

1. Optional: The Assign Incident window may appear.

- If the leave is not the cause of a previous incident, click the New button to create a new incident for tracking purposes.

- If the FMLA leave is due to a previous safety incident click the Assign button to associate the incident to the leave. You will then be presented with the Unassigned Incidents window. Select the appropriate incident and click Assign. This will bring up the incident for your review. Notice that the OSHA incident type is automatically set to FMLA.

1. In the Start date field, enter the date on which the employee will begin leave.

1. In the End date field, enter the date on which the employee will return to work. If this information is not known at this time, leave this field blank.
