# My Subcontracts

The My Subcontracts app provides summary information about open subcontracts and facilitates access to enter progress billings.

- The list displayed includes subcontracts and job descriptions where the operator has a linked contact (set up in Operator Maintenance) and the contact is listed as both a job contact and a vendor contact. Adding a contact to the subcontract sets that contact up in both places.

- Each item displays as subcontract, job description.

- Select a subcontract to open the My Subcontract Manager screen and view the vendor code, subcontract number, and job.
