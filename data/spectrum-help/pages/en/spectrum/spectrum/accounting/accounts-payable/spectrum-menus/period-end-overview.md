# Period End Overview

The Period End menu is used at the
 end of fiscal periods to reset dollar amounts and delete historical information that is no
 longer required. Additionally, check reconciliation may be performed at month-end, and
 1099-MISC/NEC forms may be printed at year's end.
Please refer to the Procedures section of the help files for detailed
 information about monthly, quarterly, and yearly procedures.Important: Use
 Extreme Caution When Performing Purges!
