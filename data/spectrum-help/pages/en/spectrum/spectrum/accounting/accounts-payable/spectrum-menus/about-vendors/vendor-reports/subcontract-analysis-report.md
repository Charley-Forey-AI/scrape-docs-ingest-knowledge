# Subcontract Analysis Report

The Subcontract Analysis Report prints summary information from the subcontract file, and includes the vendor telephone number. This report prints the information that displays on the Subcontract Inquiry by Vendor and Subcontract Inquiry by Job screens. The report may be printed in sequence either by job or by vendor.
