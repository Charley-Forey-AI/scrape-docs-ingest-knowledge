# Subcontract Analysis Report - Cost Center Information

 If cost centers are being used, when printing this report Spectrum validates that the cost center assigned to the job is an authorized cost center for your operator. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned cost center scheme, and if the job's cost center is not present, then subcontracts for that job will be omitted. The cost center associated with the job is shown on the report.
