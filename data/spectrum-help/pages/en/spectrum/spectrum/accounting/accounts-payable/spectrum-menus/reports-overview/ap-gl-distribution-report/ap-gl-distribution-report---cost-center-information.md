# A/P G/L Distribution Report - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, for all formats of the G/L Distribution Report, Spectrum includes invoices only if your operator has permission to access the cost center assigned to the G/L posting account The G/L Distribution Report shows the cost center associated with each transaction line on the report.
