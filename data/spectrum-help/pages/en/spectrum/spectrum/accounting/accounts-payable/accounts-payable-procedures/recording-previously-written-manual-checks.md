# Recording Previously Written Manual Checks

You can record payments that are made before the corresponding invoices are in Spectrum.
You may use this procedure, for example, your company's owner writes a check for the company's picnic before the receipts/invoice are entered into Spectrum and you need to record the check.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Payable  >  Data Entry  >  Vendor Invoices.

1. Complete the Vendor field, and press Enter.

1. Enter the necessary information in the Invoice section of the screen, and select the Check/Credit Card: Unpaid button. This button does not display if the current transaction is on hold, or if there is a non-zero retention amount, and the button label varies based on the transaction type. In the Payment Details window, under Payment type, select Instant manual check.

1. Enter all the necessary pre-payment information in the Pre-Paid Detail window and click OK.Note: The Bank account field displays in the Pre-PaidDetail window only if the Cash Management module is installed.

1. The Properties window displays. In the Payment portion of the window, enter the Payment date, Discount date, Discount amount, Retention date, and Retention amount. If discount and retention information is not applicable, simply leave these fields blank.

1. Complete the line portion of the Vendor Invoice Entry screen by entering the appropriate G/L account code information to properly distribute the invoice, and click OK.

1. The Invoice totals window displays. Upon verifying that all the information you've entered is correct for the invoice totals, select the Enter next invoice option and click OK. Click OK again to return to the Site Map.

1. On the Site Map, click Data Entry  >  Vendor Invoices.

1. Click the Update button to display the A/P Transaction Register screen.

1. Complete the fields in the Sort by and Selections sections and then click OK to update the check information to the other applicable Spectrum modules. The Prepaid Manual Check Register details which checks in the batch are pre-paid.

1. Run the Transaction Register to update your entry to be included on the invoice/credit memo register.
