# A/P Payment Selection - VAT Processing Information

If VAT processing is enabled in the General Ledger Installation screen, the Current payout and Retention payout figures will include the VAT tax to be paid. In addition, the Outstanding column includes total unpaid VAT, the Previously applied column includes VAT amounts paid, and the Original amount column includes total VAT.
