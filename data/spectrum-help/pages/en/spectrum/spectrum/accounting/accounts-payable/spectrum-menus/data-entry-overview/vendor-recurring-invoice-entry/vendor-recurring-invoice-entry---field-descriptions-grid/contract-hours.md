# Contract Hours

If one or more cost types are entered in the Contract labor cost types field in the
 Accounts Payable Installation screen, then during the detail entry
 in Vendor Invoice Entry, the software will automatically open the
 Contract Hours window. This window is only available when the cost
 type of the transaction line is specified in the Accounts Payable
 Installation screen as a contract labor type.
During the A/P Transaction Register Update, hours
 recorded in this window will be recorded in the Job Cost history files and the A/P invoice
 history files. The Job Cost Payroll Hours Analysis Report offers an option to include these
 contractor labor hours.
Note: Negative hours may be entered and will display a
 negative extension amount; however the rate will remain a positive number.
