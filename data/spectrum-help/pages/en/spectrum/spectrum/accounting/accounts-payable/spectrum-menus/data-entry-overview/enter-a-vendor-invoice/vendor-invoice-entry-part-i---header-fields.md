# Vendor Invoice Entry Part I - Header Fields

Each vendor invoice requires certain entries in the header.
During invoice entry, some of these fields populate with a default value, which you may change or override.

1. Enter a Batch code or accept the default code.Information about the current batch of unposted invoices displays, including the number of invoices and credit memos.

1. Enter a Vendor code.For steps on setting up a new vendor code, see [Add a New Vendor](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/about-vendors/add-a-new-vendor).

1. Enter an Invoice # or use the drop-down arrow to select from a list of available invoices and credit memos.

1. The Invoice type defaults as Invoice but you may use the drop-down arrow to select Credit Memo if needed.

1. If the invoice should be applied to a purchase order, enter the PO number in the Purchase order field.An entry in this field enables the Details button.

1. If the invoice should be applied toward a subcontract, enter the subcontract number in the Subcontract field.Subcontract totals display in the upper-right side of the header.Note: The fields that display next depend on the type of transaction you are entering.

1. Enter the G/L date.This field displays for unposted transactions only.

1. Enter the Entry date. This field displays for unapproved transactions only.

1. Enter the Invoice date.Spectrum uses this date to calculate the due date.

1. In the Total before tax field, enter the total invoice dollar amount (without tax) as a positive number.

1. Enter the Sales tax amount, if applicable.The Total amount displays, which includes any sales tax. This field is read only.
