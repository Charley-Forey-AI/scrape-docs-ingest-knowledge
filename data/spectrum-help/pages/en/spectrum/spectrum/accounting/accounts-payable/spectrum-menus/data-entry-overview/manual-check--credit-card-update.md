# Manual Check / Credit Card Update

Once they have printed and are accurate, continue to update
 the manual check register information to the vendor's Accounts Payable records and to the
 General Ledger Summary file.
Do not continue until the Manual Check Register and/or [Manual Credit Card Check Register](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/manual-credit-card-check-register) have
 printed correctly.
Following this update, print and update the [Payments G/L Register](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/payments-gl-register). If you do not have the
 appropriate security level for accessing the Payments G/L Detail Report screen, the
 software will return you to Manual Check Entry screen or the Manual Credit Card Payment
 Entry screen.
