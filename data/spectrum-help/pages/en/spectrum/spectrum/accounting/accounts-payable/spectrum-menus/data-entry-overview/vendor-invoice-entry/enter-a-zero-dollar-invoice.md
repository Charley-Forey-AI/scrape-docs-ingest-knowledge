# Enter a Zero Dollar Invoice

Complete these steps to enter a zero dollar
 invoice.

1. In the Invoice type field, select Invoice.

1. In the Total before tax field, enter 0.00.

1. In the Sales tax field, enter 0.00.

1. In the Retention field, enter 0.00.

1. in the Payment details field, select Unpaid.

1. If all of these conditions are met and the invoice
 details are in balance with the invoice header (0.00), click
 Save.

1. Run the A/P Transaction Update. Zero-dollar adjustment invoices display on the report just like any other vendor invoice.
