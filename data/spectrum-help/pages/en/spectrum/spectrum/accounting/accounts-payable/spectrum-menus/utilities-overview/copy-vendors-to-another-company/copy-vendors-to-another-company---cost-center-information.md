# Copy Vendors to Another Company - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, during the update Spectrum adds selected vendors to the new company only if you have permission to access the vendor in the current company. Spectrum compares the vendor's list of shared cost centers with cost centers in your assigned cost center scheme, and if there are no common cost centers, then that vendor is not copied during the update. Spectrum also verifies that you have permission to access the G/L accounts assigned to the vendor in that other company. Spectrum compares each account's list of shared cost centers with cost centers in your assigned cost center scheme in the destination company, and if there are no common cost centers, then the G/L account will not be recorded for that vendor in the new company.
