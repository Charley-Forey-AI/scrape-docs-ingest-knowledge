# ePayments Vendor Spend Report

This report compiles the dollars spent by vendor for the past 12 months.
Export this as a comma separated values (CSV) format and submit it to your Account Representatives to learn about your potential rebate, in the event you enroll with Viewpoint ePayments.
The CSV version removes any headings and commas in the data before creating the export file.
