# Vendor Invoice Entry Part II - Payment Plan Fields

Complete the Payment Plan fields during invoice entry, or modify them on a posted invoice.
These instructions are part of an entry or update process. If you need to enter a vendor invoice from the beginning, see [Enter a Vendor Invoice](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/enter-a-vendor-invoice).
During invoice entry, each of these fields populates with a default value, which you may change or override:

1. Payment due - the invoice date and the vendor's payment terms dictate the default value.

1. Retention - the percentage comes from the subcontract record.

1. Retention amount - the amount corresponding to the percentage.

1. Hold payment - selected or not based on any hold status in the vendor record.Note: The Check/Credit Card, Paid by Instant Check, or Credit Card Purchase button appears only when:

- the invoice isn't on hold, and

- the Retention amount is either 0.00 or blank
