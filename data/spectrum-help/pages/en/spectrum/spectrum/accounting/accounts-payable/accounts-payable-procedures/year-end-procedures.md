# Year-End Procedures

1. Follow all of the Month-End
 Procedures. The backup performed during the Month End procedures
 should be labeled as the Year End Backup.

1. Complete Form 1099-MISC/NEC processing. Please refer
 to the Year End Help file for complete 1099 instructions. This must be done prior to
 the Vendor History Purge.
