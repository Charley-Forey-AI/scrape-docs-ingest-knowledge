# Reverse Vendor Invoice - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the cost center assigned to the original invoice displays beneath the Remarks field. Reversed invoices are assigned to the same cost centers as the one used on the original invoice. Cost centers assigned to the invoice distribution lines are shown in Expand mode on each line.
