# Vendor Document Tracking

This screen is modeled after the [Document Tracking Item Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/document-tracking-item-maintenance) screen, but only includes tracking items set up at the vendor level, across all subcontracts as well as non-subcontract transactions or events.

- If Action #2 (warning) is being used, the warning will be available during invoice entry, manual check entry, subcontract billing entry, subcontract entry and subcontract change order log.

- The [Vendor Invoice Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/about-vendors/vendor-invoice-entry) screen warns users when document tracking items that have Actions #2 (warning) and #3 (hold). Action #4 (no check) specifies that checks will not be selected a vendor or subcontractor, this occurs in the Pre-Payment Register. During A/P Check Print, actions #5 (report) and #6 (lien) will cause a check distribution report and lien release, respectively.
