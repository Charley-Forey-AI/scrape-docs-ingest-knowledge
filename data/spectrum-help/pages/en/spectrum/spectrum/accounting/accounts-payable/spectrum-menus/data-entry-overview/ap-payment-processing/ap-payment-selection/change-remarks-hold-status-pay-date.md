# Change Remarks, Hold Status, Pay Date

Provided that you have security access to A/P's
 Change Invoice Due Date screen, all of these tips can be performed in the
 A/P Payment Selection screen.

- Change Remittance
 Remarks: Move your cursor into the Check stub
 remarks field to modify.

- Modify the Estimated Pay
 Date: Move your cursor into the Due date field
 and enter a new date.

- Change the Hold Status on One
 Invoice: With your mouse, select the Hold?
 checkbox to place the invoice on hold. To clear the hold, clear the checkbox.
