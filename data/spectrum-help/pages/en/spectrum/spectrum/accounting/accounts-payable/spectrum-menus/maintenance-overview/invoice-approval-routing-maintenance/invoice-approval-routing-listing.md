# Invoice Approval Routing Listing

View a list of Routing Codes and the reviewers assigned to them.
Access this screen from the [Invoice Approval Routing Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/invoice-approval-routing-maintenance) screen, where you can also make changes to the Routing Codes.
