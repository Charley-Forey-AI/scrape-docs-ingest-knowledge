# Placing a Hold on an Invoice or Vendor

The following procedures should be used to place hold on
 vendor accounts, individual invoices, and a group of invoices. Click any of the links below to
 view:

## Placing a hold on a vendor account

This procedure explains how to place a hold on a vendor's account. All invoices and credit memos going forward will be automatically put on hold.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Payable > Maintenance > Vendor.

1. Enter a Vendor and click the Properties option on the
 Vendor Info Bar.

1. In the Invoice defaults section of the window, select the Payment
 hold checkbox. Selecting this checkbox will automatically place a
 hold on any invoices from this vendor. If sub-tier vendor tracking is being used,
 new invoices will be on hold whenever any related sub-tier vendors are flagged for
 hold status (triggered by action #3).

1. Click Save.

## Placing a hold on one invoice

This procedure explains how to place one invoice on hold after it has been entered and updated into Spectrum.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Payable > Data Entry > Change Invoice Due Dates.

1. Enter the Vendor, the type (Invoice or Credit memo).

1. Enter the Invoice number (or click the window icon to search for all open invoices for this vendor.)

1. Select the On hold checkbox.

1. Press Save.

1. Similarly, if you want to remove a hold for this vendor invoice only, manually
 clear the On hold payment checkbox.

## Placing a group of invoices on hold

This procedure explains how to place a group of invoices on hold. This is handy if you would like to stop payment on all of one vendor's invoices.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Payable > Data Entry > Payment Processing.

1. Click the Payment Selection button.

1. Click the Hold button.

1. Enter in the selection criteria of the invoices you would like to put on
 hold.Tip: Select the Include selected
 items checkbox if you would like to put the invoices previously
 selected on hold.

1. Click Save and the Hold Items
 window will appear. All invoices and credit memos that appear here will be placed
 on hold.Tip: If there are items that you do not want to put
 on hold, useDelete mode to remove them from this screen.

1. Click Save and the items selected will be placed on
 hold.

1. Similarly, if you want to remove a hold for a group of invoices, follow this
 same procedure except select the Release hold checkbox.
