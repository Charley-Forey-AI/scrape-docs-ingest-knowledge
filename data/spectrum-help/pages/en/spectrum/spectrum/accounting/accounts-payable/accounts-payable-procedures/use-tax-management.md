# Use Tax Management

If tax is not paid at the time of purchase and the goods purchased are used by the purchaser, then use tax must be paid to the state.
In some states, vendors do not charge tax on the invoice, but companies must still pay the vendor tax. Items may be considered taxable on a phase-by-phase basis. The same items may be taxable on some phases, but not on others depending on how they are used.
