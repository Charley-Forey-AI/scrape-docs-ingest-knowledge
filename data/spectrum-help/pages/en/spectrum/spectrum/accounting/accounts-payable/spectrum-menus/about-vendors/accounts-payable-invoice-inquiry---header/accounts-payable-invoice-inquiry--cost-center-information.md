# Accounts Payable Invoice Inquiry -Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, this window displays the cost center details for the selected invoice (and operators can only view items that they have security access to view). Cost centers assigned to distribution lines are shown in Expand mode, and the cost center assigned to the invoice is shown in the Vendor Detail Inquiry screen header.
