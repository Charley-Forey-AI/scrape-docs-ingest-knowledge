# Vendor Payment History - VAT processing information

If VAT processing is enabled in General Ledger Installation, the following columns include VAT information when a VAT tax code is present on the invoice:

- Paid amount includes paid current and retention VAT amounts

- Retention paid includes paid retention VAT amounts

- Invoice total includes total VAT amount
