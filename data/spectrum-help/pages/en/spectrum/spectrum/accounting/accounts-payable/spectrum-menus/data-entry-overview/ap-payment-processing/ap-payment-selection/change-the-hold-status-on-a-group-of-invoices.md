# Change the Hold Status on a Group of Invoices

1. Change the status display filter to show 'on hold –
 unable to pay'. Invoices that are currently on hold will have the
 Hold? checkbox selected and that Pay button will be dimmed.

1. Click the Release All Holds action button.
 You will see the number of invoices that will be taken off hold.

1. Click Continue to proceed.
