# View Subcontracts by Job

1. Enter a job code. A list of associated vendors and subcontracts displays. If the selected job is a Master job, this field will be labeled Master job and the grid will include sub-jobs for the master.

1. Click the Subcontract Status button and select the status types you want to see in the list.

1. Click New to begin a new subcontract for this job, and after entering a vendor and subcontract number the Subcontract Main Properties screen displays.
