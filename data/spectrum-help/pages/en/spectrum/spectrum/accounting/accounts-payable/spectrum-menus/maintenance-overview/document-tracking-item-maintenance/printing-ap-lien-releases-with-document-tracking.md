# Printing A/P Lien Releases with Document Tracking

Learn how to set up lien releases for printing with
 document tracking.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Payable > Maintenance > Document Tracking Items.

1. At the Vendor field, enter the
 vendor code of the vendor for which lien releases are being printed, and then press
 Enter.

1. At the Subcontract # field,
 leave the field blank and press Enter.

1. Click the Items button to
 display the Document Tracking Item Maintenance screen.

1. In the Tracking item code
 field, type LR for lien release, or double-click on this field
 or press F4 to select from a list of available tracking
 items.

1. In the Properties section of
 the screen, enter the necessary information at the
 Description, Trigger, and
 Message text fields, and then press
 Enter.

1. In the Actions section of the
 screen, select the checkboxes that correspond to the actions you want to appear on
 the lien release. For thorough explanations of each checkbox selection, click the
 Actions button.Important: At a minimum, you must select option
 six: Print lien release during check printing.

1. Open the Vendor Document
 Tracking screen.

1. On the Vendor Document Tracking
 screen, enter the necessary information in the first three fields:
 Vendor, Subcontract #, and
 Job, and then press Enter. Press
 F4 or double-click on this field to search the
 Vendor or Subcontract # fields.


1. At the Tracking item field,
 type LR for Lien Release, or press F4
 to select this option from the Tracking Item search
 window.

1. At the Actions field, the action numbers you selected on the Document Tracking Item Maintenance
 screen will display.Note: At a minimum, option number six (Print lien release
 during check printing) should display. If this field appears blank, you have not
 set up the document tracking items correctly. Click the Items button to return to the
 Document Tracking Item
 Maintenance screen, and repeat steps 1 - 6.

1. Enter the remaining line transaction field information
 at the appropriate fields: Date entered,
 By, and Comment, and then click
 OK.

Later, when you pay this subcontract, the lien
 release automatically displays. To verify that your lien release is displaying properly,
 print the Document Tracking Report for liens. Accessing the
 Document Tracking Report screen

- On the Site
 Map, click Accounts Payable > Reports > Document Tracking
 Reports. Be sure to select the Lien release option.
Note: Lien releases apply to the vendor or
 subcontractor until you remove them. To remove a lien release, open the
 Vendor/Subcon Document Tracking screen.
