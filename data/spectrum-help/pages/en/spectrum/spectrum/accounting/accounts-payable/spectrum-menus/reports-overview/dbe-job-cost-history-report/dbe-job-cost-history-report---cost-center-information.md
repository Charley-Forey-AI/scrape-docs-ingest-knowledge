# DBE Job Cost History Report - Cost Center Information

If the cost centers are being used, when printing the DBE J/C History Report Spectrum validates that the cost center assigned to the job is an authorized cost center for your operator. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your assigned cost center scheme, and if the job's cost center is not present, then that job will be omitted. Spectrum also compares the cost centers assigned to invoices applied to each job with cost centers in your assigned cost center scheme, and if there are no common cost centers, then those invoices are not included in the report totals.
