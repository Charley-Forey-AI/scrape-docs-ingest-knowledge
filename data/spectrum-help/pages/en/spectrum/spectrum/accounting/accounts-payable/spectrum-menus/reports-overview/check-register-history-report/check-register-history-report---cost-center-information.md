# Check Register History Report - Cost Center Information

 If cost centers are being used, for all formats of the Check Register History Report, Spectrum includes the checks on the report only you have permission to access the cost center assigned to the payment. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned in the payment with cost centers in your assigned cost center scheme, and if the payment cost center is not included, then that check is not shown on the report. The detail formats of the Check Register History Report show the cost center assigned to each payment.
