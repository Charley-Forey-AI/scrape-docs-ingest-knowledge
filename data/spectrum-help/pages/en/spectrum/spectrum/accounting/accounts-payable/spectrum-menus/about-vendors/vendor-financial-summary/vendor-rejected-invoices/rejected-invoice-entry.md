# Rejected Invoice Entry

Use this screen to keep track of disputed or "do not pay"
 invoices. Select a vendor and enter the invoice numbers that will be disallowed for entry.
 Once entered here, the software recognizes and validates the rejected invoices wherever
 invoices can be added, including: Unapproved Vendor Invoice Entry, Subcontract Billing,
 Purchase Order Receiving, and the Materials Management reconciliation screens.
