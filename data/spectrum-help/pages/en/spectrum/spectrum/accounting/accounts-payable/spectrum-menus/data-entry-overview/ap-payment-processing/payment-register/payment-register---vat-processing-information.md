# Payment Register - VAT Processing Information

If VAT processing is enabled in the General Ledger Installation screen, the following calculations include VAT:

- Invoice / Credit Memo Amount includes total VAT

- Previously Applied includes VAT amounts paid

- Total Payment includes total VAT being paid

- Retention Paid includes retention VAT being paid
