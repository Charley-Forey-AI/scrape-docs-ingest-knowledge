# Vendor Invoice Entry Part III - Optional Windows

During invoice entry or when editing a posted invoice, you can use a series of buttons to access windows containing details about each invoice.
These instructions are part of an entry or update process. If you need to enter a vendor invoice from the beginning, see [Enter a Vendor Invoice](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/enter-a-vendor-invoice).
These buttons open windows that allow you to review, change, or add details about the invoice, such as discount and routing information, user-defined information, and purchase order details. They're enabled or disabled based on certain vendor settings and your previous entries.
Depending on your setting preferences, you may not need to use them to open the corresponding window. Click the Preferences button to view these settings.

1. More info - click to display secondary
 invoice details, such as routing and discount information.

1. Routing - click to view any invoice routing history. If there is none
 for the selected invoice, the window appears blank.

1. User-Defined - click to open the Invoice
 User-Defined Fields window.

1. Details - click to open the Purchase Order Receiving Detail window.Enabled only when you are applying the invoice to a purchase order.
