# Display Total A/P Balance

This screen is used to view total Accounts Payable information by vendor and vendor type.
After you have selected the vendor and vendor type you want to view, click OK to advance to the [Total A/P Balance Display](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/inquiries-overview/total-ap-balance-display) screen.
