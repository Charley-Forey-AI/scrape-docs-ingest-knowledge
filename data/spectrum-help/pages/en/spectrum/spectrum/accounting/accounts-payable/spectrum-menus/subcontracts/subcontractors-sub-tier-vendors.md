# Subcontractor's Sub-Tier Vendors

Use this screen to record any sub-tier vendors on a
 specified subcontract. Setup here works in conjunction with Document Tracking to allow you to
 track certain documentation (Certified Payroll Reports, safety reports, etc.) about
 subcontracts pertaining to the subcontractor's subcontractors and other vendors.
