# Rejected Invoice Entry - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, when entering the vendor code Spectrum compares the vendor's list of shared cost centers with the cost centers in the operator's assigned scheme, and if there are no common cost centers, then the operator will not be allowed to proceed.
