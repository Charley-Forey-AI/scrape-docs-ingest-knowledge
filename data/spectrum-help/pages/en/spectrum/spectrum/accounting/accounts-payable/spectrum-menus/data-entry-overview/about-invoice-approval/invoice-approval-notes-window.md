# Invoice Approval Notes window

Use this window to record any notes pertaining to the
 invoice approval process.
The notes entered in this window will remain available in the
 Vendor Invoice Entry screen for the purposes of review, even
 after the invoice is confirmed, updated, and paid. Once the invoice is updated, the notes
 cannot be altered.
Fields
 Descriptions

VendorThe vendor code and name default from the
 Invoice Approval screen.
Entry typeThe entry type (Invoice or Credit Memo) defaults from the
 Invoice Approval screen.
Invoice noThe invoice number defaults from the Invoice Approval
 screen.
SeqThe system automatically assigns this note a sequential
 line number.
Reviewer / Notes
 The reviewer's logon ID and name display and cannot be
 changed. Enter any notes pertaining to this invoice.
