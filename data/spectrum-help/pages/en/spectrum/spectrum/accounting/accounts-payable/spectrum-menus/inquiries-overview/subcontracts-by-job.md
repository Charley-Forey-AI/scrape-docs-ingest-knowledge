# Subcontracts by Job

The Subcontract Inquiry by Job screen displays all the subcontracts for a specified job, either by phase or by vendor. Information appearing here is stored in the [Subcontracts](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/subcontracts) screen.
