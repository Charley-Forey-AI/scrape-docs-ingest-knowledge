# Manual Credit Card Payment Entry

Manual Credit Card Payment Entry is used to enter credit card payment information into the Accounts Payable system. This screen is only available only if the Cash Management module is installed.
Multiple invoices may be paid with one credit card. Retention amounts can also be paid by manual credit card entry.
