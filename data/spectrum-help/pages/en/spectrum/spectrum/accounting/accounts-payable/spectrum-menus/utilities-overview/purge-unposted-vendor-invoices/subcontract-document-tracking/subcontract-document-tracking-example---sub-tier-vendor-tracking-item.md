# Subcontract Document Tracking Example - Sub-Tier Vendor Tracking Item

Clicking the Sub-tier vendor compliance hyperlink will open the Job Compliance Tracking screen.
An Edit Date Range button may display, allowing the Subcontract Administrator to optionally specify Starting date and Ending dates for sub-tier vendors for the particular recurring tracking item. This is an optional feature--the software will auto-set the Starting date in Job Compliance Tracking when the user completes the first iteration of the recurring item. Likewise, when the job is complete, the compilation will cease automatically.
