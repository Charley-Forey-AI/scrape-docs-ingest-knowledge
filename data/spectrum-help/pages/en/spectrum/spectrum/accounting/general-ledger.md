# General Ledger

The General Ledger (GL) module maintains accounting information needed to track company and division performance and to make important financial decisions.
Since your Spectrum application retains all prior fiscal years' data, you can prepare current-year statements without closing the prior year.
With the General Ledger module you can also:

- Produce financial balance sheets and track company accounting activities.

- Design a wide variety of customized financial statements.
