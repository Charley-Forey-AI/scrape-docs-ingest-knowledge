# Labor Billing Rates Maintenance

Use this screen to establish labor Billing codes and up to five pay rate levels for each billing code. These billing codes are entered in Employee Maintenance, Union File Maintenance, Wage Code File Maintenance, and Non-Union Pay Group Maintenance in Spectrum Payroll.
The billing level is entered in the T+M Job Billing Setup screen as the labor level number. Once these are set up, then they are used in Billing Transaction Entry or Time Card Entry to determine the hourly rate to charge the client when actual labor hours are entered.
Click New or Edit to add or modify the information displayed in the list box. The [New/Edit Labor Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/labor-billing-rates-maintenance/newedit-labor-billing-rates) window displays.
