# Charge a Time + Material Change Request to a Fixed Job

If you need to record a time + materials change request to a fixed price job, complete the following steps. These steps assume that you are creating additional phases to track this T+ M activity.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Job Cost  >  Maintenance  >  Phase.

1. Enter a Job
 code.

1. Complete the fields in the New
 Phase window. In the Price type field, select Time + Material.

1. Click OK until you return to the Site
 Map screen.

1. Click Time + Material >  Maintenance >  Job Billing Setup.

1. Complete the Job and Customer
 fields.

1. Enter a Job and Customer code, and then complete the relevant fields in the
 Properties and Billing details and Billing
 address sections.

1. Enter any Mark-ups, Add-ons, and
 Fees for this job.

1. Click Save to return
 to the Site Map.

The following steps are optional. Complete them only if you want to record that there were estimates and a change request.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  > Change Request Entry.

1. Complete the Job, Customer,
 Change request, and Current status
 fields.

1. Add a change request to the newly created phase using the detail portion of
 the screen.Note: The information is automatically sent to the
 phase file in the Job Cost module.

1. Click Save to return to the Site
 Map.
