# Billing Transaction Entry: AP - Vendor

 This screen allows entry of invoices for materials and
 rented equipment which are to be updated to the Time + Material module as "AP" transactions.
When the AP - Vendor tab is selected, additions are made to the [Billing Transaction Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/billing-transaction-entry) screen. They do not
 affect the Accounts Payable or Job Cost modules.
