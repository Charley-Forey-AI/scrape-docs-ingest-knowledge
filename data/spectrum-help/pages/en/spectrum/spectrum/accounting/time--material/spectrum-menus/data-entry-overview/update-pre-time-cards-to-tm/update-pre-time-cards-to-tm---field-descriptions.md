# Update Pre-Time Cards To T+M - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Field
Description

Job
Enter the job number.

Period end date
Enter the period-end date of Pre-Time Card Entry records you want to include.
This is the date that will, in the future, be the period-end date reference in
 Set Payroll Cycle for these labor hours. Two-digit date entry is allowed and
 corresponds with the optional two-digit method for entering dates in Payroll > Pre-Time Card Entry.

Through date
Enter the work date through which information is to be included. All Pre-Time
 Card Entry for the specified job(s) prior to and including the date entered
 here will be included in the update. Two-digit date entry is allowed and
 corresponds with the optional two-digit method for entering dates in
 Payroll Pre-Time Card Entry.
