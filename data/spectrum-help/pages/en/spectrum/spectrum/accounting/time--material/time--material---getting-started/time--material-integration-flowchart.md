# Time + Material Integration Flowchart

The Time + Material module is integrated with Payroll, Job
 Cost, Inventory Control, Accounts Payable, Document Imaging, and Equipment Control, as well as
 the General Ledger and Accounts Receivable, to accumulate all billing amounts automatically.
