# Time + Materials Extras On A Fixed Price Contract

The following steps will help you establish a T+M phase
 within a fixed price contract.
Everything associated with the new phase will go to the Time
 + Materials module, unless the job is not set up in T+M Job Billing Setup. Use this
 procedure only when setting up T+M phases for jobs that are fixed or unit price
 contracts.

1. Enter the job in the Time + Material >  Maintenance >  Job Billing >  T+M Job Billing Setup screen.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Job Cost >  Maintenance >  Phase to open the Job
 Phases screen.

1. Enter the job number, new phase number, and
 description for the new phase.Note: When you press Enter to exit the
 Description field, the Phase
 Defaults window opens.

1. Complete the Phase Defaults window.Note: In the Unit of Measure field, use
 the drop-down menu to select the TM (Time + Materials) option.

1. Click OK to return to the Job
 Phases screen.

1. Complete the detail portion of the Job Phases screen.

1. Open and complete the Properties window.Note: You must select the Time + material option in the Price
 section in order to have this phase bill as Time + materials; otherwise, select
 Cost plus.

1. Click OK until you return to the Site Map screen.Note: It is necessary to add a T+M contract to identify billing criteria for these
 extras. Click Time + Materials >  Maintenance >  T + M Job Billing Setup and add the "extras" contract as required.
