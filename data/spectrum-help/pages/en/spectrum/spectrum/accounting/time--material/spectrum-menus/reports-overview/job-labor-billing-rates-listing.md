# Job Labor Billing Rates Listing

You can use the Job Labor Billing Rates Listing report to
 view special billing rates for jobs.
Use this report to view special billing rates set up for specific jobs
 in [T+M Job Labor Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/job-overview/tm-job-labor-billing-rates). The listing displays first in job number, then in billing code sequence.
