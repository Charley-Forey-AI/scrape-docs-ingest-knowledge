# Set Up a T+M Phase for a Fixed or Unit Price Job

Sometimes a fixed price job will have a phase, possibly due
 to a change order, that needs to be billed as Time + Materials or Cost plus. The following
 procedure will guide you through the process of adding a T+M or Cost plus phase.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Job Cost  >  Job  >  Phases.

1. Complete the Job,
 Phase, and Description fields.

1. Enter the cost type code, or press
 F4 or double-click on this field to select from a list of
 available cost type codes.

1. From the Info Bar, complete the fields in the T+ M Billing Setup link as needed, and then click Save.

1. On the Info Bar, click the Main link.

1. In the Price type field, select
 Time + material or Cost plus. Complete the remaining fields with any other applicable
 information.

1. Repeat steps 3-6 until all Time + Material and/or Cost plus phases and cost types are entered.

1. Click OK until you return to
 the Site Map screen.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Time + Material  >  Maintenance  >  T+M JobBilling.

1. Enter the job code in the Job
 field.Note: If necessary, make adjustments to the billing and tax
 information.

1. Verify the billing levels for equipment, material, and labor.

1. Add any necessary markups, add-ons, and fees.

1. Save your entry and return to the Site
 Map screen.
