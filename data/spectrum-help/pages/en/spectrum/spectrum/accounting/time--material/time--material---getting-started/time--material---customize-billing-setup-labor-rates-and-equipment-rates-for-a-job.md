# Time + Material - Customize Billing Setup, Labor Rates, and
 Equipment Rates for a Job

After you set up a Time + Material job, complete the
 billing setup, and review or customize labor and equipment rates as needed.

1. Select Job Cost > Jobs > Jobs.

1. Select the Time + Material job to review.

1. In the Info Bar, select Properties > T+M Billing Setup.For field descriptions, see [T+M
 Job Billing Setup](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/job-overview/tm-job-billing-setup).

1. Set a Not to exceed amount.
 This is the maximum dollar amount allowed for billings on this job. This amount may
 established by contract terms or based on your organization's standards.Important: If job billings exceed this amount, the
 system provides a warning and automatically generates the Not to Exceed Billing
 Exception Report.

1. For Billing method, select A/R
 invoice.

1. If billings for this job are subject to tax, select
 the Taxable? checkbox. The Sales tax code prefills based on
 the job location, but you can edit it as needed.

1. Under Billing details, the
 following fields prefill based on the customer's setup: Equipment
 level, Material level, and Labor
 level. However, you can change these entries as needed.

1. The Next billing # and Next transaction # default to
 1.

- The billing number refers to where this invoice falls in the
 sequence of invoices for this job (not the invoice number). For example, the
 first invoice is billing 1, the second invoice is billing 2, and so on.

- The transaction number refers to the number of transactions
 in this job that were sent to the Time + Material module for billing. For
 example, if 100 labor lines have been sent to Time + Material for billing, the
 next transaction number is 101.

1. Verify Billing address information.

1. Click the buttons labeled [Time + Material - Create Default Markups](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/installation-overview/time--material---create-default-markups), [Time + Material - Create Default Add-Ons](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/installation-overview/time--material---create-default-add-ons), and
 [Time + Material - Create Default Fees](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/installation-overview/time--material---create-default-fees) to
 review defaults set up in the Time + Material Installation screen. You can add,
 modify, or delete these default settings as needed.

1. When you are finished reviewing T+M Billing
 Setup, click Save.

1. In the Info Bar, select Properties > T+M Equipment Rates. Review any job-specific equipment billing codes that you have set
 up:Note: For field descriptions, see [New/Edit Job Equipment Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/tm-job-equipment-billing-rates/newedit-job-equipment-billing-rates).

1. Select the [Time + Material - Set Up T+M Equipment Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/time--material---getting-started/time--material---set-up-tm-equipment-billing-rates) to review.

1. To modify the code, click
 Edit.

1. Add, edit, or delete equipment rates as
 needed.

1. When you are finished, click
 OK

1. In the Info Bar, select Properties > T+M Labor Rates. Review any job-specific labor billing rates that you have set
 up.Note: For field descriptions, see [New/Edit Job Labor Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/tm-job-labor-billing-rates/newedit-job-labor-billing-rates).

1. Select the [Time + Material - Set Up T+M Labor Billing Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/time--material---getting-started/time--material---set-up-tm-labor-billing-rates) to review:

1. To modify the code, click
 Edit.

1. Add, edit, or delete labor billing rates as
 needed.

1. When you are finished, click
 OK.
