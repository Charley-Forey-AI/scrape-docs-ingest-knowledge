# Add a Line to a T+M Billing

Complete the following steps to add a line to a billing in
 the Time + Material module.

1. On the Site Map, click Time + Material  >  Data Entry  >  Billing Transaction
 Entry.

1. In the Transaction Date field, enter the date of the transaction and then
 click one of the transaction type tabs (JC - Job, AP -
 Vendor, EQ -
 Equipment, IC -
 Inventory, or PR -
 Employee).Note: The date entered in this field must be within the billing cycle.

1. If you click the AP - Vendor tab in step 2, the Time +
 Materials Accounts Payable Transaction Entry window displays. Complete the Vendor code, Job, Phase, Ct (cost type), Invoice #, and Amount fields.

1. Once the applicable transaction windows have been completed, click Save.

1. Click the Update button. The Time + Materials Journal screen
 displays.

1. Complete the Job , From date, and Through date fields.

1. In the Transaction type section, select a specific
 type of transaction based on the entry made in the Time + Materials Transaction Entry
 screen, or select All to
 include all transaction types.

1. Click Preview.

1. In the T+M Transaction Update screen, select
 Continue and then click
 OK. This update causes
 the transaction to display as a T+ M billing, but it does not display in the General
 Ledger.Note: If you pre-enter an A/P invoice, make sure you create a
 negative invoice to off-set the positive transaction that will come from the
 source later. This is done when a company wants to include an expected invoice in
 the current bill cycle but the invoice hasn't arrived yet.
