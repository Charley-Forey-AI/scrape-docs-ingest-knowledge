# Modify T+M Billing - Billing Summary - VAT processing
 information

If the 'Utilize value added tax (VAT) tracking?' checkbox
 is selected in General Ledger Installation, a 'VAT tax' field and a 'VAT tax percent' field
 displays after the 'Sales tax' field. After these fields are completed, the system will
 calculate the VAT tax amount (calculated as Subtotal x VAT percentage/100).
In the Invoice defaults portion of the screen, a 'Retention VAT' field displays (calculated as VAT tax x retention percent/100). The 'Current due' amount is calculated as 'Total earned less retention' minus 'Previous certificates' of payment' + Sales tax + Current VAT Tax.
