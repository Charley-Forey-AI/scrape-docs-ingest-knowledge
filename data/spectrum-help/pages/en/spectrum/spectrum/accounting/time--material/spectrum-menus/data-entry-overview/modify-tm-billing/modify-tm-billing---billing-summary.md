# Modify T+M Billing - Billing Summary

Use this screen to review the pertinent add-ons, fees, sales tax, invoice total, retention, and balance amounts.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Financials

Billing total
The non-taxable and taxable amounts, as well as the sum of both columns for this billing display.

Add-ons
The taxable, nontaxable, and total add-on amounts for this billing display.

Fees
The total amount of fees for this billing displays.

Subtotal
This is the sum of the Billing total plus Add-ons plus Fees.
The Billing method will display to the right of the subtotal amount. If the billing method is a Change request, the rest of the fields will be hidden and this line will be the total amount.

Sales tax
The total sales tax amount displays.

Invoice total
The invoice total (sum of the "billing amount" column, "add-ons", "fees", and "sales tax") displays.

Override Add-ons
Click to open the [Override Add-ons](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/modify-tm-billing/override-add-ons) window.

Override Fees
Click to open the [Override Fees](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/time--material/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/modify-tm-billing/override-fees) window.

Invoice defaults

Invoice date
Press Enter to accept the current Time + Materials processing date, or click the arrow to enter a new Invoice date.

G/L date
Press Enter to accept the current G/L date for the invoice, or click the arrow to enter a new date.
In Accounts Receivable Installation, if the Invoice date must be in G/L period checkbox is selected, the G/L date must be in the same period as the Invoice date.

Retention %
Press Enter to accept the current retention percentage for this billing or enter a new percentage.

Retention
Press Enter to accept the current retention amount. If you enter a new number, the Retention % and amount are recalculated.

Current due
The current amount due displays. If you enter a new amount, the Retention % and amount are recalculated.
