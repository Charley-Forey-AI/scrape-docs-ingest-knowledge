# Use T+M to Compile Job Extras Before the Work Becomes a Change Request

Complete the following steps to compile extras on a fixed
 price job before the work becomes a change request.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Time + Material  >  Reports  >  Pre-Billing.

1. Run the Pre-Billing Report to determine what work is awaiting the billing process, and then return to the Site Map screen.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Time + Material  >  Data Entry  >  Summary Billing
 Selection.

1. On the Summary Billing Selection screen, select the billing transactions for the current cycle and then select Continue before clicking OK until you return to the Site Map screen.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Time + Material  >  Data Entry  >  Detail Billing Form.

1. On the Detail Billing Formscreen, complete the fields as needed and then print the report and then return to the Site Map screen.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Time + Material  >  Data Entry  >  Update Billings.

1. On the Update T+M Billings to Invoice Entryscreen, select the Job and AR invoice batch for all billing items that need to be transferred to Accounts Receivable.

1. Click OK
 and then click Continue.

1. Preview the Time + Material Processing Billing Register Updated to A/R Invoice.

1. With the invoice's dollar amount, which displays on the Billing Register, you can now create the change request in Accounts Receivable.
