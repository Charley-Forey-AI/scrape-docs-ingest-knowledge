# Billing Transaction Entry: EQ - Equipment

When the EQ - Equipment tab is selected, additions are made
 to the Billing Transaction Entry screen.
 This screen allows entry of Time + Material equipment usage which is to be updated to the
 Time + Material module as "EQ" transactions. They do not affect the Equipment Control or
 Job Cost modules.
