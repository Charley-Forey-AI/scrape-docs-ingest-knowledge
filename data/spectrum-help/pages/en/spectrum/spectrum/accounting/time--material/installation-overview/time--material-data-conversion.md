# Time + Material Data Conversion

Below is a summary of the steps needed to complete
 installation. It is important that these steps be performed in the order listed.

1. Complete the Time + Material Installation screen.

1. Enter the Time + Material billing rates for labor using Labor Billing Rates Maintenance.

1. Print the Time + Material labor billing rates and check for accuracy.

1. Enter the Time + Material billing rates for equipment using Equipment Billing Rates Maintenance.

1. Print the Time + Material equipment billing rates and check for accuracy.

1. Enter the standard Time + Material markups using Cost Type Markup File Maintenance.

1. Print the standard Time + Material markups and check for accuracy.

1. Enter the standard Time + Material add-ons using Add-on File Maintenance.

1. Print the standard Time + Material add-ons and check for accuracy.

1. Complete the Time + Material portion of the Processing Date Change screen.

1. Enter all Cost Plus and Time + Material jobs in process as of the conversion date using T+M Job Billing Setup. Note that jobs entered through Jobs with price types Time + Materials or Cost Plus will automatically be set up in T+M Job Billing Setup when the job is added.

1. Enter any special markups, add-ons, or fees for Time + Material or Cost Plus jobs in process as of the conversion date using T+M Job Billing Setup. If nothing is added in this screen for a job, then the standard markups, add-ons, and fees from the installation screen will be used.

1. Print the job billing listing and review for accuracy.

1. Enter any special labor rates for specific Time + Materials or Cost Plus jobs in process as of the conversion date using Job Labor Billing Rate Maintenance.

1. Print the job labor rate listing and review for accuracy. The Copy Job Labor Billing Rates option can be used to copy these rates from one job to another, which can save time on step 14 above.

1. Enter any special equipment rates for specific Time + Materials or Cost Plus jobs in process as of the conversion date using Job Equipment Billing Rate Maint.

1. Print the job equipment rate listing and review for accuracy. The Copy Equipment Billing Rates option can be used to copy these rates from one job to another, which can save time on step16 above.

1. Complete the "Time + Material" portion of the Processing Date Change screen, setting the minimum and maximum dates as desired, and "current" to the actual calendar date.
