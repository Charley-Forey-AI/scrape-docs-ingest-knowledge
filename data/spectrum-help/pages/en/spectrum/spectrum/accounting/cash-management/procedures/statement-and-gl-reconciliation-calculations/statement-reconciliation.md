# Statement Reconciliation

Learn about the Statement Reconciliation.

## Opening statement balance

+ Cleared deposits (per Bank Reconciliation Detail Listing)
- Cleared checks (per Bank Reconciliation Detail Listing)
=Sub-total ending calculated statement balance
-Entered ending statement balance
=Calculated balance (difference between entered and calculated ending
 statement balance)
(The beginning statement balance is either brought forward from the
 prior month's ending balance or it can be entered manually depending on the
 Reconciliation recalculation option selected on the Cash Management  >  Properties screen.)
