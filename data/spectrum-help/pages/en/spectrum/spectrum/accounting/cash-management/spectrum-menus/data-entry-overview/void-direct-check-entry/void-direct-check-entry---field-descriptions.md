The name and address of the check recipient, check date, check amount and any notes associated with the check display.
The check date is the date typed on the check, which can either be the date of issuance or the date of adjudication by the software. Governments payroll tax filings are all based on check date, not on period end dates. The check date is not necessarily the date the item is cleared (processed) by the bank.
