# Bank Reconciliation Transaction Update

Use the Bank Reconciliation Transaction Update to post the
 transactions entered to G/L and onto the Bank Reconciliation list as part of the
 Reconciliation processing.
 "Balanced entry" protection is incorporated into this function to assure the General
 Ledger will stay in balance. If debits do not equal credits (such as due to a power failure
 or data error), the software will provide a special screen message and disallow update
 until the problem is corrected. Consult the support desk at Viewpoint for instructions if
 it is necessary to correct an out-of-balance error.
