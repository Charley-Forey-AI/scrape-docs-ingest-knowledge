# Check Float History Listing

Use the Check Float History Listing screen to print a
 listing of check history float data.

1. Enter the bank account code, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.

1. Enter the check date.

1. Enter the source: AP, PR, CM, or ALL.

1. The current Cash Management processing date defaults. Press Enter to accept this default or enter the through clear date.

1. Click Preview.
