# Void Direct Check Entry - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the header cost center associated with the direct check transaction is displayed on the screen after the check number is entered. This cost center will be assigned to the void transaction. Cost centers assigned to the distribution detail lines of the original check will also be assigned to the void entry.
Operator scheme override settings are not applicable in this screen.
