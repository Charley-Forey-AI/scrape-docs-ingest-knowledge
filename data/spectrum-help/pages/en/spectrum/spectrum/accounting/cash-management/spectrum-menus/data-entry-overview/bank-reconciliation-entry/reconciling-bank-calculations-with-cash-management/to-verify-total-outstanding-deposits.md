# To Verify Total Outstanding Deposits

Learn how to verify total outstanding deposits.

1. Add the total outstanding deposits from the previous
 month to the total deposits from this month. (Hint: Use the A/R Cash Receipts G/L
 Distribution Report to obtain the total deposits for this month.)

1. To this sum, subtract the total cleared deposits; the
 result equals this month's outstanding deposits.

1. Record any wire transfers in Cash Management and run
 the update.

1. Record any miscellaneous fees in Cash Management and
 run the update.

1. Out of period transactions must be recorded (and
 reversed) in another period, otherwise the account will not balance.
