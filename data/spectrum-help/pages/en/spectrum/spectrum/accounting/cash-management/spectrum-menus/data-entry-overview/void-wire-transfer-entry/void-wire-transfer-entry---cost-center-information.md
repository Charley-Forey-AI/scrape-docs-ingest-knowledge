# Void Wire Transfer Entry - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the cost center associated with the wire transfer transaction is displayed on the screen after the wire transfer number is entered. This cost center will be assigned to the void transaction.
Operator scheme override settings are not applicable in this screen.
