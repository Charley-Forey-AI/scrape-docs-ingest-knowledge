# Credit Card Reconciliation Listing

The Credit Card Reconciliation Listing provides a listing
 of transactions recorded for credit card reconciliation. Credit card reconciliations being
 updated will open in a separate tab for review.
Once the credit card reconciliation has been updated, you
 cannot print the Credit Card Reconciliation Listing for the time period that was updated.
 We recommend that you print and archive the Credit Card Reconciliation Listing prior to
 updating.

1. Enter the credit card account code, or press
 Enter to accept the default of ALL.


1. Select the listing type:
 Detail, Summary, or
 Card-specific.

1. Click Preview.Note: The software verifies that only credit
 account codes are included on this listing. Bank accounts are excluded from this
 listing by Spectrum.
