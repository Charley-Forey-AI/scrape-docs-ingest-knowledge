#  Wire Transfer Listing

Use the Wire Transfer Listing to review all wire transfers
 for the selected bank accounts and wire transfer numbers recorded since the last
 update.

1. Enter the bank account code, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.

1. Enter the wire transfer number, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.

1. Click Preview.
