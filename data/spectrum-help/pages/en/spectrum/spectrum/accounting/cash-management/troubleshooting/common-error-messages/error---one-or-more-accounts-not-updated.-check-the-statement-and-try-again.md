# Error - One Or More Accounts Not Updated. Check The Statement And Try Again.

At least one of the bank accounts listed on the Bank
 Reconciliation Update start screen was not in balance when you performed the
 update.

1. Verify that the bank accounts you would like to update
 are reconciled.

1. Run the Bank Reconciliation Update again, making sure that the appropriate bank account codes are entered.
