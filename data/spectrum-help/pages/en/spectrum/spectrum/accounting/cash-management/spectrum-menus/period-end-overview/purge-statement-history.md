# Purge Statement History

The Purge Statement History screen lists and removes items from the Bank Statement History and Credit Card History files. Items are removed based on the selected statement date. Only items that have been reconciled are stored in the Bank Statement History file or Credit Card History file. A purge listing report can be printed before the update proceeds.
Important: This report should be retained as a permanent
 audit record of the company.
