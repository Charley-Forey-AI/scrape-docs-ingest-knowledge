# To Verify Total Cleared Checks

Learn how to verify total cleared checks.

1. Use the [Statement History Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/cash-management/spectrum-menus/reports-overview/statement-history-report) to determine the total outstanding checks in Cash Management from the previous month.

1. Run the A/P Check Register History Report to determine the total checks for the current month and add this amount to the total outstanding checks.

1. From this sum, subtract the total cleared checks; the result equals this month's outstanding checks.
