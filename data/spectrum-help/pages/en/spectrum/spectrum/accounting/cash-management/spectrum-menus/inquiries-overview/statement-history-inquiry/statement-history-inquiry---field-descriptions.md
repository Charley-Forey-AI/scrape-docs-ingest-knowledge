# Statement History Inquiry - Field Descriptions

Use this table for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Field
Description

Account
Enter the bank account code. The corresponding name of the bank displays.

Statement date
Enter the statement date. Based on the statement date , the associated G/L period end date displays below.

Account type
The statement account type will display in this field: Bank or Credit card

Account balance
The account opening balance, deposits, checks and ending balance totals display on the right side of the screen.

Deposits tab
This tab displays transaction details for all deposits and wire transfers for the selected bank account and statement date.
The total number of deposit transactions that have been reconciled and the total dollar amount of the deposits display below.

Checks tab
Select this tab to display transaction details for all checks for the selected bank statement and statement date.
The total number of check transactions that have been reconciled and the total dollar amount of the checks display below.
