# New/Edit Account - Properties tab

Use the Properties tab to enter general information about the account and contact information.
FieldsDescriptions
Account codeFor a new account, enter an account code. This can be short, convenient codes such as 1, 2, 3, or can be designed to match the General Ledger account code for the associated cash account.
Account
Account nameEnter the account name to appear throughout Spectrum. This could be the name of the bank or credit agency, for example, U.S. Bank, or the purpose of the bank account, for example, Investment Account.
Account numberEnter the account number.
Telephone/FaxEnter the primary telephone number and fax number of the bank or credit card company.
Account typeSelect an account type:

- Bank

- Credit card

The account type you select determines the fields you see on the [Details tab](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/cash-management/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/account-code-file-maintenance/newedit-account---details-tab).

Contact
Account representativeEnter the name of the account representative and their branch/division, telephone, FAX, and email address. If the bank or credit card company has a web site, enter the URL.

NotesEnter any notes related to this account.
