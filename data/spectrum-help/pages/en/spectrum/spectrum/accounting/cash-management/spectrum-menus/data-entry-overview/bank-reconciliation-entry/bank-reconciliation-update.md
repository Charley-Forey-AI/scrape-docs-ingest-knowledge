# Bank Reconciliation Update

 Use the Bank Reconciliation Update screen to update information to Cash Management history files and set the beginning balance for the next statement.
During this update, Spectrum automatically deselects any accounts that are not bank accounts. In other words, all credit card type accounts are excluded from this update.
