# Void Direct Checks Listing

The Void Direct Checks Listing is used to review a listing
 of the voided checks recorded since the last update.

1. Enter the bank account code, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.

1. Enter the check number, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.

1. Click Preview.
