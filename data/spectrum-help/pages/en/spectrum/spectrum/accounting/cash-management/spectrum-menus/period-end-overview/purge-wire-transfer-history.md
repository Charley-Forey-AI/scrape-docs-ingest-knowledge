# Purge Wire Transfer History

The Purge Wire Transfer History screen lists and removes items from the wire transfer history file. Items are removed based on the selected transaction date. Only items that have been updated are stored in the wire transfer history file. A purge listing can be printed before the update proceeds.
Important: This report should be retained as a permanent
 audit record of the company.
