# Void Direct Checks Register

This update posts the void transactions to the bank
 reconciliation file and then posts this as a B source in the General Ledger. This update will
 also debit the cash account and credit the General Ledger accounts from the original
 distribution

1. Enter the bank account code, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.

1. Enter the check number, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.

1. Click Preview to generate a listing report of the selected void transactions.

1. When this update is run, a second [Void Direct Check Update](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/cash-management/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/void-direct-check-entry/void-direct-check-update) screen displays.

1. Select Continue to complete the update.Important: This report should be retained as a permanent audit
 record of the company.
