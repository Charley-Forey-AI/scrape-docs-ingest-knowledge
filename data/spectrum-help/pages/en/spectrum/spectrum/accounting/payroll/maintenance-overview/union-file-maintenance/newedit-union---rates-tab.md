# New/Edit Union - Rates tab

This tab is used to enter up to nine wage rates for every
 union code.
For example, someone working in the Laborer's Union would be paid one rate while working
 as a flagger, a different rate while working with a jackhammer, and still a different
 rate while working with a shovel.
The usual wage rate (pay) level may be entered in Employees, and the current payroll processing date determines which rate displays in this screen, but any of the wage levels (1-9) may be entered to automatically record the regular, overtime, and double overtime rates for that particular union code. This is beneficial even for those companies that pay union wage scale but are not necessarily union contractors.
