# Changing the Certified Report Week Number

The software looks to find the first date that payroll was
 charged to the job.
Follow the steps below to change the Payroll No. on the Certified Payroll Report if it currently
 displays the wrong week number. Important: You will need
 to have posted at least one time card to the certified job to perform this procedure!

To change the initial date in the software:

1. Go to Payroll > Period End > Employee Certified
 History.

1. Call up all employees with time cards prior to the
 certified date. If only one employee is selected and if the employee is not the
 oldest time card on the job, no change will occur!

1. Highlight the appropriate check and click the
 Time Card button.
 Clearing the Certified checkbox on the time card allows the
 Certified Payroll Report to use a later time card date for the report number.

1. Change the Work date to the first day the job
 was certified.Note: These steps only have to be
 performed on one time card line. It is not necessary to perform these steps on
 multiple time card lines as the SQL view looks to find the first date payroll was
 charged to the job.

1. Rerun the Certified Payroll
 Report. Notice that the week number has changed accordingly.
