# Non-Union Pay Group Employee Overrides

Use this tab to review the employees' wage information in detail. Any employee-specific wage rates entered here will override the pay group rates, but if the phase overrides checkbox is selected in the Phase Rate Override screen, then the phase rate will receive the highest priority.
