# Payroll Reports

The Reports menu provides various period end reports.
Available reports include worker's compensation tax, union information, and many more.
Use these to obtain employee information such as earnings history, individual employee records, listings of all birth dates, hire and termination dates, seniority, or supervisor.
A report is also available that displays all employee information changed since the last time the change log was purged.
