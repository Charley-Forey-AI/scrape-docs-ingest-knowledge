# Birthday List - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields
 on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Date
Enter the begin and end months and days to be
 included on this report, or press Enter to
 include ALL days.

Employee status
Enter an employee status to include on the
 report, or press Enter to include ALL records.

Include spouse?
Select the checkbox to include employee's
 spouses on this report, otherwise leave the checkbox clear to
 exclude spouses.
