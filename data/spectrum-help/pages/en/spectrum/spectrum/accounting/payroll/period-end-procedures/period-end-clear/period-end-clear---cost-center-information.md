# Period End Clear - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in this company, then when
 performing the month-end or quarter-end update, Spectrum will zero the employee balances only
 if you have permission to access the employee.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the employee
 with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is
 not included the employee will not be cleared.If the year-end update
 is performed, you must have security authorization for all affected employees in order
 to perform the update, otherwise the update will not be allowed. Spectrum will also
 assure that no terminated or deceased employees marked for deletion at year end are
 assigned to disallowed cost centers.
If cost center entities
 are being used in this company, an Entity field displays and allows you to perform the
 clear on an entity-by-entity basis. Leaving the 'Entity' field blank will perform the
 clear for the main company entity.
Important: While
 the month-to-date and quarter-to-date clears are controlled on an entity by entity
 basis, the Year End Payroll Clear must be coordinated among all Payroll users because
 year-end validations and tax rate adjustments affect all users.
