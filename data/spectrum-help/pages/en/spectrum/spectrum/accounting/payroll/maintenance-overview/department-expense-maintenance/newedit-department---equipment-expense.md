# New/Edit Department - Equipment Expense

This tab is enabled only if Direct cost = Equipment cost.
Field
Descriptions

% payroll burden debit
Enter the G/L account that will serve as the percent payroll burden expense account, or press F4 to select from a list of available accounts. No entry is required if the account status is Not Used.

% payroll burden credit
Enter the G/L account that will serve as the percent payroll burden liability account, or press F4 to select from a list of available accounts. No entry is required if the account status is Not Used.
