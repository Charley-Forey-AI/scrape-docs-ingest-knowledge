# New/Edit Department - Direct Cost

Use this tab to enter the corresponding G/L codes for the payroll burden, overhead, and JX pay type.
This tab will be disabled if the Direct Cost = Non-direct cost on the Properties tab. The Job cost, Equipment cost and Work order cost fields will only be available if they are selected in the Direct cost field on the Properties tab.
