# Utilities Overview

Use selections from this menu for a variety of maintenance actions.
A few examples include:

- change employee codes

- copy information from one union file to another

- copy non-union pay group information from one group to another

 On the Site Map screen, go to Payroll > Utilities, and then one of menu selections listed here.
