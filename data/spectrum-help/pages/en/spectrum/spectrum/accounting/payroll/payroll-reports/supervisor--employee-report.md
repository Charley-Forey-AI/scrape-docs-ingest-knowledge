# Supervisor / Employee Report

The Supervisor / Employee Report prints a list of
 supervisors and the employees under their supervision.
The report prints in supervisor code sequence. Within each supervisor, employees are listed
 in alphabetical sequence. Employees that have not been assigned a supervisor in Employees
 do not appear on the report.
