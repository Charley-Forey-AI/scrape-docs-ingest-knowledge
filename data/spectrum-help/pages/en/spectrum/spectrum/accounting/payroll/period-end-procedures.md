# Period End Procedures

The Period End menu is used at the
 end of every payroll period (month/quarter/year). This menu normally would not be accessed
 until after all required reports are obtained through the Reports
 menu.
From the Site Map screen, click Payroll > Period End > Period End menu. The selections are discussed below.
Menu offerings shown below are in alphabetical order.Important: Extreme caution should be
 exercised when performing purges!
