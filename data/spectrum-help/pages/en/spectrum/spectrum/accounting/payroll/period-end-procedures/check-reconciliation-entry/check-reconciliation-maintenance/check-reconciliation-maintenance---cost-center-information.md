# Check Reconciliation Maintenance - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in this company, then when
 adding checks, Spectrum will prompt for a cost center to assign to the transaction.
Spectrum verifies that the operator has permission to assign that cost center. When
 accessing existing checks, Spectrum displays the item only if you have permission to
 access the check. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned in the check with cost
 centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center of the
 check is not included, then the check will not appear. Likewise, Spectrum also assures
 that the employee's assigned cost center is valid for your operator and if not, then
 checks for that employee will not display.
