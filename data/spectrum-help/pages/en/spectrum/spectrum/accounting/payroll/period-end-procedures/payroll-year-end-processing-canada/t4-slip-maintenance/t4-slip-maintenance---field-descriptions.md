# T4 Slip Maintenance - Field Descriptions

To make changes, select a row and click the Edit or Delete
 action buttons.
Information in the maintenance screen columns displays after being entered in
 the [New T4 Slip](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/period-end-procedures/payroll-year-end-processing-canada/t4-slip-maintenance/new-t4-slip) screen.
Buttons
Descriptions

New
Click this button to open the [New T4 Slip](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/period-end-procedures/payroll-year-end-processing-canada/t4-slip-maintenance/new-t4-slip) screen.

Build
Click this button to open the [Build T4 Slips](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/period-end-procedures/payroll-year-end-processing-canada/build-t4-slips) starting screen.

Listing
Click this button to open the [T4 Slip Listing](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/period-end-procedures/payroll-year-end-processing-canada/t4-slip-listing) screen.

Print T4
Click this button to open the [Print T4 Slips](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/period-end-procedures/payroll-year-end-processing-canada/print-t4-slips) screen.

Export
Click this button to open the [Export T4 File](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/period-end-procedures/payroll-year-end-processing-canada/t4-slip-maintenance/export-t4-file) screen.

Backup
Click this button to open the Backup T4 Records to History window. This utility stores the current T4 information into a history table for future use.

Columns
Descriptions

Payer tax ID
When adding a new record, the software auto-assigns the tax ID number stored during the Build T4 Slips Update.

Employee code
The employee code or press F4 and select one from the available Search Employees window.

Province
The employee's province or territory of employment displays.

Last name First name
The employee's last and first name display.

Social insurance #
The employee's Social Insurance Number (SIN) displays. If the employee doesn't have a SIN, enter nine zeros.

14 income
This is the total employment income before deductions. It includes all salary, wages, bonuses, vacation pay, tips, honorariums, director's fees, management fees, and executor's and administrator's fees for administering an estate.

22 income tax
This is the total income tax deducted from the employee's remuneration and retiring allowances.

Tax code
Tax codes reflect any employment commissions, taxable allowances and benefits, deductible amounts, fisher's income, and other entries if they apply.
