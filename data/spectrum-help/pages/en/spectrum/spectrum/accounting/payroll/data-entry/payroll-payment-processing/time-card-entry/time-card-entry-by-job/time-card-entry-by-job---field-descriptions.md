# Time Card Entry by Job - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Company
The current company code displays in this field.

Job
Enter the job number.

Totals

Regular hours
This field displays the total of the non-overtime hours entered for the job.

Overtime hours
This field displays the total of the worker's overtime hours entered for the job.

Double time hours
This field displays the total number of the worker's double-time hours entered for the job.

Equipment hours
The equipment hours for this job display, if applicable.

Extension
This field displays the gross payroll dollars due this employee, based on time card entry below.

Use these buttons to add, make changes, or delete time card lines.
