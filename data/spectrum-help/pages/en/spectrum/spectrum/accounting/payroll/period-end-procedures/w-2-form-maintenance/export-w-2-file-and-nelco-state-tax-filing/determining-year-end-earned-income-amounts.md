# Determining Year End Earned Income Amounts

Follow these steps to calculate Year End earned income
 amounts.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Payroll  >  Period End  >  W-2 Form Maintenance to open the screen.

1. Enter the employee's information and click the Build button to open the Build W-2 Forms screen.

1. Complete the update screen and select Continue.

1. Preview the Deduction History Report for the full year (see the [Advanced Earned Income Credit](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/maintenance-overview/deduction--add-on-code-maintenance/advanced-earned-income-credit) topic for directions) to determine the total amount paid.
