# Copy Employees to Another Company - Confirmation - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the destination company,
 then the cost center assigned to the employee in the current company are stored in the
 destination company during the update.

- If cost centers are being used in the destination company, then the cost center assigned to the employee in the current company are stored in the destination company during the update.

- If cost centers are being used (or are set to Pending) in the currently company but they are NOT being used in the destination company, then the cost center is automatically set to <blank> in the destination company during the update.
