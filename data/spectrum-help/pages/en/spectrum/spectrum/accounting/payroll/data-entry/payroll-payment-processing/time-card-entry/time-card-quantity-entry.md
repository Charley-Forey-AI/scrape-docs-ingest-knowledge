# Time Card Quantity Entry

This screen is not used in calculating payroll, but is intended to facilitate entry of job cost information.
These quantities will update to Job Cost when the Payroll Update function is executed. Use of this feature may serve as a substitute for use of Quantity Complete Entry in Job Cost.
