# Quantity Complete Entry

The Quantity Complete Entry screen is not used in calculating payroll, but is intended to facilitate entry of job cost information.
This feature is convenient for companies who have unit priced jobs and whose quantity-complete information flow from the field corresponds with the Payroll process. Once the Payroll Update is run, the information in this screen updates to the Job Cost module.
