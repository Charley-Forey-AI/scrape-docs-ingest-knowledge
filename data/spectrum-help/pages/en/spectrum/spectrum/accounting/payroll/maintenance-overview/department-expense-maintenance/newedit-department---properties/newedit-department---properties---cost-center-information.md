# New/Edit Department - Properties - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, the Cost Center Overrides
 will display to allow you to set up cost center overrides in the Salary/Wages section of the
 screen.
For more information, see [New/Edit Department - Cost Center Overrides](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/maintenance-overview/department-expense-maintenance/newedit-department---properties/newedit-department---cost-center-overrides).
