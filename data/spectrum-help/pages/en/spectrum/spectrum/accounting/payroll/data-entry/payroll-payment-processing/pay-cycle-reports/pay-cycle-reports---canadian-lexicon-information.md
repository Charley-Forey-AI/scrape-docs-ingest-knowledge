# Pay Cycle Reports - Canadian lexicon information

If the Canadian lexicon designation (en-ca) is selected in
 Company Installation, some labels on all pay cycle reports will display using the preferred
 Canadian spelling or reference.
For example, "cheque" instead of "check" and "Province" instead of
 "State".
