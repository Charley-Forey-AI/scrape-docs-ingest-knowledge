# Correcting Details on the Employee Certified History Report

Occasionally you may find that you need to reclassify the
 Employee Certified History Report.
If you print the report and notice that some data is
 missing, you can correct this issue by performing the following steps.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Payroll  >  Period End  >  Employee Certified History.

1. Enter the employee code for whom you wish to make a modification, or press F4 to view the search window and select.

1. Select the check number that is missing the data and click the Time Card button. The Time Card History Detail window displays.

1. Modify the Cert (certified) column by selecting the checkbox.

1. If necessary, change the Work date to the correct work date (make sure the Work date is what the certified date should have been).

1. If the wrong number of hours has been entered, you
 cannot correct this here. See [Correcting Payroll Hours](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/inquiries-overview/employees/employee-financials/employee-certified-history/correcting-details-on-the-employee-certified-history-report/correcting-payroll-hours).

1. Click OK to save.
