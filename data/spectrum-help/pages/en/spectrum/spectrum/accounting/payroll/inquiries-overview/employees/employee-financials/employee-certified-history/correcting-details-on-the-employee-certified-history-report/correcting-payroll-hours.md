# Correcting Payroll Hours

Follow these steps to modify payroll hours.

1. On the Site Map, click Payroll > Period End > Employee Certified
 History.

1. Enter the employee code for whom you wish to make a
 modification.

1. Select the check number that is missing the data and
 click the Time Card button.
 The Time Card History
 Detail window displays.

1. Make sure the Work date is what the certified
 date should have been.

1. In the Data Entry > Pre-Time Card Entry screen, enter a negative time card line for the wrong hours.

1. Enter a positive time card line for the correct
 hours.Note: This can be a new check.

1. If the amount is negative, the new amount must be
 included on the next time card.
