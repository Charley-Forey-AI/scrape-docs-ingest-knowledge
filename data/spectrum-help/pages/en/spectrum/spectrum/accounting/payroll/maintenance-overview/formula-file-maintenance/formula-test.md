# Formula Test

Use this screen to enter the quantities for the formula test. In addition, these formula calculations are reflected in the Layoff Check Entry and Payroll Check Calculation screens.

## To Get Started

- Complete the fields in this screen that are applicable for the formula you want to test. For example, if your 1st factor is set to Gross Amount on the starting screen, you will need to enter a value in the Gross amount field. Amounts entered in the Federal, State, County, and Local fields are additional 'subject to' wages, and the income tax can be calculated accordingly (rather than always calculating the income tax based on the gross amount).

- After all of the applicable fields have been completed, click the OK button to save your changes and test the formula. The result will display in the Answer field, which shows the total calculated amount of the pre-check.
