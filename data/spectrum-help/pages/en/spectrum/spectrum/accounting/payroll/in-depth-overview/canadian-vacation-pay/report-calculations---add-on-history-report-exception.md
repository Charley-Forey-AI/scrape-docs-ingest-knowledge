# Report Calculations - Add-on History Report, Exception

The following table describes how each column is calculated on this report.
Column
Descriptions

Limit
This amount comes from the Employee Deduction/Add-on Maintenance screen, using the employee and add-on code selected for this report.

Amount Taken
This amount is the total add-on amount for the month for the employee and add-on code specified.

Scheduled Amount
For Fixed Amount add-ons, this amount is equal to the Pay Periods Remaining multiplied by the Add-on amount.
For Regular Equivalent Hours, Overtime Equivalent Hours, and All hour add-on methods, this amount is equal to the Pay Periods Remaining times the Add-on amount times the Standard hours for the employee.
For Percent of Gross and Percent of Net add-on methods, this amount is equal to the Standard Hours multiplied by the Pay Rate, multiplied by the Pay Periods Remaining, multiplied by the Add-ons Percent divided by 100.

Difference
This amount is equal to the Employee Monthly Limit less the Amount Taken, less the Scheduled Amount.
