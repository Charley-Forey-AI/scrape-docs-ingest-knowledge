# Employee Master Report - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
FieldDescriptions
EmployeePress Enter to print the records of all employees on file, or enter the desired employee code. Typically ALL would be selected at conversion, so that all employee records print and can be verified. After conversion, you might print these one at a time as new employees are hired.
Employee statusEnter the employee status type(s) to be included on the report, or press Enter to accept the default of ALL.
EntityIf cost centers and entity tracking are enabled in Enterprise Installation, enter a cost center entity to display entity-specific earnings, tax, and 'time off bank' information.
