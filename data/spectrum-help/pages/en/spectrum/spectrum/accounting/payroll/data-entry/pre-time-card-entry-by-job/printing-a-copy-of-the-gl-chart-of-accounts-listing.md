# Printing a copy of the G/L Chart of Accounts Listing

Follow the steps below to print a copy of the G/L Chart of
 Accouts Listing.

- On the Site Map screen, click General Ledger  >  Maintenance  > Master File.

- Click the Listing button.

- On the Chart of Accounts Listing screen, select the information you want to appear on your report, and click Print.
