# Quarterly Reporting

Run the following reports to complete quarterly reporting
 in Payroll.

- Print the [Payroll Register](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/payroll-register),
 selected for Quarter-to-Date, and save the report.

- Print the [Quarterly Federal Tax Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/quarterly-federal-tax-report) to obtain the necessary information for your 941 report.

- Print the [Unemployment Tax Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/unemployment-tax-report) to determine the amount of unemployment taxes due.

- Print the State,
 County, and Local Report or [Subject-to-Tax Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/subject-to-tax-report).

- Print the [Worker's Compensation Rate Per $100 Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/workers-compensation-rate-per-100-report) to determine the amount owed by your company.

- Print the [Worker's Compensation Rate Per Hour Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/workers-compensation-rate-per-hour-report) for the quarter.

- If needed, print the [Union Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/union-report).

- If needed, print the [Employment Utilization Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/payroll-reports/employment-utilization-report).

- Perform the [Period End Clear](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/period-end-procedures/period-end-clear), selected for Quarter
 End.
