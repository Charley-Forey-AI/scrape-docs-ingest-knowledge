# Transfer Pre-Time Cards to Pay Cycle - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields
Descriptions

Select by
Select pre-time cards by Batch, Job, or All.

Selections
Enter the work date through which to update pre-time cards, or press Enter to accept the current period-end date of the current pay cycle.
Enter the Batch or Bob number corresponding to the pre-time card information to be updated, or press Enter to print ALL batches and jobs entered in Pre-Time Card Entry. If All was selected above, you only need to specify the through work date.

Alert
If there are still records in the error logs during the transfer of pre-time cards to time cards, alerts will be included in this section. You can still proceed with the transfer, but the records in the error log will not be included.
 If one or more employee errors, quantity errors, replacement checks, or layoff checks are present, click the hyperlink to open the appropriate screen to correct errors.
