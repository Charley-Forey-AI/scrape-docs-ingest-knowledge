# Purge G/L History - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in this company, then when
 purging G/L distribution history, Spectrum will remove the history records only if the
 operator performing the purge has permission to access the cost center assigned to the G/L
 transaction.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the G/L entry with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then the
 G/L distribution history will not be purged.
