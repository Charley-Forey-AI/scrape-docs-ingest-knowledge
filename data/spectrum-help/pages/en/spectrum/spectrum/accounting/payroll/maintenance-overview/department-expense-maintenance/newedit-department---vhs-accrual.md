# New/Edit Department - V/H/S Accrual

Use this tab to enter General Ledger account codes to be
 used for the vacation, holiday, and sick pay accruals.
All account codes entered in this tab must be valid, non-direct G/L
 account codes; direct job cost and equipment cost account codes will not be allowed, even
 in direct cost departments. The Department File Listing includes the G/L accrual account
 when the Human Resources module is present. The G/L codes entered here will be used when
 the Human Resources > Vacation, Holiday and Sick Accrual History by G/L Account Code Report and
 Update are run.
Note: The V/H/S Accrual tab is only available if the Human Resources module is
 installed.
