# Quarterly Federal Tax Report - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in this company, when running
 the Quarterly Federal Tax Report, Spectrum includes the employee on the report only if you
 have permission to access the employee.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the employee with cost centers
 in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included,
 then earnings and tax information will not be included for that employee.
