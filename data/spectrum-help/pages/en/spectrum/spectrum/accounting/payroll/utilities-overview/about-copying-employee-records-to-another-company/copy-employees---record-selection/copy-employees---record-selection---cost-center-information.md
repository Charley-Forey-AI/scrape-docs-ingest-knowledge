# Copy Employees - Record Selection - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, then
 only those employees that the operator is permitted to access display.
Overrides are not applicable.
If cost centers are being used in the destination company, the Record Selection screen allows the operator to select to copy any displayed employee, even if the operator does not have security access for that employee in the destination company.
