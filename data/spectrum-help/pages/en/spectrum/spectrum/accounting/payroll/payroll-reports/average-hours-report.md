# Average Hours Report

This report is provided to support compliance with the
 Patient Protections and Affordable Care Act.
Under this act, employers must determine for each employee whether health coverage must be
 provided, based on whether the employee is full time or part time. For employees with
 variable hours worked, the Department of Labor and Internal Revenue Service have provided
 look back guidance in determining coverage requirements, based on past employment hours,
 to identify full time employees.
This report provides the Benefits Manager with average weekly hours worked by each employee to use in performing this analysis.
