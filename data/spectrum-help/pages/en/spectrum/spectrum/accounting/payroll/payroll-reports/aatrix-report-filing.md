# Aatrix Report Filing

Use the Aatrix Report Filing screen to generate the electronic reports for Federal or State tax forms.
Note:

- Aatrix is available only via Trimble Construction One.

- If you use HR Management for Spectrum, using Aatrix for W2s is not recommended.

To use Aatrix for your report filing:

1. Go to Payroll > Reports > Aatrix Report Filing.

1. Make your selections and complete the relevant fields.

1. Select Continue.Spectrum generates the Aatrix-compatible data file (Aatrix Universal File, or AUF) on the server, which you can then upload directly to Aatrix, who will submit the electronic file on your behalf.

To view completed or in-process reports, select the History button, log in to the Aatrix site, and review saved forms.
