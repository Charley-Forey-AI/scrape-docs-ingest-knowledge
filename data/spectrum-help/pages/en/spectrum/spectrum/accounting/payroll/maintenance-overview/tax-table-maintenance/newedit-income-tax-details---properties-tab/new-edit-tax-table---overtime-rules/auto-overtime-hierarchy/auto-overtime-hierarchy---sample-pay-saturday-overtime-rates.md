# Auto-Overtime Hierarchy - Sample Pay Saturday Overtime Rates

In this example, the employee works in one state that requires overtime to be paid on hours worked in excess of 8 per day and in excess of 40 per week and Saturday at Overtime Rates. The company deems that all "travel time" will be paid at $10.00 per hour. During the week, the employee worked 30.0 hours at $25.00 with 14.0 hours of travel time at $10.00.
No existing weekday time card rows is switched to 'Overtime' but all Saturday time is overtime.
