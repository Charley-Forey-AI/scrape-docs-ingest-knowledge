# Employee Earnings History Purge

 This update will delete employee history records for the
 current company.
This is not a typical process and should only be used in extreme situations. These is no
 way to reverse this update once it is performed.
Records in the following tables will be purged:

- Employee Earnings History

- Wage and Tax History

- Worker's Compensation History

- Employee Add-ons and DeductionsItems are removed based on the selection of year. Items with a check date on or before the selected date will be purged.
Important: This purge is not recommended for use until at least
 one full year has passed. For example, 2025 records would not be purged until at
 least 2027.
