# Adding Pay Groups to a Job

Once the pay groups have been set up and the employee has been classified (assigned a Level), you need to add the Pay Group Code to the job.

- On the Site Map screen, click Job Cost  >  Maintenance  >  Jobs.

- Enter the Job code to display the job information.

- Click Propertiesin the Info Bar.

- Click Payroll Setup.

- Click Save.
Alternate steps:

- Select Job Cost  >  Jobs  >  Job Payroll Setup, and enter a job code.

- Complete the Pay group field.

- Click Save.
