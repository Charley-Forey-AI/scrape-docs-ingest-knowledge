# Correcting Jobs That Are Supposed To Be Certified

If a job was originally set up as a private or
 non-certified job, but you later learn that it is a certified job, complete the following
 steps to correct the error.

## PART A – Correcting the Job Setup

- On the Site
 Map screen, click Job Cost  >  Maintenance  >  Job.

- In the Job field, enter the number of the job to be corrected and then press
 Enter.

- Click the Properties button.

- Select the Certified checkbox and then click OK until you return to the
 Site Map screen.

## PART B – Correcting Historical Information

- On the Site
 Map screen, click Payroll  >  Period End  >  Employee Certified
 History.

- Select the employee whose history you need to correct. In the
 Employee code field,
 enter the first employee associated with the job or press F4 or double-click on this field to
 select from a list of valid employee codes.

- Locate the summary time card information for the paycheck to be
 corrected.

- Click the Time
 Card button.

- In the In the Time
 Card Detail window, select the Cert checkbox for each time card
 line that was supposed to be certified. If necessary, enter the work date and level
 information.

- Repeat steps 3 – 5 until all time card lines have been
 corrected.

- Repeat steps 2 – 6 for all remaining employees that have worked
 on this job.

- Click OK until you return to the Site Map screen.
