# Check Detail

Use this read-only window to review the selected employee's check information, including hours/earnings, taxes, and totals.
The buttons on the right-hand side of the window provide more detailed information on specific items. For example, clicking the FICA button displays a window with the employee's social security, medical aid, and the combined total of these amounts.
