# Subject-to-Tax Report

The Subject-to-Tax Report screen reports the information necessary for preparation of state, local, and county taxes based on the state tax history file.
For each employee, this report provides:

- gross wages

- exempt wages and adjustments for the pre-tax deductions

- net wages subject to tax

- amounts of income tax withheld

See also [Wage and Tax History Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/employees/employee-reports/wage-and-tax-history-report).
