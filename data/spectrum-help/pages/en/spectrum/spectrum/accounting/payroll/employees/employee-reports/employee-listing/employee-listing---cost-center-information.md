# Employee Listing - Cost Center Information

If the Enterprise Installation option to use cost centers
 is set to Yes or Pending for this company, Spectrum includes
 the employee on the listing only if you have permission to access the employee.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the employee
 with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is
 not included, then that employee is not shown on the listing.If cost
 center entities are being used, an 'Entity' selection field displays in order to allow
 you to filter results based on the employee's inclusion in a particular entity.
