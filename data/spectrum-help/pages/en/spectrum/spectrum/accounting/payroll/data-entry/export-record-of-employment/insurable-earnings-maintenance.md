# Insurable Earnings Maintenance

This screen is applicable for Canadian customers only.
 Insurable earnings include most
 of the different types of compensation provided to employees in which employment insurance
 (EI) premiums are paid.
While the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) determines what types of earnings and hours are
 insurable, it is Service Canada who manages the EI benefits.
Use the Insurable Earnings Maintenance screen to map pay types, add-ons and deductions to the appropriate Reporting status and Payment type. Not making an assignment and leaving the fields <BLANK> indicates that these are not part of the ROE compilations.
Note: Press F4 to view your options
