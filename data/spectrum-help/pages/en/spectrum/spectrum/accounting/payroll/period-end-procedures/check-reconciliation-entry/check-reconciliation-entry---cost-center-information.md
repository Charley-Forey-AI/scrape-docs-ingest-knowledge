# Check Reconciliation Entry - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in this company, then when
 reconciling checks, Spectrum displays items only if you have permission to access them.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned in the check with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center of the check is not
 included, then the check will not appear. Likewise, Spectrum also assures that the
 employee's assigned cost center is valid for your operator.
