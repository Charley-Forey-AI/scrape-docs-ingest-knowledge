# New Rate Revision

Use this window to schedule automatic pay rate increases for future dates. Any rate changes made using this window will display in the Employee Pay Rates screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Pay type
Enter the pay type for the selected employee: Hourly, Salary, Overtime salary, or Commission.

Frequency
Enter the pay frequency for the selected employee: Daily, Weekly, Bi-weekly, Semi-monthly, Monthly, Quarterly, or Annually.

Standard hours
Enter the standard number of hours worked in one pay period by the employee.

Salary rate
Enter the scheduled salary rate increase for the selected employee.

Hourly rate
Enter the scheduled hourly rate increase for the selected employee.

Annual rate
Enter the scheduled annual rate increase for the selected employee.

Effective date
Enter the date on which the scheduled pay increase will take effect.

Comments
Enter any comments related to the pay increase.
