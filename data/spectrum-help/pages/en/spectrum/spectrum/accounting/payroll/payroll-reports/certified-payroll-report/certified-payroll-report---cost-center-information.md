# Certified Payroll Report - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in this company, when
 running the Certified Payroll Report, Spectrum includes the job only if you have permission to
 access the job.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then
 entries for that job will not be shown. Likewise, Spectrum also includes the employee on
 the report only if you have permission to access the employee.
