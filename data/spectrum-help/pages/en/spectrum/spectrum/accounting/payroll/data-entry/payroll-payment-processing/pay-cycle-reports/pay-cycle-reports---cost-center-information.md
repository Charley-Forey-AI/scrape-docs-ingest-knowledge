# Pay Cycle Reports - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in this company, when running the Pay Cycle Reports, if a cost group or cost center is specified on the starting screen the reports will show only employees assigned to cost centers in that group (based on the cost center assigned in Employees).
Spectrum includes the employee on the reports only if you have permission to access the employee, and when an employee is included on the reports, all information for that employee will appear, even if your operator does not have permission to access certain time card lines for that employee.

- The Selections window for the Job Distribution Report includes an option for a job cost group. When a cost group or cost center is specified, then the report will show only jobs assigned to cost centers in that group (based on the cost center assigned in Job File Maintenance).

- The Selections window for the Labor Distribution Report includes an option for a department cost group. When a cost group or cost center is specified, then the report will show only departments associated with cost centers in that group (based on cost centers assigned in Department Expense Maintenance). This window also offers the option to sort either by Department or Cost center.
