# Purge Copied Employees

Purging is not a mandatory step, but it allows you to remove records from the source Payroll company once the records have been reviewed in the destination company.
If the option to flag employees for later removal from this company was selected in the [Copy Employees to Another Company - Confirmation](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/utilities-overview/about-copying-employee-records-to-another-company/copy-employees-to-another-company---confirmation) screen, then this screen displays a list of the employees who have been copied to another company, but who have yet to be deleted from the current company.

1. Review the list of employees that may be purged from the current company.

1. Select the Purge checkbox corresponding the to those employees that you wish to purge. Note that all associated payroll history will also be purged.

1. Click Save-and-End to display the Proceed with update checkbox; and select Continue.

1. Click OK to confirm your changes and complete the purge.If cost centers are being used in the current company, this screen will only display employees that your operator is authorized to access.
