# Copy Employees - Record Selection

Use this screen to edit the list of employees that will be copied to another company.

1.  Initially, all the employees in this list will be selected for copy; to remove an employee from the copy list, simply clear the corresponding Include checkbox.

1. After finalizing your list, click the OK button to proceed to the Print Report confirmation window.

1. Click Preview to review the Copy Employee Listing report (and Exception Report, if generated) before proceeding to the [Copy Employees to Another Company - Confirmation](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/utilities-overview/about-copying-employee-records-to-another-company/copy-employees-to-another-company---confirmation) confirmation screen.
