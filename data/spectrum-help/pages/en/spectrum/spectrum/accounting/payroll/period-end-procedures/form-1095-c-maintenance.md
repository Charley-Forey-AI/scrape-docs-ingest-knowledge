# Form 1095-C Maintenance

Form 1095-C is used by employers that are required to
 report their offers of health coverage to the IRS and employees.
It is issued to an individual by an employer subject to 'employer shared responsibility'
 for providing minimum essential health coverage to any of its employees. Use this screen to
 add and edit employee-specific reporting information for employees who are eligible for
 health care coverage in the selected calendar year.
Click the New button to open the New Form 1095-C window and make changes to an individual employee after importing from the Employee Worksheet. The 'Offer' fields and the 'Safe Harbor' fields each provide convenient combo-box pull-down lists for IRS-designated codes.
Once all changes are made on this screen, export the worksheet to a CSV file for processing in Nelco (see below).
Note: These forms are exported from each of the companies
 where the employees are set up. It is no longer necessary to coordinate all companies in
 order to submit these forms.
