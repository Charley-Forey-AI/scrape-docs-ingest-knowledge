# Employee Pay Group Rates

The Employee Pay Group Rates screen is use to maintain pay
 group rates for specific employees.
This screen is only available if the Pay groups option is selected as
 the Payroll Installation > Default rate table.
Note: Click the Switch button at any time to display a
 different employee.
