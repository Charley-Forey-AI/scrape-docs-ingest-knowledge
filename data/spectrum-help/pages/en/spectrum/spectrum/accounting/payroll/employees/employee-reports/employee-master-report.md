# Employee Master Report

The Employee Master Report produces a hard copy of the
 information entered in the Employees screens.
This report prints one screen per employee. The information is also available on-screen
 through Employee Inquiry. Benefit totals will be obtained from the Payroll Time Off Bank
 Log Table using the check date for 'YTD Earned'.
