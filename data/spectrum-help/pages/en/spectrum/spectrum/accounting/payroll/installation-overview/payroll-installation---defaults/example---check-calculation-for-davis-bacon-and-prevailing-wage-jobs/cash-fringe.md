# Cash Fringe

This amount is calculated by subtracting the Employer
 Benefit and 401(k) Benefit from the Fringe Pay amount (when the Calculate actual pension benefits for the D-B/Prevailing
 wage employer benefit credit checkbox is selected in the Payroll Installation > Defaults tab).
In this example:
$150.00
 -60.00
 -60.00
=$30.00
