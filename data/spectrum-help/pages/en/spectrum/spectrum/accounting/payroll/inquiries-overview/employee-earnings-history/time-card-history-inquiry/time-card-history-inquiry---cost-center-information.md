# Time Card History Inquiry - Cost Center Information

If your operator is permitted to inquire on the employee, then any images stored for that employee check will also be accessible, based on the current Document Imaging settings. The job's cost center is not validated against the operator security scheme settings for cost centers before displaying job images.
