# Payroll Liabilities Reconciliation - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, a cost
 group selection field will be available.
This allows users to filter the report based on the 'Check cost
 center' assigned during the pay cycle, in order to include values from employee checks
 for a particular segment of the company.
