# Correct Payroll Errors - OPTION A - no access to the Job Cost
 module

This option is used when you are processing an employee's
 current time card lines in conjunction with correction the erroneous Payroll
 transactions.
Taking these steps does not change the employee's actual
 check.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Payroll > Data Entry > Payment Processing > Set New Cycle.

1. In the Set Payroll Cycle screen, complete the Period end date and Check date fields.

1. Complete the remaining fields as necessary and then
 click OK to return to the
 PayrollPayment Processing screen.

1. On the Payroll Payment Processing screen, click the Time Card Entry button.

1. On the Time Card Entry screen, enter the employee's code in the Employee code field.

1. Press Enter until the cursor displays in the details portion of the Time Card Entry screen.

1. Enter the original time card data in the Pt, Dept, Day, Job, Phase, Ct, and Wage/Tax fields.Note: Complete these fields so they match the erroneous time
 card line(s).

1. In the Hours field, enter the incorrect hours as a negative number for the
 specified day/job/phase.Note: For example, if the employee erroneously entered 8 hours
 for job 200 instead of job 300, enter 200 in the Job field
 and –8 in the Hours field.

1. On the next transaction line, enter the correct time
 card information in the Pt,
 Dept, Day, Job, Phase, Ct, Wage/Tax, and Hours fields.
