# Copy Employees to Another Company - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Copy to company Employee Department
Enter the destination company code, employee code, and department code or press Enter to include ALL records.

Active employees only?
Select this checkbox if you only want to include Active employees in the copy (that is, retired or terminated employees will not be included).

Purge
Click this button to open the [Purge Copied Employees](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/utilities-overview/about-copying-employee-records-to-another-company/purge-copied-employees) screen where you can select to remove copied employees (and their payroll history information) from the current company.
