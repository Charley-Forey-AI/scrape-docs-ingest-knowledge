# Layoff Check Entry - Pay Groups

When a pay group is specified on a time card line, the
 specified deductions and add-ons for this pay group will be calculated for the employee and
 included on the check.
Any scheduled pay rate adjustments will default based on the specified period-end date or
 check date, depending upon your installation settings. If no date is defined, then rate
 adjustments will default based on the current Payroll processing date. The computation for
 the check will be based on either the time card line or the pay group total depending on
 the formula file setup. If the pay group contains prevailing wage information, Health &
 Welfare and Prevailing Wage Adjustment information will be computed and applied to the
 check. Any non-prevailing wage adjustment will also be computed and applied on the
 employee's behalf. If the rate for a deduction or add-on is left blank in the pay group
 file, then the computation will be based on the rate specified in the Employee Deduction /
 Add-on Code Maintenance screen.
