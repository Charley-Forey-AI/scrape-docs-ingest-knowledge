# Worker's Compensation Rate Per Hour Report

The Workers' Compensation Rate Per Hour Report prints, by
 employee, the information required for Labor & Industries tax reports, where Worker's Compensation is computed based on hours worked.
This report includes hours worked by employee within a job classification, the employee withholding
 amount, if any, and the employer's liability. It may be printed in detail, showing each pay
 period, or in summary form (one line per employee).
Note: In
 the current company, if the Entity Tracking checkbox is selected in Enterprise Installation > Edit Company Settings screen, the 'Rate ($)' column title and detail values do not display in the
 header.
