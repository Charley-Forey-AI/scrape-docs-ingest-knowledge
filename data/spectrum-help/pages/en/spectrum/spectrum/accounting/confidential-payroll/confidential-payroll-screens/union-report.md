# Union Report

 The Union Report calculates report hours worked, earnings, union deductions, union add-ons and union fringe benefits for a specified date range and as paid through Payroll Processing. This report allows you to review actual employee-specific union deductions and add-ons when rates are maintained in the Employee Deduction / Add-on Maintenance screen instead of in the Union setup (for example, for Credit Union, 401k and employee-specific add-ons). This benefit is particularly useful for deductions and add-ons where the employees themselves can determine the rate, and are permitted to change their election at any time.
