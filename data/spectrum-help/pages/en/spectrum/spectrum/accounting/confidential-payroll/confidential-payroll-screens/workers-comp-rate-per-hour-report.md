# Worker's Comp Rate Per Hour Report

The Worker's Comp Rate Per Hour Report prints (alphabetically by employee) information required for the Washington State Labor & Industries tax report, and for other states with Worker's Compensation computed based on hours worked. It includes hours worked by employee within a job classification, the employee withholding amount, if any, and the employer's liability. This report can be printed in detail (showing each pay period) or in summary form (one line per employee). This report includes employees from all companies identified in CP Company Maintenance.
