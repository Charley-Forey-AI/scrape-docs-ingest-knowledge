# Purging Employee Data from the Original Company

After copying employee data from one company to another,
 use the Purge button to delete the original data.

1. On the Copy Employees to Another Company screen, click the Purge button.

1. Review the list of employees flagged for deletion. Only employees who have been copied and flagged for deletion will appear on this screen.

1. Select the Purge checkbox as appropriate.

1. Click Continue.
