# Confidential Payroll Report Companies

Use the Confidential Payroll Report Companies screen to specify the companies that are to be included in Confidential Payroll reports.
On line one, the confidential company displays.
The System Administrator must specify the company to which confidential records are posted, as well as any other companies that are to be included in payroll reporting.

## Consistent Use of Entity Tracking Required

All consolidating companies must be consistent in their use of entities or not. That is, if one company uses entities, then all companies listed on the page must be set to use entities.
In order to add a company to this list, the Entity tracking checkbox setting must match that of the company name listed in line one. This setting is at System Administration > Installation > Enterprise > Company (tab) > Edit Company Settings.

1. Navigate to the Edit Company Settings screen for the company named in line one.

1. Note whether the Entity tracking checkbox is selected or not.

1. Navigate to the Edit Company Settings screen for any company you want to add to the list.

If the setting is the same, the company can be added to the list.
