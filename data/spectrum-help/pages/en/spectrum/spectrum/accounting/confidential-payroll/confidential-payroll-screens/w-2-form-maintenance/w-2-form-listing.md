# W-2 Form Listing

W-2 Form Listing is used to print information that was entered in W-2 Form Maintenance. Proof the information carefully before proceeding.
Note: When this report is run for totals only, additional
 information displays on the printed report including the selected employee's state income
 tax, wages, and tips information.
