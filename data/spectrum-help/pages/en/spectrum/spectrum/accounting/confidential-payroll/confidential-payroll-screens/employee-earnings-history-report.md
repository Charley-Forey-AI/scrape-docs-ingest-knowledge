# Employee Earnings History Report

The Employee Earnings History Report lists history of all employee earnings (regular and overtime), FIT, FICA, and other taxes. This report is printed in alphabetical sequence by employee code, and is available in three formats and includes employees from all companies identified in CP Company Maintenance.
