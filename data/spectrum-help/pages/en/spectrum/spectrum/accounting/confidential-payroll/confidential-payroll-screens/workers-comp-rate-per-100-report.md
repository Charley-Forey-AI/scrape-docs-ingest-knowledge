# Worker's Comp Rate Per $100 Report

The Worker's Comp Rate Per $100 Report lists the employer's
 contribution to worker's compensation for one or all worker's compensation codes for a
 specified date range. This report may be printed in sequence either by state or by worker's
 compensation code. This report includes employees from all companies identified in Confidential Payroll > Company Maintenance.
