# FLSA Qualified Overtime Compensation Report

This report is designed to assist you with compliance and accurate reporting of overtime calculations under the Fair Labor Standards Act (FLSA). It calculates the overtime premium that an employee may be able to deduct.

For tax year 2025, it is optional to add the calculated amounts to the employee's W-2.

- If you add the amounts to the W-2, you must do so manually.

- If you do not add the amounts to the W-2, you must furnish this report to the employee.
