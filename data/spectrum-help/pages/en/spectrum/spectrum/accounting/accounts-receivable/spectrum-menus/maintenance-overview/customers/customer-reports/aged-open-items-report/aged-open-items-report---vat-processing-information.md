# Aged Open Items Report - VAT Processing Information

When VAT processing is being used, the following columns
 will include VAT amounts when calculated:

- Original amount

- Open Balance - Total

- Open Balance - Retention

- Open Balance - Non-Retention

If the Allocate PST on holdback option is selected, the PST amount will be included in
 the invoice amounts, and holdback PST will be included in retention amounts.
