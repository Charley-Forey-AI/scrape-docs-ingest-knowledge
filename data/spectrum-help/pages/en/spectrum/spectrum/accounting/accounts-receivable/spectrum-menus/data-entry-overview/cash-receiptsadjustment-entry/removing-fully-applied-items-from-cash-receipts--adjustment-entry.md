# Removing Fully Applied Items From Cash Receipts / Adjustment Entry

Periodically, you may want to remove fully applied items
 from the Accounts Receivable Cash Receipts / Adjustment Entry
 screen.
There are two methods available for accomplishing this task.
 The first option is to perform the Clear Closed Items Update, which is an automated process
 within Spectrum. The second option is to perform the procedure below.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable > Data Entry > Cash Receipts/Adjustment.

1. In the Customer code field,
 enter the code of the customer from whom you are receiving the payment.

1. In the Transaction type field,
 enter the transaction type code to indicate the payment/adjustment being entered.


1. In the Reference # field, press Enter to accept the
 next sequential number.

1. In the Date field, enter the
 date you want this transaction to post to.

1. In the Amount field, enter the number zero (0).Note: You will receive a mandatory input message if you attempt
 to press Enter to bypass
 this field.

1. Complete the fields in the Cash Receipts /
 Adjustments Entry Selections window as desired and then click
 OK.

1. In the details portion of the Cash Receipts
 / Adjustments Entry screen, locate the items that you want to net
 together.

1. In the Paid/adjusted field,
 enter the open amount for each item until the amount in the Distribution balance
 field is zero.Note: Enter the number as it displays in the Balance field. For example, if
 the Balance field has a
 negative number, enter a negative number in the Paid/adjusted field.

1. Click OK until the Update
 button displays and then click the Update button.

1. On the Cash Receipts Journal
 screen, complete the Customer, FromG/L
 date, and Through G/L date fields and then
 click Preview.

1. Review the listing.

1. On the Cash Receipts Update
 screen, select Continue and then click
 OK to post the adjustment(s).
