# Error - Date Out of Range. Please Re-Enter Date or Reset Minimum/Maximum Date.

In Admin  >  Installation  >  Accounts Receivable  > Properties, if the Invoice date must be in G/L period checkbox is selected, then the Invoice date must be in the same fiscal year and period as the G/L date. This error message displays in the Invoice Entry screen. The invoice date will reset to match the G/L date.
