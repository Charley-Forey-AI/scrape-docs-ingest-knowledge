# Applying Finance Charges to a Specified Customer

Complete the following steps to apply finances charges to a
 customer.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable >  Maintenance >  Customer.

1. At the Customer code field,
 enter the customer code for whom you are applying finance charges, and then press
 Enter.

1. Click the Customer Defaults
 link on the Info Bar.

1. At the Finance charge code
 field, select the finance charge code you want applied to this customer's account
 from the drop-down list.

1. Click OK .
