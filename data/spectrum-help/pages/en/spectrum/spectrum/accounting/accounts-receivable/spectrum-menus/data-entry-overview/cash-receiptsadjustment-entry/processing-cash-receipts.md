# Processing Cash Receipts

Follow these steps to make adjustments to a customer's
 accounts.
Transactions entered here are stored in a temporary
 transaction file until the update is run. After updating, new transactions must be entered
 to correct any errors.

1. On the Site Map, click
 Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  >  Cash Receipts / Adjustment.

1. Enter any cash receipts by completing the top portion of the screen. (The ABA# entered here will print on the deposit slip.)

1. Click the Listing button to
 proceed to the Cash Receipts Entry Edit List screen.

1. Click Preview to review the
 Cash Receipts Entry Edit List, make any necessary changes,
 and review the listing again.

1. On the Cash Receipts / Adjustments
 Entry screen, click the Deposit button to
 display the Deposit Slips screen, if desired.

1. Select the appropriate check dates and print the deposit slips.

1. Attach the deposit slips to your bank-supplied deposit slips and the checks to be deposited.

1. On the Cash Receipts / Adjustments Entry
 screen, click the Update button to display the
 Cash Receipts Journal screen.

1. Select the appropriate customer and G/L date range
 then click Preview to open the Cash Receipts
 Update.

1. Click Continue and then click
 OK. This audit record should be retained permanently.
