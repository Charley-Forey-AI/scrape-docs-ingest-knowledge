# Set Customers' Credit Limit

This screen is used to establish or change the amount of
 credit that a customer range is allowed.

1. Enter the customer code for which you wish to
 establish a credit limit, or press Enter to accept the
 software default of ALL.

1. Enter the customer's credit limit. Enter a whole number without decimals or dollar signs; the software will provide the necessary formatting (for example, typing "400" will appear as "400.00" in this field and will establish a credit limit of $400.00).

1. Select Continue.
