# Example: Setting Up Bill Groups

In the example provided, Mob/Demob, Repave Three Miles of
 Road, and Landscaping are the three bill groups.

1. First enter Mob/Demob as bill group number one,
 then enter line transactions for the two components listed and save your
 entry.

1. When the cursor returns to the Bill group field, enter Repave Three Miles of Road as bill group number two, then enter line transactions for the five components listed. Click OK.
