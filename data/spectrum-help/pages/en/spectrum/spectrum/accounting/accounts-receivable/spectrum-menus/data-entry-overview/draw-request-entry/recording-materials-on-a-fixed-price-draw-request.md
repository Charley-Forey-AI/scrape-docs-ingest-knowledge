# Recording Materials on a Fixed Price Draw Request

To track stored materials on a draw request, make sure you
 have selected the Transfer materials stored to previous app checkbox on the Accounts Receivable Installation  >  Draw Request screen.
To track stored materials on a fixed price draw request,
 make sure you have selected the Transfer materials stored to previous app checkbox on the
 Accounts Receivable Installation  >  Draw Request tab. Materials stored on a draw request for a fixed price contract will
 display in the Previous application and Materials stored fields in the Fixed Price Contract
 Detail window. Use this procedure to record stored materials on a fixed price draw
 request.Note: To determine whether the contract is
 fixed price or unit price, on the Site
 Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Maintenance  >  Contract. Type in the Job
 number, press
 Enter, type in the Customer
 code, press Enter, and then click the Properties button. If the Use unit pricing checkbox is blank then the job has a fixed priced
 contract. Otherwise, if the checkbox is selected, the job has a unit price
 contract.

1. On the Site Map, click
 Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  >  Draw Request.

1. At the Job field, enter the job
 number for the draw request you are modifying, and then press
 Enter.

1. Enter a Customer code, or press
 Enter to accept the customer code that defaults based on
 your job selection.

1. Complete the Application #
 field.

1. The CreateDetail dialog box
 displays. If this is the first draw request application for this contract, select the
 Create from details checkbox.

1. Click the Details button to
 display the Fixed Price Contract Detail window.

1. At the Item field, navigate to the billing item that
 stored materials will be recorded on. Your cursor will move to the Percent
 complete field. Holding the Shift key down
 press the Tab key and your cursor will enter back to the
 Materials stored field. Enter the amount of materials
 stored, and then press Enter.

1. At the Percent complete field
 enter the percent complete, or press Enter to accept the
 software's calculation.

1. Once you have added all the necessary materials stored
 information for this job number, click OK.

1. To review the materials stored information you entered
 in this window, click the Summary button on the
 Draw Request Billing Entry screen.

1. Click the Update button.

1. Enter a Batch code, select the
 Entry by application number option, and then click
 Display. Type in the Job number, Customer
 code, and the current Application number. The
 G/L date and Invoice date will
 default.

1. Select the Next field and
 OK fields. Press OK and proceed with
 the update.

- If the Transfer materials stored to previous
 app checkbox is selected in the Accounts Receivable  >  Installation screen, the software will automatically add the
 Materials stored column to the previous applied
 amount and clear the Materials stored when the
 application is updated.

- If the Transfer materials stored to previous
 app checkbox is clear in the Accounts Receivable  >  Installation screen, the Materials stored column
 will remain the same on new draw requests. To clear previously-stored
 materials: manually clear the dollar amount for the Materials
 stored line items and add the same amount to This
 period.
