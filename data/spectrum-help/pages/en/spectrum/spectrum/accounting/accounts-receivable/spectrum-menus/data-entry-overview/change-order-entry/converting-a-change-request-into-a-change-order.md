# Converting a Change Request into a Change Order

To convert a change request into a change order, complete
 the following steps.

1. On the Site Map, click
 Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  > Change Order Entry.

1. Enter your Job number and
 Customer code and press Enter.


1. At the Change order field,
 enter a change order number, or press Enter to have Spectrum
 assign the next change order number.

1. Enter the Description of the change order and
 press Enter.

1. Enter the Approved date.Note: The Date issued key field is used when updating your contract files and the
 contract status report.

1. Enter the Current status of this change order.

1. Complete the remaining fields in the header of the change order, as appropriate.

1. Click the Add CR button to add change requests to this change order.

1. Click OK and select
 Save to save your changes.
