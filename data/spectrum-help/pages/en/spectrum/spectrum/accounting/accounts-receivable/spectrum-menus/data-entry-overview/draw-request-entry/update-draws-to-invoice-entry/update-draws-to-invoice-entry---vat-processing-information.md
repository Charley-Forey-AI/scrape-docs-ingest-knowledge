# Update Draws to Invoice Entry - VAT Processing Information

Draw Request Update includes VAT tax on the new customer
 invoice when a 'VAT tax code' is present on the draw. If the Allocate PST on holdback checkbox
 is selected on the sales tax code, the update will store the sales tax amount as the Total
 PST/Sales Tax less Holdback PST/Sales Tax.
