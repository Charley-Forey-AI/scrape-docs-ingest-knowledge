# Applying a Miscellaneous Check

The following procedure explains how to record
 miscellaneous cash transactions.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable > Data Entry > Cash Receipts/Adjustment.

1. Click the Non-Customer button.


1. At the
 Transaction type field, enter the transaction type code.Note: The user-defined transaction type codes are set up in
 Accounts Receivable > Maintenance > Transaction Code File
 Maintenance.

1. Complete the Check #,
 Date, and Amount fields.

1. In the G/L code field, enter the General Ledger account code that the funds
 apply to and then complete the remaining fields in the details portion of the
 screen.Note: This G/L account is the account that will be credited
 when the transaction is updated.

1. Click OK until the
 Cash Receipts / Adjustments Entry screen opens.

1. On the Cash Receipts / Adjustments Entry screen, click the Update button. The CashReceipts Journal screen
 displays.

1. Complete the Customer, From G/L date, and Through G/L date fields.

1. Click Preview. After you
 preview the journal, the Cash Receipts Update screen
 automatically displays.

1. Select Continue and click OK.
