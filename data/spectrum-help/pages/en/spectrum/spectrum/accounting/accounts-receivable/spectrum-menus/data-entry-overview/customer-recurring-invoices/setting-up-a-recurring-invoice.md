# Setting up a Recurring Invoice

The following procedure describes how to set up a recurring
 invoice.

1. On the Customer Recurring Invoices screen, click New.

1. Complete the Customer code and
 Job fields.

1. In the Recurring # field, enter
 the invoice number you want to assign to this recurring invoice, or press
 Enter to accept the default.

1. In the Type field, use the
 drop-down menu to select Invoice and press
 Enter.

1. In the Batch freq field, press
 Enter to accept the default of M for
 'Monthly'. You will update recurring invoices based on their batch frequency codes.
 Other batch frequency codes include Q
 for 'Quarterly' or Y for 'Yearly'. You can also create your own type of coding scheme.
 For example, if you have different monthly recurring invoices for Division 1 and
 Division 2.

1. In the Customer PO# field,
 enter the customer purchase order number, if necessary, and then press
 Enter.

1. Select the Print status
 checkbox if this invoice has already been printed; otherwise, leave this checkbox
 clear.

1. The Properties window displays.
 Complete this window and click OK.

1. In the detail section of the screen, enter the details for the recurring invoice transaction you are creating and click Save twice.

1. Complete the Sales tax and
 Retention fields as needed, and then press
 Enter.

1. Press Enter and then click
 OK until you return to the main menu.
