# Total Accounts Receivable - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, when inquiring on Accounts Receivable outstanding totals Spectrum will display only the total of open invoices that the operator has permission to access. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to each open invoice with cost centers in the operator's assigned scheme; if the invoice cost center is not present, then that invoice amount is not included.
