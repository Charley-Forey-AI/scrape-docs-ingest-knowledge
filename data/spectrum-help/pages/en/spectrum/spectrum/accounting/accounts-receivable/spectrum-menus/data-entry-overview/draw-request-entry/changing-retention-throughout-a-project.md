# Changing Retention Throughout a Project

The Draw Request Billing Entry screen allows you to enter
 retention amounts or percentages for each detail line of the contract.
These retention allocations override the retention rate of
 the draw as a whole. This is particularly useful when certain billing items, such as change
 orders, afford a different retention requirement, including none (0.0%).

1. On the Site Map, click
 Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry > Draw Request.

1. Complete the Job,
 Customer, Application, Application date, and
 Period to fields.

1. Click the Details button.

1. Complete the % complete field for each bill
 item.

1. Click the Expand button.

1. Select the Retention % override
 checkbox.

1. Complete the Retention % or
 Retention amt fields, and then click
 OK.

1. Complete any other retention overrides and click
 OK until you return to the Site Map.
