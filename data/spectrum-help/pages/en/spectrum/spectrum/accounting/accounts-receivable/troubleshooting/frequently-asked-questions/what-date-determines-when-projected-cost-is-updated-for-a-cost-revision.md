# What date determines when Projected Cost is updated for a cost revision?

Answer — When a Change Request status is changed to an Action Type #3 (cost revision),
 the system populates the current date into the Submitted date field
 on the Dates tab.
Change Requests use the following hierarchy to determine
 when to impact Projected Cost:

1. C.O. Date (Change Order date)

1. Approval Date

1. Submitted Date

1. Current Date
