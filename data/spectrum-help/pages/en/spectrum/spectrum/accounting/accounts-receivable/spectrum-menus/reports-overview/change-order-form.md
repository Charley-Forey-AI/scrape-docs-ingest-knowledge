# Change Order Form

Use this screen to print contract change orders. This screen is modeled after the standard American Institute of Architects (AIA) change order form.
The 'Net change by previously authorized change orders' amount will be calculated based on origination date of change orders and approval date of change requests with a status type of #1 or #2. Change orders and change requests executed/approved later than the origination date of the selected change order will be excluded from this amount.
