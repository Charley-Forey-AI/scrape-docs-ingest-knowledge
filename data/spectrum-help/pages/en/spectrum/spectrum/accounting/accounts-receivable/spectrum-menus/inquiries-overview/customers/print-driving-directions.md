# Print Driving Directions

Follow these steps to call up a customer location in the map pane and then print out driving directions.

1. On the Customer Main Properties page, call up the customer desired.

1. Using your mouse, open the slider bar on the right side of the workspace to
 view the map.

1. Click the View Larger Map link and the map will open in a new browser tab.

1. From here, you can print the map as well as get driving directions.
