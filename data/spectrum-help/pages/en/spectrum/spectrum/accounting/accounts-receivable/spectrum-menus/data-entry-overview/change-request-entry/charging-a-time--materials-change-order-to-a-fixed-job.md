# Charging a Time + Materials Change Order to a Fixed Job

If you need to record a time + materials change order to a
 fixed price job, complete the following steps.
The following procedure assumes that you are creating
 additional phases to track this T+M activity.

1. On the Site Map, click Job Cost  >  Maintenance  >  Phase.

1. Complete the Job, Phase, and
 Description fields and then press
 Enter. The Phase Defaults window
 automatically displays.

1. Complete the Billing item, Est.
 quantity, Unit of measure,
 Worker's comp, and Default status
 fields and then click OK.

1. In the Ct field (located in the details portion of the
 Phases screen), enter the cost type and then press
 Enter.

1. The Financials window automatically displays. Complete the
 applicable fields and then click OK.

1. Complete the details portion of the Phases screen and then
 click the Properties button.

1. In the Price option field, use the drop-down menu to select
 Time + Material.

1. Click OK until you return to the Site
 Map.

1. On the Site Map, click Time + Material  >  Maintenance  >  Job Billing.

1. Complete the Job and Customer fields
 and then press Enter.

1. Complete the relevant fields in the Details and
 Billing portions of the screen.

1. The Markups window automatically displays. Complete the
 Ct, Description,
 Type, and Amount fields for each
 markup (if applicable) and then click OK twice.

1. The Add-ons window automatically displays. Complete
 the Add-on, Description,
 Rate, and Costtype fields for
 each add-on (if applicable) and then click OK twice.

1. The Fee window automatically displays. Complete the
 Description, Type,
 Baseon, Cost type, and
 Taxable fields (if applicable) and then click
 OK until you return to the Site
 Map.

Optional: Complete the following to record that there was a
 change order and estimates.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  > Change Requests.

1. Complete the Job, Customer,
 Status, and New status fields.


1. Click the Add button.

1. Complete the fields to add the change request to the newly created phase and then
 click the Contract Pricing button.

1. Select the Add checkbox to add the corresponding change requests to the change
 order.

1. Click OK until you return to the Site
 Map.

Optional: Complete the following to record that there were
 estimates and a change order.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry Change Order .

1. Complete the Job, Customer,
 Status, and New status
 fields.

1. Click the Add button.

1. Complete the Owner change order number,Description, Date issued, and
 Current status fields.

1. Press Enter until the cursor is in the Change
 Requests included on this Change
 Order portion of the screen.

1. Click the Add button.

1. In the Add Change Requests to Change
 Order window, select the Add checkbox next to the change
 request(s) you want to include on this change order.

1. Click OK until you return to the Site
 Map.
