# Reclassifying a Retention Receivable Balance to Current

Use this procedure if you are reclassifying a retention
 receivable balance to current without a draw request.
If you are reclassifying a retention receivable
 balance to current with a draw request, see [Reclassifying a Retention Receivable Balance to Current Using Draw Requests](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-receivable/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/draw-request-entry/reclassifying-a-retention-receivable-balance-to-current-using-draw-requests).

1. Go to Accounts Receivable > Data Entry > Customer Invoices.

1. Enter an invoice for the contract.

1. In the header, select Override calculated
 retention.

1. In the Retention field, enter the retention you want to
 reclassify with a negative sign.

1. The Current amount will be the positive of your
 retention entry.

1. In the grid, enter 'Reclassify Retention' in the
 Description field (with no amounts).

1. Save and update the invoice.
