# Billing Quantity History Report

Use the Billing Quantity History Report to print the billing history by billing item. It can be run for the Engineering quantity and amount, Actual quantity and amount, or Both. The differences are shown at the billing item subtotals. In the Detail report, a new screen is printed for each new job/customer. The Summary report is almost identical to the Detail report except the information is only shown at the subtotal level for the entire billing item, and it is not summarized by application number.
