# Reports Overview

Selections from the Reports menu are used following conversion to obtain reports regarding existing customer contracts and change orders. It also provides reports pertaining to customers, aged open item and sales tax reporting. Statements can be generated, as well as G/L distribution reports detailing invoice and cash receipts activity.
