# Revising a Billing After It Is Sent To the Customer

If a billing needs to be corrected after it has been sent
 to the client, complete one of the following options.
Follow the steps in Option 1 if a draw request needs to be revised; follow the steps in
 Option 2 to revise an invoice.

## Option 1: Revising a Draw Request

This option reverses the selected draw request and creates a credit memo.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry >  Draw Request.

1. Click the Reverse button to display
 Reverse Last Draw Request screen.

1. On the Reverse Last Draw Request screen,
 complete the Job and Customer
 fields.

1. In the Application field, enter the
 number of the draw request that is to be reversed.

1. Complete the G/L date and
 Batch code fields and then click
 Preview. Note: Do not select the Clear current period amounts
 checkbox.

1. Click Preview buttons.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  >  Invoice / Credit Memo.

1. Click Preview to review the A/R credit
 memo resulting from the draw reversal.

1. Click the Update button.

1. In the Sales Journal / Update screen,
 complete the Selections portion of the screen and click
 Preview.

1. Review the listing for accuracy and then select
 Continue.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  >  Draw Request and make the appropriate changes to the draw.

1. Click the Print button.
 Preview the listing. Review the data for accuracy.

1. Update the new draw request.

## Option 2 - Revising an Invoice

This option reverses the incorrect invoice and creates a credit
 memo.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  >  Invoice / Credit Memo.

1. On the Invoice Entry screen, click the
 Reverse button.

1. Complete the Customer code and
 Type fields. Tip: The Type is the type of
 transaction you are reversing. For example, to reverse an existing invoice,
 select Invoice.


1. In the Invoice # field, enter the number
 of the invoice or credit memo to be reversed.

1. The invoice's details, including description and totals,
 displays at the bottom of the screen. Review the information to ensure you have
 selected the correct invoice to reverse, and click OK.

1. The Reverse Invoice window automatically
 displays. Complete the G/L date and Batch
 code fields.

1. Select the Create reverse item option
 and then click OK.

1. Click the Update button. The
 Sales/Journal Update screen displays the invoice
 information that will be updated. Review the information and make any necessary
 changes.

1. Click Preview and then click the
 OK button.

1. The Sales JournalUpdate screen displays.
 Select Continue and then click OK.

1. Once the software has performed the update, it will return you
 to the Invoice Memo Entry screen. Proceed to re-create the
 invoice using the correct billing amount.

1. Once all of the necessary invoice information is entered into
 Spectrum, click OK until you return to the Site
 Map.
