# Reversing a Cash Receipt

The following procedure explains how to correct an Accounts
 Receivable check that has been misapplied.

1. On the Site Map, click Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry >  >  Cash Receipts / Adjustment.

1. Complete the Customer code and
 Transaction type fields.Note: In the Transaction type
 field, select an adjustment type transaction code.

1. In the Reference # field, press
 Enter to accept the next sequential number assigned by
 Spectrum.

1. In the Date field, enter the
 appropriate G/L date.

1. In the Amount field, enter the
 transaction amount as a negative number. The Cash Receipts / Adjustment
 Entry Selections window automatically displays.Important: It is critical that you enter a negative number, not a
 positive number.

1. Complete the fields in the Cash Receipts /
 Adjustment Entry Selections window as appropriate and then click
 OK.

1. In the Details portion of the
 Cash Receipts / Adjustment Entry screen, locate the invoice
 that requires the cash reversal.

1. In the Applied Amount field,
 enter the amount as a negative number.Important: It is critical that you enter a negative number, not a
 positive number.

1. Click OK until the cursor
 returns to the Customer code field.

1. Click Preview to review the
 Cash Receipts Entry Edit List; make any necessary changes
 and review the listing again.

1. On the Cash Receipts / Adjustments
 Entry screen, click the Update button to
 display the Cash Receipts Journal screen.

1. Select the appropriate customer and G/L date range
 then click Preview to open the Cash Receipts Update.

1. Click Continue and then click
 OK. This audit record should be retained permanently.
