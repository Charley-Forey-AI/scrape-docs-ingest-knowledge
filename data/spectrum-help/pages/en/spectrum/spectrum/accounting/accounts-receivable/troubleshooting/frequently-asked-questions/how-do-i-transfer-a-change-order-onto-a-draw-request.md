# How do I transfer a change order onto a draw request?

1. After creating an executed change order or change request, select your draw request in Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  >  Draw Request.

1. Click the Transfer CO button.

1. Press Enter until the cursor is in the detail section.

1. Select the desired change order/change request.

1. Click OK.
