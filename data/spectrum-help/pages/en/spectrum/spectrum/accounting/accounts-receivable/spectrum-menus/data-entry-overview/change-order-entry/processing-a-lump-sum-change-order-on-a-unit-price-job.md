# Processing a Lump Sum Change Order On a Unit Price Job

Complete the following steps to process a unit priced job's
 lump sum change order.

1. On the Site Map, select
 Accounts Receivable  >  Maintenance  >  Unit of Measure.

1. Click the New button to enter a new unit of measure.

1. Enter a Unit of measure code
 and Description.

1. Select a Price method and
 whether this is subject to a Markup.

1. Repeat until all units of measure are in the software,
 and click OK until you return to the Site
 Map.

1. On the Site Map, click
 Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry  > Change Request.

1. Complete the Job,
 Customer, and enter a new Change request # , or press
 Enter to allow the
 system to assign the next available number.

1. Enter a Description,
 Current status, and Origination date
 fields.

1. Click the Costs tab and add any subcontractor costs.

1. Click Save.

1. On the Site Map, click
 Job Cost  >  Maintenance  >  Phase.

1. Enter the Job code.

1. Click the Begin Editing button to edit existing rows or add new rows.

1. When all detail lines are complete, click
 Done Editing and close this screen to return to the
 Site Map.
