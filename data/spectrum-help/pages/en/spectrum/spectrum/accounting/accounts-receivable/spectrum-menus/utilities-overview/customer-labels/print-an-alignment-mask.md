# Print an Alignment Mask

1. Load the labels into the printer.

1. Select the Alignment mask option to print an alignment mask. This prints XXXs on the labels where information will be printed.

1. Check whether the XXXs are aligned correctly. If the alignment is incorrect, adjust the printer. Select the Alignment mask option to print the alignment mask again. Repeat until alignment is correct.

1. When alignment of the XXXs is correct, click Labels to print the labels.
