# Change Order Profit Reports

The Change Order Profit Reports screen is used to print profit information for a specific change order. The Detail report format shows the total cost of each change order broken out by up to five cost types, as well as total revenue and estimated profit. As an option, the detail report can include subcontract and contractor costs, hours, and quantity for each phase. The Summary report option shows the total cost of each change order broken into the five cost types, along with total revenue, estimated profit, and estimated profit %. Options are available on both reports to include related change requests and notes.
