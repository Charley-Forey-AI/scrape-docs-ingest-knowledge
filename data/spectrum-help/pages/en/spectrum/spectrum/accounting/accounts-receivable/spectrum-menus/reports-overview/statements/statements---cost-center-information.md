# Statements - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the starting screen includes a Invoice cost group field. When a cost group or cost center is specified, the report will show only invoices assigned to that cost group/cost center. When the operator specifies a cost center, Spectrum verifies that the operator has permission to access that cost center before proceeding.
