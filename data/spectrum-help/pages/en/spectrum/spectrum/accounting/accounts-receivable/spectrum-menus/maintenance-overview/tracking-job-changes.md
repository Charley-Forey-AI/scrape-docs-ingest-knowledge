# Tracking Job Changes

Use the following status codes to manage change requests and change orders.These codes are defined by the client and can have one of five actions associated with it.

## Change Requests

Action
Description

1
Recognize revenue & cost revisions; billing allowed

2
Recognize revenue & cost revisions; billing not allowed

3
Recognize cost revisions; billing not allowed

4
No recognition of revenue or cost; no billings

5
Reverse revenue and cost recognition

## Change Orders

Action
Description

1
Recognize revenue & cost revisions; billing allowed

2
Recognize revenue & cost revisions; billing not allowed
