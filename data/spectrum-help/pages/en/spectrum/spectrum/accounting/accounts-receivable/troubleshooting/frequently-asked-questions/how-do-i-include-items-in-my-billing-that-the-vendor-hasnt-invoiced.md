# How do I include items in my billing that the vendor hasn't invoiced?

Answer: Use a memo billing, which can be used to include costs for material purchased and delivered but not yet invoiced.
The Accounts Receivable Draw Request Cost Selection screen allows entry of memo billings. These memo billings would then be reversed in the next billing, after the invoice for the material has been entered.
