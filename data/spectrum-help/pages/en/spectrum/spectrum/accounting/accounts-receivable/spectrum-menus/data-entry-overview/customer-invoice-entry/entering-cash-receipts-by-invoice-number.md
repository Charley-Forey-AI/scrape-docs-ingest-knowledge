# Entering Cash Receipts by Invoice Number

The following procedure explains how to enter cash receipts
 by invoice number.

1. On the Site Map, click
 Accounts Receivable  >  Data Entry Cash Receipt /
 Adjustment.

1. Complete the Customer code,
 Transaction type, and Check #
 fields.

1. Optional Step: In the
 Aba # field, enter the ABA number for the checked.

1. Complete the transaction Date
 and check Amount fields. The Cash Receipts /
 Adjustment Entry Selections window automatically displays.

1. Click the Invoice button. The
 Invoice Selection window automatically displays.

1. In the Inv/chk # field, enter
 the invoice number.

1. In the invoice Type field,
 enter I for Invoice for C for Credit
 memo.

1. Complete the Inv/chk # and
 invoice Type fields for any remaining invoice numbers for this
 payment.

1. Click OK until the cursor is in
 the Discount field on the Cash Receipts /
 AdjustmentEntry screen and then press Enter
 once.

1. In the Paid/adjusted field,
 enter the amount received on the check.Tip: Click the Apply
 button to automatically default the current balance due for this
 transaction.

1. Repeat until all of the cash is applied.

1. Click OK until you return to
 the Site Map.
