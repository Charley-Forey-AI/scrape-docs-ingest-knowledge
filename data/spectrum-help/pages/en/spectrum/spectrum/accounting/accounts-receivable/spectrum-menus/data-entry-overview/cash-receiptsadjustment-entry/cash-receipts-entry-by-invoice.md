# Cash Receipts Entry by Invoice

Use this window to enter the list of invoice numbers listed
 on the remittance advice of the payment.
This window is useful when applying a cash receipt to a
 customer's open items. If the particular invoice has already been applied, this window will
 retain the applied amount(s). Otherwise, if the payment has not been applied to the
 invoice, the window will default the 'current applied' amount using the same standard rules
 as when the Apply button is clicked on the Cash Receipts/Adjustment Entry
 screen.

1. Enter a valid invoice or transaction number in the
 Invoice/check
 field.

1. The Transaction type will default based on the invoice or credit memo
 entered above.

1. The Invoice date and Invoice total will display below. Click the lookup icon to view
 additional information for the invoice.

1. The Current balance for the invoice displays.

1. Enter a Current applied amount for this receipt. If the invoice number is
 already applied to this receipt, the current applied amount previously recorded will
 default in this field. Otherwise, this field will display the 'Applied amount' from
 the Cash Receipts/Adjustment
 Entry screen based on the outstanding current balance of the
 transaction on that row.

1. The Retention balance for the invoice displays.

1. Enter a Retention applied amount for this receipt. If the invoice number is
 already applied to this receipt, the retention applied amount previously recorded
 will default. Otherwise, this field will default to 0.00.

1. The Undistributed balance amount will update based on the amounts entered
 in the Current applied and
 Retention applied
 fields.

1. Click OK to record the entry and start a new entry. The Previous entry half of this window
 will display the last entered receipt. This is helpful when adding a long list of
 invoices.
