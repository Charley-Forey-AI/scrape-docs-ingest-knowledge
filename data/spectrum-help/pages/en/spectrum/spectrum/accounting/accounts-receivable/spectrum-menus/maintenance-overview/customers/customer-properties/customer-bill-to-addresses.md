# Customer Bill-to Addresses

The Customer Bill-to Addresses screen will display alternate billing addresses currently set up for the customer. Once established, alternate bill-to addresses are integrated with the following Spectrum screens:

- Accounts Receivable Contracts

- Accounts Receivable Customers

- Accounts Receivable Recurring Invoices

- Materials Management Customer Material Contracts

- Service Contract Billings and Manufacturer Warranty

- Time + Material Job Billing Setup

- Work Order Sites
