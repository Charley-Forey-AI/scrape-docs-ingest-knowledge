# Customer Labels

The Customer Labels screen is used to print mailing labels for all or selected customers. If printed on a dot-matrix printer, these should be printed at 10-pitch on 3½" x 15/16" labels. If printed on a laser printer, these should be printed at 10-pitch on 2.63" x 1" labels.
To ensure that labels are positioned correctly in the printer, an alignment mask may be printed; this puts a pattern of XXXs where label information will print.
