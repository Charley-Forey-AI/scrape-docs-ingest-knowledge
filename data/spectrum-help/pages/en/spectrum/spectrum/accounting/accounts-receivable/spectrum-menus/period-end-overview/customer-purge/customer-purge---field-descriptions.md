# Customer Purge - Field Descriptions

Use this table for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields
Description

Customer
Enter the customer code(s) to be deleted, or press Enter to delete ALL customer codes.

Customer type
Enter the customer type(s) to be deleted, or press Enter to delete ALL customer types.

Purge through date
Enter the purge through date for deleting customers. Customers will not be deleted if their last bill date or paid date is later than this purge through date; they have work orders with a complete date later than this purge through date; or if they have a service contract with an end date later than this purge through date.
Press the Preview button to preview the Customer Purge Report and continue with the purge.
