# Why is the contract amount incorrect on the draw request?

If your original or revised contract amounts are incorrect,
 two common reports may help discover why.

1. On the Site Map, click
 Contract File Report in Accounts
 Receivable > Maintenance > Contract.

1. Click the Listing button.

1. Enter the job number.

1. Select the Include detail checkbox.

1. Click the Preview button.

1. Review the header and details of the report.Note: Printing the Change Order Log Report in
 Accounts Receivable > Data Entry > Change Orders often provides details if the discrepancy is in the changes to the
 original contract.
