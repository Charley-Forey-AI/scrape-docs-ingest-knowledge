# Can I delete a billing item/change my schedule of values on a draw request?

You can delete specific line items from a draw request if billings have not been done for that billing item(s) on any draw requests. Keep in mind that the line items should also be deleted from the contract. If the billing items have been billed on previous draw requests, revisions should be made using change orders and change requests.
