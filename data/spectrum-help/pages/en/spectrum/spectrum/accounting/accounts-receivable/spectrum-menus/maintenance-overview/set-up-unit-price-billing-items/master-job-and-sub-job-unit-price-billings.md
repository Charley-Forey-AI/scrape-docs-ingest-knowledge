# Master Job and Sub-Job Unit Price Billings

A "master contract" is the combination of a 'Customer' and master 'Job'. Items defined on the master contract will be available to the sub-job contracts as well.
Items and rates defined by contract, master contract, customer and company-wide pricing tables will be available for selection in Customer Invoice Entry. The system will default in a non-blank rate using the following hierarchy:

1. Contract

1. Master Contract

1. Customer

1. Standard (Company-wide)
You can also use this screen to set up a contract-specific price list for use in [Customer Invoice Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-receivable/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/contracts/contract-entry/customer-invoice-entry). Select the Copy Items button to copy the setup from a template as needed.
Important: The job and contract must both be set up as 'Unit
 price' to do this.
