# Period End Overview

The Period End Menu is used to delete closed purchase
 orders and to obtain a printed log of purchase orders.
It is recommended that printed reports be obtained and checked for accuracy before
 information is removed from the software.
Important: Extreme caution should be exercised when
 performing purges!

- [Purge Closed Purchase Orders](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/purchase-order/spectrum-menus/period-end-overview/purge-closed-purchase-orders)
