# Packing List Log - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled, only purchase orders you are allowed to access will display on this page. This is determined by comparing the cost center assigned to the purchase order with cost centers in your scheme, and if the purchase order cost center is not present, then the purchase order will not be displayed.
