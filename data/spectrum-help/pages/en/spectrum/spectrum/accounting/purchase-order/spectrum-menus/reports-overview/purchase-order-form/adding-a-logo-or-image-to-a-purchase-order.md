# Adding a Logo or Image to a Purchase Order

Follow these steps to include a company logo or other image
 in the heading area on your purchase order.

1. Locate the image or logo that you want to include.

1. In Company Installation - Print Options tab, in the Company logo field, click the Upload button and then click the Choose File button.

1. Navigate to the image file location on your network, select the image file and click Open.

- File names are limited to 30 characters.

- All major image file types are supported (.gif, jpg, .bmp, .png).

- There are no restrictions on the image size, but you will want to preview your report before printing to make sure the image file you choose meets your own standards for professionalism.

1. Save your changes!

1. Now your purchase orders will automatically include the image in the heading.
