# Vendor Special Cost Maintenance

This feature may be used to enter discounts allowed by
 specific vendors.
It is particularly useful when a pricing service is used, as it allows you to adjust
 industry standards costs according to arrangements with particular vendors. Special pricing
 can be applied by Item or by Category, depending on your selection in the Type field.
