# P.O. Receiving History Inquiry - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for assistance when completing the fields on this screen.
Field/Button
Description

Purchase order
To display the receiving history for a
 particular purchase order, enter the purchase order number. The purchase order
 number entered here must have been previously defined in Purchase Order Entry.
 The vendor associated with this purchase order will display to the right of
 this field
Job / Work Order / Warehouse
The job, work order or warehouse code
 will display in this field if specified in the purchase order header.
Ordered amount
The purchase order total (including
 tax) displays in this field.
Outstanding
The dollar value (including tax) of the
 portion of the purchase order placed but not yet received displays. The
 outstanding amount is calculated by multiplying the number of units not yet
 received by the unit cost. This figure may not equal ordered amount less
 received amount if items have been received at costs other than the unit cost
 on the P.O. Closed purchase orders automatically show the outstanding amount
 equal to $0.00, even if not received-in-full. Note: When the Received amount is greater than the Ordered
 amount, the amount in the Remaining on-order field is computed as zero
 unless the Ordered amount is also a negative number.
