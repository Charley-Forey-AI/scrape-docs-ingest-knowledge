# Do I need to perform Payroll year-end processing when I roll the fiscal year forward?

No. The Payroll fiscal year is always based on the calendar year due to IRS regulations. If you are running anything besides a calendar-based fiscal year for accounting system, the General Ledger and Payroll year-ends will not coincide.
