# Why can't I delete out the date on the draw's Period to field?

I can't delete out the date on the draw's Period to field because this is a mandatory input field. How can I remove the G/L date so this doesn't appear as an unposted draw?
Answer:
Navigate to Draw Request Entry and click the Update button. This will take you to the Update Draws to A/R Entry screen. Continue navigating the screen and clear the G/L date for any application that you do not want to consider "unposted."
