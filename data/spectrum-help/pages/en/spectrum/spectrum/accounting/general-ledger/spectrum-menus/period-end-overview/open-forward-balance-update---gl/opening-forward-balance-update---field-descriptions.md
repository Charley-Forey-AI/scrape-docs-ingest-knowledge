# Opening Forward Balance Update - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Field/ButtonDescription
ContinueSelect to proceed with the update.
From fiscal yearEnter the year from which the balances are to be carried forward. For example, to update fiscal 2025 balances to 2026, enter 25.
The year entered here must be included within the current fiscal calendar. During this update, the G/L account's status is used to determine if the G/L account's balance will roll forward from the previous year to the next year.
