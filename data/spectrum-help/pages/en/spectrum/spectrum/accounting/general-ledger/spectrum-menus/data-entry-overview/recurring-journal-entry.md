# Recurring Journal Entry

In Spectrum, a preformatted, standardized journal entry can be generated on a recurring basis without having to re-enter the information every time the journal entry needs to be entered. These can be used for depreciation or other recurring transactions.
All recurring journal entries display in this list box unless cost centers are used.
