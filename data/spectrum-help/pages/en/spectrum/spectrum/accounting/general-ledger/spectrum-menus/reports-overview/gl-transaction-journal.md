# G/L Transaction Journal

Use this screen to print a journal of General Ledger transactions in date sequence.
Several options are available to create the report you need, such as an alignment mask.
You can review the total lines for large reports printed across multiple periods.The report can display numbers up to 13 digits, allowing large figures to display instead of a string of asterisks (*).
