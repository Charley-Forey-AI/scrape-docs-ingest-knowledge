# Journal Entry History Report - Cost Center Information

If your company is using cost centers , Spectrum includes G/L account activity if you have permission to access the cost center assigned to the G/L transaction. Spectrum compares the cost center assigned in the G/L transaction detail with cost centers in your assigned scheme, and if the transaction cost center is not included, then that transaction is not shown.
