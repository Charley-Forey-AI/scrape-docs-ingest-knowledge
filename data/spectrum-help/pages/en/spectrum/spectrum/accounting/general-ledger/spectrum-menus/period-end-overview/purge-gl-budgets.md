# Purge G/L Budgets

Use this screen to purge the entire General Ledger budget file; otherwise, individual accounts may be deleted in the [G/L Budget File Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/general-ledger/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/gl-budget-file-maintenance) screen.
Information removed with this purge will not be available for inquiry or financial reporting. Budget records are removed based on your selection of fiscal year.
