# Print Financial Reports

Use the Print Financial Reports screen to print financial reports based on the parameters you enter. Up to 99 reports may be queued to print from each workstation.
To navigate to this screen, go to General Ledger > Reports > Print Financial Reports.
If your company uses the multi-company feature, you may use this screen for all your printing needs from all companies.
To design a financial report, go to General Ledger > Maintenance > Design Financial Reports. See [Design Financial Reports](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/general-ledger/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/design-financial-reports).
