# Fiscal Calendar Changes - Transactions Entered

If the ending date for your fiscal calendar changes and if
 you have entered data into the first of month or more of your new fiscal year, follow the
 instructions below to correct your fiscal calendar.
Note: This is not a routine
 procedure.

1. Update all open transactions to General Ledger (A/P
 Invoices, PR cycle, A/R Cash Receipts, and so forth).

1. Run a G/L Summary Trial Balance Report for each period to be
 corrected.

1. Prepare a [Journal Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/general-ledger/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/journal-entry) for each
 period, being certain to enter a credit in the amount of each debit and a debit in
 the amount of each credit.

1. Click Update to zero out all the active accounts listed in the report
 above.

1. On the Site Map, click General Ledger  >  Reports  >  Trial Balance and run the G/L Trial Balance Report to verify that all ending balances have been zeroed out.Follow the [Fiscal Calendar Changes - No Transactions Entered](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/general-ledger/spectrum-menus/period-end-overview/fiscal-calendar-maintenance/fiscal-calendar-changes---no-transactions-entered) instructions for changing your fiscal calendar.
Prepare a Journal Entry and run an Update to add the original debit/credit figures to the new fiscal year.

1. On the Site Map, click the General Ledger > Period End > Opening Forward Balance
 Update from the prior year to bring the General Ledger costs in line with the
 correct year. All income and expenses will move to retained earnings while all
 assets, liabilities, and capital will be carried over into the new year.

1. On the Site Map, click Equipment Control > Period End > Opening Forward Balance
 Update to bring the prior year's Equipment Control costs in line with the
 current year.If you have AP and/or AP invoices created in this interim
 period, their invoice headers will have the wrong fiscal year and period. Please
 contact Support if you wish to have these corrected to show the proper fiscal year
 and period. Support will refer your incident to Special Projects for consultation
 and estimates.
