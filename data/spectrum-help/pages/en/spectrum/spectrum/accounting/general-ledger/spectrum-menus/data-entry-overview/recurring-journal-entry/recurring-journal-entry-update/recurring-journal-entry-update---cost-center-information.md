# Recurring Journal Entry Update - Cost Center Information

If your company is using cost centers, the Cost group field is available. When a cost center is specified in this field, then the update will include only recurring journal entries assigned to cost centers in that group (based on the cost center recorded in the heading portion of the Recurring Journal Entry screen).
When you specify a cost center on the starting screen, Spectrum verifies that you have permission to access that cost center before proceeding.
