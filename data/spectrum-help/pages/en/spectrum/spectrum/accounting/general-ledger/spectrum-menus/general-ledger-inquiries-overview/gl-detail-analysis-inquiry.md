#  G/L Detail Analysis Inquiry

Use this screen to view detail transactions across accounts
 and periods. You can view one account, selected accounts, or a range of accounts.
The grid can be sorted by re-arranging columns, grouping by specified
 columns, or filtering based on additional settings by clicking the [Selections, Detail](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/general-ledger/spectrum-menus/general-ledger-inquiries-overview/gl-summary-analysis-inquiry/selections-detail) button.
