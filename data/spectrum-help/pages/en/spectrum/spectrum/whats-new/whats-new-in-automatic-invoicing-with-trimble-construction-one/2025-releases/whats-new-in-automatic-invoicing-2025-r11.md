# What's New in Automatic Invoicing 2025 R11

With this release, Accounts Payable Admins are able to assign
 companies to users and inboxes to enable company-specific access to the inboxes. Users will
 only have access to inboxes associated with their assigned companies.

## Enable Company-Specific Access

With this release, Company Settings enable you to assign users and
 inboxes to specific companies. In Company Settings, toggle on Enable Company-Specific Access,
 then assign companies to users. In Inbox Settings, add specific companies to a forwarding inbox. Users
 will only see the inboxes for companies that they are assigned to.
For details, see [Edit Company Settings](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/accounts-payable/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/automatic-invoicing-for-trimble-construction-one-users/edit-company-settings).
