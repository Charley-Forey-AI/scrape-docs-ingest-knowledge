# What's New in Automatic Invoicing with Trimble Construction One

With Automatic Invoicing, Trimble Construction One users can
 automatically enter invoice headers into Spectrum without performing manual data entry, saving time and
 avoiding potential mistakes.
Use Automatic Invoicing
 to enter validated vendor invoices.
You can take advantage of Automatic Invoicing if your company's subscription for Spectrum is part of our
 Trimble Construction One contract.
Users who access Automatic Invoicing must be using single sign-on in Spectrum. For details
 on enabling single sign-on for your users, see [Single Sign-On](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:spectrum_000001:Spectrum:Spectrum).
For instructions on how to enable and use Automatic Invoicing, see [Automatic Invoicing (for
 Trimble Construction One Users) ](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:spectrum_000008:Spectrum:Spectrum) .
