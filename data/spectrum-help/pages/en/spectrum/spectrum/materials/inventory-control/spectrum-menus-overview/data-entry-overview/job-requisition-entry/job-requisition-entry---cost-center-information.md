# Job Requisition Entry - Cost Center Information

When the job cost center is different from the warehouse cost center, an inter-posting transaction will be created for Job Requisitions. In this situation, a credit entry is made to the job/phase cost center and a debit entry is made to warehouse cost center, and both debit and credit entries include markup and tax along with inventory cost.
This is the only screen in Inventory Control where multi-company entries can be recorded. In order to use this feature, select the multi-company checkbox in the [Inventory Control Installation - Properties](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/inventory-control/installation-overview/inventory-control-installation---properties) screen and enter the inter-company accounts before returning to this screen to complete your entries.
