# How should I reconcile inventory asset accounts with Items-On-Hand
 Valuation Report?

1. Look for items that are negative on the report. If a physical inventory was done, no items should be less than zero.

1. Use the Item History
 Report to look for negative items. This report gives a list of sales
 and receipts for inventory items.
Note: This reports rarely provides you with a good inventory
 valuation amount because its history can be purged.

1. Print the Valuation Report (in Detail) as of the current processing date to look for an up-to-date balance between your assets and General Ledger.

1. Once the inventory item is located, perform an adjustment in Inventory Control >  Data Entry > Adjustments.
