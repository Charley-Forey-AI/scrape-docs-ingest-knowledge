# Adding Freight or Other Charges After an Item has been Received

Learn how to how to add freight or other miscellaneous
 charges to an inventory purchase after it has been received.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Inventory Control >  Data Entry >  Receipts.

1. In the Transaction reference # field, enter the reference number of the
 document from which this receipt is being entered.

1. In the Transaction type field, press Enter to accept the Receipt default.

1. In the Transaction date field, press Enter to accept the current
 processing date.

1. Click the Freight / Misc. button.

1. Complete the Freight and Misc. sections of the window for
 the charges that are being added to the item. Those fields are not applicable to the
 transaction should be left blank.

1. Click OK until you return to the main menu.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Inventory Control >  Data Entry >  Cost Adjustments.

1. In the Transaction reference # field, enter the same reference number that
 was entered in step 2.

1.  In the Transaction sequence # field, press Enter to accept the default
 sequence number. Complete the Cost adjustment section of the
 screen.Note: The amount entered in the Total adjustment amount field
 should be the amount entered in the Freight and Misc.
 Charges window (step 6). If both freight and miscellaneous charges
 were added in this window, the sum of these charges should be entered in this
 field.

1. Click OK until you return to the main menu.
