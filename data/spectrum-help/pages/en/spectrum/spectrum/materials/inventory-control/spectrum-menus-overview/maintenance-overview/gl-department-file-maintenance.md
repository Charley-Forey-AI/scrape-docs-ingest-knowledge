# G/L Department File Maintenance

The G/L Department File Maintenance screen is used to enter General Ledger department codes. The G/L codes associated with the inventory departments will be utilized during receipt and adjustment of the inventory. In addition to establishing inventory departments, companies may use this screen to create sales departments for use during Order Processing. While the user may specify an inventory department during order or invoice entry that will designate the desired G/L income account, it is not required that the G/L account code specified in the category code of the inventory item be used. A sales department may be defined instead. For example, the company may wish to separate sales in the General Ledger by wholesale and retail, or by location of customer, or even by region or salesperson. In any of these cases, sales departments is used.
Note: Regarding Inventory Transfers and G/L CodesInventory transfers do not update the General Ledger because the inventory item is not
 changing value, it is simply moving from one warehouse to another. In the Materials
 Management module you can have scale tickets that are transfers, and this G/L account is
 used on the freight cost update as the location for freight charges related to the
 transfer.
