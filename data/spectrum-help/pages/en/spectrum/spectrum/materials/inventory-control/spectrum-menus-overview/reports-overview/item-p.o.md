# Item P.O. Receiving Report

The Item P.O. Receiving Report screen will show receipt of
 items from P.O. by warehouse in order to review purchase prices of inventory items over time.
This report will show information sorted
 primarily by warehouse (and items within the warehouse) rather than grouping items by P.O.
 This report excludes items not purchased for inventory, such as non-stocks and job
 purchases.
