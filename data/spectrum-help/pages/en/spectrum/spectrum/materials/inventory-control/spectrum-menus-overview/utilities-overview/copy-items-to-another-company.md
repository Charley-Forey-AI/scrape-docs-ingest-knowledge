# Copy Items to Another Company

The Copy Items to Another Company screen is used to copy
 Inventory Item File Maintenance records from one company to another.
This update creates records in the
 IM.MSTR file in the destination company and builds associated cross-reference files
 (IM.CXRF and IM.SXRF). Caution should be exercised when performing this function.
