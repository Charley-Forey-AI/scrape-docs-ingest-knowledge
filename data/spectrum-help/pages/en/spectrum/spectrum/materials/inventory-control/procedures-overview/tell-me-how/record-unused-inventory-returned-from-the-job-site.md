# Record Unused Inventory Returned from the Job Site

At times, too much inventory is requisitioned to a job. When this happens, the excess inventory is returned to your warehouse from the job. To keep the job from being charged for the unused inventory, complete the following steps.
PART A – Requisitioning Inventory to a Job

1. On the Inventory Control > Data Entry > Job Requisition.

1. In the Requisition # field, enter the inventory's requisition number.

1. In the Status field, use the drop-down menu to select the Shipped option.

1. Complete the Ship via, Ship date, Job, and Instructions fields.

1. In the Item code field, enter the inventory item's code or press F4 to select from a list of valid item codes.

1. In the Markup field, enter the markup percent (if applicable) or press Enter to accept the software default.

1. Complete the Order qty, G/L code, Job, Phase, Phase, and Ct fields.

1. In the Ext field, enter Y to include the requisition's extension; leave this field blank to exclude the requisition's extension. Press F4 to view the extension amount.

1. When all requisition items are in the detail section, click OK until the Print Job
 Requisition window displays.

1. In the Print Job Requisition window, select the
 Yes, print this
 requisition and click OK.

1. Print or preview the requisition and then click OK to return to the main menu.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Inventory Control > Data Entry > Transaction Update to open the Inventory Transaction Report
 screen.

1. Complete the Inventory Transaction Report screen and then
 click Print or Preview. The Inventory
 Transaction Update screen displays.

1. Select Continue and
 then click OK. The
 Inventory G/L Summary Update screen displays.

1. Select Continue and then click OK.

PART B – Recording Unused Inventory from a Job

1. On the Site Map screen, clickInventory Control > Data Entry > Job > Requisition to open the Job Requisition Entry screen.

1. In the Requisition #
 field, enter a new inventory requisition number.
Note: Do not use the original requisition number.

1. In the Status field, use the drop-down menu to select Shipped.

1. Complete the Ship via, Ship date, Job, and Instructions fields.

1. In the Item code field, enter the inventory item's code or press F4 to select from a list of valid item codes.

1. In the Markup field, enter the markup percent (if applicable) or press Enter to accept the software default.

1. In the Order qty field, enter the amount of returned inventory as a negative number.

1. Complete the Dept, G/L code, Job, Phase, Ct, and Phase fields.

1. In the Ext field, enter Y to include the requisition's extension; leave this field blank to not include to the requisition's extension. Press F4 to view the extension amount.

1. When all requisition items are in the detail section, click OK until the Print Job Requisition window displays.

1. In the Print Job Requisition window, select the
 Yes, print this
 requisition and click OK.

1. Click Print or Preview, review the requisition, and then click OK to return to the main menu.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Inventory Control > Data Entry > Transaction Update to open the Inventory Transaction Report
 screen.

1. Complete the Inventory Transaction Report screen and then
 click Print or Preview. The Inventory
 Transaction Update screen displays.

1. Select Continue and
 then click OK. The
 Inventory G/L Summary Update screen displays.

1. Select Continue and then click OK.
