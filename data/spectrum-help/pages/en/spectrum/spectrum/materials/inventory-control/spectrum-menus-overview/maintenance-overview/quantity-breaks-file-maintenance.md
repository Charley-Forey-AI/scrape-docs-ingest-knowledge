# Quantity Breaks File Maintenance

The Quantity Breaks File Maintenance screen is used to
 establish and maintain quantity discounts for favored customers. It may be used in conjunction
 with the Order Processing and Work Order modules.
Quantity discounts may be offered on an item-by-item basis or for categories as a whole. The item quantity discounts may be a percentage discount or a specified price at each level of quantity break. A date range for the quantity discount is provided. While it would be possible to make the quantity discount effectively permanent by authorizing the arrangement through "12/31/09" or "12/31/10", it may be prudent to review quantity discount pricing on a regular basis to determine whether such pricing should be continued or suspended. Some companies find this is useful on an annual basis, perhaps during a slow period of the year.
