# Inventory Control

Inventory may be requisitioned to jobs or sold directly to
 customers. Inventory Control tracks quantities and costs for each stock item, and is
 integrated with Time + Material, Sales Analysis, Materials Management, Equipment Tracking,
 Preventive Maintenance, Work Order, Job Cost, Purchase Order, Order Processing, and the
 General Ledger.
