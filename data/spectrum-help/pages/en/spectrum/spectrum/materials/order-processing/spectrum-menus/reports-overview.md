# Reports Overview

To access the Order Processing Reports menu, click Order Processing, and then click Reports.
The Reports menu is used to print information about orders and invoices. It includes reports detailing sales tax and sales commission activity, as well as G/L distribution. The Reports menu provides the ability to print specified order and invoice information.
