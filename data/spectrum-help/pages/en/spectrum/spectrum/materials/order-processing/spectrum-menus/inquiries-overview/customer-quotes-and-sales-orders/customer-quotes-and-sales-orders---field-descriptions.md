# Customer Quotes and Sales Orders - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields
Descriptions

Total quotes
This is the sum of the Subtotal and Tax columns (including any VAT tax amounts, if applicable) for all displayed active and inactive quotes.

Total sales orders
This is the sum of the Subtotal and Tax columns (including any VAT tax amounts, if applicable) for all displays sales orders.
