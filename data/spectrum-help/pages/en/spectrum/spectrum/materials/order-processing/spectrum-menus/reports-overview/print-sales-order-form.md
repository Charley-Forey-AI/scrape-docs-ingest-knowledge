# Print Sales Order Form

The Print Sales Order Form screen is used to print sales
 orders for a specified range of orders. If a sales order has already been printed once, you
 must specify that the sales order should be printed again. One company might print all sales
 orders that have not yet been printed while other companies may opt never to print sales
 orders, or perhaps to print sales orders on a selective basis. It is not necessary to print
 sales orders before confirmation of shipment or invoicing.
