# Period End Overview

The Period End menu is used at the end of the fiscal period to clear month/quarter/year-to-date sales and cost of sales in the customer file. At the same time, you can choose to clear the sales tax file and the salesperson.
Important: If Accounts Payable or Payroll is installed on
 Spectrum, and customers purchasing
 products for resale are to be included, do not perform year end processing until 1099
 processing has commenced using 1099-Misc Form Update.
CAUTION: Extreme caution should be
 exercised when performing purges.
