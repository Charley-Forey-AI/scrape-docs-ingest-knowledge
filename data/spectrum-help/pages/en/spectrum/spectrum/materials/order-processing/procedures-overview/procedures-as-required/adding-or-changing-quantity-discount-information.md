# Adding or Changing Quantity Discount Information

You can add or change quantity discount information in
 Order Processing.

1. On the Site Mapscreen, click Order Processing  >  Maintenance  >  Quantity Discount.

1. Enter quantity discount information, if applicable.Adding Carriers
Use this procedure to add or change carrier information in Order Processing.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Order Processing  >  Maintenance  >  Ship Via.

1. Add ship via information, and then click OK.

1. On the Site Mapscreen, click Order Processing  >  Maintenance  >  Shipping Terms.

1. Add shipping terms information, and then click OK.
