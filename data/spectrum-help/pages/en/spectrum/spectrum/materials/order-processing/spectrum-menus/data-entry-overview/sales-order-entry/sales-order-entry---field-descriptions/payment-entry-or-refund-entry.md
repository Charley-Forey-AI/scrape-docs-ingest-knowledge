# Payment Entry or Refund Entry

The fields that display in this window will depend on whether you are making a payment or issuing a refund.
Fields
Descriptions

Total sale
No changes can be made to these fields.

Previously paid

Balance

Properties

Payment
Enter the payment amount in dollars and cents.

Amount due
The remaining amount due displays. No entry is allowed.

Payment date
Enter the date of payment.

Payment code
Enter a payment code. Up to 10 characters are permitted.

Reference
Enter a reference number for this transaction.
