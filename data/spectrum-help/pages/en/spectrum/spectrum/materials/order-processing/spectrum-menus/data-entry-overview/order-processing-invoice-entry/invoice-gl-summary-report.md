# Invoice G/L Summary Report

The Invoice G/L Summary
 Report provides an accounting record of invoice / credit memo processing, and
 is followed by an optional update.
General Ledger accounts are debited and credited based on information processed through the
 Invoice Register and
 Update. The posting to the
 General Ledger year and period is determined by the date of the invoice or credit memo. The
 report should be checked for accuracy; if correct, you can proceed with the update.
Important: This report should be retained as a permanent record of the company.
