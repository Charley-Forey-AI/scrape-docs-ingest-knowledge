# Customer Quotes and Sales Orders

Use this screen to review orders in detail for a selected customer.

- Click the Status button to define the list of records that display on the screen by selecting one or more record status types: Active quotes, Inactive quotes, and/or Sales orders.

- Click the Newbutton to open the [Sales Order Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/order-processing/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/sales-order-entry) screen, where you can add a new record.

- Click the Switch button to conveniently open a different record without going through the Spectrum Site Map.
