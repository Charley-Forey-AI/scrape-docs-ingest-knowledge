# Freight Recon. History Report - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Field
Description

Hauler
Press Enter to select tickets from all haulers for this report, or click the
 arrow to search for valid hauler codes.

Invoice
Press Enter to select all invoices for this report, or click the arrow to search for valid invoice numbers.

From invoice date To invoice date
Enter the beginning and end invoice dates to display on the report.
Leave blank to accept the first date and current Materials Management
 processing dates. Click the [Date Calculator](/en/spectrum/spectrum/getting-started/spectrum-interface-faqs/user-interface-elements/date-calculator)
 icon to select a calculation method.
