# Sales Total Breakdown

Use this window to display summary amounts.
Fields
Descriptions

Total extension
Once the specified tickets have been selected, the extension amount displays.

Freight charges
Once the specified tickets have been selected, the amount of freight charged displays.

Misc. charges
Once the specified tickets have been selected, any miscellaneous charges display.

Total $ (before tax)
This is calculated as the Total extension plus Freight charges plus Misc. charges.

Sales tax
Once the specified tickets have been selected, the sales tax displays.

Total $
This is calculated as the Total $ (before tax) plus the Sales tax.
