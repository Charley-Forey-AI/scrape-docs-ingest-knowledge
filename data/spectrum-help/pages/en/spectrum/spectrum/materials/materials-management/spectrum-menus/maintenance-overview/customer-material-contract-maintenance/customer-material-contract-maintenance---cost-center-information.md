# Customer Material Contract Maintenance - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the Customer Material Contract Maintenance screen includes a Cost center field. Entry in this field is an optional entry and may be left blank.
The operator must have security for the cost center to assign it to a record. Spectrum will compare the valid cost centers based on the operator's assigned cost center scheme. The cost center must also be active to assign it; this validation only happens when the user is modifying the Cost center field's data or adding a new customer contract. The Costcenter field must also be valid for the customer.
The operator will be unable to access a record if he or she does not have cost center security for the cost center assigned to a record.
The cost center will be used when posting from the Materials Management module to either Order Processing or Accounts Receivable invoices. A cost center may be set up for a customer and specific customer's job, or for a customer and blank customer's job.
