# Hauler Payable Report

The Hauler Payable Report provides a complete recap of the daily, weekly, or monthly freight cost journals. Some Spectrum users submit a copy of this report with the hauler's check. It is also used for reporting deliveries for the user's own deliveries and how much quantity has been delivered by their own trucks versus outside haulers. The Summary format sorts by hauler and date. The Detail format sorts by hauler, date, plant and ticket number.
The unit of measure (UM) shown on the report is based on the unit of measure stored in Inventory Item File Maintenance. The net weight is shown in pounds (lb), and is calculated from gross weight minus tare weight. The freight cost/quantity that is shown on the report is the actual freight cost quantity, if available; otherwise, it is the cost/quantity stored when the ticket was posted. The hauler rate is calculated by dividing the earnings by the quantity amount.
