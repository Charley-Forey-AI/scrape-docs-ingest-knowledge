# Purge Scale Ticket History - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Field
Description

Job number
Enter or search for a job number, or press Enter to accept the software default of ALL.

Customer
Enter or search for a customer code, or press Enter to accept the software default of ALL.

From date
Enter the date from that you want to purge, or press Enter to leave blank and purge from the beginning date.

To date
Enter the date with which to end the purge, or press Enter to accept the default current Materials Management processing date.
