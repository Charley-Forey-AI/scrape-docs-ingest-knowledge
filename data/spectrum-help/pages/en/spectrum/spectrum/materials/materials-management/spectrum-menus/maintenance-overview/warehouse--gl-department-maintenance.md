# Warehouse / G/L Department Maintenance

Use this screen to view general ledger departments for each warehouse. Each warehouse will transfer scale tickets to Inventory Control Job Requisition Entry or Order Processing Invoice Entry according to the settings established in this screen.
When receipt or transfer scale tickets that include freight or miscellaneous charges are updated to Inventory Control, these charges will increase the extension for each item, thereby increasing the unit cost by these amounts. Lump sum freight and miscellaneous costs will allocate to the items by quantity.
