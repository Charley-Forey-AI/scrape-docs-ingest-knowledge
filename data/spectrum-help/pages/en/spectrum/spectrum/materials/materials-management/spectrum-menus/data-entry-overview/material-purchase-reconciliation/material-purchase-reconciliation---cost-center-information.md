# Material Purchase Reconciliation - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the cost center entered on this screen will become the cost center in the A/P Invoice header once the Material Purchase Journal is run. This cost center will also be used when selecting the invoice for payment.
