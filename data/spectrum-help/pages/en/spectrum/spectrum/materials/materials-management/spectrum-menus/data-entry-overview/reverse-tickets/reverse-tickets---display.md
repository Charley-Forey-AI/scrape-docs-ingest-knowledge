# Reverse Tickets - Display

The second Reverse Tickets screen allows you to preview all tickets slated for reversal and remove the tickets you don't want included on the reversal.
Once you have made any necessary adjustments and deletions to the tickets on this screen, click Continue to begin the reversal process. When the ticket reversal is updated, credit memos, negative job requisitions, credit returns, imported freight costs, or reverse transfers will be added to the software to offset the original transactions, and the tickets will be moved from history to Scale Ticket Transactions for editing.
