# Job Material Contract Maintenance - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise Installation screen, the Operator will only be able to view records they have cost center security for. The operator will be unable to access a record if he or she does not have cost center security for the cost center assigned to the record.
The cost center will be used when posting from the Materials Management module to either Order Processing or Accounts Receivable invoices. A cost center may be set up for a customer and specific customer's job, or for a customer and blank customer's job.
