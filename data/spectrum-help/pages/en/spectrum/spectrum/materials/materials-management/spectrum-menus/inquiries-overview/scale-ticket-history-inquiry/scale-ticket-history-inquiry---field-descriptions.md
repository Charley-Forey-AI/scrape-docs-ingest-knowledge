# Scale Ticket History Inquiry - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Selections
Click to open the [Scale Ticket History Inquiry Selections](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/materials-management/spectrum-menus/inquiries-overview/scale-ticket-history-inquiry/scale-ticket-history-inquiry-selections) window.

From/To date
Enter the beginning and through dates, or press Enter for the first and current Materials Management processing dates.

Total quantity
Once the specified tickets have been selected, the delivered quantity displays.

Total amount
This is the sum of amounts from tickets displayed in the list box. Click the lookup icon to open the [Sales Total Breakdown](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/materials-management/spectrum-menus/inquiries-overview/scale-ticket-history-inquiry/sales-total-breakdown) window.

Ticket
Click to open the [Scale Ticket Inquiry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/materials-management/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/scale-ticket-transactions/scale-ticket-inquiry) window.

Form
Click to generate the Print Ticket form.

Ticket type
Click to select one or more ticket types. They are:

- Customer

- Job

- Receipt

- Transfer
