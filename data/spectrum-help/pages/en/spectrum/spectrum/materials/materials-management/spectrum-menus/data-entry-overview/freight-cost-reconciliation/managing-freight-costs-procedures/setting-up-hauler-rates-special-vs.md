# Setting Up Hauler Rates (Special vs. Standard)

Standard rates and special rates can be established both
 for internal and external haulers. Note that when outside haulers are used, Spectrum will
 generate the freight reconciliation data needed for creating an A/P invoice based on the
 vendor code entered in this screen.
Spectrum also offers
 the ability to set up special rates specifically for job contracts and customer sales. [Setting Up Hauler Rates for Job Contracts](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/materials-management/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/freight-cost-reconciliation/managing-freight-costs-procedures/setting-up-hauler-rates-for-job-contracts)
[Setting Up Hauler Rates for Customer Contracts](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/materials-management/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/freight-cost-reconciliation/managing-freight-costs-procedures/setting-up-hauler-rates-for-customer-contracts)
Important: If no vendor code entered, then Spectrum
 assumes that the hauler being used is internal and will not create the necessary
 reconciliation data needed to generate hauler information on future invoices.


1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Materials Management >  Maintenance > Hauler.

1. Complete the Properties,
 Freight cost, Freight billing,and
 Default freight rate type sections of the screen for the
 hauler.Note: If you are setting up a hauler with standard rates, leave
 the Vendor code field blank.

1. If you are setting up a standard hauler, the process
 is now complete. If you are setting up a special rates (outside) vendor, proceed to
 step 4.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Materials
 Management >  Maintenance >  Customer Contract.

1. At the Customer and
 Customer job
 fields, enter the customer code and customer job number (optional) for the
 special hauler rates you are establishing. Press F4 at
 either field to search for the necessary data.

1. Click the Hauler
 Rates button. The Customer and
 Customer job fields will default based on the
 customer information you selected in the previous screen.

1. At the Hauler code
 field, enter the hauler for which you are setting up special rates, or press
 F4 to select from a list of valid hauler codes.Note: You can enter multiple haulers for one
 customer.

1. At the Default rate
 type field, select either Ton or
 Hour. This default setting will override the default
 rate type setting you established for this hauler on the Hauler File
 Maintenance screen.

1. In the Properties
 section of the window, enter the special rates information for the selected
 hauler. Once you have completed this window, click
 OK.
