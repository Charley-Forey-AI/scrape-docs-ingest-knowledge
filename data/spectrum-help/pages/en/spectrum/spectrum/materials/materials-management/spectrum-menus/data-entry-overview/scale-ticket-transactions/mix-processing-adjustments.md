# Mix Processing Adjustments

Use this screen to adjust the actual quantities and/or costs of components used to create mix items through Scale Processing.
Standard mix information may be entered into this screen all the time so that if you change it, this mix is always used. If the mix is not changed, the standard mix is used (programming option). You can also preview the items and their components for company records. Once the tickets are updated, this special mix is cleared.
