# Freight Hours Entry - Reconcile Freight Hours

View the following steps on how to reconcile freight
 hours.

1. Click to highlight the truck you want to adjust.

1. Click the Truck button.

1. In the Rate type column,
 highlight the rate type corresponding to the ticket you want to edit. Adjust the rate
 types as needed.

1. Adjust the Freight rate,
 Hours, and Freight charge columns as
 needed. Click Continue once you have made the necessary
 adjustments.
