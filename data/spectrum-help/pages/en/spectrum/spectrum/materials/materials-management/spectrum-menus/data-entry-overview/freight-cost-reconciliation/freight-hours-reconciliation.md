# Freight Hours Reconciliation

Use the Freight Hours Reconciliation screen to reconcile
 freight costs to determine the difference between estimated and actual freight costs. The
 software will post any variances to the applicable jobs and General Ledger accounts when the
 Freight Cost Update is run.
Freight can be reconciled either by cost or by hours.
After tickets have been imported (or manually entered) using Scale Ticket Transactions, and the Update has been run, freight charges can be reconciled here.
