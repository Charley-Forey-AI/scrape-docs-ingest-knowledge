# Adding Multiple Item Codes To a Single Ticket

Use this procedure when adding multiple scale items to a
 single ticket.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Data Entry >  Scale Ticket
 Transactions.

1. Click the Add button.

1. Enter the ticket's information in the
 Ticket portion of the screen.

1. In the detail portion of the screen, enter each of the
 items line by line.
