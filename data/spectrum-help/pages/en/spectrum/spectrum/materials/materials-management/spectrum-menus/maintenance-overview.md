# Maintenance Overview

Materials Management streamlines the process of sales and job costing by directly importing weight tickets. It integrates with Inventory Control and Job Cost for materials requisitioned to jobs, Order Processing and Accounts Receivable for customer sales, and Accounts Payable for the creation of invoices.
