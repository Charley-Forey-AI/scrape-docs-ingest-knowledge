# Combining a Customer's Invoices and Purchase Orders Onto One Order Processing Invoice

You can learn how to combine a customer's invoice and
 purchase orders into one processing invoice.

1. In the Scale Installation screen, complete the following.

1. In the Update scale invoices to field, select the
 Order Processing option.

1. Set the dollar limit for the customer invoice generation higher than the
 expected invoice total or leave the field blank.

1. Complete the Materials Management Customer Material Contract Maintenance screen,
 with or without a customer job.Note: The Invoice dollar limit defaults from the Materials Management Installation
 screen, but can be modified for this specific customer; to modify the limit, set
 the dollar amount higher than the expected invoice amounts.

1. Complete the Scale Ticket Transactions screen.

1. The Type should be Customer sale.

1. The Customer should be the "customer contract"
 customer.

1. The Customerjob should be the same for each
 ticket.

1. The Salesperson code must be the same for each ticket.


1. The Salestaxcode must be the same for each
 ticket.

1. The COD checkbox must be same for each ticket.

1. The Purchaseordernumber must be entered, and it must
 be the same for each ticket.

1. When the Materials Management tickets are updated, Preview the Scale TicketJournal
 Report. After you review the report, the Scale Ticket Update screen displays. This
 update produces a report that lists the invoice number(s) that was created in Order
 Processing.

Once you complete these steps, several tickets
 for the same customer, customer job number, sales tax code, COD, purchase order number, and
 salesperson will have been combined into one Order Processing invoice.
