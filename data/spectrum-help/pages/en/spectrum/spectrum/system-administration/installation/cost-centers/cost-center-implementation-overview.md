# Cost Center Implementation Overview

Viewpoint has developed tools to help existing Spectrum users implement cost centers. In general, the cost center implementation process can be summed up in three phases: Plan, Test and Go Live.

## 1. Plan

This is the most critical of all phases when implementing cost centers!
A strong understanding of what cost centers can do along with the understanding of your organization is essential here. Important decisions are made at this time. Attention to detail is critical during these meetings because the information will lay the foundation for the implementation.
Depending on the size of the organization, our Consultant typically spends three to five days at your office planning and designing your setup.

## 2. Test

In this stage, a test company is set up for testing and reviewing our decisions. Historical information will be populated with cost centers for testing.
While there probably won't be significant changes to the way staff perform their work, there will be changes. During this time it is important to train staff on using cost centers, and depending on the situation, introduce them to the new Chart of Accounts.
The amount of time required for this phase will be developed during the planning phase with your Consultant.

## 3. Go Live

All production companies are first set to "Pending" status. This status allows cost center information to be set up without requiring the security rules to be in place.
Next the GL structure is finalized and historical transactions are populated with cost centers.
Going live with cost centers is relatively easy. By now the planning and design has been reviewed and tested. At this time, the cost center functionality is enabled and you are live.
