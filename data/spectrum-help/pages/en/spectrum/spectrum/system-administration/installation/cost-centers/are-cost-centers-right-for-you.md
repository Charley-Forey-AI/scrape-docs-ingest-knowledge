# Are Cost Centers Right for You?

Your organization should consider implementing cost centers
 when the following statements are true:

- Your organization needs additional security.

- You want to streamline your Chart of Accounts.

- You would like the "auto default job division to G/L department"
 feature to be available throughout Spectrum.

- Your organization is growing and its financial reporting
 requirements change rapidly.

- You need the ability to create financial information combining
 multiple cost centers.

- You need the ability to split the costs and revenue for one job to
 multiple cost centers.
