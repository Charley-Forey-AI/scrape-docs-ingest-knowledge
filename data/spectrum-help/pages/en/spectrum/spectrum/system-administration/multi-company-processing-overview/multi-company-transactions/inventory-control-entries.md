# Inventory Control Entries

Multi-company inventory transactions may be recorded in one
 company to charge jobs in other companies for the use of Inventory owned by the first company.
 Spectrum allows the user the flexibility to transfer inventory from one company and charge it
 directly to expense for a job set up in another company.
