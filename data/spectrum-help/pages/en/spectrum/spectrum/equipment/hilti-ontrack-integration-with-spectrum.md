# Hilti ON!Track Integration with
 Spectrum

The Hilti ON!Track
 integration with Spectrum streamlines and
 automates the equipment management process, allowing for precise tool tracking and charging
 tool use and costs to a job.

## Integration Requirements

You must meet the following requirements in order to use this integration
 with Hilti ON!Track:

- Trimble Construction One
 subscription to Spectrum

- Spectrum version 2023 R1 or higher

- Enterprise ID for your company on the Trimble Platform

- Access to Hilti ON!Track Pro Plus

You must also set up the following in Spectrum:

- Equipment Control and Equipment Tracking modules

- Unique Equipment Type that identifies Hilti ON!Track assets

## Integration Overview

The Hilti ON!Track integration with
 Spectrum helps you better
 manage your smaller assets and tools, providing precise equipment tracking that
 leads to more accurate job billing.
This integration unites the tool tracking capabilities of Hilti ON!Track with the equipment management and job
 billing features of Spectrum.
 Tool / equipment data properly set up in Spectrum flows into Hilti ON!Track, where all tool movements are tracked and recorded.
 This data then flows back into Spectrum in the form of Hilti Equipment Requisitions. You can
 review these requisition entries and create transactions that will charge equipment
 usage to the job.

## Set Up
 Spectrum

In addition to having the Spectrum Equipment Control and Equipment Tracking modules
 configured, you also need to create equipment records with a Hilti-specific
 equipment type.
To get started, see [Set Up the Hilti ON!Track Integration in Spectrum](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/hilti-ontrack-integration-with-spectrum/set-up-the-hilti-ontrack-integration-in-spectrum).
