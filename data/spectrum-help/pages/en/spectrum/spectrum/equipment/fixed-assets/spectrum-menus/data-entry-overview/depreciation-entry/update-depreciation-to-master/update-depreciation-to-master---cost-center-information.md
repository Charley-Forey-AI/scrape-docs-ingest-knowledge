# Update Depreciation to Master - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, the cost center assigned to the fixed asset is recorded for both the depreciation expense (debit) and accumulated depreciation (credit) entries during the update to General Ledger. If the fixed asset cost center is blank, then the department file cost center is used. The cost center associated with each General Ledger entry is shown on the Fixed Asset G/L Summary Report.
During the update to Equipment Control, the cost center assigned to the piece of equipment is recorded in the equipment control depreciation file. If the equipment cost center is blank, the fixed asset cost center is used. If the fixed asset cost center is also blank, then the department cost center is used.
