# Meter Replacement

Question: When a meter is replaced on a piece of equipment
 due to damage or other causes, how do I keep the proper amount of hours/miles being
 accumulated in Preventive Maintenance?

Answer: Use the Replace Meter window accessible
 from the Equipment Control > Data Entry > Meter Transaction Entry screen to track equipment usage without interruption when a meter needs to
 be replaced. The window allows you to record information for the new (replacement) meter
 and the old (broken) meter separately.
