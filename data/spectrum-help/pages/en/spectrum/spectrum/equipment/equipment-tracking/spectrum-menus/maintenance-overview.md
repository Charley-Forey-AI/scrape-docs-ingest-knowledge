# Maintenance Overview

Equipment Tracking is used to charge company equipment, small tools and inventory to jobs. A single, easy-to-use screen allows shop personnel to track all movements of equipment (for example., issues, returns). This module ensures that the equipment never gets "lost." This module integrates with Equipment Control, Job Cost, Inventory Control and the General Ledger.
