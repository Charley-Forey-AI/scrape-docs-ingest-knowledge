# How do I charge inventory to a work order?

Use the Inventory Adjustments screen (type Equipment) to charge inventory to a work order.
Why doesn't anything happen when I click the Item Detail button on the Parts Used tab of the work order?

1. Navigate to Inventory Control > Data Entry > Adjustments.

1. In the Adjustments type field, select Equipment.

1. Enter the transaction date and equipment work order.

1. Enter the inventory item and quantity desired. Remember to use a G/L code that is set up for direct equipment costing.
