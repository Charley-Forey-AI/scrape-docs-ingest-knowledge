# P.M. Schedule Report - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise
 Installation screen and cost centers are set to 'Yes' in the current company, the Cost group field displays in the Selections
 section to specify cost centers to include on the report.
Spectrum will compare the cost center assigned to the equipment with
 the operator's cost center security scheme, and if the equipment code's cost center is
 not present, the transaction record will be skipped.
