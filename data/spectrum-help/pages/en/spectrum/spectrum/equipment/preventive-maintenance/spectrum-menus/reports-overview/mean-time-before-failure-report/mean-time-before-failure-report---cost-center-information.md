# Mean Time Before Failure Report - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise
 Installation screen, the Cost Group
 field will appear in the Selections section of this screen.
This field will accept entry of a valid cost group or cost center code. Spectrum will
 verify that the Operator has permission to access that cost center before proceeding.
 The software compares the cost center assigned to the equipment with the cost centers in
 the Operator's assigned scheme, and if the cost center is not included then that piece
 of equipment is not shown on the report.
In addition, Spectrum filters which equipment is included on the report using the operator's cost center security scheme.
