# Equipment P.M. Service History - Field Descriptions

Use the table below when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Buttons

Selections
In the Advanced Selections window, you
 can search for the task code, from P.M. date and to P.M. date (which will
 default to the current Preventive Maintenance processing date).
Service Notes
Click to view and edit notes in the
 [P.M. Service Performed Notes](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/preventive-maintenance/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/batch-p.m.-service-entry/p.m.-service-performed-notes) window. Click OK in the window to save
 current service history notes.
List box

P.M. date
The date when the equipment service was
 performed displays.
Task
The task identification number and
 description display.
Meter #1 reading
The corresponding meter reading and
 unit of measure display.
Personnel
The name of the person who performed
 the service displays.
Summary comment
Service notes recorded in Batch P.M.
 Service Entry or Equipment Work Order Entry display here.
