# P.M. Service - Parts Used

Use this window to enter parts that regularly need replacing in equipment, including information about quantity, unit of measure, warehouse, and cost category.
Parts used may only be entered for a completed task.
You can access this window from [P.M. Schedule](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/preventive-maintenance/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/p.m.-schedule) and [Batch P.M. Service Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/preventive-maintenance/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/batch-p.m.-service-entry).
Note: The [New, Edit, View, and Delete buttons](/en/spectrum/spectrum/materials/order-processing/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/customer-ship-to-addresses/customer-ship-to-addresses---field-descriptions/new-edit-view-and-delete-buttons)
 buttons will not display if you do not have security authorization.
