# Are work orders in Preventive Maintenance and Work Order modules
 the same?

No. Work orders in Preventive Maintenance are used to track
 the cost of repairs to company-owned equipment.
By contrast, in the Work Order module, this term is used to refer to the
 service work done for a customer.
