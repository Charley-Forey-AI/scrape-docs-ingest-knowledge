# View Upcoming Maintenance for Equipment Already in the Repair Shop

If you have a piece of equipment that is in the repair
 shop, it may make sense to review the equipment's upcoming maintenance needs and take care of
 them now instead of at a later date when it may be needed on a job.
Use the P.M. Schedule Report to
 determine what work is almost due for each piece of equipment.

- On the Site
 Map, click Preventive Maintenance  >  Reports >  P.M. Schedule.

- In the Report type
 section, select Exceptions
 only.

- In the Minimum
 percent due field, enter a number based on how far in advance you want
 the work to be done. For example, enter 100 to view work that is due immediately enter 50 or 75 to view work that is half or three
 quarters of the way to being due.

- Click Preview.
