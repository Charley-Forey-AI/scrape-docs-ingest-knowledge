# Equipment Work Order Entry - Parts Used

This tab displays inventory history details for equipment
 type entries and A/P invoice history containing item codes from purchase orders associated
 with the work order.
Items entered in this tab are validated against Inventory Control items, and update to
 Inventory Control and General Ledger.
