# Maintenance Overview

The Maintenance menu is used during conversion to define
 tasks that must be performed for preventive maintenance, to enter inventory items that will be
 used during maintenance, and to schedule maintenance tasks.
After initial installation, the options from this menu can be used in any order to update
 the information as necessary.
