# Mean Time Before Failure Report

The Mean Time Before Failure Report is a management report
 that shows the expected length of time until the failure of a piece of equipment.
This is a statistical value that is meant to represent the mean over a
 long period of time and large number of units. This value will be calculated based on the
 'order' dates of successive work orders.
