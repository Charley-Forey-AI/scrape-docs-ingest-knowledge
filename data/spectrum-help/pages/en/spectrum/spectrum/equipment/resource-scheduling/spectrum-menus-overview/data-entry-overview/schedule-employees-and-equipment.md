# Schedule Employees and Equipment

Use this screen to schedule employees and piece of
 equipment to jobs.
The Schedule Employees and Equipment screen is protected by company-based security and only
 those operators with the required security settings can access this screen's
 information.
Click the New button to add a resource assignment, or you can quickly assign resources from the listboxes on the main screen. The Resources listbox will display a complete list of unassigned resources, and the Jobs listbox will display a complete list of active jobs. Click the Details button to see all the resources assigned to a selected job. From this view you can edit the assignment, or set up a new job crew.
