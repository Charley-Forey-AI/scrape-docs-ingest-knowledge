# Maintenance Overview

On the Site Map, click the Resource Scheduling button, and
 then click the Maintenance tab if it is not already indicated.
Use the Maintenance menu selections to add and maintain crews, movers and scheduling status
 codes in Spectrum.

- [Crew Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/accounting/payroll/maintenance-overview/crew-maintenance)
