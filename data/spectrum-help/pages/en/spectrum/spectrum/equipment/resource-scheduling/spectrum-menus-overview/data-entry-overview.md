- [Employee Availability](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/resource-scheduling/spectrum-menus-overview/data-entry-overview/employee-availability)

- [Schedule Employees and Equipment](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/resource-scheduling/spectrum-menus-overview/data-entry-overview/schedule-employees-and-equipment)

- [Schedule Equipment Moves](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/resource-scheduling/spectrum-menus-overview/data-entry-overview/schedule-equipment-moves)
