# Schedule Employees and Equipment - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise
 Installation screen, then only jobs which the operator has cost center security for (using the
 cost center scheme 'Job' override) will be visible in the selection window or considered valid
 entries.
The operator will only be able to see employees and equipment on the left side of the
 screen if they have cost center security (using the scheme override) for that resource.
 When zoomed in on the right side, all assigned resources will be visible regardless of
 cost center security.
