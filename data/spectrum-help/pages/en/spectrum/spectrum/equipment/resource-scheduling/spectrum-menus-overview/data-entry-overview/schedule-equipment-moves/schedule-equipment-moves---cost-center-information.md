# Schedule Equipment Moves - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise
 Installation screen, equipment codes will not display on this screen unless you have security
 for the piece of equipment's cost center.
Likewise, you can only add new pieces of equipment if you have security to do so.
