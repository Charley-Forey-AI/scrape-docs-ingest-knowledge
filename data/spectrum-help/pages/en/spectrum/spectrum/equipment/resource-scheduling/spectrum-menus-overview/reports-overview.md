# Reports Overview

On the Site Map, click the Resource Scheduling button, and
 then click the Reports tab if it is not already indicated.
Use the Reports menu selections to preview and print information about resource
 scheduling.

- [Equipment Status Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/resource-scheduling/spectrum-menus-overview/reports-overview/equipment-status-report)

- [Move Schedule Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/resource-scheduling/spectrum-menus-overview/reports-overview/move-schedule-report)

- [Work Schedule Report](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/resource-scheduling/spectrum-menus-overview/reports-overview/work-schedule-report)
