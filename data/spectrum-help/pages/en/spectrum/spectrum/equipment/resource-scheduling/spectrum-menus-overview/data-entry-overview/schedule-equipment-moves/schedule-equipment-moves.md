# Schedule Equipment Moves

Follow these steps to schedule equipment moves using the Build feature on the Schedule Equipment Moves screen.

- Navigate to Resource Scheduling  >  Data Entry  >  Schedule Equipment Moves.

- Click the Build
 button. The Build Unassigned
 Equipment Moves window displays.

- Complete the fields in the Selections section as needed for the
 move and then click Continue.

- Select one or more items from the Unassigned moves listbox on the
 left and a mover from the Movers listbox on the right.

- Specify a start date and time.

- Click the Assign
 button.

- When all moves are assigned, click the Report button.

- Preview the listing to confirm the moves.
