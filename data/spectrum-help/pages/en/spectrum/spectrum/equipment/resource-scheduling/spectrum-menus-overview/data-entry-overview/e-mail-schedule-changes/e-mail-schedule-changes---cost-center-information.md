# E-mail Schedule Changes - Cost Center Information

If the cost center feature is enabled in the Enterprise
 Installation screen, then cost center security is applied to jobs (using job override).
 Scheduled employees will be shown if the operator has cost center security for that job.
Movers will be shown based on the cost center of the employee or vendor.
