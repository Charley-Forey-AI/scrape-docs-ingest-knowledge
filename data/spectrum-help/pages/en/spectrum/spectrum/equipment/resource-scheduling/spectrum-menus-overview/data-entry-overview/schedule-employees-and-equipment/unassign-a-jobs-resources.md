# Unassign a Job's Resources

Follow these steps to unassign all of a job's resources
 (employee and equipment) for the selected date or date range.

- Navigate to Resource Scheduling  >  Data Entry  >  Schedule Employees and
 Equipment.

- On the Jobs listbox, select the desired job you wish to change. Click the
 Details button to view all
 resources assigned to the job.

- Specify a date range for the selected job.

- Click the Unassign button to remove all resources from the job for the specified
 date range.
