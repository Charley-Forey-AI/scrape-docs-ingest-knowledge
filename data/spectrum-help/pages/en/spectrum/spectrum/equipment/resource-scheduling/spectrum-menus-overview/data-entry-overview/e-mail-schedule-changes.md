# E-mail Schedule Changes

Use this screen to automate the process of contacting your
 contact employees or vendors in the event of scheduling changes or regarding equipment moves.
This screen is protected by company-based security and only those operators with the
 required security settings can access this screen's information.
Once the selections have been entered, the Send Schedule Change Emails screen displays a list of the selected items. This list will be built based on the email status and other selections, creating one record for each new job in the employee schedule file, one record for each crew leader, and one record for each mover code.

- The e-mail subject for the mover e-mail is "Move schedule for" and
 it includes the job address for any "from" or "to" jobs used in the move record.

- The e-mail subject for crew leaders e-mail is "Crew assignment for"
 and includes the job address and the list of the crew employees assigned to the job.

- After you have edited the e-mail message, you must send it as it
 will be assumed the e-mail has been sent and the record will be deleted.
