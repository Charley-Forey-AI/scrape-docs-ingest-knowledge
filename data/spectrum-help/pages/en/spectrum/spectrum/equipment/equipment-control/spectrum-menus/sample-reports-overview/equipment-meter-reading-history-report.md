# Equipment Meter Reading History Report

The log operator listed on this report is the operator who performed the update, not necessarily the person who originally entered the meter reading, and the corresponding date and time stamps are recorded based on the server's system settings.
Information is sorted by equipment code, then by meter number, and then by transaction date.
