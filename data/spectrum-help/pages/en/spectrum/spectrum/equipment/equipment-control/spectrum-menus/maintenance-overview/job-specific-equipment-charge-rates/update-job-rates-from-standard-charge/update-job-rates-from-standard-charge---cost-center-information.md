# Update Job Rates from Standard Charge - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, only the equipment codes
 the operator is allowed to access are included in the update.
The equipment's assigned cost center is compared with cost centers in the operator's
 assigned cost center scheme, and if the equipment's cost center is not present, then
 that equipment code is not accessible.
