# Renew Equipment License

Once the Create renewal section of the screen has been completed and you click
 OK, a new compliance record will
 be added and the original compliance record will automatically be flagged as Closed, and the closed date will be set to
 the current Equipment Control processing date.
In addition, the Authorized field
 will be set to your operator name and the Expiration type will be set to Renewal.

- The exact window title will vary based on the type of compliance
 record.

- No changes are allowed in the header or Copy from sections of this screen; the
 information displayed here defaults from the selected compliance record.
