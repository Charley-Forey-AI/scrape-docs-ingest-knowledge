# Equipment Attachments - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, then
 when the operator adds a new attachment or clicks to display the Edit Attachment window, the software
 determines whether the operator has authorization for the cost center associated with the
 attached equipment, based on the same cost center validations performed in the
Equipment screen for accessing equipment. No operator scheme overrides apply in this
 screen, and the attached equipment will generate a transaction regardless of the cost
 center security authorization. In this way, all authorized attachments (entered in this
 screen) will produce transactions.
