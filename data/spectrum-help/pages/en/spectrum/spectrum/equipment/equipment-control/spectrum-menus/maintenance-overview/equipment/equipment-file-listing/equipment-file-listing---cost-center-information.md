# Equipment File Listing - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, when
 running the Equipment File Listing, Spectrum includes the piece of equipment on the listing
 only if your operator has permission to access the equipment.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the equipment with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then
 that piece of equipment is not shown on the listing.
