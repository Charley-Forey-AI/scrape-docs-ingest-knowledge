# Equipment Chargeable Report

The Equipment Chargeable Report screen is used to obtain a
 report of equipment chargeables.
The chargeable percentage that is calculated on this report describes how many hours each
 piece of equipment is actually charged versus the total hours actually incurred on the
 hourly meter.
Equipment chargeability is only as good as the employee's ability to charge or report the time to management. Equipment, unlike employees, doesn't get a paycheck, so the only control management has on the chargeability of equipment is to compare the usage hours reported versus the actual hours on the hour meter. This comparison shows the percent that equipment is charged to a job or department versus actual hours run on the meter. This report is best used if the meter readings are taken at set intervals (for example, weekly or monthly) and is run within a "from year and period" and a "to year and period" time frame.
