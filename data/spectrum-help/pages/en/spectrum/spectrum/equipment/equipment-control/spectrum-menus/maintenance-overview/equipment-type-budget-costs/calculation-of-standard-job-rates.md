# Calculation of Standard Job Rates

For each qualifying equipment code, conditionally hourly standard rental rates are stored.
Full charge hourly rate = the sum of the 'Operating hourly rate' and the 'Stand-by hourly rate' (detailed just below)
Operating hourly rate = hourly job operating rate assigned to this equipment x (1 + rental markup/100)
Stand-by hourly rate = hourly job stand-by rate assigned to this equipment x (1 + rental markup/100)
Example:
If the new hourly job rate is $25 and the markup is set to 84%, the new
 rental rate is as follows:
$25 x (1 + 84/100) = marked-up rental rate
$25 x 1.84 = marked-up rental rate
$46 = marked-up rental rate
