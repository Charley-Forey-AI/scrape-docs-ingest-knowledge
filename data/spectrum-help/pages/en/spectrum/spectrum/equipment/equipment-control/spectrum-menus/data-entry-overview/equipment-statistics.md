# Equipment Statistics

Use this screen to record statistical readings and tests
 relevant to each piece of equipment.
Examples of statistics you may want to track include: oil sample tests, tire pressure,
 compression test results, and tire wear. You can also construct a list of standard
 statistics by equipment type and then conveniently select from the type-specific statistics
 list when adding equipment statistics.
The statistics list shown on this screen sorts primarily by examination date and secondarily by statistical code.
