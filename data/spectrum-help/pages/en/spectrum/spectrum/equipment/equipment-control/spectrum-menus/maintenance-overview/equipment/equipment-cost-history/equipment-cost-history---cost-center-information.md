# Equipment Cost History - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, when
 requesting a piece of equipment in Equipment Cost History, Spectrum displays the equipment
 only if you have permission to access the equipment.
As you request each piece of equipment, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to
 the equipment with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if
 the cost center is not included, then inquiry on that equipment will not be allowed.
In addition, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to each cost category, if any, with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then that cost category's activity is not shown.
All cost details will appear for authorized equipment, regardless of cost center setup for the individual transaction or vendor/employee reference.
