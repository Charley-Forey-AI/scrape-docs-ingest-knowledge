# Purge Equipment Revenue History - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, when
 purging equipment revenue history, Spectrum removes history only if you have permission to
 access the piece of equipment.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the equipment with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then the
 equipment history will not be purged.
