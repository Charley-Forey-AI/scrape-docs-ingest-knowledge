# Equipment Financial Summary - Field Descriptions

Use this table for reference when completing the fields on
 this screen.
Fields
Descriptions

Ownership info

Purchase date
The purchase date for this piece of equipment
 displays.

Purchase cost
The purchase cost for this piece of equipment
 displays.

Current market value
The current value of the equipment displays. The value
 entered here cannot exceed the Original cost value.

Equipment status
The current equipment status displays in this field.

Last job
The job number where this equipment was most recently
 used displays.

Year-to-date

Service/rental revenue Job revenue
[Total
 revenue ](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-revenue-history)
[Total
 cost](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-cost-history)
Year-to-date revenue for this equipment displays. Revenue
 is broken down by service/rental revenue and job revenue; totals are also
 displayed.

- Click the revenue hyperlink to display the
 Equipment Revenue History screen where you can see all revenue records
 from the beginning of the current .

- Click the cost hyperlink to display the Equipment
 Cost History screen where you can see all cost records from the
 beginning of the current .

Net profit
The year-to-date profit for this piece of equipment
 displays. The profit is computed as the total revenue less cost.

Life-to-date

Service/rental revenue Job revenue
[Total
 revenue](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-revenue-history)
[Total cost](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-cost-history)
Life-to-date revenue for this equipment displays. Revenue
 is broken down by service/rental revenue and job revenue; totals are also
 displayed. Click on the revenue and cost links to display all revenue and
 cost history, instead of just the current year.

Net profit
The life-to-date profit for this piece of equipment
 displays. The profit is computed as the total revenue less cost.

Charge rates

Standard/service rental rate Standard job rate
The equipment charge rates will display in these
 fields.
Select the [Charge rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-charge-rates) link to add or change the job
 rates for a specific piece of equipment.
