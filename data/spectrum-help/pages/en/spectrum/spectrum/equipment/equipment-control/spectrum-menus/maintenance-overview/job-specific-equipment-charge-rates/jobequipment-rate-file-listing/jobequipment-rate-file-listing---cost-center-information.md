# Job/Equipment Rate File Listing - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, when
 running the Job/Equipment Rate File Listing, Spectrum includes the job on the listing only if
 you have permission to access the job.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost
 centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not
 included, then that job is not shown on the listing. In addition, Spectrum compares the
 cost center assigned to the piece of equipment with cost centers in your operator's
 assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then that equipment
 is not shown on the listing.
