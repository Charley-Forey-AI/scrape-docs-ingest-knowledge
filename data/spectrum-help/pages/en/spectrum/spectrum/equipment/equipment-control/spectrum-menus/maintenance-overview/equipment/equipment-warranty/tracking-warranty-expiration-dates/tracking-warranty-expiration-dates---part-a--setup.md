# Tracking Warranty Expiration Dates - Part A – Setup

Use this procedure to enter equipment warranty
 information.

1. On the Site Map screen, click
 Equipment Control > Maintenance > Equipment.

1. In the Code field, enter the
 equipment code, or press F4 or double-click on this field to
 select from a list of available equipment codes.

1. Click the More Info button.

1. In the More Info window, click the
 Warranty button.

1. Complete the Expiration date,
 Expiration meter, and Description
 fields.

1. Repeat step 5 for all remaining components, if
 applicable.

1. Click OK until you return to the Site Map
 screen.
