# Ownership Cost Entry - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, then
 when entering or reviewing depreciation, license and insurance costs in Ownership Costs,
 Spectrum displays the equipment only if you have permission to access the
 equipment.
The system compares the cost center assigned to the equipment with
 cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is
 not included, then that equipment is not shown. When adding a new transaction line,
 Spectrum verifies that your operator is authorized to access that equipment before
 proceeding.
When your operator's cost center scheme includes override settings
 for equipment entries in Cost Center Scheme Maintenance, this screen validates the cost
 center assigned to transaction detail lines based on these overrides. The override cost
 center(s) supersede the cost center list defined for the scheme in general. Spectrum
 compares the cost center assigned in the entry screen detail with equipment override
 cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is
 not included, then the transaction entry line will not be allowed.
Expand mode is available for this paging screen. These lines display
 the debit cost center below the debit G/L account, and this information is for lookup
 only. The credit cost center will display below the credit G/L account; it will default
 from the equipment cost center but may be changed (the cost center must be a valid entry
 for your operator).
When you do not have authorization to access the credit cost center
 assigned to a DLI transaction of an authorized piece of equipment, then the entry
 appears on the screen but no changes are allowed.
