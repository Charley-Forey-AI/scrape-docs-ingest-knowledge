# Understanding How Meter Readings are Updated

Updating a meter reading can happen in one of the following three ways.
Note: If the meter readings aren't regularly updated, then the
 Preventive Maintenance module is unable to perform as designed, unless tasks are based on
 specific dates or days of use instead of meter readings.

- Current equipment meter rates can be recorded in Equipment Control  >  Data Entry >  Equipment Meter Reading.

- If the equipment's meter type is set to Usage (hours) on the Equipment Control  >  Equipment screen, when you updated Payroll and if usage hours are recorded
 through the Payroll module, then Spectrum automatically updates the meter readings.

- If the selected meter reading entered in the Preventive Maintenance module
 exceeds the meter reading supplied to Preventive Maintenance by the Payroll or
 Equipment Control modules, then Spectrum uses the Preventive Maintenance meter
 reading.

- The Last meter reading and Last meter date in the Equipment Master Table will reflect the latest meter activity of the Primary meter only.
