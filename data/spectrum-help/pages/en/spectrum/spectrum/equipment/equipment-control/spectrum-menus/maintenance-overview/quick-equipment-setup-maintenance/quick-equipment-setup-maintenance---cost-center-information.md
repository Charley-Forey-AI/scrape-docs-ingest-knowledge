# Quick Equipment Setup Maintenance - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, then
 when adding or editing a Quick Equipment Setup Maintenance code, Spectrum permits your
 operator to assign a Copy from equipment only if you have permission to access the piece of
 equipment.
The system compares the cost center assigned to the equipment with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then
 that equipment is cannot be assigned as the source equipment for Quick Equipment Setup
 Maintenance.
