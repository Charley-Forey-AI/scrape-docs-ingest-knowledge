# Transfer Component to Other Equipment

Use this screen to move a component from one piece of
 equipment to another piece of equipment.
This update also moves certain maintenance history records, including statistical and
 warranty details pertaining to the component. For example, if an engine was removed from
 one piece of equipment and overhauled and then reinstalled in another vehicle, you would
 use this screen to make sure the maintenance and warranty records stayed with the engine
 when it was reinstalled.
Note: Inactive components may be transferred, but Retired
 components may not be transferred.
