# Update Charge Rates from Budget Cost

Use this screen allows to store the hourly budget values from the new Budget Cost maintenance screen into equipment codes assigned to the particular equipment type and weight class.
Complete the Equipment type selection field and click Continue. This update writes hourly rates to the Equipment Master Table.
