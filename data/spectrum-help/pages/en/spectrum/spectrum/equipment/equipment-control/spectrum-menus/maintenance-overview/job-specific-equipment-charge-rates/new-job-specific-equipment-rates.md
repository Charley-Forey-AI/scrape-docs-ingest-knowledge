# New Job Specific Equipment Rates

Use this window to enter new or special equipment rates or
 make changes to job usage rates for a specified equipment code.
This window is accessible from the [Job-Specific Equipment Charge Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/job-specific-equipment-charge-rates) screen and the [Equipment Charge Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/equipment-charge-rates) screen.
Fields
Descriptions

Equipment code
The selected equipment code defaults.

Job
Enter a valid job number. The job should been previously set up the Jobs screen. If the job number entered is for another Spectrum company, the job name will not display. Rates entered on this screen for this job/equipment combination will default into Payroll Time Card Entry, and will be used instead of the standard rates set up in the Equipment. Press F4 to select from a list of valid job numbers.

Rates

Hourly
Daily
Weekly
Monthly
Rates of up to 99,999.99 per period may be entered for any of the standard job fields below. Enter the actual total daily, weekly, and monthly charges here so that your recurring rates are charged correctly.

- Full Charge Enter
 the full-charge equipment rate to be used for this job. The rate
 specified here will default in Payroll > Time Card Entry and in Equipment Control > Transaction Entry when the (H)ourly pay rate is designated.

- Operating Enter the
 operating equipment rate per hour/day/week/month to be used for this
 job. The rate specified here will default in Payroll > Time Card Entry and in Equipment Control > Transaction Entry when the (H)ourly pay rate is designated.

- Stand-by Enter the equipment job standby rate per hour/day/week/month for this job. For example, Hourly = 50.00 Daily = 300.00 Weekly = 1200.00 Monthly= 4,000.00
