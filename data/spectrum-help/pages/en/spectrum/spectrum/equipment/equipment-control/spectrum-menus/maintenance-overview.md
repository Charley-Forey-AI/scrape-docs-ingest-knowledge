# Maintenance Overview

The Maintenance menu is used during conversion to enter
 each piece of equipment.
It is also used to specify equipment types, equipment cost categories, and the various
 kinds of fuel and oil used.
After initial installation, the options from this menu can be used
 whenever new equipment is acquired and to update existing equipment information as
 necessary.
