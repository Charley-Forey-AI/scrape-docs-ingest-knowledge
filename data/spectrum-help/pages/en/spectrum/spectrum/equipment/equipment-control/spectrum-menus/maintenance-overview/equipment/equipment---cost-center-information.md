# Equipment - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, the
 Equipment screen includes a Cost center field.
This field associates the equipment with a particular cost center.
When adding a new piece of equipment, the Cost center field must be completed, and the
 entry must be a valid cost center for your operator. When editing, viewing, or deleting
 a piece of equipment, Spectrum compares the equipment's assigned cost center with cost
 centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the equipment's cost
 center is not present, then that equipment code is not accessible.
The New / Edit windows validate whether the operator has security authorization to enter a job or employee code, but disallowed jobs and employees will appear in this screen if already assigned. To determine whether the operator may add a job or an employee code, the software compares cost center assigned to the job or employee) with cost centers in the operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not present, then that job or employee code will not be allowed.
