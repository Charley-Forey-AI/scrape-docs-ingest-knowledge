# Equipment Revenue History - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, when
 requesting a piece of equipment in Equipment Revenue History, Spectrum displays the equipment
 only if you have permission to access the equipment.
As you request each piece of equipment, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to
 the equipment with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if
 the cost center is not included, then inquiry on that equipment will not be allowed.
All revenue details will appear for authorized equipment, regardless of cost center setup for the individual transaction or job reference.
