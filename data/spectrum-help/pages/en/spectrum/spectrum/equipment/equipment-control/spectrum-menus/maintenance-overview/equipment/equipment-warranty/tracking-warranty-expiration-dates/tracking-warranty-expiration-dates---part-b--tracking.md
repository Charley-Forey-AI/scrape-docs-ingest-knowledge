# Tracking Warranty Expiration Dates - Part B – Tracking

Follow the steps below when tracking warranty expiration
 dates.

1. On the Site Map screen, click Equipment Control  >  Reports Warranty Expiration.

1. Complete the Equipment code, Equipment
 type, From expiration date, and To expiration
 date fields.Note: You can enter a future date in
 the To expiration date field to see which items have warranties that will
 expire soon.

1. Select the Miles, Hours,
 Kilometers, and Usage checkboxes as
 appropriate.

1. Click Preview to review the
 report.
