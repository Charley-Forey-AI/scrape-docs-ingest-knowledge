# Equipment Charge Rates - Field Descriptions

Use this table for reference when completing the fields on
 this screen.
Fields
Descriptions

Standard job and service cost rates

Hourly Daily Weekly Monthly
Rates of up to 99,999.99 per period may be entered for
 any of the standard job fields below. Enter the actual total daily, weekly,
 and monthly charges here so that your recurring rates are charged correctly.

- Full
 Charge: Enter the full-charge equipment rate to be used
 for this job. The rate specified here will default in Payroll > Time Card Entry and in Equipment ControlTransaction Entry when the (H)ourly pay rate is designated.

- Operating: Enter the operating equipment rate per
 hour/day/week/month to be used for this job. The rate specified here
 will default in Payroll > Time Card Entry and in Equipment Control > Transaction Entry when the (H)ourly pay rate is designated.

- Stand-by: Enter the equipment job standby rate per
 hour/day/week/month for this job. For example, Hourly = 50.00 Daily =
 300.00 Weekly = 1200.00 Monthly= 4,000.00

Standard rental and service bill rates

Hourly Daily Weekly Monthly
To add or change the equipment's Full charge, Operating,
 and Stand-by rates for different rental time spans, place the cursor in the
 applicable field and then type the new rate (up to $99,999.99 per period).
 Otherwise, press Enter to leave the rates unchanged.

New / Edit / Delete
Use these buttons to display the [New Job Specific Equipment Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/job-specific-equipment-charge-rates/new-job-specific-equipment-rates)
 and Edit Job/Equipment Rate windows where you can enter new or special
 equipment rates or make changes to job usage rates for a specified equipment
 code.
