# Equipment Financial Summary

The Equipment Financial Summary screen is used to determine
 both year-to-date and life-to-date costs and revenue from usage for a specific piece of
 equipment.
At the bottom of the screen the net profit, based on usage and cost transaction entries,
 displays.
