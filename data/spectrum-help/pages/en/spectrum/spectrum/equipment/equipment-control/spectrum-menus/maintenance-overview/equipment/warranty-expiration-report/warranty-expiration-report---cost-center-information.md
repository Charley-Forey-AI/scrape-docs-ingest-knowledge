# Warranty Expiration Report - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, when
 running the Warranty Expiration Report, Spectrum includes the equipment on the report only if
 you have permission to access the piece of equipment.
The system compares the cost center assigned to the equipment with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then
 that piece of equipment is not shown on the report.
The Cost group field on this screen will default to the 'Report default' of the operator's cost center scheme, if specified, and otherwise it will default to ALL.
