# Fuel Utilization Report

Use this report in conjunction with Spectrum's Equipment
 Service System to review information gathered in the field about fuel usage for equipment at
 job sites.
This report can also be used to review fuel consumption that has not been allocated to a
 particular piece of equipment or a job.
When printing or previewing this report, it is possible that the job associated with the fuel transaction may be set up in a different company. In this case, the job number will display on the report, but no job name will display.
