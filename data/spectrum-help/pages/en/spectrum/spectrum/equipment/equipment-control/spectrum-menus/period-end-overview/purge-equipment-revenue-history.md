# Purge Equipment Revenue History

The Purge Equipment Revenue History
 screen removes usage entries that are dated within the specified range.
Warning: Purges permanently delete transactions. Before
 you perform a purge, back up your data.

There are several parameters that may be specified to purge the exact transactions
 needed.
After the purge is completed it is not possible to look up this detailed
 revenue from usage information in the Equipment Revenue History
 screen. Likewise, it is also no longer possible to print the information on the Equipment
 Revenue History Report.
