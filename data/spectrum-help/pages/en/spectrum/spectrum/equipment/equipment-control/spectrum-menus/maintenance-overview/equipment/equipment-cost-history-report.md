# Equipment Cost History Report

The Equipment Cost History Report is a transaction record
 of all of the equipment costs incurred, sorted by piece of equipment.
It provides information such as which employees worked on repairs, what
 parts might have been purchased through accounts payable, and any Equipment Control
 transaction entries. The figures on this report provide the detail for information found on
 the Equipment Cost Report. (The Equipment Cost Report provides cost-to-date information
 through a specified period, but this report may be printed for any desired date range.)
 Remarks added during cost entry will be included on the report underneath the
 transaction.
