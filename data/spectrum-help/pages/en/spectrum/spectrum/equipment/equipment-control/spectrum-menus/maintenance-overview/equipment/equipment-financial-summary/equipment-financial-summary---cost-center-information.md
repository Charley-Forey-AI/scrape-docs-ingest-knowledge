# Equipment Financial Summary - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, when requesting a piece of equipment in Equipment Cost Inquiry, As you request each piece of equipment, Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the equipment with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then inquiry on that equipment will not be allowed.
Spectrum does not compare the cost center assigned to each cost category, if any, with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme to compute equipment cost and cost category totals. Instead, activity for all cost categories of authorized pieces of equipment are included in the equipment totals and the Cost History Transaction drill-down windows of the inquiry screen. Further drill-down below the cost category totals is not allowed if you do not have permission for the cost center assigned to the cost category.
