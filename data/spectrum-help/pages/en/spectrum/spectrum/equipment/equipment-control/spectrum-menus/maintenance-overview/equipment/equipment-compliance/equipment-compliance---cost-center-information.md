# Equipment Compliance - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, the
 Equipment Compliance screen will validate whether the operator has security authorization to
 access the piece of equipment and disallowed equipment will not appear in this screen.
To determine whether the operator may view (or add) compliance information for an
 equipment code, the software compares the equipment's assigned cost center with cost
 centers in the operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the equipment's cost
 center is not present, then that equipment code is not accessible. Operator scheme
 overrides are not applied. Cost center restrictions within the New/Edit windows will
 work the same as in the Equipment screen.
