# Tire Statistics Entry

Use this window to conveniently record information for all tires in a single event.

- Zero entries are allowed in this window in the Test/reading entry field (for
 example, the air pressure of a flat tire or the wear percentage for a new tire) are
 allowed.

- Click the Expand button to view any Comments entered in the New Equipment Tire window (no changes are allowed).

- Entry is not required for all available lines.
Note: Note Regarding Statistics for Individual Tires: As the tire statistics
 are entered and saved, any user-defined entries recorded in the New window will be
 stored with each of the new tire statistic records. If you need to enter
 tire-specific user-defined information (or other information, such as comments) that
 is not related to all tires, then you should return to the main screen and open the
 [New Equipment Statistic](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/data-entry-overview/equipment-statistics/new-equipment-statistic) window to
 enter the user-defined information for individual tires.
