# Tracking Warranty Expiration Dates

Use the Equipment screen to track warranties on various
 components of the piece of equipment.
For example, you may have a 3 year / 50,000 miles warranty on an asset's engine and a 5
 year / 75,000 miles warranty on its transmission.
