# Equipment Charge Rate Workbook

Viewpoint has teamed up with Grantlun Corporation to
 provide the Equipment Charge Rate Workbook utility.
Use this workbook to better manage equipment charge rates and fleet composition for up to
 2500 pieces of equipment. This utility will generate an Excel® spreadsheet that then
 populates the Grantlun Equipment Charge Rate Workbook with data from Spectrum. Records on
 this spreadsheet sort in left-justified equipment code order.
Complete the fields on this screen to specify the data that will be exported. Click theExport button to generate an Excel spreadsheet that can then be used to populate the Grantlun Workbook.
Note: This spreadsheet is formatted to print on Legal-sized paper.
 Please adjust your printer setup to accommodate this larger paper.
