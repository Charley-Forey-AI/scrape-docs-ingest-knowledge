# Open Forward Balance Update - Equipment

The Open Forward Balance Update screen resets the opening revenue and cost balances for a new fiscal year from the previous year for each piece of equipment.
This is a necessary update when moving from one year to the next, and may be run numerous times.

1. Select the fiscal year most recently completed.

1. Select Continue.

If doing year-end processing, proceed as directed from the [Year End Processing Screen (U.S.)](/en/spectrum/spectrum/year-end/year-end-for-u.s.-payrolls/processing/year-end-processing-screen-u.s.) or the [Year End Processing Screen (CAN)](/en/spectrum/spectrum/year-end/year-end-for-canada-payrolls/processing/year-end-processing-screen-can).
