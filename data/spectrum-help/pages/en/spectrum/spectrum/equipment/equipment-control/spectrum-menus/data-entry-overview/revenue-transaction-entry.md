# Revenue Transaction Entry

Use this screen when you are entering revenue transactions,
 including EU and ES transaction types.
Cost transactions should be entered using the [Cost Transaction Entry](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/equipment/cost-transaction-entry) screen.
After completing the Transaction date and Batch fields, click one of the available tabs (EU - Usage or ES- Stand-by).
