# Update Job Rates from Standard Charge - Example

Each value to be updated is calculated as
 follows.

-  Job-specific rate = <Standard rate> times <% from starting window> / 100

- The result is rounded to the nearest penny and saved.
Example:

Standard hourly rate = $25.25 and job rate is set to 90.00 percent of standard rate:
Job-specific rate = 25.25 x 90 / 100 = 22.725 and then rounded to 22.73
