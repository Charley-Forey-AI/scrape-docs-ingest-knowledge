# Update Job Rates from Standard Charge

This update allows you to generate job-specific equipment
 rates using a percentage of the standard equipment charge rate.
This will typically be used at the outset of the job, to create equipment cost estimates.
 It will also be useful for long-term (multi-year) jobs in order to adjust charge rates for
 the job, as costs change.
Decide which equipment to include, and you will be prompted for the percentage of the standard rate to assign to that particular job; for example, 75%, 90% or 125% of the standard equipment charge rate.
Security access to this feature is determined by the operator's ability to access the particular job in [Job-Specific Equipment Charge Rates](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/job-specific-equipment-charge-rates) and the operator's permission for Equipment File – [New Equipment](/en/spectrum/spectrum/equipment/equipment-control/spectrum-menus/maintenance-overview/quick-equipment-setup-maintenance/new-equipment).
