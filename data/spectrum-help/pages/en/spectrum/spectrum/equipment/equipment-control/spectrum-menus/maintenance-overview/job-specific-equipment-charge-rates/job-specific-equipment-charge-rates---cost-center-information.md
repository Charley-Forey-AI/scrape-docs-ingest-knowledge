# Job-Specific Equipment Charge Rates - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used in the current company, then
 the Job-Specific Equipment Charge Rates screen allows you to access a job only if your
 operator has permission for that job.
The system compares the cost center assigned to the job with cost centers in your
 operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then
 that job is not shown.
Spectrum also compares the cost center assigned to the piece of equipment with cost centers in your operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included, then that equipment is not accessible.
