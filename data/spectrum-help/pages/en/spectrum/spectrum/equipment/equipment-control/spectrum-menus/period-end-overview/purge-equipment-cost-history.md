# Purge Equipment Cost History

The Purge Equipment Cost History
 screen removes transaction entries that are dated within the specified range.
Warning: Purges permanently delete transactions. Before
 you perform a purge, back up your data.

There are several parameters that may be specified to purge only the exact transactions
 desired.
After the purge is complete, it is not possible to look up this detailed
 cost transaction information in the Equipment Cost History screen.
 Likewise, it is no longer possible to print the information on the Equipment Cost History
 Report.
