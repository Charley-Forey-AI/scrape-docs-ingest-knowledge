# Workflow

The Workflow module allows the Spectrum Administrator to define a series of steps to meet your organization's needs and objectives. The Workflow functionality will be fully integrated through the Spectrum info bar. Workflows will use the 'Status' field to prevent a record from being processed until the workflow has been completed.
The following workflows are currently available in Spectrum:

- Change Requests

- Draw Requests

- Journal Entry

- New Vendors

- New Customer

- New Jobs

- Project Log

- Proposed Purchase Orders

-  Time Card Approval
