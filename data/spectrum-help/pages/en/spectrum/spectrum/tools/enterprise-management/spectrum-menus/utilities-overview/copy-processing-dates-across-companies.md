# Copy Processing Dates Across Companies

Learn how to copy the processing dates across specified
 companies for certain modules. This feature allows you to close periods easily without having
 to change to each company and revise the minimum/maximum dates.

1. In the Copy from section, the current company defaults. The update will be based on the settings in this company.

1. Enter the Module abbreviation (for example: AR, AP, GL) you want to copy from, double-click in this field to display the Spectrum modules window and select.

1. In the Copy to section, enter the company you want to copy to. If you use the Search Companies window to select, only those companies that you have security access to will display.
