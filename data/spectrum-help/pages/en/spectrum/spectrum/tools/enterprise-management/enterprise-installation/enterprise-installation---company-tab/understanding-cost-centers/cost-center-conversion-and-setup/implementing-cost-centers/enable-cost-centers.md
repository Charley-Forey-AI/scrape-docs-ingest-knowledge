# Enable Cost Centers

Learn how to enable cost centers.

1. Navigate to the System Administration  >  Enterprise Management  >  Maintenance  >  Enterprise Installation screen.

1. Click the Company tab, highlight the appropriate company, and then click the Edit button.

1. Set the Use cost centers option to Pending.

1. Click OK and then Save and then log out of the company.

1. Log on to the company selected in step 2.
