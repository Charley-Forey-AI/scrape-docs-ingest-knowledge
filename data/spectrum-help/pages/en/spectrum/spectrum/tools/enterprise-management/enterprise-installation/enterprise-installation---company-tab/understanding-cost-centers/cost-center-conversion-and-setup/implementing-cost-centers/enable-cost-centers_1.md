# Enable Cost Centers

Learn how to enable cost centers.

1. Navigate to Enterprise Management  >  Maintenance  >  Enterprise Installation.

1. On the Company tab, select the appropriate company code.

1. Click the Cost Centers button and change the Use cost centers option to Yes.
