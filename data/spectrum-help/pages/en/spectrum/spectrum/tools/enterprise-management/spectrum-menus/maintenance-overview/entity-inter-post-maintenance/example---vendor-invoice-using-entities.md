# Example - Vendor Invoice Using Entities

In this example, the invoice header was assigned cost center 200 and G/L account 002000 and the line transactions were assigned to cost centers 202 (entity 802) and 203 (entity 803). The software only reclassifies the entries in the 'inter-post' account(s), and of those entries, only reclassifies entries that are assigned to cost centers in different entities.
