# Create a Full Access Cost Center Scheme

Learn how to create a full access cost center scheme.

1. Navigate to Enterprise Management  >  Maintenance  >  Cost Center Scheme.

1. To create a full access scheme, click New.

1. In the Scheme field, type a name for the new scheme.

1. Leave the Report default field blank and set the Cost Center selection option to ALL.

1. Click Save to save your changes.Apply Full Access Scheme to Your Operator Code

1. Navigate to Admin  >  Security  >  Operator Maintenance.

1. Select your operator and click the Edit Operator button.

1. On the Companies tab, select the company with Pending cost centers and click Edit Company.

1. In the Cost center scheme field, enter the full access cost center scheme created above. Click Saveto return to the Site Map .
