# Complete Cost Center Scheme Setup

Learn how to complete cost center scheme
 setups.

1. Navigate to Enterprise Management  >  Maintenance  >  Cost Center Schemes.

1. Click New and create additional cost center schemes.

1. Decide whether you want your scheme to include all cost centers, a list of cost centers, or a list of cost groups.
