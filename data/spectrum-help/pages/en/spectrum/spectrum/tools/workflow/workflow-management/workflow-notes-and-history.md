# Workflow Notes and History

Use this window to review and enter notes about the current workflow. Click the New Note button to add a 250-character note to this workflow. Once entered, notes may not be edited or deleted.
This window also includes a list of all steps in the process, including a date stamp with each person who performed the particular step. Workflow steps will be shown in oldest-to-newest order.
