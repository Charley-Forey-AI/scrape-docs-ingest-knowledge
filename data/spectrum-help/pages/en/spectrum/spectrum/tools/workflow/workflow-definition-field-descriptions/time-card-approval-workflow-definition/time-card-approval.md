# Time Card Approval

This Workflow screen can be used by the assigned reviewer
 to Approve or Reject or
 Edit sets of pre-time card records. This screen can also be used by
 Payroll administrative staff to review progress during the approval process and to handle
 'rejected' items.
This screen can be accessed from the My Time Cards to Review app (if
 setup on the Spectrum dashboard), or from the [Time Card Approval Workflow Overview](/en/spectrum/spectrum/tools/workflow/workflow-definition-field-descriptions/time-card-approval-workflow-definition/time-card-approval/time-card-approval-workflow-overview)
 window, or from the Payroll > Data Entry menu on the Site Map when the Workflow module is
 present and the Time Card Approval workflow is active.
