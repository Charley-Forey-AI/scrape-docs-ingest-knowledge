# Time Card Approval - Possible actions

Select a record and click:

- Approve - the record will then be ready for transfer to Time Card Entry. When all records are approved, the Workflow is set to 'Complete' and disappears.

- Reject - you'll be prompted to enter a reason and the record will be returned to the Workflow Group. (more info on next slide)

- Pre-Time Cards /
 Notes - here you can make changes, including adding, editing, and
 deleting time card lines. When adding or editing records, any related tax codes will
 automatically be attached. You can also review items moved to history and see the
 reason for any rejections.

- Refresh - this updates the data that displays after records have been approved or rejected.
