# Multi-Currency - Treatment of Unposted Transactions

Unposted costs are handled similar to open commitments in that they will be translated into the reporting currency at the current exchange rate.

## Presentation in Job Cost

Unposted costs in Job Cost will be translated into the reporting currency.
