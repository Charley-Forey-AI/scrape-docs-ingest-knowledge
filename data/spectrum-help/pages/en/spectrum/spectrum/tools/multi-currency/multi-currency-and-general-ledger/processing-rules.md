# Processing Rules

View the processing rules in the Multi-Currency and General
 Ledger.

- Transactions originating in G/L will continue to be in the reporting currency.

- Transactions posting to G/L from Accounts Payable and Cash Management will be recorded using the reporting currency, even if the source transaction is tracked in the other module in a different currency.

- Transactions posting to G/L from Accounts Receivable, Job Cost and Payroll will be recorded using the reporting currency, which is the currency of these modules as well.

- Each Spectrum company can have only one reporting currency.

- A <BLANK> currency code is deemed to be the reporting currency.

- Each Spectrum Company shall have one defined currency that will be used as the reporting currency. This shall be the currency used in Job Cost and General Ledger.

- Payroll and Human Resources shall be processed in the reporting currency of the company. Multi-company transactions shall post to Job Cost and General Ledger using the destination company’s reporting currency. Confidential Payroll shall use the reporting currency of its associated main payroll company.
