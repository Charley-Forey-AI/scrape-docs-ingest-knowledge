# Multi-Currency - Treatment of Foreign Currency Open Commitments

All open commitments will be translated into the reporting currency using the current exchange rate. Between the moment the commitment is created and when it is relieved, its value will float in the reporting currency until it is locked in with a posted vendor invoice.

## Presentation in Job Cost

Open commitments in Job Cost will be translated into the reporting currency.

## Open Commitments Window on Cost Activity

Selected screens allow the user to drill down into the source transaction. The Cost Activity screen is such an example. Clicking the Commitments button displays values in the reporting currency.

## Subcontract Presentation

The Open Commitments window displays all values in the reporting currency. Drilling into the supporting detail of the commitment will open the Subcontract Commitments window. By convention, the total in the upper right-hand corner is displayed in the reporting currency, where individual commitments will be displayed in the vendor currency.
For clarity, the currency code will be displayed on rows with a different currency.

## Purchase Order Presentation

The Open Commitments window displays all values in the reporting currency. Drilling into the supporting detail of the commitment will open the Purchase Order Commitments window. By convention, the total in the upper right-hand corner is displayed in the reporting currency, where individual commitments will be displayed in the vendor currency.

## Job Requisition Presentation

Note that Job Requisitions are already in the reporting currency. Multi-company job requisitions in a different currency is not currently supported.
