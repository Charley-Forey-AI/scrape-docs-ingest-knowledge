# Period Summary - Labor Summary

In the Labor Summary screen, employees assigned to the job are displayed with their total individual hours for the pay period selected. Tap on an employee's name to view a more detailed summary of hours separated by days.
If you find you need to change time that has been entered or add new timecards to the selected employee's name, you can tap the date on this screen. You will be immediately taken to the employee's timecard screen for the selected date, where you can add, remove, or change timecards. When you are finished, tap the Save icon in the top-right corner, and you will be returned to the Labor Summary screen automatically.
