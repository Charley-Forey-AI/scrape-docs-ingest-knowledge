# Period Summary - Equipment Summary

The Equipment Summary page works the same way as the Labor Summary. You can view all of your Equipment Only hours for the week organized by equipment, view an individual piece of equipment's daily hours, and add or remove timecards directly from the Equipment Summary screen.
