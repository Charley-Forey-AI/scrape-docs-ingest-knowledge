# Signature Entry

Once time has been entered and synced for all employees,
 select the Signature Entry link at
 the bottom of the Current Job screen.

1. Select the employee you wish to collect a signature for.

1. All the time entered for the current pay period will default on this screen, but the Pay Period can be changed as necessary.

1. Click ALL to select all the entered time, or manually select the days to include.

1. Selected days will be marked with a green check. Enter a Signature and click the Accept button.The foreman can select the next employee to request a signature from until they complete the approval process for all employees.
