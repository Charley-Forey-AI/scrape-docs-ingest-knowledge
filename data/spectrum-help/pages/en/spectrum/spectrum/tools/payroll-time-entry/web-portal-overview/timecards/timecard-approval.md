# Timecard Approval

The Timecard Approval page displays all entered time for pay period by employee. Specify a Payperiod, Job and/or Company and click the Query button, and employees that have timecard entries based on the query will display below.

- Disputed timecard entries will be listed in the Has Disputes column.

- Timecard entries highlighted in red have not been signed/approved by the employee.

Expand the employee to view details and signatures for each timecard entry, and any disputes and comments.
