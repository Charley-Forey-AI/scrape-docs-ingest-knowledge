# Enter Time - Equipment

To enter a timecard for a piece of equipment without labor
 attached to it, select the Equipment Time page from the home screen.
A list of all of the equipment assigned to your selected job displays,
 and the job name and date will be at the top of the screen. As with the Labor Time page,
 if you are entering timecards for a date other than the one displayed, you must change
 it here by tapping the plus sign icon.

On the equipment timecard page, you can adjust hours for an individual
 timecard by tapping the clock icon in the top-right of the screen. You will also have to
 select a Phase Code from the list by tapping the plus symbol. On Equipment Charge Labor
 Timecard, a work order field allows you to associate work order numbers with EC records.
As with the labor timecards, you can add or remove timecards at the
 upper-right corner of the screen. When you have entered all of your timecards for the
 selected date, tap the Save icon at the top right corner to save the information and you
 will be taken back to the Equipment Time page.
