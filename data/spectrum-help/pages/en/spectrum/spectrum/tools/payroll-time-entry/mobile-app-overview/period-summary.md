# Period Summary

The Period Summary page allows you to view a summary of
 labor and equipment timecards or employee add-ons over your company's set pay period.
You can select the period you wish to view by tapping the right and left arrows at the top
 of the screen. Once you have selected a period, you can then choose to view either a Labor
 or Equipment or Add-on summary by tapping one of those entries on the screen.
