# Timecard - Payroll Notification

The Payroll Notification tab is used to notify employees via
 text message and/or email with a summary of their hours for a given pay period.

1. Select a Job and Payperiod to push notify employees who have their email address and/or phone numbers entered in the Employees list viewed earlier.

1. Select an employee to Send Notification.

1. The employee will receive either a text, email, or both which will give them a list of their total hours for the selected payperiod as well as a day-by-day breakdown of their hours.
