# Signature Entry - Disputes

Timecard entries can also be disputed from this same
 screen.

1. Select the day(s) to dispute and enter a comment below
 by typing or utilizing the voice-to-text feature of your phone or tablet.

1. Click the Submit button and the dispute will
 be synced with the PTE web portal for review by a Supervisor or Administrator.
