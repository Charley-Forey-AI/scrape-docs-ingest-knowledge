# Sign in to Service Hub

The Service Hub login page allows for access in different business contexts.
Once you have been invited to join Service Hub, you will receive an email invitation to create a Trimble ID or link an existing one. You must do one or the other before you can sign in at [servicehub.trimble.com](https://servicehub.trimble.com/).If a message displays indicating you do not have access to Service Hub, contact your administrator.
Note: Depending on what roles are assigned to your Trimble ID, you may be able to access Service Hub in different business contexts. If so, select either Customer User or ERP Administrator to reach the appropriate context. For more information, see [Business Contexts](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-hub/manage-users/business-contexts).

For instructions on how to sign in using Trimble ID, see: [Sign in with Trimble ID](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:accountservices_002:accountservices:accountservices).
