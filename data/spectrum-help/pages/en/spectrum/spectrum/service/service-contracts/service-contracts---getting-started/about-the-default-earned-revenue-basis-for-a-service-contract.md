# About the Default Earned Revenue Basis for a Service
 Contract

To define service contract types and their associated
 earned revenue basis, use the Service
 ContractsMaintenanceContracts/Contract menu.
For Service Contracts, the default earned revenue basis is determined by
 the [Contract Type File Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/maintenance-overview/contract-type-file-maintenance).
The system offers the following options for recognizing service contract revenue:

- Straight-line across
 fiscal periods

- Revenue is recognized evenly over the life of the contract.
 The amount recognized equals the total contract amount (Service Contract Billings
 tab) divided by the number of fiscal periods.

- Apportioned by
 scheduled visit dates

- Revenue is allocated over the life of the contract. The
 system divides the total contract amount (Service Contract Billings tab) by the
 number of scheduled visits (Service Contract Visits tab). That apportioned amount
 is assigned to the fiscal periods in which visits are scheduled.

- Apportioned by budget
 cost of scheduled visits

- Revenue is recognized over the life of the contract. The
 system divides the total contract amount (Service Contract Billings tab) by the
 budgeted cost of scheduled visits (Service Contract Visits tab). Those apportioned
 amounts are assigned to the fiscal periods in which the visits are scheduled.


- As
 billed

- Revenue is recognized when the Service Contract is invoiced.
