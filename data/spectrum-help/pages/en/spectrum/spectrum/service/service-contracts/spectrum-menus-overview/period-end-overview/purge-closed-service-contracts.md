#  Purge Closed Service Contracts

This option may be used to delete records from the Service
 Contracts and all associated files (notes, equipment, components, and schedule files).
Important: Caution should be used when deleting
 records!
When a specific contract is purged, the software will remove any
 associated contract-specific labor billing records.
Use the fields to create the report that will be most useful to you.
 After specifying your report criteria, if you wish to save your entries, click the
 Save Current button in the Sidebar.
The purge update will remove records associated with the contract from the new Service Contract tables for contract billings and visits. The tables noted below storing Service Contract equipment and component references to Work Order site tables will also be purged, but the equipment and components themselves will not be removed.

- SC_CONTRACT_MC

- SC_CONTRACT_EQUIPMENT_MC

- SC_CONTRACT_COMPONENTS_MC

- SC_CONTRACT_VISIT_MC

- SC_CONTRACT_VISIT_EQUIP_MC

- SC_CONTRACT_BILLING_MC

- SC_CON_REVENUE_SCHED_MC

- WO_CONTRACT_LABOR_BILLING_RATES_MC
