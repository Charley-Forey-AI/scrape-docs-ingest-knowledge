# Service Contract Cost History Report - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, the Cost group field will be available on this screen beneath the Salesperson field. The Cost group field will accept entry of a valid Cost group or Cost center code.
Spectrum includes a particular contract on the Service Contract Profitability Report only if you have permission to access that contract. If you have permission to access a particular contract, the report will include all associated revenue and costs for that contract, regardless of the cost center assignment of any detail transaction.
