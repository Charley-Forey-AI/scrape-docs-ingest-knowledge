# Site Service Contract Analysis - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Site
Enter the site you want to
 review.
Total revenue
This field will display the sum of the
 Earned revenue and
 Extra work
 totals.

- If the 'Selections' checkbox for 'Include any
 warranty revenue in work order totals?' is selected, the revenue includes
 "Warranty extension" amounts billed to manufacturer.

- If the 'Selections' checkbox for 'Include
 non-contract revenue and cost?' is selected, the revenue includes and
 additional line in the grid showing all revenue and costs not associated
 to a contract, starting with the specified Order date.

Total cost
The summary cost total displays in this
 field.
Gross profit
The gross profit total displays in this
 field. Click the lookup icon to the right of this field to open the [Profit Summary](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/maintenance-overview/service-contracts/profit-summary) window which displays
 Financial and Contract status totals.
Selections
Click this button to open the Advanced
 Selections window and select to include warranty revenue and/or non-contract
 revenue and cost.
Status
Click this button and select to include
 any combination of contract status types in the grid results.
Hyperlinks

Earned revenue
Click this option to open the [Earned Revenue Processing](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/data-entry-overview/earned-revenue-processing) screen.
Work order revenue
Click this option to open the [Service Contract Work Order Revenue](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/service-contracts/service-contract-work-order-revenue)
 screen, which shows revenue breakdown for work orders referencing the selected
 contract. The amounts in this window do not include unposted revenue for work
 order, and do not include revenue history that does not reference the selected
 contract.
Actual cost
Click this option to open the [Work Order Cost History
 Display](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/work-order/spectrum-menus-overview/inquiries-overview/work-order-cost-history) screen. All records for the selected contract will appear in
 this window, including any history records dated earlier or later than the
 current contract and Service Contract processing date.
