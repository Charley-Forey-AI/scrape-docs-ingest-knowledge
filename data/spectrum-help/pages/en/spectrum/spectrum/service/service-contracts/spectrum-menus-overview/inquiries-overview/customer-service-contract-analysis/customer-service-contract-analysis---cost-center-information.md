# Customer Service Contract Analysis - Cost Center
 Information

If cost centers are being used, the Site Service Contract
 Analysis screen will display the contract only if the Operator has permission to access the
 cost center associated with that contract.
The software compares the cost center assigned to the contract with
 the cost centers in the Operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center
 is not included, then that contract is not shown on the Site Service Contract Analysis
 screen.
All actual cost totals will appear on the main screen, but detail
 records assigned to cost centers that the Operator is not authorized to access will not
 appear in the drill-down window. Cost center security will be provided for the
 information drill-down button to ensure that the customer or site details are not
 accessible if the Operator is not allowed to make use of that code.
If the option for 'Show non-contract revenue and cost?' is selected
 in the Advanced window, then cost center validation will be performed to ensure that the
 Operator is permitted to access the specified Site or Customer in order to display the
 non-contract amount. If the Operator has permission for the Site or Customer, then the
 amounts and drill-down capability will be provided to view work orders.
