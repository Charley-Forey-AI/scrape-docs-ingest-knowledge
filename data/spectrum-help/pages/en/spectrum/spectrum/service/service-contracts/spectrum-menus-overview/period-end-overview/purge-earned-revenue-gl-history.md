# Purge Earned Revenue G/L History

The Purge Earned Revenue G/L Distribution screen will purge
 records from the earned revenue G/L distribution history table through the specified G/L
 date.
Important: Before completing the purge, print, review and
 retain the Earned Revenue G/L Distribution Report.
This purge is independent of the Purge Service Contract option in order to maintain a longer record retention schedule for G/L source transaction records. Service contracts that have been purged can still be stored indefinitely in the Earned Revenue G/L Distribution History table as backup information for G/L entries originating from Service Contract Earned Revenue and Reverse Earned Revenue Processing.
If cost centers are being used, the software will only purge records if the Operator has permission to access the cost center assigned to that history record.
