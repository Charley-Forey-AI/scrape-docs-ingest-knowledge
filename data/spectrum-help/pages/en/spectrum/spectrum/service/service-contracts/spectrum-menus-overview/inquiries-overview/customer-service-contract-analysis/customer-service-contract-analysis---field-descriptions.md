# Customer Service Contract Analysis - Field Descriptions

Use the table below for reference when completing the fields on this screen.
Fields/Buttons
Descriptions

Customer
Enter the customer you want to
 review.
Total revenue
This field will display the sum of the
 Earned revenue and
 Extra work
 totals.
Total cost
The summary cost total displays in this
 field.
Gross profit
The gross profit total displays in this
 field. Click the lookup icon to the right of this field to open the [Profit Summary](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/maintenance-overview/service-contracts/profit-summary) window which displays
 Financial and Contract status totals.
Selections
Click this button and select to include
 warranty revenue and/or non-contract revenue and cost.
Status
Click this button and select to include
 any combination of contract status types in the grid results.
Hyperlinks

Earned revenue
Click this option to open the [Earned Revenue Processing](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/data-entry-overview/earned-revenue-processing) screen.
Work order
Click this option to open the [Service Contract Work Order Revenue](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/service-contracts/spectrum-menus-overview/service-contracts/service-contract-work-order-revenue)
 screen, which shows revenue breakdown for work orders referencing the selected
 contract. The amounts in this window do not include unposted revenue for work
 order, and do not include revenue history that does not reference the selected
 contract.
Actual cost
Click this option to open the [Work Order Cost History](/en/spectrum/spectrum/service/work-order/spectrum-menus-overview/inquiries-overview/work-order-cost-history) screen. All records
 for the selected contract will appear in this window, including any history
 records dated earlier or later than the current contract and Service Contract
 processing date.
