# Service Contract Earned Revenue - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, the cost center assigned to
 the site of the contract will be used in Service Contract Earned Revenue.
The cost center associated with the contract is assigned to the earned revenue entry.
 The software will include the contract in the temporary table only if you have
 permission to access that contract. Cost center overrides are not applicable for this
 update.
