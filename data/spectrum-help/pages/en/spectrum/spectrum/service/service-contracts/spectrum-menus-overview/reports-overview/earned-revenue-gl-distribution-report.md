# Earned Revenue G/L Distribution Report

The Earned Revenue G/L Distribution Report is used to
 review earned revenue G/L history transactions over a specified range of fiscal years and
 periods.
This report includes transactions from Service Contract Earned Revenue and Reverse Earned
 Revenue Processing, plus contract billing amounts invoiced (or reversed) while the
 contract's 'earned revenue basis' was set to 'As billed'.
