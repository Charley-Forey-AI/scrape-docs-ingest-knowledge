# Service Contract Visits Report - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, Spectrum includes the
 contract on the report only if the Operator has permission to access the cost center assigned
 to that contract.
Spectrum compares the cost center assigned to the contract with cost centers
 in the Operator's assigned cost center scheme, and if the cost center is not included,
 the contract is not shown on the report.
