# Earned Revenue G/L Distribution Report - Cost Center Information

If cost centers are being used, a Cost group field displays beneath the
 G/L account field.
This field will accept entry of a valid Cost group or Cost center code. Spectrum
 includes the contract on the report only if the Operator has permission to access the
 cost center assigned to that history record. The software will filter which G/L history
 records are included on the report when a single cost center or cost group is specified.
