# What is included on Work Order invoices

Based on the 'Price' type selected in Work Order Entry, the Accounts Receivable > Work Order Invoice Print will include the following:

- If 'Flat rate' work order is selected, always include labor and material lines that are flagged 'bill to customer', and conditionally include labor and material lines that are not flagged 'bill to customer' when the 'Print detail for flat rate Work Order invoices' checkbox is selected on the A/R Invoice Form screen. Always show other charge lines with non-zero bill amounts.

- If 'T+M' work order is selected, always include labor and material lines that are flagged 'bill to customer', and always show other charge lines with non-zero bill amounts.
