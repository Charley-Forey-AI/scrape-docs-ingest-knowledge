# Work Order Equipment Billing History Inquiry

The Work Order Equipment Billing History Inquiry window will display billing summary information and a history of transactions associated with the selected equipment for Labor hours, Material transactions, Other charge transactions and Flat-rate tasks.
Field
Description

Site
The site number and description of the
 associated work order will display in this field. Click the lookup icon to open
 the View Site window.
Equipment
The equipment code and description of
 the associated work order will display in this field.
G/L date
The specified from and to G/L dates
 will display in this field.
Summary
This section of the window contains summary billing totals for Labor hours, Material, Other charges and Flat rate tasks. The tabs beneath this section will contain more detailed billing information for these billing transactions.

Labor Hours tab
Detailed billing information for labor
 hours associated with the selected equipment are available on this tab. All
 labor transactions across work orders and billings will be listed on this tab.
 Select an item from this list, and click the View button to open the
 View Labor Hours
 History window.
Material tab
Detailed billing information for
 material hours associated with the selected equipment are available on this
 tab. All material hours across work orders and billings will be listed on this
 tab. Select an item from this list, and click the View button to open the
 View Material
 History window.
Other Charges tab
Detailed billing information for other
 charges associated with the selected equipment are available on this tab. All
 other charge transactions across work orders and billings will be listed on
 this tab. Select an item from this list, and click the View button to open the
 View Other Charges
 History window.
Flat Rate Tasks tab
Detailed billing information for flat
 rate tasks associated with the selected equipment are available on this tab.
 All flat rate tasks across work orders and billings will be listed on this tab.
 Select an item from this list, and click the View button to open the
 View Flat Rate Tasks
 History window.
