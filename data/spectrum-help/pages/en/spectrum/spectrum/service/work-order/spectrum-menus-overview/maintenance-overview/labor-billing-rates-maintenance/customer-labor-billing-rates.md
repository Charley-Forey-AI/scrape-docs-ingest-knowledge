# Customer Labor Billing Rates

Use the Customer Labor Billing Rates screen to define
 customer-specific billing rates for a selected customer.
For example, B1 might have been previously defined as the Standard Billing Rate of $100 per
 hour ($100 regular time, $150 overtime). However, relatives of the owner get a discount, so
 they are billed only $70 per hour. Or perhaps a certain customer is so difficult that
 technicians will only go there if paid double.
In these situations, this screen is used to re-define B1 rates for the customer in
 question. Later, when work orders are entered, the person entering the work orders does not
 need to be aware of any special circumstances for the correct dollar amount to be
 automatically billed.
