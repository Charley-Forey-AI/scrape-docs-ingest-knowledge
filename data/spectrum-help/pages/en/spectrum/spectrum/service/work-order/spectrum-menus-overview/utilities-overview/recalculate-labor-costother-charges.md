# Recalculate Labor Cost/Other Charges

The Recalculate Labor Cost/Other Charges utility will
 refresh the estimated labor cost currently recorded for unposted labor transactions.
This utility also refreshes the estimated billing and cost amounts of other charges that
 are based on other transactions on the work order. This update will be useful when
 technicians' union and/or employee pay rates have been revised in Payroll, and when
 standard billing and/or cost rates have recently been revised in Other Charges Maintenance.
 This update will ensure that inquiries and reports that include unposted costs provide the
 most recent estimation.

- This update only affects labor transactions that originated in Work
 Order and have not yet been transferred to Pre-Time Card Entry. Labor entries that have
 already been transferred will display cost based the rate specified on the associated
 time card entry.

- This update only affects other charge transactions that originated
 in work order with a rate type of 'hourly', 'Percent of bill' or 'Percent of cost'. For
 these types of charges the billing and cost values will be reset based on the current
 standard rates in Other Charges Maintenance. Other charge entries with a rate type of
 'Fixed amount' and all historical transaction are unaffected.
