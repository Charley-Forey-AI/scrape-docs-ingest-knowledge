# Purge Completed Work Orders

This update will delete all completed work order numbers
 within the requested range, along with the corresponding labor, materials, and other cost
 history.
Warning: Purges permanently delete information. Before
 you perform a purge, back up your data.

Work orders flagged as 'Proposed' will automatically be deselected. This information is
 purged only from the work order module; these deletions do not affect Accounts Receivable
 or Job Cost.
