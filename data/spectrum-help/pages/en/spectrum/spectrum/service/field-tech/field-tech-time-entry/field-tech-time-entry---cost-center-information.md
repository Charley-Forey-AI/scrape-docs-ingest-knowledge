# Field Tech Time Entry - Cost Center Information

Field Tech entry will not validate phase-level cost center overrides on a Job Work Order. The Search Phases window will serve as the primary cost center filter; it will only show phases the current operator is authorized for in order to assist the user in selecting the proper phase for the work order transaction.
