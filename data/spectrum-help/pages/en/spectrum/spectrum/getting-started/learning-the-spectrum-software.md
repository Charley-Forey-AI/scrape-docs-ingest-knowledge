# Learning the Spectrum Software

The Spectrum interface, also called the Spectrum UI, has been designed to be user-friendly and intuitive.
The Spectrum application is comprised of "modules." Modules are the basic building blocks of your system and most likely include these six core modules:

- Accounts Payable

- Accounts Receivable

- Cash Management

- General Ledger

- Job Cost

- Payroll

 To allow you to customize your software, there are over 20 additional modules, each designed to meet specific business needs.
