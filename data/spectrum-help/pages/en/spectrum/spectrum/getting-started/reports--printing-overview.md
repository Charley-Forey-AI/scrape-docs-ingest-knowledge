# Reports & Printing Overview

The reports available throughout Spectrum provide a wide variety of options to display and share data. Spectrum's flexible reporting structure allows you to keep real time tabs on every aspect of your businesses such as job profit trends, change order status, over/under billing, responses to bid invitations, and much more.
