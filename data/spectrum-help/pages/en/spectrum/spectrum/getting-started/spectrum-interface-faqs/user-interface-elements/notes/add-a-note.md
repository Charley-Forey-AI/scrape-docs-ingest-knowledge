# Add a Note

1. From the Info Bar, click the Documentation category and then
 click the Notes link.

1. Select a note topic from the available list and click
 Edit.A notepad frame displays with your cursor in the
 workspace.

1. Type your note. There are no length
 restrictions - allowing you to be as detailed as necessary.

1. (Optional) Use the formatting icons along the top of the
 workspace to further customize your note.

1. Click Save.
