# Info Bar

Info Bars display to the right of Spectrum screens where easy access to inquiry information is most helpful (for example, vendors, subcontracts, employees, customers, etc.).
The Info Bar provides quick access links to the following types of information: properties, financial details, user-defined, images, and notes.
