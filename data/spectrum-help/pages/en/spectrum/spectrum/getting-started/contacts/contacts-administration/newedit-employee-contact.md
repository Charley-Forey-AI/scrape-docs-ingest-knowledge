# New/Edit Employee Contact

This window displays when the contact is an employee.
The information comes from the Payroll module. The fields are more limited than on the [New/Edit Contact](/en/spectrum/spectrum/getting-started/contacts/contacts-administration/newedit-contact), and most of the information is display-only.
