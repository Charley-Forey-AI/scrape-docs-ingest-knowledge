# Date Shortcuts

Use these keyboard shortcuts to quickly enter dates in Spectrum.
Shortcut
Date Automatically Entered

T
Today's date.

H
The last day of the current month.

M
The first day of the current month.

+
Tomorrow's date.

-
Yesterday's date.
