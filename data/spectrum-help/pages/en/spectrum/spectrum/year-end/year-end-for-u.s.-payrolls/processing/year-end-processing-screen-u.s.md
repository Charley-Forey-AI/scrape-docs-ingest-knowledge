# Year End Processing Screen (U.S.)

This screen is a convenient aid for completing your year-end to-do list.
To access the Year End Processing screen, go to System Administration > Year End > Year End Processing.
The Year End Processing screen links to other needed screens, but these screens remain accessible from within each module, just as they always are.

Only the modules licensed and installed on your system appear on this screen. After completing the tasks for a given module on this screen, a green check mark appears next to the module button.
