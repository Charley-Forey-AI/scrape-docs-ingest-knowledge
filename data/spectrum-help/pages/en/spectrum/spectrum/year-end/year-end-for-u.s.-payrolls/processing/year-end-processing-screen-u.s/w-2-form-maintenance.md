# W-2 Form Maintenance

Use this screen to add or edit information produced during the W-2 build process.
If doing year-end processing, first [Build W-2 Forms](/en/spectrum/spectrum/year-end/year-end-for-u.s.-payrolls/processing/year-end-processing-screen-u.s./build-w-2-forms).

- Enter numeric fields in dollars and cents (up to $99 million); cannot be negative.

- Do not enter dollar signs ($) or commas. Use a decimal point even if the amount is an even dollar amount.

If doing year-end processing, go next to [Tax Table Maintenance](/en/spectrum/spectrum/year-end/year-end-for-u.s.-payrolls/processing/year-end-processing-screen-u.s./tax-table-maintenance).
