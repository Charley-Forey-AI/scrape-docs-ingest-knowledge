# Field Definitions: IN Rollup Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the IN
 Rollup form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Through Month

 Specify the month through which to roll up inventory details.

##  Location Group

 Specify the location group for this rollup. Press F4 for a list of valid location groups.
