# Field Definitions: IN Material Copy Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the IN
 Material Copy form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Location

 Enter the location (IN Locations) from which to copy materials.

##  Category

 Enter the category (HQ Material Categories) for the materials to copy.
Note: Copy must be either by category or by material; you cannot copy both. Only enter a category here if you do not plan to copy by material.

##  Material

 Enter the material (IN Location Materials) from the source location that you wish to copy.
Note: Copy must be either by category or by material; you cannot copy both. Only enter a material here if you did not specify a category to copy.

##  Location Display Options

- All Categories and Materials – When selecting this radio button, all categories and materials are displayed in the Location grid.

- All Locations – When selecting this radio button, all Locations are displayed, regardless of the Material entered in the Material field.

##  Active Locations

 When the Active Locations box is checked, the Material Copy form displays only active locations. If the box is unchecked then the form displays all locations (active or inactive).

##  Copy all Locations

 When selecting this checkbox, all locations are checked in the location grid. To uncheck all of the locations, deselect the checkbox.
