# About the IN Material Order Confirm Init Form

Use this form to initialize material order
 confirmations.
Note: Although you can manually confirm material orders in the IN
 Material Order Confirmation form (File > Initialize
 Confirmations ), you may find it quicker to initialize them using
 this form.
