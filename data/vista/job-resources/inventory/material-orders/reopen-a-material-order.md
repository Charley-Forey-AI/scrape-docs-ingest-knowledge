# Reopen a Material Order

A material order can be reopened after it has been closed by pulling it into an open
 material order entry batch, changing the Status to "Open", and posting the batch.
Note: If any remaining units or costs existed when the material
 order was closed, they are not restored, nor are allocated units updated in the IN
 Location Materials form.
