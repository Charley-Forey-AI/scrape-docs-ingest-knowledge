# Field Definitions: IN Bill of Materials Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the IN Bill
 of Materials form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Location Group

Location Group field on the IN Bill of Materials form.
Enter the location group (IN Location Groups) to which this Bill of Materials applies. All locations within this group will use this Bill of Materials whenever this finished good is produced, unless overridden in IN Bill of Materials Override.

Related information

- [About Setting up a Bill of Materials](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-setting-up-a-bill-of-materials)

- [About the IN Bill Of Materials Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-the-in-bill-of-materials-form)

## Finished Material

Finished Material field on the IN Bill of Materials form.
Enter the material code of the finished good. This must be a valid “stocked” material set up in HQ Materials.

Related information

- [About Setting up a Bill of Materials](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-setting-up-a-bill-of-materials)

- [About the IN Bill Of Materials Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-the-in-bill-of-materials-form)

## Material

Material field on the IN Bill of Materials form.
Enter the component material that will be used in the production of the finished good. This must be a valid stocked HQ Material, unique for this Bill of Materials, and cannot be the same as the finished good.

Related information

- [About Setting up a Bill of Materials](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-setting-up-a-bill-of-materials)

- [About the IN Bill Of Materials Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-the-in-bill-of-materials-form)

## Units

Units field on the IN Bill of Materials form.
Enter the number of units of this material needed to complete one unit of the finished good.

Related information

- [About Setting up a Bill of Materials](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-setting-up-a-bill-of-materials)

- [About the IN Bill Of Materials Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/production/about-the-in-bill-of-materials-form)

## % Weight

% Weight field on the IN Bill of Materials form.
 Entry in this field is only necessary if the amount of the component material is based on a percentage of the finished good’s weight.
 Specify what percent of the finished good’s weight is comprised of this component material. For example, if the component material makes up 6% of the finished good’s weight, enter .06.
