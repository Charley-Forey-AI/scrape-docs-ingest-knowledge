#  About the IN Location Groups Form

Use this form to classify inventory locations for Bill of
 Materials setup, Material Sales pricing, and haul rate setup.
Once a group has been defined, Inventory locations
 are assigned to the group in the IN Locations form.
 To delete a group, you must first delete the group
 assignment for each location in the group (in the IN Locations
 form).
