# About the IN Location Co & Category Override Form

Use this form to override sales, haul revenue, and haul/material expense accounts associated with a location by company and material category.
Initially, these accounts are defined by location in
 the IN Location form. Use this program only if you want to
 classify sales revenue and quantities and/or hauling
 revenue/expense by purchasing company and material category
 within your General Ledger.
