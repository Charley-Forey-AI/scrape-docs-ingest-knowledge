#  About the IN Location Category Override Form

Use this form to override the cost method, receivable type,
 and the Inventory and Customer GL account assignments for a location by
 material category.

Initially, this information is defined for a
 location in the IN Locations form. Use this form only if you
 need to classify Inventory and/or Customer sales revenue within
 General Ledger, based on the material category.
