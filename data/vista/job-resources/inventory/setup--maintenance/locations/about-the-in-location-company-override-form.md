#  About the IN Location Company Override Form

Use this form to override sales, haul revenue, and haul/material expense accounts associated with a location by company.
Initially, sales accounts, haul revenue, and haul/material expense accounts are defined by location in the Location Master. Use this form only if you need to classify sales revenue and quantities, hauling revenue, and/or haul/material expense by purchasing company within your General Ledger.
