# Beginning/Ending Material

Specify the beginning and ending in a range of materials to initialize from HQ Materials to this location, or leave blank to initialize all materials. Only materials within this range, that are also within the range of categories specified above, and that are flagged as “active” and “stocked” will be initialized.
