# Field Definitions: IN Material Order Purge Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the IN
 Material Order Purge form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Purge All Material Orders Closed Through Month

 This is the month through which to purge material orders. Defaults the last month closed in Subledgers. May be overridden, but must be a closed month.
