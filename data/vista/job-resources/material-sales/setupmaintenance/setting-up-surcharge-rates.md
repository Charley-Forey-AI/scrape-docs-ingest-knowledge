# Setting Up Surcharge Rates

Set up surcharge rates.

1. Click the Rates tab.

1. In the Sequence field, enter
 N or + to add a new rate sequence.

1. In the Location Group field, enter
 the location group to which this surcharge rate applies.

1. Use the Location, Category, Material, Truck
 Type, UM, and/or Zone fields to enter additional rate criteria.Note: The more criteria you enter, the more specific your
 rates will be. This means the system will only apply the rates if the data
 entered on the ticket matches the criteria specified here exactly. For more
 information, see [Surcharge
 Rate Hierarchy](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/material-sales/setupmaintenance/surcharge-rate-hierarchy).

1. In the Surcharge Rate field, enter
 the rate to use for all surcharges calculated on or after the Effective
 Date.

1. In the Min Amt field, enter the
 minimum amount you require for surcharges. Enter 0.00 if you do not have a
 minimum amount. This amount will be used for surcharges applied on after the
 Effective Date.

1. In the Old Surcharge Rate field,
 enter the rate to use for surcharges calculated prior to the Effective
 Date.

1. In the Old Min Amt field, enter the
 minimum amount you require for surcharges. Enter 0.00 if you do not have a
 minimum amount. This amount will be used for surcharges applied prior to the
 Effective Date.

1. Save the record.

1. Repeat to enter additional surcharge rates.
