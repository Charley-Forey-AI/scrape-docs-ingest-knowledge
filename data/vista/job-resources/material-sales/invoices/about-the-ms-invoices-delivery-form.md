# About the MS Invoices Delivery Form

 Use this form to deliver print or email invoices. Access this
 form via MS Invoice Edit by selecting File > Deliver Invoices.

 You can designate whether recipients should receive print or email invoices by specifying either P-Print or E-Email in the Delivery Method field of the Recipients tab of the MS Invoice Edit form.
