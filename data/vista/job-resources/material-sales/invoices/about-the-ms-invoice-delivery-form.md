# About the MS Invoice Delivery Form

Use this form to redeliver Material Sales invoices. Access this form via MS Invoices by selecting one or more invoices in the grid and then clicking the Deliver button.
 You can deliver invoices as many times as needed. The grid displays all invoices that you selected in the MS Invoice form for redelivery.
 You cannot edit or remove invoices in this form; however, you can edit the recipient information for each invoices, as well as add or remove attachments.
Note: If you need to edit invoice information, use MS Invoice Edit. If you need to remove an invoice, close the MS Invoice Delivery form, reselect the appropriate invoices, and then click Deliver.
