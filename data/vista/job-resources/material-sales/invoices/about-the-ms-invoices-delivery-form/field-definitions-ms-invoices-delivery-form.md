# Field Definitions: MS Invoices Delivery Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the MS
 Invoices Delivery form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Include Cash and Credit Card Invoices

Include Cash and Credit Card Invoices checkbox on the MS Invoice Deliver form.
Select this checkbox to include invoices created from cash or credit card transactions when printing invoices.

## Print Notes

Print Notes? checkbox on the MS Invoice Deliver form.
Select this checkbox to print notes on invoices.
