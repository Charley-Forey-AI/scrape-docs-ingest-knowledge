# Field Definitions: MS Hauler Time Sheet Tickets Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the MS
 Hauler Time Sheet Tickets form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Verify

 Select this checkbox for each ticket that matches the information on the hauler’s time sheet. Once you close this form, the system automatically updates the Ticket Detail table (MSTD).
Note: To verify all tickets in the grid, click the Verify All button.
