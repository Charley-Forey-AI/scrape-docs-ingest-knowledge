# Field Definitions: MS Move Transactions to Target Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the MS Move
 Transactions to Target form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Target Month

Target Month field on the MS Move Transactions to Target form.
Enter the target month to copy/move the source transactions to and press Enter. (For example, if you enter "2" and press Enter, the date will render as "02/18".) 
Use the up/down arrows to adjust the month/year.

Click the Select button when you are ready to apply the new batch month.
