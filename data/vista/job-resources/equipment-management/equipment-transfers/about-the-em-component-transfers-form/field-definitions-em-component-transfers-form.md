# Field Definitions: EM Component Transfers Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM
 Component Transfers form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Component

 Specify the component to transfer. Component must be set up in EM equipment and flagged as a ‘Component’ type.

##  Seq

 Enter the sequence number of an existing entry (0-255) or enter N, New, or + to add a new sequence. The system will automatically assign the next available sequential number for this component.
Note: If you have previously transferred this component, once you tab off this input, the equipment to which the component is currently attached displays in the Prior Master Equipment field below. If this is the first transfer for this component, the Prior Master Equipment defaults as ‘No Info’.

##  Reason

 Enter the reason for this component transfer, if applicable, up to 60 characters.

##  Date On

 Enter the date on which this component was transferred to the new equipment. Date must be equal to or less than the current date.

## Transfer to Equipment

Specify the piece of equipment (from EM Equipment) to which you are transferring this component.
Note: Once you enter the equipment, the system displays the hours, miles/kilometres, and fuel readings at ‘transfer in’ time for both the equipment and the component in the Equipment On inputs below.

## Equipment On: Equip Hours

This field defaults the current hours for the 'transfer to' equipment. You may override the hours as necessary; however, the system will not update the hour meter for this equipment in EM Equipment.

##  Date Off

 This field is only accessible after transferring this component to another piece of equipment.
 Initially defaults the date you transferred this component off the specified equipment (from the ‘Date On’ entered in the succeeding transfer sequence). You may override the default if necessary.
