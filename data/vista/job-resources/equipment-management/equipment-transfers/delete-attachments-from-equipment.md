# Delete Attachments from Equipment

You can delete attachments to a piece of equipment in the Attachments tab of the EM Location Transfer form.
When you delete an attachment, the system removes the attachment from the primary equipment.
Note: If you want to delete the attachment and assign to a new job/location in a single step, select the attachment as the equipment to transfer on the Info tab of the EM Location Transfer form.
To delete attachments:

1. In the EM Location
 Transfer form, click the Attachments tab.

1. Select an attachment record.

1. Click the Delete button on the toolbar.
