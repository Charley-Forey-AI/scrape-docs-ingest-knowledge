# Filter Equipment Transfers

You can filter equipment transfer records by job and location.
The Search Criteria section in EM Location Transfers allows you to filter equipment transfer records for a specific JC Co, Job, Location, and/or transfer date range. The grid will show only those transfer records meeting all the search criteria.

1. From the Vista main menu, select Equipment
 Management > Programs > EM Location Transfer.

1. Use one or more of the following fields to filter equipment transfer records:

- JCCo - to show transfer records for a specific JC company

- Job - to show transfer records for a specific job

- Location - to show transfer records for a specific equipment location

- Date - to show transfer records within a specific date range

1. Click Search.

The grid displays all equipment transfers meeting the search criteria.
