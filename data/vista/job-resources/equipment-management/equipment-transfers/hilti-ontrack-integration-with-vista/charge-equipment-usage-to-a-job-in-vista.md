# Charge Equipment Usage to a Job in Vista

Create, process, and post usage batches in Vista for the equipment that was used on a
 particular job.

1. In EM Automatic Usage,
 create and process a new batch for the equipment that was used on the job.For more information about automatic usage, see [About the EM Automatic Usage Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-automatic-usage-form).

1. Go to EM Usage Posting,
 select the batch from the grid, and post the batch. For more information about posting usage batches, see [About the EM Usage Posting Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-usage-posting-form).

1. On the Grid tab, you can
 view the equipment used on the job and the charges to the job for each piece of
 equipment. Note:Hilti ON!Track does not currently transfer allocated job costs to Vista. Job costs must be allocated in Vista.
