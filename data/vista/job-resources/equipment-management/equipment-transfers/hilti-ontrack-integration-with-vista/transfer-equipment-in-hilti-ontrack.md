# Transfer Equipment in Hilti ON!Track

Once the applications are connected, log in to Hilti ON!Track to transfer tools and equipment to different projects, other locations, and employees.
For more information about Hilti ON!Track
 and transferring equipment (called assets in ON!Track), see the [Hilti ON!Track Help](https://www.hilti.com/kb-search?continue=true) or contact your consultant.

1. Choose to transfer assets (equipment) to a location, storage asset, or employee.

1. Assign or change the employee responsible for each asset.

1. Set transfer and return dates for each asset.

These changes made in ON!Track automatically update the
 associated EM Location Transfer records in Vista.
Return to Vista to create, process, and post usage batches for the
 equipment that was used on the job. See [Charge Equipment Usage to a Job in Vista](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/equipment-transfers/hilti-ontrack-integration-with-vista/charge-equipment-usage-to-a-job-in-vista).
