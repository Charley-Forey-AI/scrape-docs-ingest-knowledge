# Enter Employee Data in Vista

Enter or update employee data in the Vista PR Employees record. This data will go into Hilti ON!Track Employees.

1. From the main menu, select Payroll > Programs > PR Employees.

1. Enter the Employee ID
 number.

1. Enter the employee's First and
 Last name.

1. Enter the employee's Email address and
 Phone number.

1. Fill out all other required fields, and save the
 record.
