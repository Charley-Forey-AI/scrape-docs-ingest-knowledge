# Field Definitions: EM Mass Location Transfers Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM Mass
 Location Transfers form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Show Equipment Assigned To: JC Co

Show Equipment Assigned To: JC Co field on the
 EM Mass Location Transfer v11 form.
 Entry in this field required if restricting by Job.
 Specify the JC Co (from JC Company
 Parameters) by which to restrict the equipment search or press  F4 to select
 from a list of valid Job Cost companies. Leave blank to include all companies.
 Click the Equipment Refresh button to populate the Equipment list with all equipment meeting the search criteria. Use the [Equipment/Equipment to Transfer](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/equipment-transfers/about-the-em-mass-location-transfer-v11-form/field-definitions-em-mass-location-transfers-form#ID-0000c71f--en) lists to indicate the equipment to transfer.

##  Equipment / Equipment to Transfer

 The Equipment list shows all equipment meeting the search criteria. Use any of the following methods to move equipment to the Equipment to Transfer list:

- Select the equipment to transfer and double-click the mouse. This method supports single and consecutive selection (using Shift key) only.

- Select equipment and click the Add
 button. This method supports single, consecutive, and random selection (using Ctrl
 key).

- Click the Add All button to add all equipment
 in the Equipment list to the Equipment to Transfer list.

 The Equipment to Transfer list shows all equipment you have selected for transfer. To remove equipment from this list:

- Select the equipment to remove and double-click the mouse. This method supports single and consecutive selection (using Shift key) only.

- Select equipment and click the Remove
 button. This method supports single, consecutive, and random selection (using Ctrl
 key).

- Click the Remove All button to move all
 equipment back to the Equipment list.

##  Transfer To: In Time

Transfer To: In Time field on the EM Mass
 Location Transfer v11 form.
 Specify the time, in 24-hour format (00:00 - 23:59) to use as the 'transferred in time' for all equipment being transferred.
Note: Note that “24:00” is not a valid hour. To enter a
 24-hour period that starts and ends with midnight, enter 00:00 as both
 the day start and day stop times.

## Transfer To: Est Out Date

Transfer To: Est Out Date field on the EM Mass
 Location Transfer v11 form.
Specify the estimated transfer date for all equipment included in this transfer (i.e., the date you expect to transfer the equipment from the new job/location to another job/location).
Note: If you use the automatic usage posting feature and you
 selected the Use
 Est Out Date checkbox for a category or piece of equipment on a usage
 posting template that is associated with the job to which you are transferring the
 equipment, entry in this field is required.
