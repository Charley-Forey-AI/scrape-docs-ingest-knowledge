# Field Definitions: EM Job Templates Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM Job
 Templates form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  JC Company

 Enter the Job Cost company for the job specified below.

##  Job

 Specify the job to which you are assigning revenue and/or auto usage templates.
Note: If you enter a job having a status of ‘2-Soft Close’
 or ‘3-Hard Close’, the status displays in red beside the job description; however, entry is
 allowed.

##  Auto Usage Template

 If this job is to be charged using the auto usage processing program (EM Automatic Usage), specify which Auto Usage Template will be used to calculate and post usage for each category or piece of equipment assigned to this template.

##  Revenue Template

 If you will be charging equipment to this job using revenue rates other than the standard rates (defined in EM Revenue Rates by Category or EM Revenue Rates by Equipment), specify the Revenue Template to use when posting usage. Equipment will be charged to this job based on the override rates specified on the Revenue Template.
 If a revenue template is not assigned here, this job will use the standard rates when posting usage.
