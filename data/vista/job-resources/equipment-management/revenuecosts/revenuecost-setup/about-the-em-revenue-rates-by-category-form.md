# About the EM Revenue Rates by Category Form

Use this form to set up revenue usage rates for equipment categories.

A revenue rate should be set up here for every category/revenue code combination that will be used when posting equipment usage.
The rates set up here will default into the EM Revenue Rates by Equipment screen if the Override Rates option is checked for the specified revenue code, and can be overridden in that form for those pieces of equipment with unique rates.
Note: If you change the standard rate for a category here, it will be updated to EM Revenue Rates by Equipment for all equipment assigned to that category; however, it will only update equipment where the standard rate has not been overridden.
