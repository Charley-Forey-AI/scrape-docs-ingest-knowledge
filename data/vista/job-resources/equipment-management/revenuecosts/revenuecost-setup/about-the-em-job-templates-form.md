# About the EM Job Templates Form

Use this form to assign revenue and/or auto usage templates to jobs.
Revenue templates (set up in EM Revenue Templates) allow you to establish override revenue rates for equipment (by category and/or equipment) that will be used on jobs. You can define rate overrides as a specific rate or a percent of the standard rate designated in EM Revenue Rates by Category or EM Revenue Rates by Equipment. If you do not assign a revenue template to a job here, the standard revenue rates will be applied when posting usage.
Auto usage templates (set up in EM Auto Usage Posting Template) are used to specify categories or pieces of equipment that are to be charged to the job automatically (based on the time they are located at the job) when posting usage in EM Automatic Usage.
