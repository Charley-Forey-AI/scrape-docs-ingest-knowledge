# Field Definitions: EM Revenue Template Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM
 Revenue Template form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Revenue Template

 Enter a code, up to 10 characters, that uniquely identifies this revenue template.

##  Description

 Enter a description of this revenue template, up to 30 characters.

##  Template Type

 Specify the type of rate used for this template.

- Percent Of Standard Rate – Select this option to base rates for this template on a percentage of the standard revenue rates. Both the EM Rev Rates by Catgy Template and the EM Rev Rate by Equip Template forms will prompt for the ‘% of Rate’ to use.

- Rate Override – Select this option to use specific override rates for this template. Both the EM Rev Rates by Catgy Template and the EM Rev Rate by Equip Template forms will prompt for the override ‘Rate’ to use.
