# About the EM Fuel Posting Form

Use this form to enter individual fuel usage transactions.
You will typically use this program if you prefer the more traditional method of entering transactions rather than initializing a group of transactions based on a set of criteria (as in EM Fuel Initialize).
One of the advantages to using this program is that it provides much more flexibility. Because entries are not initialized based on certain criteria, it is easier to enter transactions for the equipment you want. In addition, although most of the data defaults based on information entered for the equipment (in EM Equipment), you have the ability to override it as necessary.
The informational display (top of form) shows the current hours and odometer readings for the equipment entered on the transaction. As you enter the new odometer and hour readings, it will also show the difference between the new and current readings for the equipment.
Note: Updates to the equipment’s odometer, hour meter, and meter reading dates (in EM Equipment) will depend on the update option selected for fuel posting in EM Company Parameters (Updates tab). For more information about the update options, see [Meter Updates: Equipment Usage](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/setupmaintenance/about-the-em-company-parameters-form/field-definitions-em-company-parameters-form#ID-0000b8af--en), [Meter Updates: Costs Adj and Parts Posting](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/setupmaintenance/about-the-em-company-parameters-form/field-definitions-em-company-parameters-form#ID-0000b8c0--en), and [Meter Updates: Fuel Posting](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/setupmaintenance/about-the-em-company-parameters-form/field-definitions-em-company-parameters-form#ID-0000b8ce--en).
