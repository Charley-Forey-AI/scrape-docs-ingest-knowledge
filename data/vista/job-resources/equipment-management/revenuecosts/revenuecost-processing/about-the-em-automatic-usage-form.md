# About the EM Automatic Usage Form

Use this form to process automatic usage posting.
You specify the beginning and ending dates of the billing period to process, and then specify the template(s) and equipment categories to include. You also have the option to exclude specific equipment locations and/or non-working days (such as holidays) from the billing process, as well as to indicate whether to charge jobs for Saturdays and/or Sundays.
