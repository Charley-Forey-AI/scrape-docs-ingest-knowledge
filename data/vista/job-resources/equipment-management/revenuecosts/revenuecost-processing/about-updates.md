# About Updates

Updating a batch is the process that takes each batch entry
 and makes all the necessary transaction updates.
This may involve posting transactions to
 other modules as well as in EM. The following is a list of the updates that occur when
 you post an EM batch.

- GL Interface Adjust – Indicates
 the update level for cost adjustment transactions as specified on the
 Adjustments tab in EM Company Parameters.

- GL Interface Usage – Indicates
 the update level for equipment usage transactions as specified on the Usage tab
 in EM Company Parameters. 

- GL Interface Part – Indicates
 the update level for parts posting as specified on the Parts tab in EM Company
 Parameters.

When you are ready to process the batch,
 enter the posting date and click the Post button. Once the update is complete, the batch
 is closed.
