# About the EM Batch Process Form

Use this form (accessed from the EM Programs menu or by selecting the Process Batch option from any of the EM posting forms) to process batches created in Equipment Management.
Once you have specified the month and batch
 to process, the Info section displays information about the batch (i.e. creator,
 creation date, source, and status).
