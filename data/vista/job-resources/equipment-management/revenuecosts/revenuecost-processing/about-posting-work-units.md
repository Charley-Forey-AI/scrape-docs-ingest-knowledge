# About Posting Work Units

When you set up revenue rates (in EM Revenue Rates by
 Category or EM Revenue Rates by Equipment), you have the option to allow posting work units
 with usage by checking the ‘Work Units with Usage’ option for each applicable revenue code.
If this flag is checked and you specify the
 revenue code on a usage transaction, the ‘Work Units’ field is enabled for the
 transaction, allowing you to enter the work units as necessary (e.g. the number of yards
 of material hauled). The unit of measure in which the work units will be stored
 (specified in EM Revenue Rates by Equipment or EM Revenue Rates by Category) displays in
 the Work U/M field.
