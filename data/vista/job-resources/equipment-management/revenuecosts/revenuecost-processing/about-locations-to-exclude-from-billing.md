# About Locations to Exclude From Billing

Equipment on a job site may sometimes become temporarily
 inactive.
This may be because the equipment is not
 currently required for the work being performed or perhaps has been sent to a shop for
 repair or maintenance. This inactivity is generally not considered billable. In order to
 exclude this inactivity when posting usage, you can use EM Locations to set up these
 non-billable conditions as locations. Then use EM Location Transfer to transfer the
 equipment from a job with no location to the same job with an inactive location. When
 posting usage, you can exclude these locations from the billing process, yet maintain
 the ability to track the location of the equipment to the job.
