# Field Definitions: EM Fuel Initialize Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM Fuel
 Initialize form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Usage Date

 Specify the date to which fuel usage will be posted for all equipment in this batch. Initially defaults the current date.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## IN Loc

 Specify the Inventory location (IN Locations) that provided the fuel for equipment in this batch.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## JC Co

Enter the JC company (from JC Company Parameters) by which to filter equipment selection or leave blank to include equipment from all JC companies.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## Job

Enter
 the job (from JC Jobs) by which to filter equipment selection or leave blank to include
 equipment for all jobs.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## Location

Enter the equipment location (from EM Locations) by which to filter equipment selection or leave blank to include equipment from all locations.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## Category

Enter the equipment category (from EM Categories) by which to filter equipment selection or leave blank to include equipment in all categories.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## Dept.

Enter the department (from EM Departments) by which to filter equipment selection or leave blank to include equipment for all departments.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## Shop

Enter the shop (from EM Shops) by which to filter equipment selection or leave blank to include equipment from all shops.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## Units

 Enter the number of units of fuel used by this piece of equipment. This field will update the Fuel Used field in EM Equipment (Meters tab).

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## New Odometer

 Enter the equipment’s odometer reading at the time of refueling. Once entered, the difference between the current reading and the previous reading is calculated and displayed in the Difference field above the grid.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)

## New Hours

 Enter the equipment’s current hour meter reading at the time of refueling. Once entered, the difference between the current reading and the previous reading is calculated and displayed in the Difference field above the grid.

Related information

- [About the EM Fuel Initialize Form](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/revenuecosts/revenuecost-processing/about-the-em-fuel-initialize-form)
