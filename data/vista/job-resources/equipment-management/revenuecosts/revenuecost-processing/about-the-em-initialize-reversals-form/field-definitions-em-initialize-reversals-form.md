# Field Definitions: EM Initialize Reversals Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM
 Initialize Reversals form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Original Month

Required.
Specify the original posting month for the accrual transactions you want to reverse (i.e. the month in which you created the original transactions).
Note: The system will create reversing entries for all
 transactions flagged for reversal with the specified month and prior months.

##  Reversal Transaction Date

 Required.
 Enter the transaction date to assign to the new reversal entries.
