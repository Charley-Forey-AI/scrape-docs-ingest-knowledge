# About Disposing of Assets

If you will be disposing of an asset, you will need to recalculate the depreciation schedule to prevent any further depreciation of the asset.

- US & Canada - From the Vista main menu, select Equipment
 Management Programs. Double-click the EM Asset Setup icon to open the EM Asset
 Setup form. In the Equipment field, enter the equipment for which you are
 disposing of an asset. Press F4 to select from a list of valid equipment. In the
 Asset field, enter the asset you are disposing or press F4 to select from a list
 of valid assets for the equipment. In the Month Disposed Of field, enter the
 month in which you disposed of the asset. This will be the last month for which
 depreciation is calculated. Click Calculate. The system calculates the Amount
 Taken for the disposed of month, then clears all remaining months greater than
 the disposed of month. If you are using the Declining Balance method, you will
 be required to enter the fiscal year-end month before recalculating the
 depreciation. In the Recalculation for Year Ending
 field, enter the fiscal year-end month and click Calculate. If using the Straight Line method, the system will
 automatically recalculate the current year. Depreciation will be taken for the
 month disposed. In EM Depreciation Processing, post depreciation as normal. If
 necessary, it will back out any excess amounts already taken in.

- Australia - From the Vista main menu, select Equipment Management > Programs.
 Double-click the EM Asset Setup icon to open the EM Asset Setup form. In the
 Equipment field, enter the equipment for which you are disposing of an asset.
 Press F4 to select from a list of valid equipment. In the Asset field, enter the
 asset you are disposing or press F4 to select from a list of valid assets for
 the equipment. In the Date Disposed / Transferred field, enter the date you
 disposed of the asset or transferred the asset to a low-value pool. This is the
 last date through which depreciation will be calculated ClickCalculate. The
 system calculates the Amount Taken for the disposed of month, then clears all
 remaining months greater than the disposed of month. In EM Depreciation
 Processing, post depreciation as normal. If necessary, it will back out any
 excess amounts already taken in.
