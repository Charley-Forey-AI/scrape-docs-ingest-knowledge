# About Revenue Rates

The Revenue Rates tab of the EM Equipment form
 provides a list of revenue rates defined for the category (in EM Revenue Rates by
 Category). You can add or modify rates directly in the grid or double-click a row to
 open the EM Revenue Rates by Category form. For more information about setting up
 revenue rates by category, see Related Topics below.
