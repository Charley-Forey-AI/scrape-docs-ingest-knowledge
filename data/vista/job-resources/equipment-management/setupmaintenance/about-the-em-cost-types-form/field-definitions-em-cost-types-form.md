# Field Definitions: EM Cost Types Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM Cost
 Types form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Cost Type

 Enter a number (0-255) that will identify this EM cost type (e.g. 1=Labor, 2=Outside Repair, etc.).

##  Description

 Enter a description of this EM cost type, up to 30 characters.

##  Abbreviation

 Enter the abbreviation for this cost type, up to 10 characters. This abbreviation will be used on some reports that have limited space.
