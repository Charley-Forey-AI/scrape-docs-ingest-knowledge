# Adjust the Meter Readings - Standard Method

Adjust meter readings using the standard method.

1. From the Programs menu
 of Equipment Management, double-click on the EM Equipment icon. This opens the
 EM Equipment form.

1. Select File > Adjust Meter Readings. This opens the EM Meter Reading Adjustment form. 

1. Enter the equipment for
 which adjust meter readings in the Equipment field.

1. In the Select Meter
 Reading Type section, select Odometer to adjust odometer readings or Hours to
 adjust hour meter readings.

1. Using the Start
 Date and End Date fields, enter the
 beginning and ending dates in the range of dates containing the readings you
 wish to adjust. The grid will automatically populate with all meter readings
 meeting the selection criteria.

1. Review the entries in
 the grid to determine whether the readings you want to adjust are shown. If not,
 adjust the date range as necessary. Note: The Previous Meter Reading before
 MM/DD/YYYY and Next Meter Reading after MM/DD/YYYY informational displays
 (below grid) will show the previous and next readings for the equipment
 based on the selected date range, if applicable.

1. Once the grid includes
 the readings you need to adjust, optionally enter a month in the Mth
 field to restrict the transactions in the grid to a single month. Leave the Mth field blank if the readings to be adjusted span more
 than one month.

1. If adjusting/deleting a
 single meter reading, enter the transaction number of the reading you want to
 adjust in the Trans field.Note: The transaction number for each meter
 reading is displayed in the Trans column of the grid
 (last column).

1. Manually adjust meter
 readings.

1. Select the
 transaction in the grid to update.

1. In the
 New Meter field,
 enter the new meter reading. May leave blank if only entering a New
 Replaced Meter reading. Note: Reading cannot be less than the
 Previous Meter Reading before MM/DD/YYYY or greater than the Next
 Meter Reading after MM/DD/YYYY shown below the grid.

1. In the
 New Replaced Meter
 field, enter the new replaced meter reading. May leave blank if only
 entering a New Meter reading. Note: Reading cannot be less than the
 Previous Meter Reading before MM/DD/YYYY or greater than the Next
 Meter Reading after MM/DD/YYYY shown below the grid.
The system will
 automatically update the New Total Meter
 and New Difference
 fields based on the values entered for the line. These fields cannot
 be adjusted manually.

1. Repeat steps a-c
 for any other readings in the grid that need to be adjusted.

1. After you have
 finished entering adjustments, review all entries in the grid for
 accuracy; make any necessary changes.

1. When you are
 satisfied with all changes, click Update Meter
 Readings(button below grid).
