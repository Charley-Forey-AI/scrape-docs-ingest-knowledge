# Field Definitions: EM Parts Copy Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the EM
 Parts Copy form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Copy From Equipment

 Specify the equipment from which to copy parts. Initially defaults the equipment you specified in EM Equipment Parts.

##  Copy to Equipment

- Category – Select this option to copy parts to an equipment category (specified to the right). Parts will be copied to each piece of equipment within the specified category.

- Equipment - Select this option to copy parts to a specific piece of equipment or a range of equipment (specified below).

##  Category

 This field is only accessible if copying parts to an equipment category.
 Specify a valid equipment category (from EM Category). Parts will be copied to each piece of equipment within this category.

##  Copy to Equipment: Range

 This field is only accessible if copying parts to equipment.
 Check this box to copy parts to a range of equipment (specified below).
 Leave this box unchecked if copying parts to a single piece of equipment only.

##  Equipment #

 This field is only accessible if copying parts to equipment.
 Enter the piece of equipment to which you are copying parts. If copying to a range of equipment, enter the beginning equipment in a range of equipment to which parts will be copied.

##  End Equip #

 This field is only accessible if copying parts to a range of equipment.
 Specify the ending equipment in a range of equipment to which the parts will be copied.

##  Append or Replace

- Add New Parts Only – Select this option to add only those parts that do not already exist for the destination equipment. Existing parts will be left intact.

- Replace Existing Parts – Select this option to add all parts to the destination equipment. All existing parts will be replaced with the new parts.
