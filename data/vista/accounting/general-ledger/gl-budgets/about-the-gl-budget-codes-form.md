# About the GL Budget Codes Form

Use the GL Budget Codes form to set up and maintain budget codes.
Budget codes are used to identify different budget revisions and to group budget changes together. You must have at least one budget code to enter any budget amounts. If you modify your budgets throughout the year, it is recommended that you set up at least two—Original and Current. Other codes might be used to identify Revision 1, Revision 2, etc.
Important:Vista will allow you to enter alpha characters in your budget codes and is case-sensitive. For example, the system considers the budget codes current and CURRENT as different.
