#  About the GL Budget Compare Form

Use the GL Budget Compare form for budget comparisons.
Access this form from the GL Budgets form by clicking the Compare button or by selecting File > Compare Monthly Amounts .
When accessed, this form defaults the fiscal year ending and GL account specified in GL Budgets. You can initialize amounts from existing balances (Actuals) or budget amounts for any fiscal year ending month/GL account. Once you enter the desired criteria, click Display to initialize values to the grid. You can then place the forms side-by-side for comparison.
Note: If desired, you can open this form multiple times, allowing you to compare multiple accounts, years, and budget codes at one time.
