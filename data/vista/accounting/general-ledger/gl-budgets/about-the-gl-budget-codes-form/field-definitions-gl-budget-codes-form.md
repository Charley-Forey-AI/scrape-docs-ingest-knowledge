# Field Definitions: GL Budget Codes Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the GL
 Budget Codes form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Budget Code

 Enter a code, up to 10 characters, that identifies a user-defined budget. It is recommended that you set up at least two; one for "Original" and the other for "Current."
Important:Vista will allow you to enter alpha characters in your account number and is case-sensitive. For example, the system considers the account numbers 2125-000-HI and 2125-000-hi as different.

##  Description

 Enter a description of this budget code, up 30 characters.
