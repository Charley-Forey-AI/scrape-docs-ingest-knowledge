# About the GL Chart of Accounts Copier Form

Use the GL Chart of Accounts Copier form to copy GL accounts within a company or between companies.
If using multiple profit centers, you can save time by copying all of the accounts from one profit center to another within the same company. Just set up the accounts for one profit center and copy them to the other profit centers.
You can also use this feature to copy an entire chart of accounts from one company to another. This is useful when multiple companies use the same or similar accounts. When copying from one company to another, you will need to change the Source GL company or the Destination GL Company, depending on which company is the active company.
Note: The copy process does not overwrite existing GL accounts; only new accounts are added. In addition, only active accounts are copied to the destination chart of accounts.
