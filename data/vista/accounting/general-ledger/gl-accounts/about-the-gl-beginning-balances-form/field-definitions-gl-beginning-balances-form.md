# Field Definitions: GL Beginning Balances Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the GL
 Beginning Balances form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Fiscal Year Ending

 Enter the calendar month of the fiscal year end for which beginning balances are being entered.

##  Beginning Balance

 Enter beginning balance for this account.

##  Dr/Cr

 This field defaults the Normal Balance type (debit or credit) specified for this GL account in GL Chart of Accounts, which may be overridden.

- Debit = D, +, or 1

- Credit = C, –, or 2
