# Field Definitions: GL Prior Years Activity Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the GL
 Prior Years Activity form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Fiscal Year Ending

 Indicate calendar month of the fiscal year end these balances reflect. It must be for a closed or prior year.

##  GL Account

 Enter a valid GL account code. The description displays for verification.

##  Beginning Balance

 Enter the Beginning Balance of the account.

##  Net Adjustments

 Enter the net activity of the account for the adjustment period.

##  Dr/Cr

 Specify whether net activity amount is debit (D) or credit (C).

##  Debits

 Enter the total net debit amount for this account, this period.
Note: Although you can enter both the total debits and credits (next field) for a period, it is recommended that you enter the total net activity for the period instead. In other words, if the total of debits and credits for the period results in a debit, enter only the total debit amount. If the total of debits and credits results in a credit, enter only the total credit amount.

##  Credits

 Enter the total net credit amount for this account, this period.
Note: Although you can enter both the total debits and credits (next field) for a period, it is recommended that you enter the total net activity for the period instead. In other words, if the total of debits and credits for the period results in a debit, enter only the total debit amount. If the total of debits and credits results in a credit, enter only the total credit amount.
