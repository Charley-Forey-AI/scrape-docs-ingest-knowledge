# Delete a Fiscal Year With Beginning Balance and/or Budget Data

Delete a fiscal year with beginning balance and/or budget
 data.

1. If you have beginning
 balance data for the fiscal year, open GL Beginning Balances.

1. In the Fiscal Year
 Ending field, enter the fiscal year you plan to delete.

1. Click the Beginning
 Balance column and do one of the following:

- Click the column to sort by
 descending amounts. This will display all amounts greater than 0.00 at
 the top.

- In the Filter Bar, enter
 <>0.00. This will filter out all 0.00 amounts so that only amounts
 greater than 0.00 display.

1. For each GL Account with
 a beginning balance greater than 0.00, change the
 Begin Balance
 field to 0.00. Save the record.

1. If you have budget data
 for the fiscal year, open GL Budgets. Click on the Grid tab.

1. In the FYEMO column,
 locate the fiscal year you plan to delete. Highlight all rows for that
 year.

1. Click the Delete button
 in the toolbar.A message displays confirming deletion of multiple rows.

1. Click Yes.A second message displays indicating form has related records that will
 also be deleted and asking to confirm the action.

1. Click Yes.The system deletes all budget codes/GL account records for that fiscal
 year.

1. Open GL Fiscal
 Years.

1. In the Fiscal Year
 Ending field, enter the fiscal year to delete or press
 F4
 for a list of valid fiscal years.

1. Click the Delete button
 in the toolbar.

The Fiscal Year is deleted.
