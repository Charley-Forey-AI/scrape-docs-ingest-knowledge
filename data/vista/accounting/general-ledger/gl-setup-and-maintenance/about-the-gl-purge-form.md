# About the GL Purge Form

Use the GL Purge form to purge transaction details and/or account balances and budgets.
The GL Purge form has two tabs: Transaction Detail and Summary Balances. This allows you to perform purges individually.

## Transaction Detail

This tab allows you to purge journal
 transactions. You can specify to purge transaction details for a specific journal or
 for all journals. You may keep detail for as long as desired, and are limited only
 to the amount of disk space available.
It is recommended that you do not purge
 transactions for open years. It is also suggested that only the system administrator
 be allowed to access this program.
Note:

- This purge is not tied to
 the year end close.

- You may not purge journal
 entries from open months.

- The Journal Transactions
 Report is no longer available after the journal entry purge.

- Trial Balance in detail
 format is no longer available after the journal entry purge.

## Summary Balances

This tab allows you to purge account
 summary and reference totals, as well as account balances and budgets. You cannot
 purge balance information for the current or prior year (based on the last month
 closed in GL).

## Comparison Financial Statements

Account balances may be maintained for
 as many years as desired, limited only by the disk space available. By keeping
 balances on your system for more than a year, you can create comparative financial
 statements.
Note: It is important to note that account balance
 purges are not tied to the year end close.
