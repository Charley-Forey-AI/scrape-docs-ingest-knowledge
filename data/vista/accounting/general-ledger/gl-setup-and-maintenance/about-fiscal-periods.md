# About Fiscal Periods

Many companies use Fiscal Years instead of Calendar Years as the basis for their accounting and financial reporting.
Viewpoint’s accounting system simplifies fiscal period accounting for you. When you enter data, you are asked for the accounting month to post it to—you will always enter the calendar date. If you do not run on a calendar year, the program calculates your fiscal period.
