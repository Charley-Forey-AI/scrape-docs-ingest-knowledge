# Delete a Fiscal Year without a Data Balance or
 Budget Data

You can delete a fiscal year as long as you have not begun posting transactions to any months within the fiscal year.
In addition, there cannot be any balances or budgets
 associated with the fiscal year.If you
 have not begun posting transactions, and you need to delete the fiscal year, you can
 do so using GL Fiscal Years. If balance and/or budget data exists for the fiscal
 year, the system will display a message indicating the information you need to
 remove. Once you have removed the data, you can then delete the fiscal year.

To delete a fiscal year that has no
 data balance or budget data:

1. Open GL Fiscal Years.


1. In the Fiscal Year
 Ending field, enter the fiscal year to delete or press F4
 for a list of valid fiscal years.

1. Click the Delete button in the toolbar.
