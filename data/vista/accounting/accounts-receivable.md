# Accounts Receivable

A brief overview of the Accounts Receivable module.
Accounts Receivable provides you with the tools needed to process a complete billing
 cycle, from entering invoices, to applying payments to those invoices, to handling
 reporting requirements.
By using Accounts Receivable to its fullest capacity, you can benefit from a full range of receivables management capabilities, which include:

- Invoice Entry

- Tailored Payment Terms

- Customer Statements

- Payment Discounts

- Cash Receipt Entry

- Retainage - Application & Release

- Adjustments, Credits, and Write-Offs

- Age Analysis Reporting

- Finance/Service Charge Application

- Sales Tax Reporting

- Miscellaneous Distributions

- Customer Accounts/Credit Histories

Accounts Receivable is fully integrated with the Job Cost, General Ledger, and Cash Management systems, providing up-to-date financial tracking and management. Additionally, when used in conjunction with Job Billing and/or Material Sales, it provides a flexible invoice generating and tracking system.
