# About the AR Calculate Finance Charge
 Form

You can use the AR Calculate Finance Charge form to have the system automatically calculate finance charges based on a set of criteria.
You can access this form by clicking Calculate FC Charges in the AR Finance Charge form.
Once you specify a transaction date for all system-generated finance charge invoices, you can then enter criteria to determine how this form pulls invoices for finance charge assessment.
For more information about using this form, click the link below.
