# Field Definitions: AR Misc Distribution Codes Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the AR Misc Distribution Codes form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Distribution Code

 Enter a distribution code, up to 10 characters, to use for tracking amounts of an invoice allocated to specific areas for reporting purposes. You can use distribution codes to track revenue for a particular area or for any other totals that you may need distinguished from total revenues.

##  Description

 Enter a description of the distribution code, up to 30 characters.

##  Description Rate

 Enter a rate for this distribution (e.g. 5.000000). The distribution rate is used to calculate potential taxes or assessments when printing Report Writer reports.
