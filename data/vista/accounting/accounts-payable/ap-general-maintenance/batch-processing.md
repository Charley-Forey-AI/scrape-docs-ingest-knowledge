# Batch Processing

Most of the posting forms in Accounts Payable require the creation and processing of batches in Accounts Payable to update information to the appropriate tables in Accounts Payable, as well as updating General Ledger and other related modules (such as Job Cost, Service Management, and Purchase Orders, to name a few).
