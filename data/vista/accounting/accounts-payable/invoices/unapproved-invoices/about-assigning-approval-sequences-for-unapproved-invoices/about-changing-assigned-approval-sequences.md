# About Changing Assigned Approval Sequences

You can change the approval sequence assignments for reviewers in
 the Reviewers tab of the unapproved invoice line (in the AP Unapproved Invoices form).
 If the
 reviewer in question is in the header Reviewers tab, you can change it there to affect all
 lines at once.
If you change approval sequence numbers for reviewers in HQ
 Reviewer Group, only newly created invoices/lines will be affected; changes to approval
 sequence numbers do not update for existing invoice lines unless you remove the group from
 the line/header, save the record, then re-add the group. While this updates the new
 approval sequence assignments on the Reviewer tabs, it also removes any existing reviewer
 approvals and will require those approvals once again.
