# Field Definitions: AP Clear Transactions Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the AP Clear Transactions form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Month

The Month field on the AP Clear Transactions form.
Specify the expense month of the transaction you wish to clear.

## AP Transaction #

The AP Transaction # field on the AP Clear Transactions form.
Specify the AP transaction you wish to clear. The transaction must be open and not currently in another batch.
