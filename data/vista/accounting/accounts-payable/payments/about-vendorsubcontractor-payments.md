# About Vendor/Subcontractor Payments

Once you have entered invoices into the system, you will be able to process payments to the vendor/subcontractor.

In Vista™, there are several payment methods to choose from:

- Checks

- Credit Service (credit cards)

- ePayments

- EFTs
