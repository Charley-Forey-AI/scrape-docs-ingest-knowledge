# AP EFT PreNote Download Form

Use the AP EFT PreNote Download form to generate prenote Electronic File Transfer (EFT) files that you send to your bank.
Some banks require a prenote transaction for new vendors, or
 account changes for existing vendors, before they can actively process EFTs. The prenote
 process allows the bank to validate the vendors’ bank accounts. See

 [Process EFT Prenotes](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/payments/electronic-funds-transfers-efts/process-eft-prenotes)
 for more information.
