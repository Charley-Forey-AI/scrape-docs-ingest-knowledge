# AP FTP Form

Use the AP FTP form to transmit credit service payment request files that you have saved to your system to Comdata, as well as retrieve completed transaction response files from Comdata.
You can use this form only if you have set up Comdata as your credit service provider. See [Setting up credit services with Comdata](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/payments/credit-card-payments/set-up-credit-services-with-comdata) for more information.
Note: You can also automatically transmit credit service payment files to Comdata from the AP Credit Service Download form. See [Generating Credit Service Files](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/payments/credit-card-payments/generate-credit-service-files).
