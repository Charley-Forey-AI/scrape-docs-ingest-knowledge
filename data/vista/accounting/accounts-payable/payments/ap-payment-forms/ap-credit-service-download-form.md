# AP Credit Service Download Form

Use the AP Credit Service Download form to generate credit service request files.
Access this form from the AP Payment Posting form by
 selecting Tasks > Generate Credit Service Export
 File.
Once you add payments to a payment batch in the AP Payment Posting form,
 you can generate credit service request files for vendors that you will pay by credit card.
 You can then transmit the files to the specified credit service provider.
