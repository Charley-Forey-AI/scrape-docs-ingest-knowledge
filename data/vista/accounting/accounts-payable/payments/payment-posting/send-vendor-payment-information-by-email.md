# Send Vendor Payment Information by Email

You can send payment remittance information to vendors via
 email.
When sending remittance information emails with the AP Email Pay Info
 form, you will filter potential recipients by payment time frame (payment
 date or payment month and batch ID) and you can choose to send an email
 to all vendors with recently processed/posted payments that meet the date
 criteria, or you can specify a single vendor.
The following steps discuss how to send payment remittance information
 by email.

1. Enter the account number that you
 used to make the payment in the
 CM Account
 field.

1. In the Select Criteria section of
 the form, select the appropriate filtering option: Email by Paid Date or Email by Paid Month and
 Batch. When you select the Email by Paid Date radio button, the system
 enables the Paid
 Date field. When you select the Email by Paid Month and Batch radio button, the
 system enables the Paid
 Mth and Batch ID fields.

1. Depending on the option you selected
 in step 2, enter the paid date in the
 Paid Date
 field or enter the paid month and batch ID in the
 Paid Mth
 and
 Batch ID
 fields, respectively.

1. Select the appropriate Email
 recipient option: Email
 All or Email Individual. When you select the Email Individual radio button, enter the
 specific vendor in the Vendor field.

1. Enter the return email address in
 the
 Return Email
 Address
 field or accept the default.

1. Enter the email subject and the text
 of the email in the
 Subject
 and
 Body
 fields, respectively.

1. Click Send. The system
 displays a confirmation dialog box.

1. Click Close. The system
 sends the email to the specified recipients.Note: The system sends payment information based on the
 option you selected from the Method of Payment Info Delivery field in the
 AP Vendors form. If you selected to send an email without an attachment, the system sends an
 email based only on the information that you set up in this form.
