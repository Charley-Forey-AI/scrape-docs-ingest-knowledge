# About T5018 Reporting: Canada

The Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) requires construction businesses to report payments made to subcontractors for construction services. Vista allows you to track these payments and then use either Vista or Aatrix to print T5018 slips and summaries. You must use Aatrix to create and submit the electronic file.
