# About Generating T5018 Reports

Once you have reviewed the invoices that you are using to report for T5018s, you will use Aatrix to generate and submit your T5018 reports.
You can print slip and summary reports for manual submission using Aatrix or Vista.
To print the T5018 slips or summary reports from Vista, you can either access the reports from the Accounts Payable Reports menu or from the AP T5018 Payments form by selecting Options > Reports.
Note: Before you run the T5018 process in Aatrix, make sure that you have completed the other steps in the T5018 reporting process. For more information, see [About T5018 Reporting: Canada](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/year-end-reporting/t5018s/about-t5018-reporting-canada).
