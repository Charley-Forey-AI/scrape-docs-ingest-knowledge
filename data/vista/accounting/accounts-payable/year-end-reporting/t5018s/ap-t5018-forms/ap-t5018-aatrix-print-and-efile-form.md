# AP T5018 Aatrix Print and eFile Form

Use the AP T5018 Aatrix Print & eFile form to generate a T5018 electronic file to submit to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).
Access this form via the AP T5018 Payment form by selecting Tasks > Launch Aatrix.
Once you enter the tax year and select the T5018 form, the system launches the
 Aatrix wizard where you can complete the process. See [Generate T5018 Reports Using Aatrix](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/year-end-reporting/t5018s/about-generating-t5018-reports/generate-t5018-reports-using-aatrix).Note: Before you can use the Aatrix application, you must have
 already installed it on your system. For details, see [Enable Aatrix on a Workstation](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/quarteryear-end-reporting/aatrix-reporting/enable-aatrix-on-a-workstation).
