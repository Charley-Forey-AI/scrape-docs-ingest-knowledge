# About the AP Paid Invoice Review Form

Use the AP Paid Invoice Review form to set invoices as subject to taxable reporting. The type of reporting depends on the country that you are processing in.

## Australia

Access this form from the [AP Taxable Payments](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/year-end-reporting/taxable-payment-reporting/ap-taxable-payments-form-australia) form by selecting Tasks  >  Review Paid Invoices. For more information, see [Setting Paid Invoices as Subject to Taxable Payments](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/year-end-reporting/taxable-payment-reporting/set-paid-invoices-as-subject-to-taxable-payments).

## Canada

Access this form from the [AP T5018 Payments](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/year-end-reporting/t5018s/ap-t5018-forms/ap-t5018-payments-form)
 form by selecting Tasks >  Review Paid
 Invoices. For more information, see [Setting Paid Invoices as Subject to T5018 Reporting](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/year-end-reporting/t5018s/set-paid-invoices-as-subject-to-t5018-reporting).
