# Field Definitions: AP T5018 Aatrix Print eFile Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the AP T5018 Aatrix Print eFile form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Tax Year

The Tax Year field in the AP T5018 Aatrix Print & eFile form.
This field defaults the tax year associated with the date specified in the
 Reporting Period End Date
 field in the AP T5018 Payments form. You may override the year if needed.

## Form Type

The Form Type selection box on the AP Aatrix - T5018 Print & eFile form.
Select the appropriate form by highlighting it in the selection box.Note: Currently, the only form type available for T5018s is T5018 Official Form
