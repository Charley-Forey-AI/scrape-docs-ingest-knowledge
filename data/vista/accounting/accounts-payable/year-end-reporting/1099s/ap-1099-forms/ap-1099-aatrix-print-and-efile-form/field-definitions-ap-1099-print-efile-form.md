# Field Definitions: AP 1099 Print eFile Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the AP 1099
 Print eFile form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Tax Year

The Tax Year field in the AP 1099 Aatrix Print and eFile form.
The default value is the tax year selected in the
 Calendar Year Ending
 Month field in the AP 1099 Download form. You may
 override the default if needed.
