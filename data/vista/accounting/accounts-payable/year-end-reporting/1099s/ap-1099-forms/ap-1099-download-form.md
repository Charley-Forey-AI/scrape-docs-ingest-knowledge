# AP 1099 Download Form

Use the AP 1099 Download form to initiate electronic filing of Federal 1099s via Aatrix or to download a 1099 e-file locally for submission to the IRS.
Note: Electronic filing is the only supported method when using Vista software.

Access the AP 1099 Download form via the AP 1099 Processing form by clicking Tasks > Download.
Although there are multiple types of 1099 forms, Vista only supports e-file generation for Form 1099-NEC. If you need to e-file other 1099 forms, you must use Aatrix.
Once you enter the required information, clicking Launch Aatrix opens the AP 1099 Aatrix Print and eFile Form, which you will then use to initiate 1099 processing via Aatrix. Clicking Download saves the 1099-NEC e-file locally for submission to the IRS.
