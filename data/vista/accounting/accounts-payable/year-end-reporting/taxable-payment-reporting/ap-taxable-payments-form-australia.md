# AP Taxable Payments Form (Australia)

Use the AP Taxable Payments form as a central area for preparing taxable payments reporting data for submission to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).
Prior to preparing reports with this form, you must first set up a current tax year, along with company-specific information that is shared among the reports that you submit to the ATO. Once you set up this standard information, you can prepare the Taxable Payments Annual report.
