# Tax Year Closed

Once you have finished preparing taxable payment reporting for the current year, you should close the tax year to prevent any unintentional updates. To close the tax year, check this box for the current tax year.
For more information on taxable payment reporting, see [Generating Taxable Payments Annual Reports](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/year-end-reporting/taxable-payment-reporting/generate-taxable-payments-annual-reports-australia).
