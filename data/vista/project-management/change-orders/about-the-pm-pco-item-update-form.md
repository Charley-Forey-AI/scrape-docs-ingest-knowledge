# About the PM PCO Item Update Form

Use this form to assign a contract item to all of the items on a PCO that do not have one.

1. From the PM Pending Change Orders form, open a PCO.

1. Select Tasks > Update PCO
 Items. This will open the PM PCO Item Update form. Note: A "Selected PCO Items cannot be updated"
 message will display if all of the items on the PCO have already been associated
 with a contract item.

1. Enter the contract item
 number that you want to assign in the Contract Item field, or press
 F4 to select it from a list.

1. Click Update.
 This will close the form and assign the selected contract item to all of the items on
 the PCO that are not already been associated with a contract item number.
