# Viewpoint For Projects (VFP) Integration Overview

​Viewpoint For Projects™ integration allows you to manage the publication of Vista Project Management Word or PDF documents (e.g., RFIs, Change Orders, etc.) to the web-based, collaborative workspace provided by Viewpoint For Projects™.
This feature allows you, as an administrator, to determine who needs to integrate with Viewpoint For Projects™, how to invite users, and how to publish documents to Viewpoint For Projects™. You may choose to publish your documents to Viewpoint For Projects™ in either document-by-document or bulk uploads.
To successfully integrate with Viewpoint For Projects™, certain admin-setup steps must be performed in order to successfully integrate your Project Management documents with Viewpoint For Projects™.
