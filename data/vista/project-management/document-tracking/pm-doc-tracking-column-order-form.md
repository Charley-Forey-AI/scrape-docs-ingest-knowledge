# PM Doc Tracking Column Order Form

Use this form to rearrange the grid columns for each grid form
 on a selected document tracking view in PM Document Tracking Grids.
Access this form by clicking the Reorder Grid
 Columns button in PM Document Tracking Grids. To reposition a column, click on the
 column in the Column Name selection box and move it to the desired position. Once you
 have finished repositioning all desired columns, click the Update button to save the
 changes and return to the PM Document Tracking Grids form.
To undo changes, press the Reset button and all columns will be reset to their original positions (i.e., the positions they were in when you opened this form). You can also press the Close button to cancel changes and exit form.
