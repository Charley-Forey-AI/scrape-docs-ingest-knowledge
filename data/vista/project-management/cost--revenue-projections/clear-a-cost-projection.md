# Clear a Cost Projection

You can clear a projection in PM Cost Projections by selecting Tasks > Clear Projection.

1. Open PM Cost Projections.

1. In the Projection Date field, enter the projection date of your projection batch or press F4 to select from a list of existing projection dates.The Project field automatically populates based on the selected projection date.

1. Select Tasks > Clear Projection.A message displays asking if you are sure you want to clear the cost projection.

1. Select Yes.

The system clears all entries for the selected projection date and project and then deletes the batch.
