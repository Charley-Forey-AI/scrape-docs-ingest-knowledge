# Link a Customer / Vendor from Vista to a ProjectSight Company

In ProjectSight, import or link Vista AR customers and AP
 vendors as new or existing companies in ProjectSight.

1. Create or update a customer / vendor in Vista. For more information about setting up
 these records, see [AR Customers Form](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:11000031) and [AP Vendors Form](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:10000052).

1. Assign the customer / vendor status to Active. Important: Setting the
 Active status
 is required to sync the record to ProjectSight.

1. In ProjectSight, link a ProjectSight company to your Vista customer and vendor records using Link to Vista. ProjectSight Help: For more information about linking in
 ProjectSight, see the
 ProjectSight Help on [Company Linking](https://prod.projectsightapp.trimble.com/Web/Web%20Help/Content/ERP/Web-ERP-Company-Linking.htm).

1. After linking, you must maintain customer and
 vendor data in both Vista and ProjectSight.
