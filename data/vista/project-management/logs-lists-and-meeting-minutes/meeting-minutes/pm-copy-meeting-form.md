# PM Copy Meeting Form

Use the PM Copy Meeting form to copy meetings from one project to another.
This can save you time if you need to create a new meeting and a similar one already exists on another project, or you have a standard group of meetings that apply to each project.
Some of the meetings details will not be copied - for example, the meeting time, next meeting date/time/location, and other information that is probably specific to the meeting that is being copied.
