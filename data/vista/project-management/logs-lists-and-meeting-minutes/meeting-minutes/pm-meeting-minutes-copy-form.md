# PM Meeting Minutes Copy Form

Use the PM Meeting Minutes Copy form to copy an existing
 meeting in a project.
Once a meeting agenda has been created and you have recorded
 the meeting information, you can use the PM Meeting Minutes Copy form to create the next
 meeting.
