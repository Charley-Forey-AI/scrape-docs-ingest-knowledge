# PM Drawing Revision Initialize Form

Use the PM Drawing Revision Initialize form to create a
 revision on multiple drawing logs. For example, add revision "100-10" to several
 drawing logs at once.
You can also add a single revision to a drawing log by creating a new item in the
 lower portion of the PM Drawing Logs form.
