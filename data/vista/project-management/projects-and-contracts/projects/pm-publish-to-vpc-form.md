# PM Publish to VPC Form

Use this form to publish selected Word or PDF documents to the
 web-based, collaborative workspace provided by Viewpoint For Projects™ (VFP). You can select document types from the Document
 Category pull-down list, and also select to Include Previously Published Documents.
