# Initialize Subcontracts to Other Documents

You can initialize subcontracts to "other" documents for tracking purposes via the PM Other Sub Initialization form.

1. In PM Other Documents, select File > Initialize Subs.The PM Other Sub Initialization form displays.

1. Complete the fields in the upper portion of the form. You must complete entry in all fields to enable the Initialize button.

1. Select the subcontracts to initialize using one of the following methods:

- Click Select All Subs to select all subcontracts in the grid.

- Manually select subcontracts by clicking the column to the left of the Sub numbers. Press SHIFT for consecutive selection or CTRL for random selection.

1. Click Initialize.
