# PM SL Workflow Item Reviewers Form

Use the PM SL Workflow Item Reviewers form to review the progress of a subcontract's review/approval workflow.

Access this form by selecting the Workflow button in PM Subcontracts after a subcontract has been submitted for approval. You can access this form throughout the workflow process until the subcontract is Approved (that is, the Workflow Status field is set to Approved). Once approved, the Workflow Status button is disabled.
This form shows each step in the review/approval process, along with the assigned approver, the approval status, whether the reviewer is optional, and any comments or notes associated with the workflow step.
With the exception of the Notes field, all fields on this form are display only. The Notes field is enabled for all users, allowing anyone to enter comments/notes as needed during the review/approval process.
