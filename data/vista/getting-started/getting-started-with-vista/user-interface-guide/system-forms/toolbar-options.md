# Toolbar Options

The system has a number of standard options that display in a
 toolbar across the top of most forms. All of these options are also available from the menu
 at the top of the form.
Toolbar OptionNameDescription
Field PropertiesOpens the [Field Properties](/en/vista/vista/getting-started/getting-started-with-vista/user-interface-guide/system-forms/about-the-field-properties-form) form, which allows you
 to set up user and system overrides.You can also open this form by pressing F3 in a
 field, or selecting Tools > Field
 Properties from the menu at the top of the form.

Field LookupOpens the lookup window associated with the
 current field (if applicable), displaying a list of valid recordsYou can also open the
 lookup window by pressing F4, or selecting
 Tools > Field Lookup from the menu at the top of the form.

Field SetupOpens the setup form associated with the
 current field.You can
 also launch the setup form by selecting Tools > Field Setup from the menu at the top of the form.

New RecordCreates a new record.You can also create a
 new record by selecting Records > New from the menu at the top of the form.

Save RecordSaves the current record (new or
 existing).You can
 also save a record by pressing CTRL + S, or
 selecting Records > Save from the menu at the top of the form.

Delete RecordDeletes the current record.You can also delete a
 record by selecting it and then press DELETE, or selecting
 Records > Delete from the menu at the top of the form.

Undo RecordReset any information that you have entered
 but have not saved.You
 can also select Records > Undo from the menu at the top of the form.

Refresh RecordsRefreshes the data that displays in the
 form.You can also
 select File > Refresh from the menu at the top of the form.

Form SearchAvailable in some forms. Opens the [Form Search](/en/vista/vista/getting-started/getting-started-with-vista/user-interface-guide/system-forms/search-form-records/about-the-search-panel).You can also open Form
 Search form by selecting Records > Form Search from the menu at the top of the form.

CalculatorOpens the system calculator.You can also open the
 calculator by selecting Tools > Calculator from the menu at the top of the form.

AttachmentsOpens the Attachments window, allowing you
 to add, delete, or view attachments. If you click the black arrow to the
 right, provides a drop-down menu of the following options:

- Show Attachments – Opens the Attachments
 window, allowing you to add, delete, or view attachments.

- Index Search – Opens the Attachments
 Index Search form, allowing you to search for existing
 attachments.

ScanningOpens the Scan window, allowing you to scan
 and attach documents. If you click the black arrow to the right,
 provides a drop-down menu of the following options:

- Scan Single Image – Opens the Scan
 window, allowing you to scan and attach a single
 image/document.

- Open Image Batch – Opens the Scanning
 Batch Selection window, allowing you to select a batch of
 documents to attach or allows you to create a new batch of
 scanned documents.

SpellingChecks for spelling errors in the selected
 field. The button is disabled when it does not apply - for example on
 numeric fields.You can
 also check spelling by selecting Tools > Spelling.
Note: The
 spell check does not share the MS Office user dictionary. You
 will need to add additional words to this dictionary as
 necessary.

First Record, Previous Record, Next Record,
 Last RecordUse these buttons to move through the
 records that display in the form.
View Header, View Header and Detail, View
 DetailAvailable in Header/Detail forms only.Use these buttons to expand and collapse the
 header and footer in the form.
