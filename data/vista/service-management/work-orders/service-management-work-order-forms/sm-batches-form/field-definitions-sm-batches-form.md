# Field Definitions: SM Batches Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Batches form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Include My Batches Only

Check this box to show only batches you created in the Batches grid.
Uncheck this box to show all batches (created by all users) in the Batches grid.
Note: The grid displays all unposted batches, including those with validation errors and those that were interrupted during the validation or posting process. The Status field identifies the processing status of each batch (i.e. Open, Errors, Posting, or Valid).
