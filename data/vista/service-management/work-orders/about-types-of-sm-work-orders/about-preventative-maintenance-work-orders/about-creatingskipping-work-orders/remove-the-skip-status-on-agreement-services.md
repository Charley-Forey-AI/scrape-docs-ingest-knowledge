# Remove the Skip Status on Agreement
 Services

You can remove the "skipped" status from any scheduled
 agreement service that you previously marked to skip to allow generating work orders for that agreement service.

1. Open
 SM Generate PM Work Orders.

1. In the
 Search Criteria section, enter the applicable filter
 criteria.

1. Select the View Skipped Work
 Orders checkbox.

1. Click Search.The Skipped Work Orders grid is populated with all
applicable scheduled services that are
 flagged as "skipped".

1. Clear
 the Skip
 box for each applicable service in the grid or click
 ClearAll to clear the Skip
 checkbox for all services in the grid.

1. Click Process.

The system removes the "skipped" status
 for all selected items and displays a message indicating how many items were
 "un-skipped". Once you close the message, the system removes the selected
 items from the Skipped Work Orders grid.Note: To also generate a work order
 for the service, check the Create box
 and click Process. As a result, the system
 removes the Skipped status for all selected items, creates a
 work order for items with the Create box checked, and displays a
 message indicating how many work orders were created and how
 many were "un-skipped". Once you close the message, the
 system removes the selected items from the
 grid.
