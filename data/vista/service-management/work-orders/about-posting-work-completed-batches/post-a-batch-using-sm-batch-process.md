# Post a Batch Using SM Batch Process

You can process work completed batches that are in an unposted state due to validation errors or interruptions during the
 validation or posting process caused by power failure or system shutdown.

1. Open the SM Batch Process form.

1. Use the Month and Batch# fields to select the
 batch to process or accept the defaults (if you accessed this form by clicking
 the Post button in SM Batches).

1. Click Validate.  The system validates the batch and creates the
 applicable audit reports.

1. Click

 Preview
 to preview the reports or
 Print
 to print the reports.

1. In the
 Posting Date
 field, enter the posting date or accept the default (current
 date).

1. Click Post. The batch is posted and a message displays indicating the
 batch was posted successfully.

1. Click
 OK
 to close the form.
