# Field Definitions: SM Agreement Billings Due

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM Agreement Billings Due form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Customer

Enter the customer by which to filter agreements for billing. Only agreements for this customer that have not been billed and that meet other filter criteria will display in the grid.
Leave blank if not filtering by customer.

## Agreement Type

Enter the agreement type by which to filter agreements for billing. Only agreements assigned this agreement type that have not been billed and meet other filter criteria will display in the grid.
Leave blank if not filtering by agreement type.

## Service Center

Service Center field in the Search Criteria section of the SM Agreement Billings Due form.
Enter the service center by which to filter agreements for billing. Only divisions assigned this agreement type that have not been billed and meet other filter criteria will display in the grid.

Leave blank if not filtering by agreement type.

## Division

Division field in the Search Criteria section of the SM Agreement Billings Due form.
Enter the division by which to filter agreements for billing. Only divisions assigned this agreement type that have not been billed and meet other filter criteria will display in the grid.
Leave blank if not filtering by agreement type.

## Due Within the Next __ Days

Required.
Enter the number of days by which to filter agreements for billing. Only agreements that are due within the specified number of days and meet other filter criteria will be included in the grid.

## Create

Check this box to create an invoice for this agreement.
Leave this box unchecked if you do not want to create an invoice for agreement at this time.
Note: The system will automatically check/uncheck this box for all agreements in the grid when using the Check All or Clear All buttons (respectively).
