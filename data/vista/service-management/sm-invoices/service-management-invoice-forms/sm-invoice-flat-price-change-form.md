# SM Invoice Flat Price Change Form

Use the SM Invoice Flat Price Change form to change the billing amount for a work order scope.
You will only need to use this form if you need to bill a portion of the total flat price amount.
Click the link below for more information about using this form.
[Adjust the Billing Amount for a Flat Price Scope](/en/vista/vista/service-management/sm-invoices/about-billing-flat-price-work-orders/adjust-the-billing-amount-for-a-flat-price-scope#task-4514--en__task-4514)
