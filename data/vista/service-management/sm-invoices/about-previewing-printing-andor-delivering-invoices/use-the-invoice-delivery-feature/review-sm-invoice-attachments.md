# Review SM Invoice Attachments

Before you deliver invoices to selected recipients, you can review attachments that
 you are including with the invoice.
To review attachments for a work order invoice in SM
 Invoices:

1. From the Vista main menu, go
 to Service Management > Programs > SM Invoices.The SM Invoice form
 displays.

1. In the Invoice Status
 section, select Invoiced.

1. In the Delivery Status
 section, select Delivered.


1. Use the Search Criteria to
 filter the invoices shown in the grid and click Search.The grid refreshes to show only
 those invoices that meet the search criteria.

1.  In the grid, select the
 Select checkbox next to
 the invoice you want to review.

1. Click Open. The SM Invoice Review form appears.


1. Select the Delivery tab.

1. Highlight a sequence that
 has the Has
 Attachments checkbox selected.

1. Click the Attachments icon. The Attachment form appears, with all
 associated attachments.

1. Double-click any particular attachment to review it.
