# Edit and Process a Single Pending SM Invoice

You can edit and process a single pending invoice using the SM Invoice form.
Using the Search Criteria, you can filter the
 invoices so that only those invoices you need to work on are displayed in the grid.

1. Open the SM Invoice form.

1. In the Invoice Status
 section, select Pending Invoice.

1. In the Invoice
 Number field of the Search Criteria, enter the invoice to edit.

1. Click Search.  The grid refreshes to show only the invoice you
 selected.

1. Double-click on the
 invoice to open the related invoice form. Depending on the invoice type (work
 order or agreement), this will be either SM Invoice Review or SM Agreement
 Invoice Review.

1. Edit the invoice as
 needed.Note: For agreement invoices, only the invoice
 header information can be changed, as there are no invoice lines. For work
 order invoices, you can edit the header information and the individual
 invoice lines. For more information, see [Editing Work Order Invoices](/en/vista/vista/service-management/sm-invoices/about-editingprocessing-invoices-via-sm-invoice/edit-a-work-order-invoice).

1. Click Process.  The AR Batch Process form opens.

1. Validate and post the
 batch.

Once you post the invoice, you are returned to the SM
 Invoices form and the changes are updated to the grid; however, since the invoice is no
 longer pending, they will be only be available in the Invoiced grid (accessed by
 selecting the Invoiced status).
