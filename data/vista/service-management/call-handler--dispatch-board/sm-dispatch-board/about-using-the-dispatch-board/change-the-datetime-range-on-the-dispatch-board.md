# Change the Date/Time Range on the Dispatch Board

You can change the date range displayed on the Dispatch Board
 at any time.
The following describes the methods you can
 use to change the date/time range on the Dispatch Board.

1. Open SM Dispatch Board and select the Dispatch Board to work with.

1. Use any of the following methods to change the dates displayed on the Dispatch Board:OptionDescription
Date TabsClick on the tab
 that best meets your current viewing needs: Hourly, 1 Day, 3 Day, 1 Week, 2
 Week, or Choose Dates.
Date SelectorSelect a date using one of the following
 options:

- Today - Click on this button to show today's date. For all date tabs
 except Hourly and 1 Day, the remaining dates will shift
 accordingly.

- Go To Date - Click this button to access the Date Picker. Then scroll to
 the desired month and select a date. For all date tabs except Hourly and 1
 Day, the remaining dates will shift accordingly.

- Choose Dates - This option is only available when on the Choose Dates
 tab. Select the beginning and ending date in a range of dates and click the
 Go button.

Previous/NextClick on one of the following VCR buttons
 to change the date or time (Hourly tab):

- Click  to move to the previous period. The
 "period" will differ depending on which tab you are on. For example, if you
 are on the 1 Week tab, this will move you to the previous week; however, if
 you are on the Hourly tab, this will move you to the same time frame (e.g.
 8:00 am - 3:30 pm) on the previous day.

- Click  to move to the previous hour (Hourly tab)
 or previous day (1 Day, 3 Day, 1 Week, 2 Week, or Choose Dates tabs). The
 remaining hours or days will shift accordingly.

- Click  to move to the next hour (Hourly tab) or
 next day (1 Day, 3 Day, 1 Week, 2 Week, or Choose Dates tabs). The remaining
 hours or days will shift accordingly.

- Click  to move to the next period. The "period"
 will differ depending on which tab you are on. For example, if you are on
 the 1 Week tab, this will move you to the next week; however, if you are on
 the Hourly tab, this will move you to the same time frame (e.g. 8:00 am -
 3:30 pm) on the next day.
