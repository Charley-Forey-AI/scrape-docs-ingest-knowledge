# Field Definitions: SM Scopes On Hold Grid Only Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Scopes On Hold Grid Only form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Scope

Display only, the work order scope sequence.

## On Hold

Check this box to place this work order scope on hold.
Leave unchecked if you are not placing this work order scope on hold.

## Hold Reason

Enter the reason for placing the work order scope on hold (e.g. waiting for parts, inclement weather, waiting for another task to be completed, etc.).

## Follow Up Date

Enter the date on which to follow up on the hold for this work order scope.
 For example, say you are placing the scope on hold because the parts you need will take a week or so to arrive. In this case, this date might represent the date you would review the situation and determine whether you can remove the hold and schedule/reschedule the work order.
