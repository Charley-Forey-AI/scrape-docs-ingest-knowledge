# Field Definitions: SM Dispatch Scopes On Hold Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Dispatch Scopes On Hold form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## On Hold Reason

Enter the reason for placing the work order on hold (e.g. waiting for parts, inclement weather, waiting for another task to be completed, etc.).

## Follow Up Date

Enter the date on which to follow up on this hold.
 For example, say you are placing the work order on hold because the parts you need will take a week or so to arrive. In this case, this date might represent the date you would review the situation and determine whether you can remove the hold and schedule/reschedule the work order.
