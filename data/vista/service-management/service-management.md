# Service Management

Service Management is a tool that allows you to streamline small job work order processing, increase field technician productivity, and improve service quality and cash flow.
 Work Order Management (the first module in a multi-year release of Service Management), provides streamlined work order processing, quick customer response with inbound call taking, flexible and accurate service pricing, customer site management with work order and equipment history tracking, and integration with accounting.
