# Field Definitions: SM Agreement Service Defer Schedule Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Agreement Service Defer Schedule form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Date

Date field on the field on the SM Deferral Schedule Mod form.
Enter the deferral date. When running the deferral process, the system will use this date in conjunction with the "through date" specified in the agreement to determine if the specified amount should be recognized.

## Amount

Amount field on the field on the SM Deferral Schedule Mod form.
Enter the deferral amount. The amount specified here must be less than or equal to the Total Remaining amount for the agreement.

## Notes

Notes field on the field on the SM Deferral Schedule Mod form.
Enter any miscellaneous notes about this item.
