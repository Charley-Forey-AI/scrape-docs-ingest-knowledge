# SM Deferral Schedule Mod Form

Use the SM Deferral Schedule Mod form to modify the revenue deferral schedule on an active agreement service without creating an amendment.
You can access this form via the SM Service form by selecting the Deferral tab on a periodic service flagged for separate billing, and then clicking Modify Deferral Schedule.
Click the link below for more information about using this form.
