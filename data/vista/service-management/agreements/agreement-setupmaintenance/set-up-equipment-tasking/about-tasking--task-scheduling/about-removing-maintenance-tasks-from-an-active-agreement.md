# About Removing Maintenance Tasks from an Active Agreement

You can remove class maintenance tasks from an active agreement without creating an amendment.
You will generally remove tasks in this manner when you have no other changes needed on the agreement that would require amending the agreement.
When you remove a class maintenance task, it is removed from the Tasks list of the associated agreement service. However, it will remain on the Tasks list in SM Agreement Maintenance. To remove tasks from the agreement completely, you must remove the related serviceable item.
