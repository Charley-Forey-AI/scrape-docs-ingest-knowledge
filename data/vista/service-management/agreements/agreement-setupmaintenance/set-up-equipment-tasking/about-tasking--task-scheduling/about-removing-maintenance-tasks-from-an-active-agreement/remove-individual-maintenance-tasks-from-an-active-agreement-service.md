# Remove Individual Maintenance Tasks from an Active Agreement Service

You can remove individual maintenance tasks from an active
 agreement service.

1. From the main menu,
 select Service Management >
 Programs > SM
 Agreements.

1. In the Agreement field, enter the active agreement to work with or
 press F4 to select from a list of agreements.

1. Click Tasking. The SM Agreement Maintenance form displays.

1. From the Tasks tab,
 clear the Include checkbox for each
 maintenance task you want removed from the agreement.

1. Close the form.

The system removes the selected
 maintenance tasks from the Tasks list of the associated agreement service,
 along with the related requirements (labor, equipment, material, and
 miscellaneous).
Note: Deselecting all tasks for a class
 maintenance / serviceable item in SM Agreement Maintenance will also
 deselect the serviceable item in SM Serviceable Item Selection. However, it
 will not remove the related agreement service from the agreement.
