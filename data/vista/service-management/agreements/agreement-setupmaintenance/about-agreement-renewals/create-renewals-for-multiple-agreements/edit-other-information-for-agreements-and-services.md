# Edit Other Information for Agreements and Services

When renewing agreements, you can edit the information for the agreement and its agreement
 services using the SM Agreements form.

1. Open the SM Agreement Renewal form.

1. In the
 Grid
 Shown
 field, select Renewals.

1. Use the Type, Customer, and/or Only Show
 Agreements with Auto Renew fields to filter the renewals shown
 in the grid. As you tab to each field, the grid refreshes based on the values
 entered.For more information about each
 field, refer to the F1 help.

1. Click Refresh.  The grid refreshes to show all agreement renewals
 meeting the selected criteria.

1. Select an agreement to
 edit in the first grid.

1. Click Edit Agreement. The SM Agreements form displays.

1. Edit the agreement as
 needed. This includes budgets, agreement services, billing and amortization
 schedules, and task scheduling.

1. Save the agreement and
 close the SM Agreements form to return to the SM Agreement Renewal
 form.

1. Repeat the process for each
 agreement you want to edit.

Activate the
 selected agreements. For more information, see [Activate Renewals for Multiple
 Agreements](/en/vista/vista/service-management/agreements/agreement-setupmaintenance/about-agreement-renewals/activate-renewals-for-multiple-agreements).
