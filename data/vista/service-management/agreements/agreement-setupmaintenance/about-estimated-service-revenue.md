# About Estimated Service Revenue

 If you have agreement services that are not included in the price of the agreement, the system calculates estimated revenue for those services.
The Est. Service Revenue field in SM Agreements displays the total revenue for agreement services (set up in SM Service) that are not included in the agreement price; that is, services with a price method other than Included in Agreement. The system calculates the estimated revenue dependent on how the price is applied for the service.
