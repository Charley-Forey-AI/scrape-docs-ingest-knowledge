# Edit a Deferral Schedule

You can modify the SM Agreement Deferral Service schedule on
 an active or expired agreement without creating an amendment.
When modifying an deferral schedules for agreements, you must make sure that the
 total amount of all deferral sequences equal the total amount of the agreement. The following steps detail how to edit
 an deferral schedule using the SM Deferral Schedule Mod form in
 SM Agreements.

1. Launch the SM Agreements
 form.

1. Select the agreement for
 the deferral schedule you want to alter.

1. Select the Work Schedule
 tab.

1. Double-click the
 schedule you want to apply the deferral to. The SM Service form appears.

1. Click on the Deferral
 tab.

1. Highlight the deferral
 you want to modify, and click Modify Deferral Schedule. The SM Deferral Schedule Mod
 form appears.

1. In the Date field, adjust the deferral schedule date.

1. In the Amount field, adjust the amount for the deferral schedule.


1. In the Notes field, enter any notes that apply to the deferral.

1. Save the record.
