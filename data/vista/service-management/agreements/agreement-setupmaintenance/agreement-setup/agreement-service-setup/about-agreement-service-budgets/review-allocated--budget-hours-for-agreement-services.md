# Review Allocated / Budget Hours for
 Agreement Services

If you are using the Tasking and Budget features, you can
 review allocated and budgeted hours for agreement services prior to activating the agreement
 using the Work Orders tab in SM Agreements.
To view person hours and budget hours for
 agreement services, you must have:

- Defined average person hours for class
 maintenance tasks in SM Class Maintenance.

- Auto-generated agreement services
 using the Tasking feature in SM Agreements.

- Entered labor requirements for each
 service in SM Service or labor hours / costs in SM Service Detail.

- Set up task schedules using the Task
 Scheduling feature in SM Agreements.

- Set up budget information by division
 and/or call type on the Budget tab in SM Agreements.

- Set up labor allocations in
 SM Agreement Labor Allocations (Labor Allocation tab in SM Agreements).


The grid displays all potential work orders
 for the agreement by service site, each showing the person hours and budget hours in a
 drill-down format. The first level shows the total person hours and budget hours for the
 service site, with additional levels showing person hours and budget hours at the
 serviceable item and agreement service levels. You can toggle the display order of the
 additional levels using the Sites / Items / Services drop-down
 option (below the Current Status field).
Note: The Work Orders grid displays agreement
 services that you set up and scheduled manually; however, it does not show person hours or
 budget hours, regardless of whether you have defined them for the service.
You can review the person hours and budget hours at each
 level of the drill down. Services with the same service site and service date are
 grouped on the same work order. Services having the same service site but with differing
 service dates or those flagged for separate work orders will show as separate entries in
 the grid.

1. By default, the drill-down levels are displayed in Items / Services order. To view the drill-down levels in Services / Items order, select Sites > Services > Items from the Sites / Items / Services drop-down.

1. Click + to the left of the desired Service Site record.The grid expands to the second level.Note: If you selected the Sites / Items / Services display option, the second level shows the total average person hours and budget hours for each serviceable item. If you selected the Sites / Services / Items display option, the second level shows the average person hours and budget hours for each service by division/call type

1. To drill down to level three, click + to the left of the desired record.The grid expands to the third level.Note: If you selected the Sites / Items / Services display option, the third level shows the average person hours and budget hours for each service by division/call type. If you selected the Sites / Services / Items display option, the third level shows the average person hours and budget hours for each serviceable item.
