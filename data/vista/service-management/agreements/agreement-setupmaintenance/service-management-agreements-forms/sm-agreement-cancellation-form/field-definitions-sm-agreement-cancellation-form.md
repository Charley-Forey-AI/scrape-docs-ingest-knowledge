# Field Definitions: SM Agreement Cancellation Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Agreement Cancellation form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Agreement

Display only, the agreement number for this quote.

## Revision

Display only, the revision number of this quote.

## Type

Display only, the agreement type specified for this quote.

## Customer

Display only, the customer associated with this quote.
