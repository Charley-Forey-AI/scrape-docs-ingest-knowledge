# Tax Type

The Tax Type field on the SM Agreement Billing Schedule form.
Select the tax type for this agreement billing schedule or leave blank (default) if taxes are not applicable.

- 1 - Sales

- 3 - VAT
Note: For Australian and Canadian companies (where SM company's AR
 company is set up with a Default Country of AU or CA in HQ
 Company Setup), this field defaults to 3-VAT.
