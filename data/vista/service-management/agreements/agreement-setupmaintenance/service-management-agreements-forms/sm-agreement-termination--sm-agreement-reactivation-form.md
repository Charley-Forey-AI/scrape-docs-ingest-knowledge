# SM Agreement Termination / SM Agreement
 Reactivation Form

Use the SM Agreement Termination / SM Agreement Reactivation
 form to terminate agreements that are no longer valid or to reactivate agreements that were
 terminated prematurely or in error.
 The title and functionality of this form changes depending on the access method:

- SM Agreement Termination - Displays when you click the Terminate button in SM Agreements for an active agreement.

- SM Agreement Reactivation - Displays when you click the Reactivate button in SM Agreements for a terminated agreement.
