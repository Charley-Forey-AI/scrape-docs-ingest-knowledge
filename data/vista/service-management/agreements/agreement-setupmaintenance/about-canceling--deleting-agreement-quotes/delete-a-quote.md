# Delete a Quote

You can delete an agreement quote using the SM Agreement Cancellation
 form.

1. Open the SM Agreements
 form.

1. Select the quote to
 delete (must have a Revision status of Quote or Cancelled Quote).

1. Click Delete Quote.The SM Agreement Cancellation form displays.

1. Review the quote
 information to ensure you are deleting the right quote.Note: You cannot change the information
 displayed in this form; it is informational only.

1. Click Delete Quote.


The quote is deleted and you are returned to the SM Agreements form.
