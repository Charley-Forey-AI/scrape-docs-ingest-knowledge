# Cancel a Quote

You can cancel an agreement quote prior to activation using the SM Agreement Cancellation
 form.

1. Launch the
 SM Agreements form.

1. Select the quote to
 cancel (must have a Revision status of Quote).

1. Click Cancel Quote.The SM Agreement Cancellation form displays.

1. Review the quote
 information to ensure you are canceling the right quote.Note: You cannot change the information
 displayed in this form; it is informational only.

1. Click Cancel Quote.

The quote is canceled and you are returned to the SM Agreements
 form.
