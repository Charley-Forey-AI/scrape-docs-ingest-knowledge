# Reopen a Canceled Quote

You can reopen a canceled quote using the SM Agreement
 Cancellation form.

1. Open the
 SM Agreements form.

1. Select the quote to
 reopen (must have a Revision status of Cancelled Quote).

1. Click Reopen Quote.The SM Agreement Cancellation form displays.

1. Review the quote
 information to ensure you are reopening the right quote.Note: You cannot change the information
 displayed in this form; it is informational only.

1. Click Re-open Quote.

The quote is reopened and you are returned to the SM Agreements
 form.
