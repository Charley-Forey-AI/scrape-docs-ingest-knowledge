# Set Up Standard Item Overrides for Service Sites

Set up standard item overrides for service sites.

1. From the Vista main
 menu, select Service Management > Programs.

1. Double-click the SM
 Service Sites icon to open the SM Service Sites form.

1. In the Service
 Site field, enter the service site for which to set up standard
 item overrides (must be a customer service site or a job service site using a
 Markup costing method).

1. Click the Override Rates
 button to open the SM Rate Override Base form.

1. Click the Standard Item
 Overrides button to open the SM Standard Item Overrides form.

1. In the Standard
 Item field, enter the standard item for which you are setting up
 an override bill rate.

1. In the  Billable
 Rate field, enter the override billable rate to use for the
 specified standard item.

1. Save the record.
