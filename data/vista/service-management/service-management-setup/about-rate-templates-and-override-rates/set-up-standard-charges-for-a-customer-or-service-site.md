# Set Up Standard Charges for a Customer or Service Site

You can set up standard charges for items that you always
 charge a customer or service site when performing service work.
 Standard charges are standard items (defined in SM
 Standard Items) that you always charge a customer and/or service site when performing
 service work or small jobs (e.g. travel fees, waste disposal, etc.).Standard charges
 defined at the service site level override those defined at the customer level.
 Therefore, make sure you add any standard charges defined for the customer that also
 apply to the service site.

1. Open the SM Customers or
 SM Service Site form, depending on where you want to set up standard charges.


1. Select the customer or service site (respectively) to work with.

1. Click the Std Charges
 button (below the Rate Template field).  The SM Standard Charges form displays.

1. In the Standard
 Item field, enter the standard item to add as a standard charge
 or press F4 to select from a list of valid standard items.

1. In the Cost
 Quantity field, enter the quantity of the standard item to be
 expensed. If there are no costs associated with the standard item, set the
 Cost
 Quantity field to 0.00.Note: This value becomes the Cost Qty for the
 miscellaneous work completed line.

1. In the
 Price Quantity
 field, enter the quantity of the standard item for which you will
 charge the customer/service site.Note: This value becomes the Billable Qty for
 the miscellaneous work completed line.

1. Save the record.

1. Add additional standard
 charges as needed.
