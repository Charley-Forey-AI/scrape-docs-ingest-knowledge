# Material Rate Hierarchy

When capturing materials on a work order in the SM Work Orders form (Work
 Completed tab), the system uses a specific hierarchy to determine the markup and discount
 rates to use for calculating billable rates.
Select the links below for explanations about how the system
 derives billable rates for material-related work completed miscellaneous, inventory, and work completed purchase lines based on
 your material rates setup.
