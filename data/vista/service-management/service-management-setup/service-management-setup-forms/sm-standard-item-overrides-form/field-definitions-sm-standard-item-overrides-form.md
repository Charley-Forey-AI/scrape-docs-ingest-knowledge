# Field Definitions: SM Standard Item Overrides Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Standard Item Overrides form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Standard Item

Enter the standard item for which you are
 setting up an override bill rate. Press F4 for a list of valid standard
 items.
Note: Billable rate overrides defined for standard items at the service site or customer levels will override those defined at the rate template level.

## Billable Rate

Enter the override billing rate for this standard item.
Note: Billable rate overrides defined for standard items at the service site or customer levels will override those defined at the rate template level.

## Billable Markup Percent

Billable Markup Percent field on SM Standard Item Overrides form.
Optional.
Enter the markup percent (e.g. enter 5% as 5.00000) to define a markup instead of a flat price for a standard item.
Note: An entry in the
 Billable Markup
 Percent field replaces the Billable Rate.
