# Field Definitions: SM Part Types Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM Part
 Types form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Part Type

Enter a part type, up to 15 characters (e.g. Filter, Belt, Fuses, etc.).

## Description

Enter a description for the part type, up to 60 characters.
