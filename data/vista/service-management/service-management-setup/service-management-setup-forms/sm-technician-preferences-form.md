# SM Technician Preferences Form

Use the SM Technician Preferences form to set up technician preferences for a customer and/or service.
Access this form by clicking Advanced
 (below the Primary Technician field) in SM Customers or SM Service Sites.Technician
 preferences allow you to define the technicians that are authorized to performed service
 work for a customer and/or service site, as well as those that are not. For each
 technician set up here, you will assign one of the following statuses:

- A-Alternate — Use this status for
 technicians that are authorized to perform service work for the service site
 and/or customer. Technicians with this status will generally be used when the
 primary technician assigned to the service site (in SM Service Sites) or
 customer (in SM Customers) is not available.

- D-Do Not Use — Assign this status to
 technicians that are not authorized to perform service work for the service site
 and/or customer. You might generally use this status for technicians that the
 customer or service site has complained about, or for technicians that do not
 have the skills to perform the type of service work requested by the
 customer/service site.

Note: Technician preferences defined for a service site will override those defined for a
 customer.
