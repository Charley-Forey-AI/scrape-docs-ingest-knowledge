# Field Definitions: SM Frequency Setup Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Frequency Setup form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Description

Enter a description of the frequency code, up to 60 characters.

## Frequency

Enter a frequency code, up to 15 characters (e.g. Annual, Monthly, Quarterly, etc.).
