# SM Service Sites Form

Use the SM Service Sites form to set up customer and job service sites.
You can set up service sites to represent any location that contains serviceable items (for example, an office, building, or floor), and where technicians will be dispatched to perform service work. When entering work orders, you are required to specify the applicable service site; therefore, you must set up a minimum of one service site per customer and/or job.
