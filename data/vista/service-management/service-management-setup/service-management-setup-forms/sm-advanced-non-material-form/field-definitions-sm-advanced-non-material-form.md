# Field Definitions: SM Advanced Non-Material Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM Advanced Non-Material form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Break Point

The Break Point field on the SM Advanced Non-Material form, Rate Break Points tab.
Enter the total dollar amount to use as a break point when calculating markups on non-material purchases for an SM work order.
 When you enter a non-material work completed purchase or miscellaneous line, the system compares the line's total amount to the break points defined here to determine which markup rate to use. If the line's total amount reaches this break point amount, the system applies the markup rate specified to the right.

## Percent

The Percent field on the SM Advanced Non-Material form, Rate Break Points tab.
Enter the percent to use when calculating markups for non-material purchases on an SM work order (for example, enter 5% as 5.00000).
The system applies this percentage when the total dollar amount for the work completed purchase or miscellaneous line reaches the break point amount specified in the Break Point field for this entry.
Note: Markup rates defined by cost type (in the Cost Type Overrides tab) override the markup rates defined by here break point.
