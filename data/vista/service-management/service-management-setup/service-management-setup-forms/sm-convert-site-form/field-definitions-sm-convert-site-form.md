# Field Definitions: SM Convert Site Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SM
 Convert Site form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Customer

Enter
 the customer to assign to this service site. Initially defaults the customer assigned to
 the job in JC Jobs.
Note: The customer specified here must exist in SM Customers
 or AR Customers. If a customer does not exist in SM Customers, but does exist in AR
 Customers, the system will automatically add the customer to SM Customers once the job site
 is converted to a customer site.If the customer does not exist in SM Customers or AR
 Customers, you can press F5 to set the customer up before
 continuing here.
