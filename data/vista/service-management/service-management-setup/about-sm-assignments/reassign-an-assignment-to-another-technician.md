# Reassign an Assignment to Another Technician

You can reassign an SM assignment to a different technician using the SM Assignments form.

1. From the main menu, select Service Management > Programs > SM Assignments.The
 SM Assignments form displays.

1. In the
 Assignment
 field on the Info tab, enter an assignment number, or press

 F4
to select from a list of valid assignments.

1. In the
 Lead
 Tech
 field, enter a technician number, or press
 F4
to select from a list of valid technicians.

1. Click Save Record.
