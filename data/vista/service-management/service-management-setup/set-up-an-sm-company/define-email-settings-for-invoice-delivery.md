# Define Email Settings for Invoice
 Delivery

If you plan to use the invoice delivery feature for work order and agreement invoices, you must set up the email parameters for delivering invoices to recipients via email.
To set up email delivery options:

1. Open SM Company Parameters.

1. Click on the Email Settings tab.

1. In the From
 Address field, enter the "reply" email address for work order or
 agreement invoices delivered via email.

1. In the Subject field, enter the text to display in the Subject line
 when delivering invoices via email.

1. In the Body text box, enter the text to display in the body of the
 email. See the [F1 help](/en/vista/vista/service-management/service-management-setup/service-management-setup-forms/sm-company-parameters-form/field-definitions-sm-company-parameters-form#ID-00042a09--en) for detailed information about the format to use when
 entering the body text.
