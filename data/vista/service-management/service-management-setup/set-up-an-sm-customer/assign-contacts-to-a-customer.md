# Assign Contacts to a Customer

You can assign contacts to a customer using the Contact tab in SM Customers.
You must set up contacts in HQ Contacts before you can
 assign them to a customer in SM Customers.
 The information shown for a contact in SM Customers is display only. If you need to make changes to the contact's information, you must do so in HQ Contacts.To assign a contact to a customer:

1. Open the SM Customers form.

1. In the Customer field, enter the Customer for which to add contacts or press F4 to select from a list of valid customers.

1. Click on the Contacts tab.

1. In the Contact
 Seq field, press F4 and select the contact
 sequence to add.

1. Save the record. Note: If you are using SM Contacts with Vista Field Service, you can select the
 contact to receive Trip Closeout email notifications when a trip is closed
 in the app. Check the Include Trip Close Email box on
 the Contacts tab for the contact you wish to receive
 the notification. For more information, see [Include Trip Close Email](/en/vista/vista/service-management/service-management-setup/service-management-setup-forms/sm-customers-form/field-definitions-sm-customer-form#concept-12380002--en).
