# Installations

If you have a self-hosted version of Vista, in order to get new versions of Vista, you must download and install the appropriate files to their
 respective servers. If you have a cloud-hosted version of Vista, Trimble Viewpoint
 handles your organization's upgrade process, however, you are responsible for installing the
 Vista client on your local machine.
Find information to guide you through installing and upgrading the following:

- your Vista™ Database and Application servers

- your Reverse Proxy servers, if your organization's deployment method is VRL on-premises

- the Vista Client Application

 Also find hardware and software minimum requirements and best practices for all Viewpoint applications.
