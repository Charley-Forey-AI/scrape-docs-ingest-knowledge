# VA Report Activity Log

You can use the VA Report Activity Log report to display report activity by the most
 recent activity by selecting Viewpoint Administration > Reports > VA Report Activity Log.
VA Report Activity Log will display report activity by the most recent activity. A second section of the report will display report errors. The detected errors may be related to a specific report or an error detected within the Viewpoint software.
