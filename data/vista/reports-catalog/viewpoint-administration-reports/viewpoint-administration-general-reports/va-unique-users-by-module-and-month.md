# VA Unique Users by Module and Month

You can use the VA Unique Users by Module and Month report by selecting Viewpoint Administration > Reports > VA Unique Users by Module and Month.
This report shows by module and month, the number of unique users who have opened at least one form in the respective module and month. This report is run across all data companies in the database..
