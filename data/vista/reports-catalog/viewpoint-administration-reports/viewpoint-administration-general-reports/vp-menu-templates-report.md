# VP Menu Templates Report

You can use the VP Menu Templates Report report to list the Viewpoint Menu Templates
 by selecting Viewpoint Administration > Reports > VP Menu Templates Report.
This report lists the Viewpoint Menu Templates. For each template the list of menu items follows with its menu sequence and the item type.
Parameters

There are no run-time inputs for this report.
