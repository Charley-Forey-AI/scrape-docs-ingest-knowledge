# WF Templates

You can use the WF Templates report by selecting Workflow > Reports > WF Templates.
The option "(S)tandard, (U)ser, (A)ll Templates" (defaulted to Standard) allows for templates selections. Standard templates are the implementation checklists and processing logs included in the core Viewpoint software and may be copied to create checklists or custom templates.
When selected, "Include Header Note" will display notes at the template level on the first page only and it may have specific instructions for the template. The "Include Task/Step Notes" option will display all notes at the task and step level.
