# Cash Management Reports

The Cash Management module provides a number of reports to
 analyze bank accounts and reconcile account statements. It provides an up-to-the-minute
 inquiry of checkbook balances, allowing review of each account’s current balance and
 statement status.
Reports in Cash Management have two categories: general reports that are available to every supported geographic location and Australia-specific reports. Click on a link for more information on reports in the associated category.
