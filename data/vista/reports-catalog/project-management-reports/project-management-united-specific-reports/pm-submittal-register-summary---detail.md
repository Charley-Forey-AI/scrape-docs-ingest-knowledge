# PM Submittal Register Summary - Detail

You can use the PM Submittal Register Summary - Detail
 report by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Submittal Register Summary -
 Detail.
The PM Submittal Register Summary - Detail report lists PM Projects and their related Register Packages including Revision, Package Revision, Status, Spec Section and the Due dates for the respective parties.
