# PM Letter of Transmittal List

You can use the PM Letter of Transmittal List report to
 print a list of each transmittal by Project by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Letter of Transmittal List.
The PM Letter of Transmittal List prints each transmittal by Project. Report information includes project, transmittal, subject of transmittal, date, issue, document type, document status, description and remarks. A subreport lists the Sent To Firm and Sent To Contact for the transmittal. The report is sorted by project and transmittal with input parameters for beginning and ending project and beginning and ending transmittal.
