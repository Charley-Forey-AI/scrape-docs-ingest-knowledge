# PM Firm Contacts

You can use the PM Firm Contacts report for list of all
 the Contact people at each firm with which you may work by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Firm Contacts.
The PM Firm Contacts report is a list of all the Contact people at each firm
 with which you may work. The report may be sorted by either Firm sort name or Firm
 #, and Contact sort name or contact #. Inputs include vendor group, beginning and
 ending firm sort name and number, beginning and ending contact sort name and number,
 sort order for firm, and sort order for contact. All static information for each
 firm contact is listed including title, phone, extension, mobile phone #, fax #,
 email address, and preferred method of contact.
