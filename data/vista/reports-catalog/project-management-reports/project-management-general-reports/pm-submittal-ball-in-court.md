# PM Submittal Ball-In Court

You can use the PM Submittal Ball-In Court report to
 display the current status of the submittal items in the submission / approval process by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Submittal Ball-In Court.
This report displays the current status of the submittal items in the
 submission / approval process.
There are three sections in the report:
Requesting - A submittal will display in this section if it has a Due to Responsible Firm date, but not a Sent to Responsible Firm date.
Responsible - A submittal will display in this section if it has a Sent To and Due From Responsible Firm date, but not a Received From Responsible Firm date.
Approving - A submittal will display in this section if it has a Sent To Approving Firm and Due From Approving Firm date, but not a Received From Approving Firm date. The submittal must also have a Due To / Sent To / Due From / Received From Responsible Firm date, and a Due To Approving Firm date.
