# PM Matl And Sub Not Interfaced

You can use the PM Matl And Sub Not Interfaced report to
 see material and subcontract details that are not yet interfaced to Job Cost by selecting
 Project Management > Reports > PM Matl And Sub Not Interfaced.
The report shows material and subcontract detail that is not yet interfaced to Job Cost. The report sorts by Project, and then lists Material type detail first and Subcontract information second. For each type of Material or Sub detail, the report sorts by the type of record, Original, Approved, or Pending. Then by Approved Change Order Number 'ACO', Pending Change Order Number 'PCO', Phase, and Cost Type.
