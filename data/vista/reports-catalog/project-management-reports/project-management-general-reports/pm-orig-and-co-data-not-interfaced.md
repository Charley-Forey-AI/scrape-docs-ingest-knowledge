# PM Orig And CO Data Not Interfaced

You can use the PM Orig And CO Data Not Interfaced report
 to show which phases and cost types for projects have yet to be interfaced to the job cost
 module by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Orig And CO Data Not
 Interfaced.
The PM Orig And CO Date Not Interfaced report shows which phases and cost types for projects have yet to be interfaced to the job cost module. The report includes the units, unit cost, and dollar amounts along with much of the other significant data seen in the PM Project Phases and PM Change Orders programs. The report breaks out the phase and cost type information into three sections for a project. Original, Approved Change Orders (ACO), and Pending Change Orders (PCO).
