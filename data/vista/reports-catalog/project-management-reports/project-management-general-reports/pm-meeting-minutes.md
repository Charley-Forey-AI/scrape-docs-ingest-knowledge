# PM Meeting Minutes

You can use the PM Meeting Minutes report to print all the
 information from a meeting by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Meeting Minutes.
The PM Meeting Minutes report prints all the information from a meeting. The report sorts by Project Management Company, Project, meeting number, and whether the items have been discussed in a previous meeting or not. All attendees are then listed followed by Item information including original item, minutes, initiating firm and person, responsible firm and person, initiating date, due date, status, and issue #. The report further shows item line details.
