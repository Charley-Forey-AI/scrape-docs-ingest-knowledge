# PM Issues History

You can use the PM Issues History report to track the
 initial setups and revisions of documents related to an issue, as maintained in the PM
 Project Issues program by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Issues History.
The PM Issues History report tracks the initial setups and revisions of documents related to an issue, as maintained in the PM Project Issues program. The report, grouped by Project and Issue, includes document, revision number, action taken, action date, and user login from the PM Issue History table (PMIH).
