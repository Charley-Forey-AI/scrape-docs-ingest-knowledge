# PM Punch List with Optional Sort

You can use the PM Punch List with Optional Sort report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Punch List with Optional
 Sort.
The PM Punch List with Optional Sort report, like the PM Punch List By Item report, prints both punch list item information and item line details from the PM Punch List program. This report also provides the choice of sorting the Punch List Items by Responsible Firm, Location, or Due Date (details are displayed in Item Line order and are not affected by the Item sort). The report also pages by Project and Punch List, and prints lines for signature and date in the lower left corner.
