# PM Submittal Log – 6.5

You can use the PM Submittal Log – 6.5 report for a
 listing of submittals for a selected Project by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Submittal Log – 6.5.
The PM Submittal Log reporve gives a listing of submittals for a selected
 Project. Submittal information prints in Submittal Type and Submittal Order. Because
 submittals may be forwarded to either a subcontract or an Architect, this report
 gives information for both. If a submittal references an Architect, the Subcontract
 column contains the subcontract firm name and the Architect name will be printed in
 the Sub/Arch column. If there is a not a referenced architect, the Sub column will
 be blank and the Subcontract firm name will be printed in the Sub/Arch column. The
 Date columns print information depending on whether a submittal contains an
 architect as well. If the submittal applies to an architect, the date fields print
 as follows. Date Sent = Date sent to Arch/Eng; Date Req'd = Due Back Arch; and Date
 Back = Rec'd Back Arch. If no architect has been referenced, then the dates are Date
 Sent = Activity Date; Date Req'd = Date Req'd; and Date Back = Date Recd. The Days
 Over Due is a calculation of the Date Back minus the Date Req'd columns.
