# PM Submittal Schedule

You can use the PM Submittal Schedule report by selecting
 Project Management > Reports > PM Submittal Schedule.
This report displays information from the Submittal Register bounded by user
 selected 'from-to' Activity Dates. The report can be Grouped and Sorted using a
 variety of Submittal Item attributes. Additional report filtering options are.
 'Include Notes', 'Include Closed', and 'Show Latest Revision'.
