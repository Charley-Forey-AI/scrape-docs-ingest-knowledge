# PM Submittal Log – Architect/Engineer – 6.5

You can use the PM Submittal Log – Architect/Engineer –
 6.5 report by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Submittal Log – Architect/Engineer –
 6.5.
This report utilizes information entered in the PM Submittal - 6.5 form. The Submittal Log Arch/Eng including Sub/Rev, subcontractor, Sent-, Required-, Back-date and its status is listed by Project.
