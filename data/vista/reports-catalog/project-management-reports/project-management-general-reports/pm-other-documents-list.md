# PM Other Documents List

You can use the PM Other Documents List report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Other Documents List.
The PM Other Documents List report prints the project, document type, document, location, related firm, issue, status, responsible person, date due, date received, date sent, date due back, date received back and date returned. The sent to firm and sent to contact are then printed using a subreport. The report sorts by project, document, type document and sent to firm. Report input parameters include sent to firm and sent to contract.
