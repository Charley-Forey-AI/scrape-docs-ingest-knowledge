# PM Punch List By Item

You can use the PM Punch List By Item report by selecting
 Project Management > Reports > PM Punch List By Item.
The PM Punch List By Item report, sorted by Project, Punch List, and Punch List Item, prints both punch list item information and details from the PM Punch List program. The report also pages by Project and Punch List, and prints lines for signature and date in the lower left corner.
