# PM Late Submittals – 6.5

You can use the PM Late Submittals – 6.5 report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Late Submittals – 6.5.
The PM Late Submittals report will print the submittal (number and description), architectural firm, due date (due back from architect), and the number of days overdue when the number of days between today's date and the due date is greater than 0.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Project (Required)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the project or leave blank for all.
