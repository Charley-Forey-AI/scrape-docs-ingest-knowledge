# PM Document History

You can use the PM Document History report to print the
 modifications, or history, of documents related to Projects by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Document History.
The PM Document History report prints the modifications, or history, of documents related to Projects. The report sorts by Company, Project, Document Type, and Document, and includes document revision information such as Date, Time, Action, Transmittal, and Firm.
