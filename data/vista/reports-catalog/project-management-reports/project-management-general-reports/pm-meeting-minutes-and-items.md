# PM Meeting Minutes and Items

You can use the PM Meeting Minutes and Items report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Meeting Minutes and Items.
Project, minutes type (whether it is an agenda or minutes), meeting date, meeting number, and whether the items have been discussed in a previous meeting or not. Columns for the meeting include meeting number, meeting date, meeting time, next meeting date, next meeting time, next meeting location, preparer, the subject of the meeting, the location, and notes. All attendees are then followed by the items that have been discussed prior to this meeting, followed by the current new business. Item information includes item, original item, minutes, initiating firm and person, responsible firm and person, initiating date, due date, status, and issue #.
