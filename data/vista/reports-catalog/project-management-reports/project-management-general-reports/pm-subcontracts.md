# PM Subcontracts

You can use the PM Subcontracts report by selecting
 Project Management > Reports > PM Subcontracts.
This PM Subcontracts report is a subcontract agreement form listing the Original Item Quantities, Unit Prices, and Totals by subcontract item. This report could easily be copied to a new report and the verbiage changed to meet the requirements between the Contractor and Subcontractor. Assigned, unassigned, and interfaced subcontracts entered in the Project Management module are all included in this report.
