# PM Sub Change Order Form by ACO

You can use the PM Sub Change Order Form by ACO report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Sub Change Order Form by
 ACO.
The Subcontract Change Order Form by ACO is sorted by Approved Change Order (ACO) and includes signature lines for owner and subcontractor. Previous authorized change orders include the total of all subcontract changes with ACO Sequence Numbers less than the ACO Sequence of the current ACO change order being printed. Original Amount uses original amounts interfaced from PM to SL. Original, previous, and current change amounts also include Sales and VAT tax (non-interfaced sub change orders calculate tax using HQ Tax rate). In order for the report to correctly report the Original Amount, the SubCO number should be left blank on regular type items (SL Item Type 1) on an ACO if the SL Item has not yet been interfaced to SL.
