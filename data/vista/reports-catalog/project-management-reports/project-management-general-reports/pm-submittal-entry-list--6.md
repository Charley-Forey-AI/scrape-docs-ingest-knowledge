# PM Submittal Entry List – 6.5

You can use the PM Submittal Entry List – 6.5 report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Submittal Entry List – 6.5.
The PM Submittal Entry List is a report for verifying the proper entry of submittals. The list is sorted by Project, Submittal Type, Submittal and Rev #. The report prints most of the data from the PM Submittal Entry program including Phase, Status, Responsible Person, Issue, Subcontract Firm, Architect/Engineer Firm #, and the pertinent date information.
