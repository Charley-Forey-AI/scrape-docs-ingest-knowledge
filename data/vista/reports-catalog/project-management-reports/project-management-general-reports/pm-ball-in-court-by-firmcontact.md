# PM Ball In Court by Firm/Contact

You can use the PM Ball In Court by Firm/Contact report to
 show all open items for Transmittals, Requests for Information, Submittals, Other Documents
 and Requests for Quotes sorted by Firm and Contact by selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Ball In Court by
 Firm/Contact.
This report shows all open items for Transmittals, Requests for Information, Submittals, Other Documents and Requests for Quotes sorted by Firm and Contact. The Project parameter is required and the Date parameter is used to calculate days overdue. Items marked as 'Final' will not be included at all. The report also has an option to print Pending Change Order Items. When answering "Y" for the Include Future Due Dates input, the report will include items with due dates later than the inputted Date parameter.
