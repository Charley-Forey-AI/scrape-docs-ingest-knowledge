# PM Document Types

PM Document Types
PM Document types is a listing of possible document types that could be associated with Projects. The report includes parameters for beginning and ending document and whether to print notes. Document type and category, pending change order, pending change order items dates 1, 2, and 3, and whether to show them are provided on the report.
Access this report by clicking Project Management > Reports > PM Document Types.
