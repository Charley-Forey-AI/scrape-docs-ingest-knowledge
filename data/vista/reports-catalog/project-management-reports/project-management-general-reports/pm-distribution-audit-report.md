# PM Distribution Audit Report

You can use the PM Distribution Audit Report report to
 list contacts and firms and their respective the distribution of PM Documents by selecting
 Project Management > Reports > PM Distribution Audit Report.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Project (Required)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the project.

Firm
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the firm.

Beginning Date
Enter beginning date or leave blank for all.

Ending Date
Enter ending date or leave blank for all.

Document Type
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the document type.
