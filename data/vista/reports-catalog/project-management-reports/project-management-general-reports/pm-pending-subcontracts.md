# PM Pending Subcontracts

You can use the PM Pending Subcontracts report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Pending Subcontracts.
The PM Pending Subcontracts report, sorted by Project, Vendor, and Subcontract (SL), prints subcontract detail not yet interfaced to Job Cost. Unassigned detail entries (no subcontract #) will sort before assigned subcontracts. In addition to showing Units, Unit Cost, and Amount, the report also prints the work complete and stored material retainage for each SL item from PM Subcontract Detail.
