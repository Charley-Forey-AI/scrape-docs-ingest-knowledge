# PM Meeting Minute History

You can use the PM Meeting Minute History report by
 selecting Project Management > Reports > PM Meeting Minute History.
The PM Meeting Minute History report prints one line per Meeting Item and sorts by the group headings of Project, Meeting Type, and Original Meeting Item. The details for each original item include the item #, meeting numbers, the meeting location, all pertinent meeting date information (from PM Meeting Minutes), and the responsible firm and person.
