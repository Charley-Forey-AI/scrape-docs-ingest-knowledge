# CM PBA Reconciliation

You can use the CM PBA Reconciliation report by selecting
 Cash Management > Reports > CM PBA Reconciliation.
The CM PBA Reconciliation report pulls together transactions from
 AR and AP to calculate an expected balance for Australian Project Bank Accounts
 (PBAs). It shows the expected ending balance for an account based on project
 receipts and payments during selected dates.
