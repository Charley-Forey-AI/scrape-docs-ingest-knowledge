# CM Report By Month

You can use the CM Report By Month report to list the CM
 References for a given month by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Report By Month.
This report sorts on CM Bank Account and Source of transaction.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning CM Account
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning Bank Account. Leave blank to select all bank accounts.

Ending CM Account
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending Bank Account. Leave blank to select all bank accounts.

Month
Enter month (MM/YY) or leave blank to include all months.
