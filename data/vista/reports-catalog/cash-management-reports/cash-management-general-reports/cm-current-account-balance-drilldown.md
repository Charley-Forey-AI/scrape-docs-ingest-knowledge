# CM Current Account Balance Drilldown

You can use the CM Current Account Balance Drilldown
 report for a list of Current Account Balances in CM for a range of CM Accounts with the
 ability to drill down to transaction detail by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Current Account Balance
 Drilldown.
The first level will include the current balance of the accounts as of the day run and regardless of detail shown at lower levels. The detail may be selected by type and may be restricted to include only transactions within a range of months or dates.
All batches with CM transactions must be posted to be included in this report.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning CM Account
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning Bank Account. Leave blank to select all bank accounts.

Ending CM Account
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending Bank Account. Leave blank to select all bank accounts.

(M)onth or Actual (D)ate for Details
Enter M or D.

Beginning Month
Enter beginning month (MM/YY) if M selected in previous field or leave blank to include all months. Leave blank if selecting D in previous field.

Ending Month
Enter ending month (MM/YY) if M selected in previous field or leave blank to include all months. Leave blank if selecting D in previous field.

Beginning Actual Date
Enter beginning date (MM/DD/YY) if D selected in previous field or leave blank to include all dates. Leave blank if selecting M in previous field.

Ending Actual Date
Enter ending date (MM/DD/YY) if D selected in previous field or leave blank to include all dates. Leave blank if selecting M in previous field.

(C)hks/Efts, (D)ep, (T)ransfers, (A)d.  (Blank for All)
Enter C, D, T, A, or leave blank for all transaction types.
