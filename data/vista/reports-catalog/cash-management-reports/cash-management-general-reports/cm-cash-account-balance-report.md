# CM Cash Account Balance Report

You can use the CM Cash Account Balance report to obtain a
 list of entries for all CM Bank Accounts for a given month by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Cash Account Balance.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Month to Balance
Enter month to balance (MM/YY).
