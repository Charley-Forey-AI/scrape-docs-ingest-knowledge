# CM Daily Totals

You can use the CM Daily Totals report for a list of all
 CM References by CM Bank Account with a total for each day by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Daily Totals.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

CM Account (Blank for All)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select Bank Account. Leave blank to select all bank accounts.

Beginning Date
Enter beginning date (MM/DD/YY) or leave blank to include all entries.

Ending Date
Enter ending date (MM/DD/YY) or leave blank to include all entries.
