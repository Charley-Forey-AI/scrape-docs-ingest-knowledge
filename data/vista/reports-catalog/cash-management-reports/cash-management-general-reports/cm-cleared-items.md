# CM Cleared Items

You can use the CM Cleared Items report for a list of all
 cleared references for selected Account and Statement Date by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Cleared Items.
At runtime, if the bank is blank, then all banks will print; if the statement is blank, then all statements will be printed.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

CM Account (Blank for All)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select Bank Account. Leave blank to select all bank accounts.

Statement Date (Blank for All)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select Statement Date or manually enter Statement Date.
