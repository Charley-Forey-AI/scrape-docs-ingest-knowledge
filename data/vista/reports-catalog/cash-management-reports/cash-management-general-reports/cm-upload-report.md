# CM Upload Report

You can use the CM Upload Report to display any entries
 that did not clear out of the CM Clear Entries table when using the Upload file in CM Clear
 Entries by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Upload Report.
This report is on the menu and will display any entries that did not clear out
 of the CM Clear Entries table when using the Upload file in CM Clear Entries. It is
 identical to the report that is only available during the clear entries process.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Upload Date (Required)
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 or manually enter upload date.
