# CM Statement Drilldown

You can use the CM Statement Drilldown report -- which
 provides you with Account number and name, current balance, date, status, Balance
 information (Beginning, working, statement), cleared adjustments, checks, deposits and
 outstanding adjustments, checks and deposits) -- by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Statement Drilldown.
Level 2 of this report lists the details for cleared and outstanding
 adjustments, checks, and deposits.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

CM Account (Required)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select Bank Account.

Statement Date (Required)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select Statement Date or manually enter Statement Date.

Include Outstanding Reference Through Date
Enter through date (MM/DD/YY) for outstanding entries.
