# CM Company Parameters

You can use the CM Company Parameters report for a list of
 the CM Company set-up details by selecting Cash Management > Reports > CM Company Parameters.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Company
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning CM Company. Leave blank
 to select all CM Companies.

Ending Company
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending CM Company. Leave blank to
 select all CM Companies.

Print Notes?
checkbox to include notes attached to bank account. Leave unchecked to exclude notes.
