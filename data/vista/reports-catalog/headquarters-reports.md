# Headquarters Reports

The Headquarters (HQ) module is the foundation for all modules on the system. Each company that you set up in other modules must be set up in HQ first.
Reports in Headquarters have four categories: general reports that are available to every supported geographic location; United States-specific reports; and Australia-specific reports. Click on a link for more information on reports in the associated category.

- [Headquarters General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/headquarters-reports/headquarters-general-reports)

- [Headquarters United States-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/headquarters-reports/headquarters-united-states-specific-reports)

- [Headquarters Australia-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/headquarters-reports/headquarters-australia-specific-reports)
