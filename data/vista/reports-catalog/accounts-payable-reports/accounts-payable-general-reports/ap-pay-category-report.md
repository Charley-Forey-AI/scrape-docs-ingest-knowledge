# AP Pay Category Report

You can use the AP Pay Category report to print a detailed list of pay categories.
 You can access this report by selecting Accounts Payable >  Reports  > AP Open Payables for Subcontracts.
The use of Pay Categories is determined by a flag in AP Company Parameters.
 The report displays the values from AP Company setup and then the override values
 from AP Pay Categories.Report ParametersDescription
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Print Notes?
Select the checkbox to print any associated notes.
