# AP 1099 Vendors Less than Minimum Report

You can use the AP 1099 Vendors Less than Minimum report to list all total amounts
 for a selected vendor range and calendar year. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Payable >  Reports  > AP 1099 Vendors Less than Minimum.
The report can be printed either in a detail or summary format with a minimum
 payment amount input for printing only those vendors whose total amount paid is less
 than the minimum. The summary format prints the vendor 1099 boxes with the
 appropriate amounts and a grand total at the end. The detail format prints the
 details for transactions that have been paid. Keep in mind that if detail has been
 purged over the course of a calendar year, the detail may not match the total
