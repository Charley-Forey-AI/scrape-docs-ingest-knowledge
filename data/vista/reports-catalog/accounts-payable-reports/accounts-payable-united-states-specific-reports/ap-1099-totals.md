# AP 1099 Totals

You can use the AP 1099 Totals report to provide a summary list in vendor order,
 displaying the 1099 type, Box number, and dollar amount for the 1099 forms. You can access
 this report by selecting Accounts Payable >  Reports  > AP 1099 Totals.
If you selected the Include in
 1099 Processing (ignore minimum) checkbox for a vendor in AP Vendor
 Master, this report includes 1099s regardless of total paid amounts.Report ParametersDescription
