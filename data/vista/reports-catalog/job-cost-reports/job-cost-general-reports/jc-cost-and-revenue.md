# JC Cost and Revenue

You can use the JC Cost and Revenue report for actual
 costs for a range of months by cost type and amount billed by contract and may be useful in
 reconciling to General Ledger accounts by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Cost and Revenue.
This report provides actual costs for a range of months by cost type and
 amount billed by contract and may be useful in reconciling to General Ledger
 accounts. The first eight cost types will display in separate columns. Any cost
 types that exist beyond eight will be combined in the ninth column. If the option to
 sub-total by department is selected, the report will sort as well as sub-total by
 department. If not subtotaling by department, the report will sort in contract
 number order.
