# JC Field Ticket Status

Use the JC Field Tickets Status report to review the status and cost information for Job Cost field tickets. You can access this report by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Field Ticket Status.

This report displays Field Tickets in numerical order and shows the ticket status, when it was created, the customer PO number and owner job number (if applicable), who the billing and/or ticket was approved by, and period and life-to-date costs. You can drill down on the Period and LTD Cost fields to see additional cost detail for the specified Actual Cost Date range and for the life of the Field Ticket, respectively. If attachments exist, you can access them by clicking the Attachments link (which only displays when attachments exist for the Field Ticket).
