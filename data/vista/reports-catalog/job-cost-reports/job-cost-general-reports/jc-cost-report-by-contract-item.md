# JC Cost Report By Contract Item

You can use the JC Cost Report By Contract Item report for
 estimates, actuals, commitments, and projections for phase and cost types grouped and sorted
 by contract item by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Cost Report By Contract
 Item.
This report includes estimates, actuals, commitments, and projections for
 phase and cost types grouped and sorted by contract item. Cost at Completion is
 Projected Cost unless zero, then Estimated Cost.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract.

Ending Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract.

Through Month
Enter through month (MM/YY).
