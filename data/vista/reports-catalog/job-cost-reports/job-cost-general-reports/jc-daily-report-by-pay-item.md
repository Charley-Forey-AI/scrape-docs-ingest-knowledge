# JC Daily Report By Pay Item

You can use the JC Daily Report By Pay Item report to
 display original and current contract detail as well as looking at estimated and current
 activity on the job for today, this week, and Job to Date by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Daily Report By Pay Item.
This report displays original and current contract detail as well as looking at estimated and current activity on the job for today, this week, and Job to Date.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract.

Ending Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract.

Select by (P)osted or (A)ctual Date
Enter P or A.

Today’s Date
Accept default or enter today’s date.

Beginning Date of This Week
Enter beginning date of this week.

Exclude Jobs with No Activity for Week?
checkbox to exclude jobs with no activity for the week.

Exclude Jobs with No Activity for Today?
checkbox to exclude jobs with no activity for today.
