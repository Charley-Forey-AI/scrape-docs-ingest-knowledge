# JC Fixed Rate Template

You can use the JC Fixed Rate Template report to display
 Fixed Rate Templates and associated jobs by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Fixed Rate Template.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Rate Template
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning rate template.

Ending Rate Template
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending rate template.
