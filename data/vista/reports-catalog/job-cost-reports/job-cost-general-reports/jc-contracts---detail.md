# JC Contracts - Detail

You can use the JC Contract Master - Detail report for Contract Master Details with a choice to print static details, tax information and contract notes by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Contracts - Detail.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract.

Ending Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract.

(O)pen, (C)losed or (A)ll
 Enter O, C, or A.
Print Notes?
checkbox to print notes.
