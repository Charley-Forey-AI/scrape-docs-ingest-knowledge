# JC Contract Item Cost Estimate Recap

You can use the JC Contract Item Cost Estimate Recap
 report to compare the original contract amounts with change orders to the original estimate
 and current estimate amounts to calculate the estimated profit by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Contract Item Cost Estimate
 Recap.
This report compares the original contract amounts with change orders to the original estimate and current estimate amounts to calculate the estimated profit. The amounts are listed by contract item.
