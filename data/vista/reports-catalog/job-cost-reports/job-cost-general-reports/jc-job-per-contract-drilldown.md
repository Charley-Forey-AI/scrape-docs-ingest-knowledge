# JC Job per Contract Drilldown

You can use the JC Job per Contract Drilldown report by
 selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Job per Contract Drilldown.
This drilldown will start at the contract level then drilldown to a second level which will list all Jobs on the Contract. The following levels of the drilldown are exactly the same as the JC Cost Totals Drilldown.
Access this report by clicking Job Cost > Reports > JC Jobs per Contract Drilldown.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract.

Ending Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract.

Actual or Projected Cost
Enter A or P.

Beginning Month for Detail
Enter beginning month (MM/YY) for detail.

Through Month
Enter through month (MM/YY).

(O)pen or (A)ll Contracts
Enter O or A.
