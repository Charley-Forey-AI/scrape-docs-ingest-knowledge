# JC Revenue To Date

You can use the JC Revenue To Date report for a list of
 totals by contract item for units (contract and job billed to date), dollars (contract and
 job billed to date), and total dollars (contract and job billed to date) by selecting
 Job Cost > Reports > JC Revenue To Date.
The JC Revenue to Date Report provides totals by contract item for units (contract and job billed to date), dollars (contract and job billed to date), and total dollars (contract and job billed to date). The Contract parameter can be used to select all or a range of contracts.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract.

Ending Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract.
