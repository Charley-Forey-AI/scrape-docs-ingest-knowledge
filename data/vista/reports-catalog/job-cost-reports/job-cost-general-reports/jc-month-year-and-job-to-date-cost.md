# JC Month, Year and Job to Date Cost

You can use the JC Month, Year and Job to Date Cost report
 to print month to date, year to date, and job to date actual costs by job and phase through
 the specified month by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Month, Year and Job to Date
 Cost.
This report prints month to date, year to date, and job to date actual costs by job and phase through the specified month. The report also prints the current estimated cost and actual hours. The year to date costs are based on the calendar year of the through month inputted by the user.
