# JC Committed Cost Detail

You can use the JC Committed Cost Detail report for a
 report of the detail of commitments by Job, Phase, and Cost Type. Includes information from
 Purchase Orders, Subcontracts, and Material Orders with data coming from those source tables
 rather than Job Cost by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Committed Cost Detail.
Report of the detail of commitments by Job, Phase, and Cost Type. Includes information from Purchase Orders, Subcontracts, and Material Orders with data coming from those source tables rather than Job Cost. If sales tax is being sent to a separate phase, other Job Cost committed cost reports may not balance with this report.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job.

Ending Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending job.
