# JC Job Materials

You can use the JC Job Materials report for a list of jobs
 with Job, Description, Mail Address, Customer, and Project Manager only by selecting
 Job Cost > Reports > JC Job Materials.
This report provides a list of jobs with Job, Description, Mail Address,
 Customer, and Project Manager only. Inputs for Companies, Jobs, and Notes.
