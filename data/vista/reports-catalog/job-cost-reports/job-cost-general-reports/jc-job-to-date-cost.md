# JC Job To Date Cost

You can use the JC Job To Date Cost report for a list of
 job to date hours and cost by Job, Phase, and Cost Type by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Job To Date Cost.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job.

Ending Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending job.

Through Month
Enter through month (MM/YY).

(O)pen, (C)losed or (A)ll
Enter O, C, or A.
If Closed Jobs Only: Ending Closed Month
Enter beginning closed month (MM/YY)

Enter ending closed month (MM/YY)
