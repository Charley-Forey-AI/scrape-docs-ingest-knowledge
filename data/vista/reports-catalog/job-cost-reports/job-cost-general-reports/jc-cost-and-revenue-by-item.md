# JC Cost and Revenue By Item

You can use the JC Cost and Revenue By Item report to
 print contract amounts, cost information, and Projected Profit (Current Contract Amount -
 Projected Cost) by Contract and Item by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Cost and Revenue By Item.
This report prints contract amounts, cost information, and Projected Profit (Current Contract Amount - Projected Cost) by Contract and Item. The types of cost printed include Original Estimate, Change Orders, Current Estimated (Original + Change Orders), Job to Date Actual Cost, Remaining Committed, and Job to Date Actual + Committed.
