# JC Work Order Form

JC Work Order Form
This report prints a form to be filled out that may be used for small jobs/work orders. The Service Requested will print notes entered on the Job Master. The Mailing Address will print the Override Billing Information entered in the Contract Master on the JB Info tab, or if none there, will print the AR Customers Billing Address.
Access this report by clicking Job Cost > Reports > JC Work Order Form.
