# JC Contract Phase Cost Types

You can use the JC Contract Phase Cost Types report for
 information on the job and contract setup by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Contract Phase Cost Types.
This report contains information on the job and contract setup. It may be used to review the setup of units of measure, units and bill/item/phase flags on each phase and cost type in relation to the contract item. Only job phases with cost types set up in JCCH will be included on this report. It may be run for a range of contracts, jobs, and phases including a range of phases across multiple jobs or contracts.
