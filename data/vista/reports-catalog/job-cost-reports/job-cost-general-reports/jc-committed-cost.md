# JC Committed Cost

You can use the JC Committed Cost report for a list of
 Committed Cost for Jobs. Includes Total Committed Units and Cost, Remaining Committed Units
 and Cost, and Received/Not Invoiced Units and Cost by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Committed Cost.
This report provides a list of Committed Cost for Jobs. Includes Total
 Committed Units and Cost, Remaining Committed Units and Cost, and Received/Not
 Invoiced Units and Cost. The report prints one line per Phase Cost Type Combination
 and sorts by Job.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job.

Ending Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending job.
