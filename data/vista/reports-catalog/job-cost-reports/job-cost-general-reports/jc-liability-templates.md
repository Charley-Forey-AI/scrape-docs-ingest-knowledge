# JC Liability Templates

You can use the JC Liability Templates report for a
 listing of the Liability template codes in the Liabilities Template Setup maintenance form
 by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Liability Templates.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Template
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning template or leave blank for all.

Ending Template
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending template or leave blank for all.

Print Notes?
checkbox to print notes.
