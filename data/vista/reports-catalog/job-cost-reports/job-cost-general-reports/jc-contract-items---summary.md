# JC Contract Items - Summary

You can use the JC Contract Item Master - Summary report for Contract Item information including Item Description, Department, Unit of Measure, Original Contract Units, and Original Contract Amounts from the Contract Items form by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Contract Items - Summary.
This report provides contract Item information including Item Description, Department, Unit of Measure, Original Contract Units, and Original Contract Amounts from the Contract Items form. Change Order amounts are NOT included in these Original Amounts.
