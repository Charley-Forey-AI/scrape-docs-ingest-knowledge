# JC Projection Trend Report

You can use the JC Projection Trend Report report to print
 the projected costs updated from JC Cost Projections for the current month selected as well
 as the two prior months by Job, Phase, and Cost Type (Detail option) by selecting
 Job Cost > Reports > JC Projection Trend Report.
This report prints the projected costs updated from JC Cost Projections for the current month selected as well as the two prior months by Job, Phase, and Cost Type (Detail option). The Variance is the difference between the Current Estimated Cost through the Current Month and the Projected Cost in each monthly column.
