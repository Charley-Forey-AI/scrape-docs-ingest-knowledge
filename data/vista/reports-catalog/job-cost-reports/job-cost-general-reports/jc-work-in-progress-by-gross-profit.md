# JC Work in Progress by Gross Profit

You can use the JC Work in Progress by Gross Profit report
 by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Work in Progress by Gross
 Profit.
JC Work in Progress by Gross Profit lists contracts and their contract amount, estimate cost at completion, gross profit, gross profit %, billings to date, actual costs, % completed, gross profit to date, billings in excess of costs, costs in excess of billings, gross profit prior year and gross profit this year.
