# JC Cost to Estimates Comparison

You can use the JC Cost to Estimates Comparison report to
 compare current estimated units, hours, and dollars to job-to-date units, hours, and dollars
 through a specified month by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Cost to Estimates
 Comparison.
This report compares current estimated units, hours, and dollars to
 job-to-date units, hours, and dollars through a specified month. A balance and
 percent complete based on dollars is also included. Units are based on the cost type
 that is checked for 'Phase Units' and hours are for the labor cost types input at
 runtime. Dollars are accumulated for all cost types.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Through Month
Enter through month (MM/YY).

Beginning Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job.

Ending Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending job.

Labor Cost Types for Hours (1, 2, …)
Enter labor cost types separated by a comma.
