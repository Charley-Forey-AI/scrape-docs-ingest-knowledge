# JC Cost Report

You can use the JC Cost Report report for a summary of the
 Job Costs at the phase level, with Job Totals and Grand Totals when ran for multiple jobs by
 selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Cost Report.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job.

Ending Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending job.

Through Month
Enter through month (MM/YY).

Page by Job?
checkbox to have page break per job.

(O)pen, Open/(S)oft Closed, (C)lose or (A)ll
Enter O, S, C, or A.

If Closed Jobs Only: Beginning Closed Month Ending Closed Month
