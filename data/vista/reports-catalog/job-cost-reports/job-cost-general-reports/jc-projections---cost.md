# JC Projections - Cost

You can use the JC Projections - Cost report by selecting
 Job Cost > Reports > JC Projections - Cost.
This report shows Projected Units, Hours, Cost, and Unit Cost at the Phase/Cost Type level by Job. The Projected Over/Under (Projected Cost - Cur Estimated) and Projected Remaining (Projected - Actual (or Actual + Committed) columns also provide useful job performance information. The column Actual or Actual + Committed changes based on the Projection Method field (on the Projections tab) in JC Company Parameters.
