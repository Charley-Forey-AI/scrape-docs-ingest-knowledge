# JC Projections - Revenue

You can use the JC Projections - Revenue report to see
 contracts with current contract, projected revenue, billed amounts and variances by contract
 item by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Projections - Revenue.
This report shows contracts with current contract, projected revenue, billed
 amounts and variances by contract item. Projected amounts are from amounts posted in
 JC Revenue Projections. These columns will show any projection overrides with an
 asterisk. If no projection exists for the contract item, it will display the current
 contract. The 'Variance' column is the Projected Amount less the Current Contract
 Amount and the 'Remaining To Be Billed' column is Projected Amount less the Billed
 Amount. The report can be pulled for a range of contracts, a range of departments,
 and through a specific month. Input options include restricting by Billing Types and
 by the Contract Status.
