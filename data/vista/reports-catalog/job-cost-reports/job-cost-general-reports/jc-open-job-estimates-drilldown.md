# JC Open Job Estimates Drilldown

You can use the JC Open Job Estimates Drilldown report by
 selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Open Job Estimates
 Drilldown.
The JC Open Job Estimates Drilldown prints jobs with a status of "1" Open only.
Selections at runtime include Job range and the options to include job master information and estimates on the first level.
(Level 2) shows original estimated costs and current estimates.
The Cost Type level (Level 3) includes current estimated hours, units and cost. The units at this last level are reported in three different columns. Estimated Units for the Cost Type, Item Units. Only units where the Item flag is checked in JC Original Estimates, and Phase Units. Only units where the Phase Unit flag is checked in JC Original Estimates.
Access this report by clicking Job Cost > Reports > JC Open Job Estimates Drilldown.
