# JC Revenue Cost Drilldown

You can use the JC Revenue Cost Drilldown report to see
 contract, billed, estimated, and actual cost amounts with the ability of drilling down by
 Contract, Contract Item, Phase, Cost Type, and Job Cost Transaction by selecting
 Job Cost > Reports > JC Revenue Cost Drilldown.
This report shows contract, billed, estimated, and actual cost amounts with the ability of drilling down by Contract, Contract Item, Phase, Cost Type, and Job Cost Transaction. It also allows viewing attachments for AP invoices, purchases orders, subcontracts, and material sales tickets at the transaction drilldown level.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.
