# JC Jobs

You can use the JC Jobs report for a list of jobs by selecting
 Job Cost > Reports > JC Jobs.
This report provides a list of jobs in either a summarized format or detailed format. The summarized format includes basic information only, such as job, description, mailing address, job and fax phone numbers, project manager, customer, and job status. The detail format includes additional information such as the liability and insurance templates, tax information, and markup/discount rate, as well information entered on the Add'l Info, PR Info, and Reviewers tabs in the JC Jobs maintenance form.
