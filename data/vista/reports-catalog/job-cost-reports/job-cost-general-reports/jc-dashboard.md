# JC Dashboard

You can use the JC Dashboard report by selecting
 Job Cost > Reports > JC Dashboard.
This JC Dashboard contains 4 charts visualizing Estimated Gross -, Projected- and Actual Gross Profit by Department; Self-Performed and Subcontracts by Department; Net Cash Flow by Department; Backlog by Department.
