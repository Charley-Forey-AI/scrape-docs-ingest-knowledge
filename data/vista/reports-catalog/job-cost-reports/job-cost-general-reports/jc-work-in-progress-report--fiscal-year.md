# JC Work in Progress Report – Fiscal Year

You can use the JC Work in Progress Report – Fiscal Year
 report by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Work in Progress Report – Fiscal
 Year.
This report follows the same principle as the other JC Work in Progress Reports (Landscape and Portrait), but also prints the contract, cost, and earned revenue amounts for both the current and prior fiscal years. The report finds the fiscal year from the GL Fiscal Year setup based on the Through Month input.
