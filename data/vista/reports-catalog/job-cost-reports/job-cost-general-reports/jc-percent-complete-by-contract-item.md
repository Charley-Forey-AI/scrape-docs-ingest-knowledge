# JC Percent Complete by Contract Item

You can use the JC Percent Complete by Contract Item
 report to show, by contract item, the percent billed based on the current contract amount by
 selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Percent Complete by Contract
 Item.
Report showing, by contract item, the percent billed based on the current contract amount. The current contract amount includes the original amount plus contract changes issued on change orders on or before the Through Month. The report also shows billed and current contract units.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract.

Ending Contract
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract.

Through Month
Enter through month (MM/YY).
