# JC Metric Conversions

You can use the JC Metric Conversions report to print the
 conversion between Unit of Measure and Metric Unit of Measure by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Metric Conversions.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Unit of Measure
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning unit of measure.

Ending Unit of Measure
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending unit of measure.
