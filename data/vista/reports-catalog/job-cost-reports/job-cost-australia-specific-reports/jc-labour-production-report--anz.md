# JC Labour Production Report – A/NZ

You can use the JC Labour Production Report – A/NZ report
 to make comparisons of your weekly, job to date, and estimated labour production rates by
 phase by selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Labour Production Report –
 A/NZ.
This report allows you to make comparisons of your weekly, job to date, and estimated labour production rates by phase. The production rate is defined as labour units completed, typically entered through JC Progress, divided by hours worked. By using the difference of estimated and Job To Date (JTD) figures for both units and hours, the report will then calculate the production rate needed to meet your estimate (Production Rate to Meet Estimate column) as of the week ending date. Finally, based on your rate of production, the report will show if you are ahead or behind on hours to date (To Date Hours +/-) and for the week (Weekly Hours +/-) as well as forecast over/under hours at job completion (Job End Hours +/-). The report also subtotals hours and units by the first two characters of each phase. See the comments for the formulas listed in the Report Information report for the specific calculations.
