# JC Contract Cash Flow – A/NZ

You can use the JC Contract Cash Flow – A/NZ report by
 selecting Job Cost > Reports > JC Contract Cash Flow – A/NZ.
This report uses much of the same
 information shown on the Work in Progress and Contract Status reports to derive
 percentages of contract and gross profit net cash flows one line per contract. The
 report may be sorted by contract or project manager. Because multiple jobs with
 assigned project managers can exist for one contract, the report will sort by the
 project manager assigned to the first job linked to that contract. Like the Contract
 Status report, Cash Paid represents total actual costs minus open accounts payable.
 Net Cash Flow is therefore Cash Collected - Cash Paid. Also, amounts will include or
 exclude sales tax based on whether tax is interfaced to job cost.
