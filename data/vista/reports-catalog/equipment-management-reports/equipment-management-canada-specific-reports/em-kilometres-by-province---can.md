# EM Kilometres By Province - CAN

You can use the EM Kilometres By Province - CAN report for
 a list by Company, Province, and equipment the kilometres entered into the EMST table for
 tax reporting purposes by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Kilometres By Province -
 CAN.
This report lists by Company, Province, and equipment the kilometres entered
 into the EMST table for tax reporting purposes. Loaded, unloaded, and off road
 kilometres totaled by equipment and province or just to level of state.
