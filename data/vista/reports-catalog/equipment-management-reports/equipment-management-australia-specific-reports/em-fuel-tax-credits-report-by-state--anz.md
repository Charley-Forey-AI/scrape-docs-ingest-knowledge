# EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By State – A/NZ

You can use the EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By State – A/NZ
 report to print the Off Road Kilometres, Average Kilometres Per Litre fuel economy, and Off
 Road Litres by State, EM Category, and Equipment by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By State –
 A/NZ.
Used for fuel tax reporting, the EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By State prints the Off Road Kilometres, Average Kilometres Per Litre fuel economy, and Off Road Litres by State, EM Category, and Equipment. Similar to the other EM fuel reports, the Off Road Litres column is calculated by dividing Off Road Kilometres by the total kilometres per litre (km/L). km/L is the total distance traveled (Off Road and On Road, unloaded + loaded) divided by the total fuel purchased.
