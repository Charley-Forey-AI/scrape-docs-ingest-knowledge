# EM Kilometres By State

You can use the EM Kilometres By State report for a list
 by Company, State, and equipment the kilometres entered into the EMST table for tax
 reporting purposes by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Kilometres By State.
This report lists by Company, State, and equipment the kilometres entered into
 the EMST table for tax reporting purposes. Loaded, unloaded, and off road kilometres
 totaled by equipment and province or just to level of state.
