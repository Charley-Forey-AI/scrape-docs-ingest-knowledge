# EM Equipment Location By Current Job

You can use the EM Equipment Location By Current Job
 report to create a list ordered by Job of the last known location of equipment by selecting
 Equipment Management > Reports > EM Equipment Location By Current
 Job.
This report's location is derived from the last EM Location Transfer
 transaction and can be filtered by JCCO and Job.
