# EM Revenue, Cost, Depreciation, To Date

You can use the EM Revenue, Cost, Depreciation, To Date
 report to show (at the equipment level) the depreciation taken, depreciation costs, other
 costs, revenue, and profit by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Revenue, Cost, Depreciation, To
 Date.
This report is sorted by Category, Equipment, and Month shows at the equipment
 level the depreciation taken, depreciation costs, other costs, revenue, and profit.
 You can elect to print at the equipment level or to the detail level of month.
 Parameters are month range, equip range, and category range. Report runs on a custom
 view created from the EMAR, EMMC, and EMDS.
