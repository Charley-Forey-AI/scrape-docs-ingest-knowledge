# EM Equipment

You can use the EM Equipment report for a list of
 the setup information entered in EM Equipment for Component Type equipment by selecting
 Equipment Management > Reports > EM Equipment.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Sort by (E)quipment or (D)epartment
Enter E or D.

Beginning Department
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning department.

Ending Department
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending department.

Beginning EquipmentSelect the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Ending EquipmentSelect the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.
