# EM Equipment Parts Life

You can use the EM Equipment Parts Life report to list
 Equipment and its current Job assignment by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Equipment Parts Life.
This report in Equipment order lists the Equipment and its current Job assignment. Below each equipment is a list of transfer transactions for the time chosen in the input parameters.
