# EM Depreciation Schedule – MTD and YTD

You can use the EM Depreciation Schedule – MTD and YTD
 report to show the MTD, YTD, and accumulated prior year depreciation that has been taken by
 selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Depreciation Schedule – MTD and
 YTD.
This report shows the MTD, YTD, and accumulated prior year depreciation that has been taken. It also shows the depreciation method, number of months to depreciate the declining balance factor, first month to depreciate, and the total to depreciate.
The asset account, accumulated depreciation account, and depreciation expense account print with each asset.
The report will include assets that have not been disposed of and assets that were disposed of in the fiscal year, which is defined by the Fiscal Year End Month parameter.
Tables used are EMDP, EMDS, and EMDM.
