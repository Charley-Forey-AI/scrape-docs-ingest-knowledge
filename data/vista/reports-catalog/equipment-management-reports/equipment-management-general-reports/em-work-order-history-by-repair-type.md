# EM Work Order History By Repair Type

You can use the EM Work Order History By Repair Type
 report for a list of equipment and its work orders sorted by repair type by selecting
 Equipment Management > Reports > EM Work Order History By Repair
 Type.
This report provids a list of equipment and its work orders sorted by repair
 type. Shows total cost by work order and by repair type. Tables used; EMWI, EMEM,
 EMCD, EMRX. Parameters are choices of equipment, categories, work order status, and
 repair types.
