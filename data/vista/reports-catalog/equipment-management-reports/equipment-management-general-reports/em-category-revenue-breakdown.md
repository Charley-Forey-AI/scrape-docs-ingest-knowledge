# EM Category Revenue Breakdown

You can use the EM Category Revenue Breakdown report for a
 list of revenue and revenue breakdown codes with rates by category by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Category Revenue Breakdown.
Input parameters include beginning and ending category code, and
 group.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Template
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning template. Leave blank to include all templates.

Ending Template
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending template. Leave blank to include all templates.
