# EM Monthly Cost And Revenue Drilldown

You can use the EM Monthly Cost And Revenue Drilldown
 report as a drill-down of both EM cost and revenue information by Category and Equipment (or
 starting with Equipment) by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Monthly Cost And Revenue
 Drilldown.
This is a report that allows drilling down to both EM cost and revenue information by Category and Equipment (or starting with Equipment). After the Equipment level, users can then continue drilling down by either Cost Code or Revenue Code to the transaction detail. The report allows comparison of usage hours, dollars, and cost for one month and To Date (up to the through month).
