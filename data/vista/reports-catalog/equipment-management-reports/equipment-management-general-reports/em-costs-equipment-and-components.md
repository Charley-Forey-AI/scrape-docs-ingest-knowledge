# EM Costs Equipment and Components

You can use the EM Costs Equipment and Components report
 for a list of equipment and its costs alone, followed by a list of its components and their
 costs by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Costs Equipment and
 Components.
This report lists equipment and its costs alone, followed by a list of its
 components and their costs. Parameters are company, range of equipment and months.
 Tables used are EMCD and EMEM.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Beginning Month

Ending Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Ending Month

Sort by (E)quipment or (C)omponent
Enter E or C.

Ending Equipment
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Beginning Component
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning component. Leave blank to include all components.

Ending Component
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending component. Leave blank to include all components.
