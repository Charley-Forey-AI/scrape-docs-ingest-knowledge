# EM Allocation Codes

You can use the EM Allocation Codes report to see how the
 Allocation code is set up (and lists the Posting Options and will display the Equipment,
 Categories, and Departments that will be affected) by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Allocation Codes.
