# EM Summary of Disposed Asset Account

You can use the EM Summary of Disposed Asset Account
 report for a list sorted by company, depreciation asset account, then by equipment from
 table EMDP by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Summary of Disposed Asset
 Account.
This report sorts by company, depreciation asset account, then by equipment
 from table EMDP. Shows cost detail by source and month. Parameters are range of
 months.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Beginning Month

Ending Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Ending Month
