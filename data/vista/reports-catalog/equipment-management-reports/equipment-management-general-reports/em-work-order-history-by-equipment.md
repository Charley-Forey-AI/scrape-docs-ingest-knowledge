# EM Work Order History By Equipment

You can use the EM Work Order History By Equipment report
 for summary detail and status of work order information in order by equipment number by
 selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Work Order History By
 Equipment.
This report provides summary detail and status of work order information in
 order by equipment number. Tables used EMEM, EMWH, and EMWI.
