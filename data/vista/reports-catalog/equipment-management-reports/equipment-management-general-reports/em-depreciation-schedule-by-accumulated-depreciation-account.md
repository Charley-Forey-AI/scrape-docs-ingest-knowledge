# EM Depreciation Schedule By Accumulated Depreciation Account

You can use the EM Depreciation Schedule By Accumulated Depreciation Account report to show the accumulated depreciation amounts by accumulated depreciation account. Access this report by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Depreciation Schedule By Accumulated Depreciation Account.
 Report is sort by accumulated depreciation account, and shows the prior years taken, month-to-date taken, and year-to date-taken amounts, as well as the total accumulated depreciation amount.
