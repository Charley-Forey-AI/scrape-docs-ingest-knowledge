# EM Equipment Locations (By Category)

You can use the EM Equipment Locations (By Category)
 report for a list of equipment by Equipment Category, then Equipment by selecting
 Equipment Management > Reports > EM Equipment Locations (By
 Category).
This report is organized by Equipment Category, then Equipment. It displays the current JC Company, Job, and Location.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Category
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning category, leave blank to include all.

Ending Category
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending category or leave blank to select all.
