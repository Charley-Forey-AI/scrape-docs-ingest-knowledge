# EM Revenue Rates by Category

You can use the EM Revenue Rates by Category report to
 list the revenue rates and revenue breakdown codes set up by equipment category by selecting
 Equipment Management > Reports > EM Revenue Rates by Category.
This report lists the revenue rates and revenue breakdown codes set up by
 equipment category. Tables are EMCM and EMRR.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Category
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning category, leave blank to include all.

Ending Category
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending category or leave blank to select all.

checkbox to include details for revenue breakdown codes.
