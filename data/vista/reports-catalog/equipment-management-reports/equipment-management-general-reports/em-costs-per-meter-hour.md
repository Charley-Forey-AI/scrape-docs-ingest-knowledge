# EM Costs Per Meter Hour

You can use the EM Costs Per Meter Hour report to print
 the average cost per hour and mile by Equipment and Month by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Costs Per Meter Hour.
This report obtains the meter information from the EMMR table, which is
 updated by the EM Meter Readings program, and the costs from the EM Cost Detail
 table.
