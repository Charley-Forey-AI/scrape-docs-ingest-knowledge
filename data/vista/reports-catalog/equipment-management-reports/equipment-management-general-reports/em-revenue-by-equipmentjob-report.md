# EM Revenue by Equipment/Job Report

You can use the EM Revenue by Equipment/Job Report report
 to list Equipment that has been charged to Jobs by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Revenue by Equipment/Job
 Report.
This report lists the Equipment that have been charged to Jobs. There are two levels of detail. The Summary Level lists the Hours and Amounts totaled by Equipment and Job. The Detail Level list the transactions with totals by Month, Equipment, and Job.
