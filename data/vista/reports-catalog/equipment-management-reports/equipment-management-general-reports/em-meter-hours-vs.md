# EM Meter Hours Vs. Revenue Hours

You can use the EM Meter Hours Vs. Revenue Hours report to
 assess the accuracy of usage (revenue) hours as compared to the equipment hour meter updated
 through the EM Meter Readings program by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Meter Hours Vs. Revenue
 Hours.
This report provides a way for assessing the accuracy of usage (revenue) hours as compared to the equipment hour meter updated through the EM Meter Readings program. The report subtracts the previous hour readings from the current hour reading for each Meter Reading and Revenue record. Therefore, Revenue Hours will only include data posted to revenue codes marked for updating the hour meter, thereby excluding irrelevant usage hours, such as idle time.
