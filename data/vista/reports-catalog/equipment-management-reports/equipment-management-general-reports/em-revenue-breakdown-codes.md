# EM Revenue Breakdown Codes

You can use the EM Revenue Breakdown Codes report for a
 list of Revenue Breakdown Codes and their descriptions by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Revenue Breakdown Codes.
This report provides a list of Revenue Breakdown Codes and their descriptions.
 Report input parameters include EMGroup, beginning, and ending revenue breakdown
 code and an option to print notes.
Parameters

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Revenue Breakdown Code
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning revenue breakdown code
 or leave blank to select all.

Ending Revenue Breakdown Code
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning revenue breakdown code
 or leave blank to select all.

Print Notes?
checkbox to print notes.
