# EM Component Transfers

You can use the EM Component Transfers report for a list
 by equipment / component the component transfers made during the range of dates selected by
 selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Component Transfers.
This report shows date transferred onto the equipment and off of the equipment, hours on / off, miles on / off, and fuels on / off.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Component
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning component. Leave blank to include all components.

Ending Component
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending component. Leave blank to include all components.

Beginning Transfer Date
Enter beginning transfer date. Leave blank to include all dates.

Show Master Equipment Information
checkbox to show master equipment master information. Leave unchecked to exclude master equipment information.
