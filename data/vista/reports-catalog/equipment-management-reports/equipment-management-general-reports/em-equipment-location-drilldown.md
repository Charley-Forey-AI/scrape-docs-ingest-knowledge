# EM Equipment Location Drilldown

You can use the EM Equipment Location Drilldown report by
 selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Equipment Location
 Drilldown.
The EM Equipment Location Drilldown report has two levels. The first displays
 the job, location, category, equipment number and description from the equipment
 master record. The second level shows the history of where the equipment has been
 and when it was transferred including month, transaction, batch ID, from job, to
 job, from location, to location, date in, time in, date out, time out, memo, and
 estimated out.
