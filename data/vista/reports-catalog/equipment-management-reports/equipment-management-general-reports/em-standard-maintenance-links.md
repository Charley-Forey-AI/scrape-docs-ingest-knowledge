# EM Standard Maintenance Links

You can use the EM Standard Maintenance Links report for
 standard maintenance group and linked maintenance groups by company and equipment by
 selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Standard Maintenance Links.
This report provides the standard maintenance group and linked maintenance
 groups by company and equipment. Table EMSH.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Equipment
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Ending Equipment
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Beginning Maintenance Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning maintenance group or
 leave blank to select all.

Ending Maintenance Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending maintenance or leave blank
 to select all.
