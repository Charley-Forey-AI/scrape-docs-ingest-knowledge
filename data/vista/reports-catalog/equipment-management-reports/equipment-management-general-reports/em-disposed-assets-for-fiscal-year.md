# EM Disposed Assets for Fiscal Year

You can use the EM Disposed Assets for Fiscal Year report
 to view disposed assets for FYE showing total, MTD, YTD, accumulated, month disposed of,
 etc., by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Disposed Assets for Fiscal
 Year.
Disposed assets for FYE showing total, MTD, YTD, accumulated, month disposed of, etc. Tables used are EMDP, EMDS, and EMDM.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Current Month
Enter current month (MM/YY).

Enter Prior Fiscal Year End Month
Enter prior fiscal year ending month (MM/YY).
