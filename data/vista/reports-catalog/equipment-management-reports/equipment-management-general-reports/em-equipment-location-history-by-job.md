# EM Equipment Location History By Job

You can use the EM Equipment Location History By Job
 report to order lists the Equipment and its current Job assignment by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Equipment Location History By
 Job.
This report in Equipment order lists the Equipment and its current Job assignment. Below each equipment is a list of transfer transactions for the time chosen in the input parameters.
