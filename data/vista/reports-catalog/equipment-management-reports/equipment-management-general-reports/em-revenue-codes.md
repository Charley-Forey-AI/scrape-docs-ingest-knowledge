# EM Revenue Codes

You can use the EM Revenue Codes report for a list of
 revenue codes by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Revenue Codes.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Revenue Code
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning revenue code or leave
 blank to select all.

Ending Revenue Code
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending revenue code or leave blank
 to select all.
