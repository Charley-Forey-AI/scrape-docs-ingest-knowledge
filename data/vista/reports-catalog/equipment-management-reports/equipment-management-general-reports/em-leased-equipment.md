# EM Leased Equipment

You can use the EM Leased Equipment report for a list of
 Leased Equipment by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Leased Equipment.
This report will list Leased Equipment. Beginning and ending date parameters
 will look at the ending Lease Date.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Lease Ending Date
Enter beginning lease ending date (MM/DD/YY) for leased equipment to be included or leave blank for all.

Ending Lease Ending Date
Enter ending lease ending date (MM/DD/YY) for leased equipment to be included or leave blank for all.

Print Notes?
checkbox to print notes.
