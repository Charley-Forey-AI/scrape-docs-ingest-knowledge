# EM Assets by GL Account

You can use the EM Assets by GL Account report to list EM
 Assets in GL Account order (displaying the Equipment Code, Asset, Purchase Price, and
 optionally the Disposal Month) by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Assets by GL Account.
Note: If excluding equipment disposed of in the fiscal year being reported, expenses
 will not match GL Accounts.

Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Thru Month
Enter thru month (MM/YY).

Prior Fiscal Year End Month (Required)
Enter prior fiscal year-end month (MM/YY).

Include Disposed Assets?
checkbox to include assets that have been disposed. Leave unchecked to exclude assets that have been disposed.

Show Depreciation Expense Accounts
checkbox to show depreciation expense accounts. Leave unchecked to exclude depreciation expense accounts.
