# EM Depreciation Schedule By Asset Account

You can use the EM Depreciation Schedule By Asset Account
 report to sort by asset account by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Depreciation Schedule By Asset
 Account.
Sorted by asset account; shows prior years taken, month to date taken, year to date taken, and accumulated depreciation. Tables used are EMDP, EMDS, and EMDM.
Parameters are Company, Current Month, Prior fiscal year ending month.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Current Month
Enter current month (MM/YY).

Enter Prior Fiscal Year End Month
Enter prior fiscal year ending month (MM/YY).
