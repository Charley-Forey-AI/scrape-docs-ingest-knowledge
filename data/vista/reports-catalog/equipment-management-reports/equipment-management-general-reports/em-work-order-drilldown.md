# EM Work Order Drilldown

You can use the EM Work Order Drilldown report to drill
 down through WO details by company, category, equipment, work order, work order Item, then
 to details of costs, parts, or timecards by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Work Order Drilldown.
This report provides a drilldown through WO details by company, category,
 equipment, work order, work order Item, then to details of costs, parts, or
 timecards. Parameters are sort selection of category, equipment, shop, or mechanic.
Selections are made in ranges of: categories, equipment, work orders, mechanics, shops, status codes, and open only or all. At the equipment level, you can drill to the warranty information. The main tables used are EMWF, EMWH, EMWI, EMCD, EMWP, and EMWT.
