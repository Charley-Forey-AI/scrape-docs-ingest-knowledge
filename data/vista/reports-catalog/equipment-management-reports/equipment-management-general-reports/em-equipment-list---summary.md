# EM Equipment List - Summary

You can use the EM Equipment List - Summary report for a
 summarized list of equipment including equipment code, description, vehicle identification
 number, department, status and attach to equipment information by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Equipment List - Summary.
This report's input parameters include company, beginning, and ending
 equipment codes.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Sort By (E)quipment or (D)epartment
Enter E or D.

Department (Blank for All)
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select department or leave blank to
 select all departments.

Beginning Category
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning category, leave blank to include all.

(E)quipment, (C)omponents, or (B)oth
Enter E, C, or B.
