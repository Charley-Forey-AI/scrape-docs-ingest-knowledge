# EM Asset Depreciation Schedule

You can use the EM Asset Depreciation Schedule report to
 list the setup from the EM Asset Setup by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Asset Depreciation Schedule.
This report will list the setup from the EM Asset Setup, and will also display
 the total depreciation taken to date.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Equipment
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Ending Equipment
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Include Disposed Assets?
checkbox to include assets that have been disposed. Leave unchecked to exclude assets that have been disposed.
