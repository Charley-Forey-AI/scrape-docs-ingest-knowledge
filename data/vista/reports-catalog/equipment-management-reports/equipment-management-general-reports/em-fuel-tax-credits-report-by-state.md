# EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By State

You can use the EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By State report
 for fuel tax reporting by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By
 State.
Used for fuel tax reporting, the EM Fuel Tax Credits Report By State prints the Off Road Miles, Average Miles Per Gallon, and Off Road Gallons by State, EM Category, and Equipment. Similar to the other EM fuel reports, the Off Road Gallons column is calculated by dividing Off Road miles by the total miles per gallon (MPG). MPG is the total distance traveled (Off Road and On Road, unloaded + loaded) divided by the total fuel purchased.
