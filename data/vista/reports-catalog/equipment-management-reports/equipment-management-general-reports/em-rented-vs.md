# EM Rented Vs. Owned Equipment

You can use the EM Rented Vs. Owned Equipment report for a
 listing of equipment categorized by customer owned, company owned, or rented / leased by
 selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Rented Vs. Owned Equipment.
This report provides you with a list of equipment categorized by customer
 owned, company owned, or rented / leased. Shows equipment description, status,
 model, serial and vehicle identification numbers and customer or AP vendor (lessor).
