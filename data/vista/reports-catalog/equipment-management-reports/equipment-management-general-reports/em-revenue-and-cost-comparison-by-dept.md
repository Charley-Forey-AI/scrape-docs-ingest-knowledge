# EM Revenue and Cost Comparison by Dept

You can use the EM Revenue and Cost Comparison by Dept
 report to print monthly revenue information by equipment code by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Revenue and Cost Comparison by
 Dept.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Department
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning department.

Ending Department
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending department.

Through Month
Enter through month (MM/YY) for cost and revenue to be included.

Print Equipment Detail?
Check to see equipment detail in each department. Leave blank to print department summary only.
