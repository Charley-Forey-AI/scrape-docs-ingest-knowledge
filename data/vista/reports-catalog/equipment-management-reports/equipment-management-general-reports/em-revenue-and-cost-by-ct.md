# EM Revenue and Cost by CT

You can use the EM Revenue and Cost by CT report to print
 EM cost types by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Revenue and Cost by CT.
This report prints EM Cost Types 1 - 8 in the first 8 columns. The last cost
 column is the total for all costs types 9 and greater. The "Subtotal by Department"
 parameter gives the option of sorting and subtotaling by EM Department.
