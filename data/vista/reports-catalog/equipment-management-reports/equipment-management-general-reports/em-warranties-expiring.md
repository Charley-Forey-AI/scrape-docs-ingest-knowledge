# EM Warranties Expiring

You can use the EM Warranties Expiring report for a list
 of equipment that has expiring warranties by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Warranties Expiring.
This report provides a list of equipment that has expiring warranties. Inputs
 ask for number or hours, miles, or days prior to expiration that you want warning.
 Shows installed, current and expiration levels for miles, days, and hours. Tables
 used are EMEM and EMWF.
