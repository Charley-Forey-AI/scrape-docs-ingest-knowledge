# EM W. O. Repair Types

You can use the EM W. O. Repair Types report for a list of
 work order repair types set up in EM by equipment group by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM W. O. Repair Types.
This report provides a list of work order repair types set up in EM by
 equipment group. Table is EMRX.

Company
