# EM MTD YTD Rev and Cost

You can use the EM MTD YTD Rev and Cost report for a list
 of the revenue recorded and the actual costs associated for each piece of Equipment by
 selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM MTD YTD Rev and Cost.
This report provides a list of the revenue recorded and the actual costs
 associated for each piece of Equipment. The detail is summarized through the Month
 input. This report uses the report views brvEMRevCostMthlDetail and
 brvEMGLFiscalPeriods.
