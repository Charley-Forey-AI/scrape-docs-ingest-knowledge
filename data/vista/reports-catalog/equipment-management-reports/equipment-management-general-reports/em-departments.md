# EM Departments

You can use the EM Departments report to print a
 list of the equipment departments including cost types, revenue codes and cost codes and the
 GL accounts used for each by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Departments.
Parameters include company, beginning, and ending department code and an
 option to print notes.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Department
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning department.

Ending Department
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending department.

Print Notes?
checkbox to print notes.
