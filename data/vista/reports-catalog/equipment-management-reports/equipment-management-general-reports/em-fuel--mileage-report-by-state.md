# EM Fuel & Mileage Report By State

You can use the EM Fuel & Mileage Report By State
 report to see the number of miles and gallons ON Highway, and OFF Highway separated into two
 groups of columns for diesel and gasoline by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Fuel & Mileage Report By
 State.
Used for fuel tax reporting, this report shows the number of miles and gallons
 ON Highway, and OFF Highway separated into two groups of columns for diesel and
 gasoline.
The report uses information from the EM Miles by State Detail table, which is entered and posted from the EM Miles By State program, and EM Cost Detail for fuel purchased. Off Road and On Road Gallons are calculated by dividing Off Road or On Road miles by the total miles per gallon (MPG). MPG is the total distance traveled (Off Road and On Road, unloaded + loaded) divided by the total fuel purchased. The MPG calculation follows standard methodology from fuel tax reporting requirements for IFTA (International Fuel Tax Agreement) and other states.
