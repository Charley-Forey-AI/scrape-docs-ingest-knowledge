# EM Average Miles Per Gallon

You can use the EM Average Miles Per Gallon for a list of
 selected equipment for a range of selected months by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Average Miles Per Gallon.
This report lists selected equipment for a range of selected months. The total
 gallons will be based on fuel posted to the EM Fuel Cost Type defined in EM Company
 Parameters. Total miles will be from entries made in EM Miles by State programs.
 Tables used are EMSM, EMSD, EMCD, and EMEM.
Report Parameters

Company
