# EM Miles By State

You can use the EM Miles By State report to see EM miles
 by state by selecting Equipment Management > Reports > EM Miles By State.
This list provides EM miles by state, and lists by Company, State, and
 equipment the miles entered into the EMST table for tax reporting purposes. Loaded,
 unloaded, and off road miles totaled by equipment and state or just to level of
 state.
