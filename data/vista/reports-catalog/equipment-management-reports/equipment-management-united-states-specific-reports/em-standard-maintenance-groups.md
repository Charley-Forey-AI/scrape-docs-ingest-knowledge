# EM Standard Maintenance Groups

You can use the EM Standard Maintenance Groups report for
 a list of standard maintenance groups and setup information by equipment code by selecting
 Equipment Management > Reports > EM Standard Maintenance Groups.
This report provides a list of standard maintenance groups and setup
 information by equipment code. Linked maintenance group contained in a
 sub-report.
