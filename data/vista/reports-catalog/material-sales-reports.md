# Material Sales Reports

Material Sales Reports
Material Sales is a fully integrated module that provides a complete system with which you can track and process materials that your company produces and/or sells. Used in conjunction with Job Cost, Equipment Management, and Inventory, it eliminates duplicate entry and streamlines the flow of information.
Reports in Material Sales have two categories: general reports that are available to every supported geographic location and United States-specific reports. Click on a link for more information on reports in the associated category.

- [Material Sales General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/material-sales-reports/material-sales-general-reports)

- [Material Sales United States-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/material-sales-reports/material-sales-united-states-specific-reports)
