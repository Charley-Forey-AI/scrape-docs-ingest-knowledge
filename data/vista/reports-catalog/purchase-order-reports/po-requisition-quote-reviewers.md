# PO Requisition Quote Reviewers

You can use the PO Requisition Quote Reviewers report for
 a list of quotes assigned to reviewers which may be run by Quote Review Status for a single
 reviewer or for all reviewers by selecting Purchase Order > Reports > PO Requisition Quote Reviewers.

Company
