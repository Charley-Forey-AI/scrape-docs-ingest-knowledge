# PO Requisition Entry Report

You can use the PO Requisition Entry Report report to
 print the details entered in the PO Requisition Entry maintenance form by selecting
 Purchase Order > Reports > PO Requisition Entry Report.
This report prints the details entered in the PO Requisition Entry maintenance form. It can be ran for a range of RQ IDs with options for printing Shipping information, Quote details, or Notes.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Click the Field Lookup
