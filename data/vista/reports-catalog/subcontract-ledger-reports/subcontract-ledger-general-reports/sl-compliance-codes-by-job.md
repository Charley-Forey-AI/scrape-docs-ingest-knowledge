# SL Compliance Codes By Job

You can use the SL Compliance Codes By Job report to print
 compliance information maintained in the SL Compliance program by Job and Subcontract by
 selecting Subcontract Ledger > Reports > SL Compliance Codes By Job.
This report prints compliance information maintained in the SL Compliance program by Job and Subcontract. When choosing "Y" for the Print Non-Compliant Codes Only? Parameter, the report will only include date type compliance codes with expiration dates on or before the Expiration Date parameter. Also when selecting "Y" for Print Non-Compliant Codes Only?, Yes/No type compliance codes are only printed if not marked as complied in the SL Compliance program.
