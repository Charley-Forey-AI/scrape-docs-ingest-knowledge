# SL Subcontract

You can use the SL Subcontract report by selecting
 Subcontract Ledger > Reports > SL Subcontract.
Subcontract agreement with list of Original Items, Item Quantities, Unit Prices, and Totals. This report could easily be copied to a new report and the verbiage changed to meet the requirements between the Contractor and Subcontractor. The "Use (S)L StartDate or (A)greement Date" parameter causes the report to print either the Subcontract header start date or the inputted agreement date (Agreement Date parameter) on the form.
