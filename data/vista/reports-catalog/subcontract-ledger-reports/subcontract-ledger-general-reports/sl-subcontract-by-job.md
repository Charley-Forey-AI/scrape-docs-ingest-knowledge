# SL Subcontract By Job

You can use the SL Subcontract By Job report by selecting
 Subcontract Ledger > Reports > SL Subcontract By Job.
The Subcontracts by Job report gives the Current Due and To Be Billed for each subcontract on a Job through a selected month. The columns of Current Contract (Subcontract), Billed, Paid, and Retainage used to calculate Current Due and To Be Billed also print on the report. Sales and VAT taxes are reflected in all amounts only if the "Include Sales and VAT taxes?" parameter is checked. Use taxes are shown in a separate column only if the "Include Use Tax?" parameter is checked.
