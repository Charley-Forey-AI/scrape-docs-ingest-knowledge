# SL Drilldown By Item

You can use the SL Drilldown By Item report by selecting
 Subcontract Ledger > Reports > SL Drilldown By Item.
The report provides the ability of drilling down by Subcontract Number
 and Subcontract Item to all types of subcontract-related detail. The first two levels
 (Subcontract and SL Item) show a snapshot of the Current Subcontract Cost, Invoiced, and
 % Billed. When viewing the item level, users have the choice of drilling down to see
 either compliance detail, subcontract change orders, or AP transactions.
