# MS Quotes

You can use the MS Quotes report to display all the
 information entered in MS Quote and also displays the related Quote Detail information by
 selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Quotes.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Quote Type: (C)ustomer, (J)ob, (I)nventory, Blank for All
Enter C, J, I, or leave blank for all.

Sort by (Q)uote Type or Quote (I)D
Enter Q or I.

Beginning Customer
Press F4 to select the beginning customer for sorting.

Ending Customer
Press F4 to select the ending customer for sorting.

Beginning JCCo
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job cost company.

Ending JCCo
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending job cost company or leave blank to select all.

Beginning Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job.

Ending Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending job.

Beginning INCo
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning inventory company, or
 leave blank for all.

Ending INCo
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending inventory company, or leave
 blank for all.

Beginning Location
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning location.

Ending Location
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending location.

Active Only?
checkbox to only print active quotes.

(S)ummary or (D)etail
Enter S or D.

checkbox to print notes.
