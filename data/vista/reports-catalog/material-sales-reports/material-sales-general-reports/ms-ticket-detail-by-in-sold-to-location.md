# MS Ticket Detail by IN Sold To Location

You can use the MS Ticket Detail by IN Sold To Location
 report to list tickets for materials sold to other Inventory locations by selecting
 Material Sales > Reports > MS Ticket Detail by IN Sold To
 Location.
This reports lists tickets for materials sold to other Inventory locations. The report prints one line for each ticket and sorts by IN Company, Sold To Location, Month, Sale Date, and Ticket Number. The report will restrict tickets by month or ticket date depending on the Posted (M)ont. or Ticket (D)ate parameter.
