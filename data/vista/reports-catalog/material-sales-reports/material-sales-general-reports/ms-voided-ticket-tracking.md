# MS Voided Ticket Tracking

You can use the MS Voided Ticket Tracking report to print
 only tickets that have been voided by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Voided Ticket Tracking.
This report prints only tickets that have been voided. The report sorts by month and sale date and gives users the option to print Reason Codes and Notes.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Enter beginning month (MM/YY).

Ending Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Ending Month

Enter beginning date or leave blank for all.
