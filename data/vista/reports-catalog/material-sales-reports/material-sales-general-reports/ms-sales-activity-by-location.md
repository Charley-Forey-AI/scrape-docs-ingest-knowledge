# MS Sales Activity By Location

You can use the MS Sales Activity By Location report by
 selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Sales Activity By Location.
The MS Sales Activity by Location report prints sale units, dollars, and haul amounts by inventory location and can summarize this information one of five ways. Month (1), Matl Category (2), Material (3), Sales Type (4), Detail (5). When printing at the detail level, the report sorts and summarizes by the following fields depending on the type of sale. Cust Sale. one line per Customer/Customer Job. Job Sale. one line per Job Cost Co#/Job. Inventory Sale. one line per IN Co#/"Sold To" location. Also, depending on the level of detail selected, the report prints subtotals by material, category, and location for each month. The report then prints a total for each selected location for all months as well as a grand total for all locations.
