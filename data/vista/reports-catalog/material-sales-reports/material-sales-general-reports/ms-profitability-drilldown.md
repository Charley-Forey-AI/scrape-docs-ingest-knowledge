# MS Profitability Drilldown

You can use the MS Profitability Drilldown report to
 quickly assess profitability by Invoice, Customer, Invoice Date Range, Inventory To- and
 From Locations, Material and Material Category by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Profitability Drilldown.
The MS Profitability Drilldown allows the user to quickly assess profitability by Invoice, Customer, Invoice Date Range, Inventory To- and From Locations, Material and Material Category. There is the option to sort the report by Invoice Number, Customer, or Invoice Date.
