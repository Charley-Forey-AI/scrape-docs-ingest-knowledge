# MS Quote Detail

You can use the MS Quote Detail report for a listing of
 the MS Quote Details showing remaining units for the quote by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Quote Detail.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Sort by Quote (I)D or (T)ype
Enter I or T.

Beginning Quote ID
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning quote ID or leave blank
 for all.

Ending Quote ID
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending quote ID or leave blank for
 all.

(C)ustomer, (J)ob, (I)nventory, (Blank for All)
Enter C, J, I, or leave blank for all.

Beginning Customer
Press F4 to select the beginning customer for sorting.

Ending Customer
Press F4 to select the ending customer for sorting.

Beginning Sold to IN Company
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning sold to inventory
 company, or leave blank for all.

Ending Sold to IN Company
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending sold to inventory company,
 or leave blank for all.

Beginning Sold to Location
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning sold to location, or
 leave blank for all.

Ending Sold to Location
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending sold to location, or leave
 blank for all.

Beginning Job Cost Company
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job cost company.

Ending Job Cost Company
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending job cost company or leave blank to select all.

Beginning Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning job.

Ending Job
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending job.
