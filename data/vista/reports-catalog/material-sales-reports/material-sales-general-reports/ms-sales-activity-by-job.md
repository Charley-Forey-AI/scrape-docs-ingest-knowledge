# MS Sales Activity By Job

You can use the MS Sales Activity By Job report to print
 sale units, dollars, and haul amounts by job by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Sales Activity By Job.
The Material Sales Activity by Job report prints sale units, dollars, and haul amounts by job. Depending on the print level selected, the report summarizes the sales information by printing one line per (1) Month , (2)Location, (3) Matl Category. or (4) Material. Depending on the print level, the report also subtotals the sales data for material category, location, and month. The report then prints a total for each job selected as well as a grand total for all jobs.
