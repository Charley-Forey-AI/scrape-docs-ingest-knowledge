# MS Ticket Cost/Revenue Exceptions

You can use the MS Ticket Cost/Revenue Exceptions report
 to list the MS tickets where the material cost is greater than material revenues by
 selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Ticket Cost/Revenue
 Exceptions.
This report lists the MS tickets where the material cost is greater than material revenues. It also calculates the difference between the cost and revenue and lists it as the variance.
