# MS Quote Form

You can use the MS Quote Form report to print out the
 information from the MS Quotes program based on the type of quote that has been created
 along with Material, Haul and Discount information by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Quote Form.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Quote
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning quote or leave blank for
 all.

Ending Quote
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending quote or leave blank for
 all.

Beginning Customer
Press F4 to select the beginning customer for sorting.

Ending Customer
