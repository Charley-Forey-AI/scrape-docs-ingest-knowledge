# MS Hauler Time Sheets

You can use the MS Hauler Time Sheets report for a list of
 Haul, Pay, and Revenue detail for MS Hauler Time Sheets by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Hauler Time Sheets.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

By Posted (M)onth or Ticke. (D)ate
Enter M or D.

Beginning Month
Enter beginning month or leave blank for all.

Ending Month
Enter ending month or leave blank for all.

Beginning Ticket Date
Enter beginning ticket date or leave blank for all.

Ending Ticket Date
Enter ending ticket date or leave blank for all.

(H)aul Vendor or (E)quipment (Blank for All)
Enter H or E or leave blank for all.

Beginning Haul Vendor
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning haul vendor or leave blank for all.

Ending Haul Vendor
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending haul vendor or leave blank for all.

EM Company (Blank for All)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the EM company or leave blank for all.

Beginning Equipment
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select beginning equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Ending Equipment
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select ending equipment code or leave blank to select all equipment.

Print Header Notes/
checkbox to print header notes.
