# MS Ticket Detail by Job

You can use the MS Ticket Detail by Job report to print
 tickets for materials sold to jobs by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Ticket Detail by Job.
This report prints tickets for materials sold to jobs. The report prints one line for each ticket and sorts by JC Co, Job, material phase, month, and sale date. The report will restrict tickets by month or ticket date depending on the Posted (M)ont. or Ticket (D)ate parameter.
