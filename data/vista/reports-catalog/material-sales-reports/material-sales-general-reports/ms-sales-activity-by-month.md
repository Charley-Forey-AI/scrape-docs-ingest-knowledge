# MS Sales Activity By Month

You can use the MS Sales Activity By Month report by
 selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Sales Activity By Month.
The Material Sales Activity by Month report prints sale units, dollars, and haul amounts by month and can summarize this information one of five ways. (1)Loc, (2)Matl Category, (3) Material, (4) Sales Type, (5) Detail. When printing at the detail level, the report sorts and summarizes by the following fields depending on the type of sale. Cust Sale. one line per Customer/Customer Job. Job Sale. one line per Job Cost Co#/Job. Inventory Sale. one line per IN Co#/"Sold To" location. For each month and inventory location, the report prints subtotals for material, material category, and location. The report then prints a total for each month selected as well as a grand total for all categories.
