# MS Deleted Ticket Tracking

You can use the MS Deleted Ticket Tracking report to print
 out deleted tickets from the MSTX table by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Deleted Ticket Tracking.
This is a report that prints out deleted tickets from the MSTX table. Deleted
 ticket information is only updated when the -Saving Deleted Tickets- option is
 checked in the MS Company Parameters and tickets marked for Delete are posted from
 the MS Ticket Entry program. Deleted ticket information will be restricted by month
 or ticket date depending on the Posted (M)ont. or Ticket (D)ate parameter.
