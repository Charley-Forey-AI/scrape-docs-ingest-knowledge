# MS Payment Discount Templates

You can use the MS Payment Discount Templates report for a
 list of the details entered in the MS Payment Discount Template form by selecting
 Material Sales > Reports > MS Payment Discount Templates.
Access this report by clicking Material Sales > Reports > MS Payment Discount Templates.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Customer Number (Blank for All)
Click the Field Lookup

 all.

Enter or select the applicable beginning invoice date.

Ending Invoice Date
Enter or select the applicable ending invoice date.

SaleType: (C)ustomer, (I)nventory, (J)ob or (Blank for All)
