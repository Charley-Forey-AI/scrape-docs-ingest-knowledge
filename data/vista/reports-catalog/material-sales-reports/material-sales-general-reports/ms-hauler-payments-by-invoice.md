# MS Hauler Payments By Invoice

You can use the MS Hauler Payments By Invoice report by
 selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Hauler Payments By Invoice.
This report, summarized by Sale Type or Hauler Truck, lists MS tickets that have been reconciled to an Accounts Payable Invoice using the program MS Hauler Payment Worksheet. The reports prints the ticket, Sale date, Material, Pay Code, UM, Pay Basis, Pay Rate, and Pay Total and is totaled by AP Invoice.
