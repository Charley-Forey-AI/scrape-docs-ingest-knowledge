# MS Customer Quotes Drilldown

You can use the MS Customer Quotes Drilldown report by
 selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Customer Quotes Drilldown.
This drilldown report begins with a list of customers who have quotes. The second level of the drill lists the quotes by number and either summary or detail information based on the input choice. The third level displays any Haul Codes, Haul Zones, Job Phases, Pay Codes, Price Overrides, Discount Overrides and the Quote Detail that exists for the quote.
