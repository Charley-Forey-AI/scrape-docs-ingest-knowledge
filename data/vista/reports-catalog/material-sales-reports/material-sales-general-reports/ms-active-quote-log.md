# MS Active Quote Log

You can use the MS Active Quote Log report to see all open
 quotes that are either active with no expiration date or active and the expiration date is
 greater than the cutoff date used for printing. You can access this report by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Active Quote Log.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Cutoff Date
Enter cutoff date.
