# MS Ticket Detail

You can use the MS Ticket Detail report to show all
 tickets entered for a day, or a range of dates, regardless of sale type by selecting
 Material Sales > Reports > MS Ticket Detail.
The report shows all tickets entered for a day, or a range of dates, regardless of sale type. The report displays one line for each ticket and sorts by From Location, Month and Sale Type or SaleType and Date (run-time option). Additionally, Sale Types are further sorted in the following order. Customer Sales. Cust # and Name. Inventory Sales. Sold To Co / Location. Job Sales. Job Co / Job #. The report will restrict tickets by month or ticket date depending on the Posted (M)ont. or Ticket (D)ate parameter.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Posted (M)onth or Ticket (D)ate
Enter M or D.

Beginning Month
Enter beginning month (MM/YY).

Ending Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Ending Month

Beginning Ticket Date
Enter beginning ticket date (MM/DD/YY) or leave blank for all.

Ending Ticket Date
Enter ending ticket date (MM/DD/YY) or leave blank for all.

Beginning Material
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning material.

Ending Material
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending material.
