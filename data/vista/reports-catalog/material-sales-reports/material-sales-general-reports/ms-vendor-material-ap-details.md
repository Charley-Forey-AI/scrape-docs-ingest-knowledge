# MS Vendor Material AP Details

You can use the MS Vendor Material AP Details report to
 list the AP line detail for AP Invoices based on the Vendor, AP Reference and AP Invoice
 date ranges entered by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Vendor Material AP Details.
This report will list the AP line detail for AP Invoices based on the Vendor, AP Reference and AP Invoice date ranges entered. Then the report lists the MS Ticket details for that AP Reference. The MS Ticket detail material cost is comprised of the material cost plus tax minus discount. An option is available to page by AP Reference.
