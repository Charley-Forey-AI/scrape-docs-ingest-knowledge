# MS Sales Activity By Customer

You can use the MS Sales Activity By Customer report by
 selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Sales Activity By Customer.
The Material Sales Activity by Customer report prints sales units, dollars and haul amounts and allows the user to sort by either customer sort name or customer job code. Depending on the print level selected, the report summarizes the sales information by printing one line per (1) Month , (2)Location, (3) Matl Category. or (4) Material. Depending on the print level, the report also subtotals the sales data for material category, location, and month. The report then prints a total for each customer selected as well as a grand total for all customers.
