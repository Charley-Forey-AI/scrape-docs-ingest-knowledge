# MS Sales Activity By Material Category

You can use the MS Sales Activity By Material Category
 report by selecting Material Sales > Reports > MS Sales Activity By Material
 Category.
The MS Sales Activity by Material Category report prints sale units, dollars, and haul amounts by material category and can summarize this information one of four ways. (1) Month, (2) Loc, (3) Sales Type, (4) Detail. When printing at the detail level, the report sorts and summarizes by the following fields depending on the type of sale. Cust Sale. one line per Customer/Customer Job. Job Sale. one line per Job Cost Co#/Job. Inventory Sale. one line per IN Co#/"Sold To" location. Depending on the level of detail selected, the report prints subtotals by location (within each month), month, and material. The report then prints a total for each selected material category as well as a grand total for all categories.
