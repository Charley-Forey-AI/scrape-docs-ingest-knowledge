# General Ledger Reports

All of Viewpoint’s Accounting modules are fully integrated
 with the General Ledger.
Reports in General Ledger have two categories: general reports that
 are available to every supported geographic location and Canada-specific reports. Click
 on a link for more information on reports in the associated category.

- [General Ledger General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/general-ledger-reports/general-ledger-general-reports)

- [General Ledger Canada-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/general-ledger-reports/general-ledger-canada-specific-reports)
