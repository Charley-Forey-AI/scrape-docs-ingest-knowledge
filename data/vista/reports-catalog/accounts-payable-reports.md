# Accounts Payable Reports

The Accounts Payable (AP) module provides a number of reports to support your payables
 processing.
Reports in Accounts Payable have four categories: general reports that are available to
 every supported geographic location; United States-specific reports; Canada-specific
 reports; and Australia-specific reports. Click on a link for more information on reports
 in the associated category.

- [Accounts Payable General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-payable-reports/accounts-payable-general-reports)

- [Accounts Payable United States-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-payable-reports/accounts-payable-united-states-specific-reports)

- [Accounts Payable Canada-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-payable-reports/accounts-payable-canada-specific-reports)

- [Accounts Payable Australia-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-payable-reports/accounts-payable-australia-specific-reports)
