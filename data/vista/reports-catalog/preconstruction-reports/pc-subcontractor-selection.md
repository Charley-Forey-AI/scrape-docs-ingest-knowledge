# PC Subcontractor Selection

You can use the PC Subcontractor Selection report for a
 list of subcontractors and contact information based on search criteria. To access this report, select
 Pre-Construction > Reports > PC Subcontractor Selection.
For each subcontractor on the report, detail data related to search criteria can be drilled into for analysis.
