# PC Bid Tracking

You can use the PC Bid Tracking report to show the
 projects in the lead pipeline. To access this report, select Pre-Construction > Reports > PC Bid Tracking.
 You can use this report to forecast potential costs and revenues in addition to schedule resources in the estimating department. The report lists project and bid information for each record created in the PC Potential Projects form. It is grouped project type, department, and estimator.
