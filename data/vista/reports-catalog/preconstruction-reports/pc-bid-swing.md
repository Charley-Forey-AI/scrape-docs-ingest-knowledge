# PC Bid Swing

You can use the PC Bid Swing report to show how the
 estimating department is performing when compared to their primary competitors in shared
 markets. To access this report, select Pre-Construction > Reports > PC Bid Swing.
 Each project is broken down by bid package and can be grouped any two of the following options: Prime/Sub, Project Type, Estimator, or Department. Additional sorting can be performed using either Bid Date or Bid Status.
Parameters

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Enter Department
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the department or leave blank for
 all.

Beginning Bid Date
Enter beginning bid or leave blank for all.

Ending Bid Date
Enter ending bid or leave blank for all.

Project Type
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the project type or leave blank
 for all.

Bid Status
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the bid status or leave blank for
 all.

(P)rime/(S)ub (Blank for Both)
Enter P, S or leave blank for all.

Competitor Name
Enter competitor name.

Bid Awarded Status (Y)es, (N)o or blank for all

Group 1

Group 2
Click the Field Lookup
