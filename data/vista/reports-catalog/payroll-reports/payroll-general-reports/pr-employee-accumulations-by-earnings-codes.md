# PR Employee Accumulations by Earnings Codes

You can use the PR Employee Accumulations to provide
 employee earnings for selected earning codes based on the monthly accumulation by Earnings
 Codes by selecting Payroll > Reports > PR Employee Accumulations by Earnings
 Codes.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Month For Month Details
Enter beginning month for month details.

Ending Month For Month Details
Enter ending month for month details.

Earnings Codes (ie: 1,3,10 or Blank for All)
Enter earnings code numbers separated by comma or leave blank for all.

Sort By Employee (S)ort Name or (N)umber
Enter S or N.

Show Earnings Detail?
checkbox to show earnings detail.

Print Monthly Detail?
checkbox to print monthly detail.
