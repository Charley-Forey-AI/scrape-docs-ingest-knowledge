# PR Department Costs

You can use the PR Department Costs report to print
 payroll information by department (payroll, job cost, and service management departments)
 including overtime information and employer paid liability costs by selecting Payroll > Reports > PR Department Costs.
The Payroll Department Costs report prints payroll information by department (payroll, job cost, and service management departments) including overtime information and employer paid liability costs. The report allows selection for a range of pay period end dates and summarization by department or employee as well as the choice for printing each earning code and liability type. The report prints all payroll department information first, then job cost department information, then service management department information.
For the purposes of this report, if a time card entry is coded to both a payroll and job cost department, the time entry will be accumulated under the job cost department. For service management time entries customer work orders and job cost work orders accumulate under the service management department. The department parameter selects time card records based on the same logic.
Make sure all payrolls have been completely processed prior to running this report. This will ensure that all earnings and liabilities have been updated to the most current status.
