# PR Timecards w/Addons and Liabilities

You can use the PR Timecards w/Addons and Liabilities report to show earnings, add-on earnings, and liability amounts for employees by Payroll > Reports > PR Timecards w/Addons and Liabilities.
This report sorts by employee sort name, number, or job, and shows for each pay period end date, the hours, amount, add-on earnings, and liabilities for each employee by pay seq, timecard date, craft/class, job, phase, and earnings code. Information is pulled from the PRTA (PR Timecard Addons), PRTH (PR Timecard Header), and PRTL (PR Timecard Liabs) tables.
Note: Timecards without an associated Job Cost company are only displayed if you leave the Beginning JCCo and Ending JCCo parameters blank.
