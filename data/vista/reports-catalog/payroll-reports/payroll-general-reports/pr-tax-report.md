# PR Tax Report

You can use the PR Tax Report report by selecting
 Payroll > Reports > PR Tax Report.
The PR Tax Report may be used for State Unemployment reports instead of the Unemployment report. It may also be used for city tax reporting or for any other deduction or liability code to report an employee's current month, current quarter, or year-to-date information. The YTD Gross Amount Total will always show the total of all employees YTD amounts even when you say No to Include Employees with only Year End Totals.
