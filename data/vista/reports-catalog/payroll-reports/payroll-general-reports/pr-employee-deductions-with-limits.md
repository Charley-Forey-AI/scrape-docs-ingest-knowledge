# PR Employee Deductions with Limits

You can use the PR Employee Deductions with Limits report
 to print employee deductions with a Limit Basis of Subject or Calculated and a Limit Period
 of Lifetime or Annual by selecting Payroll > Reports > PR Employee Deductions with
 Limits.
This report will print employee deductions with a Limit Basis of Subject or Calculated and a Limit Period of Lifetime or Annual. The Amount column will be based on the Limit Basis. If the Limit Basis is Subject, it will show the Eligible Amount from Employee Accumulations; if the Limit Basis is Calculated, it will show the Deduction Amount from Employee Accumulations. Limit Period determines whether the Amount shown will be for the current year or to date.
