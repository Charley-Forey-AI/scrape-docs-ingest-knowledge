# PR Time Card Hours Rate Amount Variance

You can use the PR Time Card Hours Rate Amount Variance
 report by selecting Payroll > Reports > PR Time Card Hours Rate Amount
 Variance.
The PR Time Card Hours Rate Amount Variance only prints timecard entries where the hours multiplied by the rate is not equal to the amount, thus allowing detection of keying errors prior to printing checks or processing the pay period.
