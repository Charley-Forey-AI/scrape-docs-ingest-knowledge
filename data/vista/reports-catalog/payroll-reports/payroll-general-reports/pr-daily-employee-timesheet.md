# PR Daily Employee Timesheet

You can use the PR Daily Employee Timesheet report (a
 Timesheet form for an employee to fill out for the job they are working on) by selecting
 Payroll > Reports > PR Daily Employee Timesheet.
This a Timesheet form for an employee to fill out for the job they are working on. The report will print by Company for a range of Jobs, range of Phases, range of Contract Items, with options to sort by Phase or Contract item, to include inactive phases, and to print all Phase Cost Types or just those specified. Phases and Contract Items that are on the Job (JCJP table) will be listed and spaces are provided for the employee to log their hours worked (RT, OT, DT) and equipment used on the job.
