# PR Employee Earnings With Annual Limits

You can use the PR Employee Earnings With Annual Limits
 report to display the amounts for individual earning codes that contribute to the limit of
 an earning type by selecting Payroll > Reports > PR Employee Earnings With Annual
 Limits.
This report displays the amounts for individual earning codes that contribute to the limit of an earning type. It is designed to provide additional analysis if the PR Employee Accumulations have variances or are out of balance. The current month input parameter is a required input and will determine the year of the data that is shown on the report.
