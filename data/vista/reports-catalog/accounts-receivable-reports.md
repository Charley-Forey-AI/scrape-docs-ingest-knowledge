# Accounts Receivable Reports

The Accounts Receivable module provides a number of
 reports to process a complete billing cycle, from entering invoices, to applying payments to
 those invoices, to handling reporting requirements.
By using Accounts Receivable to its fullest capacity, you can benefit from a
 full range of receivables management capabilities.
Reports in Accounts Receivable have four categories: general reports that are available
 to every supported geographic location; United States-specific reports; Canada-specific
 reports; and Australia-specific reports. Click on a link for more information on reports
 in the associated category.

- [Accounts Receivable General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-receivable-reports/accounts-receivable-general-reports)

- [Accounts Receivable United States-Specific Reports ](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-receivable-reports/accounts-receivable-united-states-specific-reports)

- [Accounts Receivable Canada-Specific Reports ](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-receivable-reports/accounts-receivable-canada-specific-reports)

- [Accounts Receivable Australia-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/accounts-receivable-reports/accounts-receivable-australia-specific-reports)
