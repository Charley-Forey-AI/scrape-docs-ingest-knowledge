# Viewpoint Administration Reports

The Viewpoint Administration module is used to manage your
 site. For example, you can set up and manage user profiles; send messages to users;
 customize forms; manage your site settings, set up security, create inquiries within the
 Vista™ by Viewpoint system, and more.
Reports in the Service Management module have two categories:
 general reports that are available to every supported geographic location and
 Australia-specific reports. Click on a link for more information on reports in the
 associated category.

- [Viewpoint Administration General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/viewpoint-administration-reports/viewpoint-administration-general-reports)

- [Viewpoint Administration United States-specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/viewpoint-administration-reports/viewpoint-administration-united-states-specific-reports)
