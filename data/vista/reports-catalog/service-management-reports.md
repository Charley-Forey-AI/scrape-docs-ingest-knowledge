# Service Management Reports

Service Management is a tool that allows you to streamline small
 job work order processing, increase field technician productivity, and improve
 service quality and cash flow.

Reports in the Service Management module have two categories:
 general reports that are available to every supported geographic location and
 Australia-specific reports. Click on a link for more information on reports in the
 associated category.

- [Service Management General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/service-management-reports/service-management-general-reports)

- [Service Management United States-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/service-management-reports/service-management-united-states-specific-reports)
