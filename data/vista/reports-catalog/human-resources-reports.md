# Human Resources Reports

Human Resources Reports
Human Resources is a personnel management software package designed to provide a highly flexible database for maintaining information on applicants and employees from the application/interview process through retirement.
Reports in Human Resources have two categories: general reports that
 are available to every supported geographic location and United States-specific
 reports; Click on a link for more information on reports in the associated category.

- [Human Resources General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/human-resources-reports/human-resources-general-reports)
