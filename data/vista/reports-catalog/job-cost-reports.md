# Job Cost Reports

Job Cost Reports
Job Cost is the core of the Vista™ by Viewpoint integrated
 construction management system. It is the place where estimated job costs, actual job
 costs, projected and committed job costs, and contract revenue posted throughout the
 accounting modules are accumulated.
Reports in Job Cost have two categories: general reports that are
 available to every supported geographic location and Australia-specific reports.
 Click on a link for more information on reports in the associated category.

- [Job Cost General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/job-cost-reports/job-cost-general-reports)

- [Job Cost Australia-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/job-cost-reports/job-cost-australia-specific-reports)
