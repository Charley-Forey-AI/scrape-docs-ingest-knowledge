# SM Agreement Renewal Review

You can use the SM Agreement Renewal Review report by
 selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Agreement Renewal Review.
The Agreement Renewal Review report pulls together Agreements that are set to expire in the specified date range, and give the user information about the current and prior agreement terms in order to have a better understand of the customer relationship.
The report has several drill down levels:
Level 1: Provides a summary by customer for the agreements expiring in the specified date range.
Level 2: For a given customer, provides a summary of each Agreement's current and previous terms. The Agreement can be represented in one of two ways here:
-By Service Site, if the Agreement was priced at the Agreement Service level (and the Price was set to zero on the Agreement)
-By Agreement, if the Agreement Price was set on the Agreement.
Level 3: There are two ways to get to Level 3. Clicking on the Agreement number will generate Level 3 for the whole Agreement. If the Agreement was broken down by site, then clicking on the Site will generate Level 3 for that Site. This level contains a summary for that Agreement or Site at the top, and a Call Type breakdown below. Clicking on a Call Type will take you to the next level.
Level 4: A Work Order/Scope breakdown of the above Call Type.
Level 5: A detail of the above Work Order/Scope.
