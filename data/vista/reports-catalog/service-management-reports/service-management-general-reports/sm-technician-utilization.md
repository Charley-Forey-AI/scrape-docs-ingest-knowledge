# SM Technician Utilization

You can use the SM Technician Utilization report to show
 Pay Type, Hours and Amounts for SM Technicians by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Technician Utilization.
This report shows Pay Type, Hours and Amounts for SM Technicians. It is generally used in order to review how much and how long SM Technicians worked on various Pay Types.
This report consists of four part:

1. Left part side shows all SM Work Completed that are not billed yet.

1. Center part displays all SM Work Completed that are billed already.

1. Right part provides information for non-chargeable Pay Types / Earning Codes, that comes from non SM area, e.g. Construction Ops (Job Cost) or Payroll (training, vacation, etc.)
