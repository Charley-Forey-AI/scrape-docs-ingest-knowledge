# SM Contact List

You can use the SM Contact List report for a list of
 contacts and their contact information by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Contact List.
This report displays a list of contacts and their contact information. Only the Company parameter is required; all others are optional.
By default there are no groupings, simply a list of contacts and their contact information. Additionally, the report may be grouped by Customer, Service Center, Technician, or Status.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Customer
Press F4 to select the Customer

Service Center
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the service center.

Technician
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the technician.

Serviceable Item
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the serviceable item.

Serviceable Item Class
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the serviceable item class.

Serviceable Item Type
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the serviceable item type.

Customer/Site Status
Enter Y or N.

Group By
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the group option.
