# SM Labor Allocation Commitment Drilldown

You can use the SM Labor Allocation Commitment Drilldown
 report to review labor allocation commitments for a given year by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Labor Allocation Commitment
 Drilldown.
The first level of this drill-down shows you labor commitments by
 craft and class for the specified year. Double-clicking in a row drills down into
 the detail for the craft/class, providing an audit of the craft/class totals. You
 can filter the report by agreements, customers, service sites, service centers, or
 call types. If you do not use the filtering options, the report and drill downs
 match the Commitments by Labor Type grid in the SM Agreement Labor Allocation
 form.Note: In order to access this report from the Options
 menu and the Reports menu, you must assign permissions as needed in VA Report
 Security.
