# SM Work Order

You can use the SM Work Order report to list SM Work Order
 and Scope information together with Work Completed and Trip by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Agreement Analysis
 Drilldown.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Customer
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the Customer or leave blank for All

Beginning Work Order Number
Enter or select the applicable beginning work order.

Ending Work Order Number
Enter or select the applicable ending work
 order.
From Requested Date
Enter from requested date.

To Requested Date
Enter to requested date.

Site
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the site.

Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the work order status.

Lead Technician
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the lead technician.
