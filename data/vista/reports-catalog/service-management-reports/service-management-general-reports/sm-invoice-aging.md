# SM Invoice Aging

You can use the SM Invoice Aging report for SM Invoices
 with an open balance summarized by groupings defined by the user by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Invoice Aging.
Possible groupings:

- AR Company

- Agreement/Extra/Non-Agr (groups together billings for Agreements, Agreement Extra WOs, and Non-Agreement WOs)

- Agreement/WO# (groups agreement billings by Agreement #, WO billings by WO#)

- Customer (Using the Bill-To Customer)

- Division

- Invoice

- Service Center

- Service Site
