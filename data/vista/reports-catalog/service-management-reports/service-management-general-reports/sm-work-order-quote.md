# SM Work Order Quote

You can use the SM Work Order Quote report by selecting
 Service Management > Reports > SM Work Order Quote.
This report acts as a signable quote that can be printed out. The
 report is able to deal with both T&M and Flat Price (FP) scopes at the same time, as
 users could have a quote that has both types of price methods. Report can hide or show
 costs and related tasks, though it will always show related Labor, Matl, Equip, and
 Other tabs. Users are able to hide and show notes at both scope level and the quote
 level as well as hide and show the Scope's detail section.

- a single Quote

- all quotes related to a Customer

- all quotes related to a Service Site

- all quotes related to a Service Center

- Approved, Canceled, New, or all three status flags
