# SM Customer List

You can use the SM Customer List report to display a list
 of Customers, with address and contact information by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Customer List.
This report displays a list of Customers, with address and contact information. Only the Company parameter is required; all others are optional.
The list may include all Customers for the specified Company, or only active Customers. The Service Center parameter restricts the list to Customers with one or more Service Sites assigned to the specified Service Center (as "Default Service Center"). The Technician parameter restricts the list to Customers to which the specified Technician is assigned as Primary, Alternate, or with the 'Do Not Use' designation. The Customer will be included whether the specified Technician is assigned to the Customer itself, or to any Service Site for the Customer. The Serviceable Item, Serviceable Item Class, and Serviceable Item Type parameters restrict the list to Customers with one or more Service Sites to which the specified Item is assigned, or any Item of the specified Class or Type. Any combination of parameters may be used together at any time.
The Customer list may be ungrouped (by default), or grouped optionally by 'Service Center', 'Technician', or 'Customer Status' (Active or Inactive). Only one Group By option may be specified at a time.
