# SM Work Completed Cost and Revenue

You can use the SM Work Completed Cost and Revenue report
 by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Work Completed Cost and
 Revenue.
This report gives users the ability to see the cost and revenue details for a range of work orders down to the Work Completed line. Users have the ability to sort the data into, at most, three different hierarchical levels, with the first level always being required. If the levels chosen do not include Work Order AND the show Work Completed lines checkbox IS checked, the report will automatically turn on Work Order as a hierarchy level as Work Completed lines do not make sense without showing the related Work Order.
