# SM Agreement List

You can use the SM Agreement List report by selecting
 Service Management > Reports > SM Agreement List.
The SM Agreement List report allows a user to see what Agreements have been entered into the Viewpoint system, the status of those Agreements, what are the Revisions under each agreement, and the status of those Revisions. In the context of this report there are 2 Agreement statuses (Active and Inactive) and 8 Revision statuses (Original Quote, Amendment Quote, Renewal Quote, Active, Active at a Future Date, Expired, Terminated, or Cancelled). This report allows the user to filter on any combination of these 10 different status flags, along with filtering on a single Agreement, a single Customer, or a single Agreement Type.
