# SM Labor Requirements Projection

You can use the SM Labor Requirements Projection report for a
 summarized Projected Labor Resource information for single or multiple Service Centers and
 Divisions by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Labor Requirements Projection.
This report prints labor needs and technician availability for the given time period, with a drill down available on the technician.

Group 1-5: Use the lookup function to choose up to 5 group levels for this report.
This report requires a company value. The user can then select the proper date range for the report, and choose to filter the data by Service Center, Division, and Call Type.
Labor Craft, Class, and Hours are displayed and summarized on the report in one of three possible formats; (W)eekly, (M)onthly, or (Q)uarterly. Periods not containing any scheduled resources are shown, in addition to the periods where labor is scheduled. Information is displayed for Customer and Job Work Orders, and SM Agreements.
