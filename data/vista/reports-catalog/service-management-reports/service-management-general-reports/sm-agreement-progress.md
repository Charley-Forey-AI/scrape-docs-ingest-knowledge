# SM Agreement Progress

You can use the SM Agreement Progress report by selecting
 Service Management > Reports > SM Agreement Progress.
The SM Agreement Progress report is useful for analyzing agreement costs and revenue at a high level, across a company.
A note on column calculations: The "% comp" (percent complete) column is calculated by the Cost column divided by the Budget column. Earned Revenue is the Price times the % comp.
The status column is abbreviated, in order to conserve space. Q = Quote, CQ = Cancelled Quote, A = Active, E = Expired, T = Terminated.
