# SM Daily Work Ticket

You can use the SM Daily Work Ticket report by selecting
 Service Management > Reports > SM Daily Work Ticket.
This report shows for each given unique combination of Work Order and Scope all related work completed records that fall into the date range selected. It generally follows the typical LEM reporting format.
Display\Parameter Restrictions:
The report uses the Line Type value found in SM Work Order Work Completed Records to determine how records are grouped into the following sections:

- Labor

- Labor – Other

- Equipment

- Equipment – Other

- Material

- Material – Other

- Subcontract

- Miscellaneous

The ‘Other’ sections are determined by the Type Category (found in the form SM Cost Types).
Access this report by clicking Pre-Construction > Reports > SM Daily Work Ticket.
