# SM Agreement Analysis Drilldown

You can use the SM Agreement Analysis Drilldown report to
 see profitably data at various grouping levels of one or more Service Management Agreements
 by selecting Service Management > Reports > SM Agreement Analysis
 Drilldown.
The report provides the user with the ability to see profitably data at various grouping levels of one or more Service Management Agreements. Users have the ability to select on, and drill into, several levels of SM Agreement details. From the high level view of Agreement, flat price, and work completed lines, or the detailed information on Price, Amounts Remaining to Bill, Billing Amounts, Budgeted Costs, Remaining Costs, Costs, Current Gross Margin and Gross Margin Percentages, the user can evaluate performance related data for Agreement, Time and Material Work Order, and Flat Price Work Order categories of revenue.
The main report is a Summary Report with fields linked to on-demand subreports.
This report contains drilldown reports for Base Agreement Price, Base Agreement Billings, Base Agreement Costs, Extra Time and Materials (T&M) Price, and Extra Time and Materials (T&M) Costs, Extra Flat Price Price, and Extra Flat Price Price Costs. Additional drilldown reports (Extra T&M Billings, Extra Flat Price Billings) will be added in later releases.
