# HR ADA-Disabled Applicants Status

You can use the HR ADA-Disabled Applicants Status report
 for a listing of all disabled applicants by range of application dates (showing position
 applied for, application status and whether they were hired) by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR ADA-Disabled Applicants
 Status.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Application Date
Enter beginning application date or leave blank for all.

Ending Application Date
Enter ending application date or leave blank for all.
