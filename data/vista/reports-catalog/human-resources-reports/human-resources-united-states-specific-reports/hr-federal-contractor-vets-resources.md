# HR Federal Contractor VETS Resources

You can use the Federal Contractor VETS Resources report as a companion to the data export for the VETS-4212 e-file. Access this report by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Federal Contractor VETS Resources.

Use this report in Summary mode to review the aggregate resource counts that populate the various boxes of the VETS-4212 e-file.

Use this report in Detail mode to see listings of specific resources included in each aggregate count value.
When running this report, make sure you enter the same
 input parameter values that you entered when you ran the HR Federal Contractor VETS-4212 report to generate your e-file. This ensures an accurate representation of the content of the VETS-4212 e-file.
Report ParametersDescription

Reporting Period End Date (in Jul or Aug)Enter the reporting period end date. Must be a date in July or August of the reporting year.

(P)rime Contractor, (S)ubcontractor, or (B)othSelect P to include only prime contractors, S to include only subcontractors, or B to include both prime contractors and subcontractors.

Contact PhoneEnter the contact's phone number.
