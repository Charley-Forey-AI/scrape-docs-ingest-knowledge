# HR I9 Status

You can use the HR I9 Status report for a listing of I9
 information by HR resources by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR I9 Status.
This report provides a listing of I9 information by HR resources. It can be
 printed in alphabetical order or in review date order and printed for a selected
 company or all companies. The information shown is payroll company, PR employee
 number and name, the status, citizenship and the next date of review. HRRM.
