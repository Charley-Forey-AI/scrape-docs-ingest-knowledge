# HR OSHA Form 300

You can use the HR OSHA Form 300 report for information
 flagged as OSHA type for a calendar year and displays it in the OSHA 300 format by selecting
 Human Resources > Reports > HR OSHA Form 300.
This report pulls information flagged as OSHA type for a calendar year and displays it in the OSHA 300 format. The report may be printed for all OSHA locations or for one specific location, with an option to override the Establishment Name, City and State. If no override information is entered, the report will pull the information from HQCO.
NOTE. Injury/Illness types have been changed on the OSHA 300 report. Therefore, injury/illness type accidents that occurred prior to 1/1/02 will no longer show on this report.
