# HR Veteran Applicants Status

You can use the HR Veteran Applicants Status report for a
 listing of all Veteran applicants by Federal classifications and range of application dates
 by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Veteran Applicants Status.
This report provides a listing of all Veteran applicants by Federal
 classifications and range of application dates. It shows application status,
 position applied for, and whether they were hired. The report prints the position
 and date applied from the HR Resource Interview program, which is accessed by
 clicking the Interview button on the Application/Interview tab in the HR Resource
 Master.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Application Date
Enter beginning application date or leave blank for all.

Ending Application Date
Enter ending application date or leave blank for all.
