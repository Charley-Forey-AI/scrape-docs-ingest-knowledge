# HR COBRA Certificate

You can use the HR COBRA Certificate report to display a
 Certificate of Coverage available through HR Resource COBRA, Options by selecting
 Human Resources > Reports > HR COBRA Certificate.
This report displays a Certificate of Coverage available through HR Resource COBRA, Options.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Employee (Required)
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the resource.
