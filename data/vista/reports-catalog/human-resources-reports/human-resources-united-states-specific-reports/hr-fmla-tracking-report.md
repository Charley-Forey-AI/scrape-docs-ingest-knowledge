# HR FMLA Tracking Report

You can use the HR FMLA Tracking Report to show leave
 hours by week from a beginning date and ending date by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR FMLA Tracking Report.
HR FMLA Eligibility & Usage report - using the two inputs from HR Company Parameters this report is designed to be run for one employee at a time. It shows leave hours by week from a beginning date and ending date. If HR FMLA Months Required and FMLA Hours Required is left blank in the HRCo parameters the report will use th. 12 months and 1250 hours (federal guidelines).
