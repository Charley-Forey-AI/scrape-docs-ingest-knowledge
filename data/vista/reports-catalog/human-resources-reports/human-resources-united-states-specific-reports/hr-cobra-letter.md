# HR COBRA Letter

You can use the HR COBRA Letter report to display a
 standard COBRA letter available through HR Resource COBRA, Options by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR COBRA Letter.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.
