# HR COBRA Information

You can use the HR COBRA Information report to print all
 of the information as set up in HR Resource COBRA (table HRRC) including sections for the
 qualifying event, enrollment information, and the premiums by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR COBRA Information.

Company
