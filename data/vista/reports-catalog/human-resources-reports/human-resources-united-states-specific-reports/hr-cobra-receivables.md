# HR COBRA Receivables

You can use the HR COBRA Receivable report for a list employees, their dependents and the premium amount associated with the benefit that they have elected to enroll in the COBRA program by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR COBRA Receivables.
This report will list employees, their dependents and the premium amount associated with the benefit that they have elected to enroll in the COBRA program. The report will display records where the Discontinued Date or the COBRA End date is greater than the active date input.
