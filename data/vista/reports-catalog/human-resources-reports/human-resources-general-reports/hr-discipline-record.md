# HR Discipline Record

You can use the HR Discipline Record report for a
 disciplinary record by HR resource from the HRED table by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Discipline Record.
This report provides you with a disciplinary record by HR resource from the
 HRED table. Shows the date, incident description, disciplinary action taken, days
 suspended, warning notice given, and follow up date. This can be ran by selected HR
 resource and selected HR company or (0) selects all.
