# HR Resource Skills by Resource

You can use the HR Resource Skills by Resource report for
 a list by resource of the skill codes assigned to them (the date the resource was certified,
 expiration, who gave the skill test and the history sequence) by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Resource Skills by Resource.
This report provides a list by resource of the skill codes assigned to them,
 the date the resource was certified, expiration, who gave the skill test and the
 history sequence. HRRS.
