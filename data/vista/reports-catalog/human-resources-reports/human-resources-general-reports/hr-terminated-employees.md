# HR Terminated Employees

You can use the HR Terminated Employees report for a
 listing of terminated employees by range of termination date showing reason code and if
 eligible for rehire by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Terminated Employees.
This report provides a listing of terminated employees by range of termination
 date showing reason code and if eligible for rehire. This report will reflect a
 single HRCo (specified at the Company parameter) and any PRCo's that are associated
 with that HRCo.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Terminated Date
Enter beginning terminated date or leave blank for all.

Ending Terminated Date
Enter ending terminated date or leave blank for all.
