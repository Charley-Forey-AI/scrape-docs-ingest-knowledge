# HR Resource Employment History

You can use the HR Resource Employment History report for
 a listing by resource all of the history codes assigned to them with a description and date
 of the event by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Resource Employment History.
This report provides a listing by resource all of the history codes assigned
 to them with a description and date of the event. Selected HR references and
 selected history codes may be ran. This is a listing of what is stored in the HREH
 table.
