# HR Drivers License Expiration Letter

HR Drivers License Expiration Letter
A letter that can be printed for employees approaching expiration of their drivers license. An option for lead time in advance of the expiration is given.
Access this report by clicking Human Resources > Reports > HR Drivers License Expiration Letter.
