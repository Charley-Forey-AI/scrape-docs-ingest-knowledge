# HR Benefit Codes

You can use the HR Benefit Codes report for a listing of
 all benefit codes with plan information, provider information (name, address, contact, etc.)
 eligibility requirements and the deduction / liability codes that are used for each benefit
 by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Benefit Codes.
