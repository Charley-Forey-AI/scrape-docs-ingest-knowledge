# HR Company Assets

You can use the HR Company Assets report to list the
 details entered in the HR Company Assets program by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Company Assets.
This report will list the details entered in the HR Company Assets program. The report will also show to whom an asset is currently assigned. The report displays the memo fields that are available when checking assets out and back in. It can be ran for a range of Categories and Assets with an option to sort by Category or Asset.
Parameters

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Sort by (C)ategory or (A)sset
Enter C or A.

Beginning Asset Category
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning asset category or leave
 blank for all.
Ending Asset Category
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending asset category or leave
 blank for all.
Beginning Asset
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning asset or leave blank for all.

Ending Asset
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending asset or leave blank for all.

Print Notes?
checkbox to print notes.
