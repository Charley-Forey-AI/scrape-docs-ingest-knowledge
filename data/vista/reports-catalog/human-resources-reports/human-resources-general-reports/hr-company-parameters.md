# HR Company Parameters

You can use the HR Company Parameters report to print
 company parameters by HR company by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Company Parameters.
This report prints company parameters by HR company. Leave blank to print all
 companies.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.
