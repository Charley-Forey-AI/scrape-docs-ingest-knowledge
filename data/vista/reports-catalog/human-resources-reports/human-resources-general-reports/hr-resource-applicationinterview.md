# HR Resource Application/Interview

You can use the HR Resource Application/Interview report
 for a detailed list of an HR references Application, Interview, References, and Positions
 applicants are applying for by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Resource
 Application/Interview.
This is a detailed report of an HR references Application, Interview, References, and Positions they are applying for. It can be ran for a specific company or all companies, by resource or range of resources and the Interview, References, and Positions sections can be selected to print or not. Tables used are HRRM, HRAR, HRAP, HREI, HRPC.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Resource
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning resource or leave blank for all.

Ending ResourceSelect the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending resource or leave blank for all.
