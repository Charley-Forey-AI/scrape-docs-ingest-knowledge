# HR New Hires

You can use the HR New Hires report for a list of new
 hires by range of hire dates by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR New Hires.
This report provides a list of new hires by range of hire dates. This report
 lists the PR employee number, group, department, and position. It will reflect a
 single HRCo (specified at the Company parameter) and any PRCo's that are associated
 with that HRCo
Report Parameters

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.
