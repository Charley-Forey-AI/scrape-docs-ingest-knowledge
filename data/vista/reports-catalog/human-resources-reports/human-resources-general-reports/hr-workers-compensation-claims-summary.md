# HR Workers Compensation Claims Summary

You can use the HR Workers Compensation Claims Summary
 report for a summarized listing of accidents by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Workers Compensation Claims
 Summary.
This report provides a summarized listing of accidents that can be ran by Job,
 PM, Location, or Accident/Seq. Number; showing date, description, description of
 accident, etc. Tables HRAT and HRAI.
