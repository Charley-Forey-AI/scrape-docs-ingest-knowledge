# HR Physical Expiration Letter

You can use the HR Physical Expiration Letter report for a
 letter that can be printed for employees approaching expiration of their physicals / medical
 cards by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Physical Expiration Letter.
This report provides a letter that can be printed for employees approaching
 expiration of their physicals / medical cards. An input allows for lead time prior
 to expiration selected.
