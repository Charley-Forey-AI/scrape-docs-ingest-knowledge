# HR Training & Education By Training Code

You can use the HR Training & Education By Training
 Code for a list, by training code, of who has received the training by selecting
 Human Resources > Reports > HR Training & Education By Training
 Code.
This report report shows by training code who has received the training, the
 date, status, grade, if a degree was received, number of hours and whether or not
 the training is OSHA, MSHA, First Aid or CPR related. The report can be run for all
 or specific resources, training codes or class taken. Tables used are HRRM, HRET,
 & HRCM.
