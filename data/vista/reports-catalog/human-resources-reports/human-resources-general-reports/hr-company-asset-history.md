# HR Company Asset History

You can use the HR Company Asset History report for a list
 in descending order the asset check out and check in activity by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Company Asset History.
This report will list in descending order the asset check out and check in activity. It can be ran for a range of Categories, Assets or Resources with an option to sort by Category and Asset.
