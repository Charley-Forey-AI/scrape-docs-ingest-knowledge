# HR Benefits Census

You can use the HR Benefits Census report for census
 information for various requests (health insurance quote) by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Benefits Census.
This report provides census information for various requests ie; health
 insurance quote. Shows name, resource number, social security number, sex,
 birthdate, hire date, marital status, number of dependents, and what PR group and
 department they work in. The report only prints employees that have a status of
 active, for a specified HR Company with options to print for a range of hire dates
 and a range of PR Group.
