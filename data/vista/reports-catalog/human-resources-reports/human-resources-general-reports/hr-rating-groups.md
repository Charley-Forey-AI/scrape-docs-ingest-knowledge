# HR Rating Groups

You can use the HR Rating Groups report for a list of all
 rating groups and the rating codes assigned to the group by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Rating Groups.
This report provides you with a list of all rating groups and the rating codes
 assigned to the group. HRRI & HRRG tables.
