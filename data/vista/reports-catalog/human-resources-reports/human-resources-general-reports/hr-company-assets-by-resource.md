# HR Company Assets by Resource

You can use the HR Company Assets by Resource report to
 list the asset check out and check in activity by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Company Assets by Resource.
This report will list in descending order the asset check out and check in activity. It can be ran for a range of Categories, Assets or Resources with an option to sort by Category and Asset.
