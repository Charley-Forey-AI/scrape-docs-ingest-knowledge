# HR Resource Interview

You can use the HR Resource Interview report for a range
 of HR Resources, a range of interview dates, and a range of position codes by selecting
 Human Resources > Reports > HR Resource Interview.
This report can be ran for a range of HR Resources, a range of interview dates, and a range of position codes. This shows the resource interviewed, when, by whom, for what position, the status, and all information stored in the HREI table. The report can be sorted in order of position code, or in interview date order.
Report Parameters
