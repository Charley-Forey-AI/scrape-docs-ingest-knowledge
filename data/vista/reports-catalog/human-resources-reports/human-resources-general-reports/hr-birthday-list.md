# HR Birthday List

You can use the HR Birthday List report for a list of HR
 Resources and their birth dates by month by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Birthday List.
This report prints a list of HR Resources and their birth dates by month. The report is designed to sorts and pages by Month. The 'Birthday Month' input is a 2 digit MM value; such as, if you wanted to see only December birth dates you would enter '12', or leave the input blank for all months. There is also an additional sort option to sort by 'Sort Name' or 'Birth Date'.
