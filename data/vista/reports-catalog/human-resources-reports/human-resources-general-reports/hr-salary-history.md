# HR Salary History

You can use the HR Salary History report for employee
 salary history (sorted by Resource or Position and will list all salary adjustments per
 employee) by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Salary History.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Sort by (R)esource or (P)osition
Enter R or P.

Beginning Resource Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning resource or leave blank for all.

Ending Resource Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending resource or leave blank for all.

Beginning PositionSelect the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning position code or leave blank for all.

Ending PositionSelect the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending position code or leave blank for all.

Beginning Effective DateEnter beginning effective date or leave blank for all.

Ending Effective DateEnter ending effective date or leave blank for all.

Include Inactive?checkbox to include inactive resources.

(H)ourly, (S)alary (Blank for All)Enter H, S, or leave blank for all.
Page By Sort?checkbox to have page by sort order.

checkbox to print notes.
