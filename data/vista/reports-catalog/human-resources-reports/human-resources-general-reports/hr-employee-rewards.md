# HR Employee Rewards

You can use the HR Employee Rewards report for a listing
 by HR resource the rewards received by code, description, date and reason by selecting
 Human Resources > Reports > HR Employee Rewards.
This report provides a listing by HR resource the rewards received by code,
 description, date and reason. HRRD table.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Resource
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning resource or leave blank for all.

Ending Resource
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending resource or leave blank for
 all.
