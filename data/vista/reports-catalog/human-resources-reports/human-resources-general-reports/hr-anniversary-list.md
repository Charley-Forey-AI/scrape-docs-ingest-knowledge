# HR Anniversary List

You can use the HR Anniversary List report for a list in
 month order and then in date order of all HR references and their hire date from the HRRM
 table by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Anniversary List.
This report provides a list in month order and then in date order of all HR
 references and their hire date from the HRRM table. A single month and company can
 be selected or selecting (0) selects all.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Anniversary Month (Ex: 8 for August)(Blank for All)
Enter month number or leave blank for all.
