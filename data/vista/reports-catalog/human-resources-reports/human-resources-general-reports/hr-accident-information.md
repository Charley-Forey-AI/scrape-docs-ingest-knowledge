# HR Accident Information

You can use the HR Accident Information report to print
 out ALL accident information from ALL of the HR accident tracking tables by selecting
 Human Resources > Reports > HR Accident Information.
This report that prints out ALL accident information from ALL of the HR
 accident tracking tables. Selections can be made by range of dates, range of
 accident number and they can select whether to print claim information, lost days
 information, contacts, etc. Tables used are HRAT, HRAI, HRCL, HRCC, HRAL. OSHA used
 different illness codes prior to 1/1/02. Any accidents prior to that date will no
 longer print due to OSHA changing their reports and redefining the illness
 types.
