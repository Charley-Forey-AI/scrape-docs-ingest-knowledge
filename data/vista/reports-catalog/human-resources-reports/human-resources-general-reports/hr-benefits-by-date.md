# HR Benefits by Date

You can use the HR Benefits by Date report for a listing
 of resource benefits and their effective dates for individuals by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Benefits by Date.
This report lists resource benefits and their effective dates for individuals. The report has the option to select resources benefits by eligibility date or reminder date. If the relationship is not listed, it is the individual themselves.
