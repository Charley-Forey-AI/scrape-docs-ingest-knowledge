# HR Employee List

You can use the HR Employee List report for a list of
 employees with PR Employee number by SortName, Department, or Payroll Group by selecting
 Human Resources > Reports > HR Employee List.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Sort by (S)ortName, PR(G)roup, (D)ept, (H)Rresource
Enter S, G, D, or H.

Include Inactive?
checkbox to include inactive resources.
