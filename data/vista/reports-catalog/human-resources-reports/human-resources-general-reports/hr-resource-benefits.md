# HR Resource Benefits

You can use the HR Resource Benefits report for a list of
 benefits by employee and for their dependents including deductions / earnings set up for the
 employee by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Resource Benefits.
This report provides a list of benefits by employee and for their dependents
 including deductions / earnings set up for the employee. Tables are HRBE, HREB,
 HRDP, and HRBL.
