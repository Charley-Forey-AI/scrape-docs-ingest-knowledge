# HR Training & Education By Resource

You can use the HR Training & Education By Resource
 report to list all training codes by date and by HR resource showing all training they have
 had by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Training & Education By
 Resource.
This report provides a list of all training codes by date and by HR resource
 showing all training they have had; class name, status, grade, hours and credits,
 description of training and institution that provided the training. The report can
 be ran by selected companies, HR resources, and by training codes. Tables used are
 HRRM, HRET, & HRCM.
