# HR Resources

You can use the HR Resource Master report to print all
 static information for HR resources and allows you to select the sections to print by
 selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Resources.
This report prints all static information for HR resources and allows you to select the sections to print. General Info (Name, address, phone, etc.), Defaults (payroll defaults such as home state, w.comp code, etc.), Other Info (Misc. info such as spouse, I9 status, drivers license info, veteran status, etc.), Education, W4 Info, and Notes. This is ran off of the HRRM table and can be ran by selected range of resources, a selected company or (0) for all, and can be paged by employee.
