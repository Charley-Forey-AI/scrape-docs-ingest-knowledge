# HR Resource Phone List

You can use the HR Resource Phone List report for HR
 resources phone numbers (home, work, pager, cellular and their position from HRRM) by
 selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Resource Phone List.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Resource
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the resource or leave blank for all.

Include Inactive?
checkbox to include inactive resources.
