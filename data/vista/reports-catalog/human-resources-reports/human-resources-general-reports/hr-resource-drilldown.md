# HR Resource Drilldown

You can use the HR Resource Master Drilldown report to
 show selected HR Resource Master details by selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Resource Drilldown.
This drilldown report shows selected HR Resource Master details. The report can be run for a range of HR Resource numbers or HR Resource Sortnames, with the option to include inactive resources. If including inactive resources, a specific employment status may be selected, which will restrict report to only inactive resources with the selected status. Otherwise, report will include all employees, regardless of their employment status.
