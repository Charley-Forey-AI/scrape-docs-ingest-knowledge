# HR Hospital Information

You can use the HR Hospital Information report for a
 listing by HR resource the rewards received by code, description, date and reason by
 selecting Human Resources > Reports > HR Hospital Information.
This report provides a listing by HR resource the rewards received by code,
 description, date and reason. HRRD table.
