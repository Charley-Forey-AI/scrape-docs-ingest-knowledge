# Project Management Reports

The Project Management module is used by operations staff like project managers and
 administrators to create and track project documents and logs (RFIs, submittals, change
 orders, daily logs, etc.), and it includes change management and procurement
 features.
Reports in Project Management have four categories: general reports
 that are available to every supported geographic location; United States-specific
 reports; Australia-specific reports; and create and send documents reports. Click on a
 link for more information on reports in the associated category.

- [Project Management General Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/project-management-reports/project-management-general-reports)

- [Project Management United-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/project-management-reports/project-management-united-specific-reports)

- [Project Management Australia-Specific Reports](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/project-management-reports/project-management-australia-specific-reports)
