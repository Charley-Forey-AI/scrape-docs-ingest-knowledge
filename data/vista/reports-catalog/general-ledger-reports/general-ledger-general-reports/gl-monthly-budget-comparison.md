# GL Monthly Budget Comparison

You can use the GL Monthly Budget Comparison report for a
 list of Accounts and budget amounts by month by selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL Monthly Budget Comparison.
This report provides a list of Accounts and budget amounts by month. List of
 two budget codes and their difference.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Account
Enter or press F4 to select a beginning GL account.

Ending Account
Enter or press F4 to select an ending GL account.

Beginning Month
Enter beginning month (MM/YY) or leave blank for all.

Ending Month
Enter ending month (MM/YY) or leave blank for all.

First Budget Code (Required)
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select first budget code.

Second Budget Code (Required)
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select second budget code.
