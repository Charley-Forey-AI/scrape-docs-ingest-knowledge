# GL Rolling 12 by Part3

You can use the GL Rolling 12 by Part3 report to sort by
 the 3rd Portion of the GL Account by selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL Rolling 12 by Part3.
This report sorts by the 3rd Portion of the GL Account. Within our report descriptions, we are assuming the 3rd portion is Department, but this description may be changed to fit the needs of the company using the report. Please note that any added GL Account Part 2 or Part 3 will not be accessible through the F4 lookups unless they were created using the process described in the Help file section for GL Account Parts.
