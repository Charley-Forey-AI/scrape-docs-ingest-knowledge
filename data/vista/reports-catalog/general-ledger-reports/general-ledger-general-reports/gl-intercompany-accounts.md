# GL InterCompany Accounts

You can use the GL InterCompany Accounts report for
 intercompany GL accounts for AR to AP and AP to AR transactions between companies by
 selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL InterCompany Accounts.
This report provides intercompany GL accounts for AR to AP and AP to AR
 transactions between companies. Table is GLIA.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.
