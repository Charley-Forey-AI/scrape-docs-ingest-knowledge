# GL Chart Accounts – GL Part1

You can use the GL Chart Accounts – GL Part1 report for
 printing account and description by selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL Chart Accounts – GL Part1.
GL Chart of Account for Part 1 of the GL account only. Prints Account and Description. If the Main portion of your GL Account is not the first part, you may want to copy this report and make the necessary changes or create a new report for the account part you want listed.
