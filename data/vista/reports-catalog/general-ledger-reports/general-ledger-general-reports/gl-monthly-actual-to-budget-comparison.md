# GL Monthly Actual to Budget Comparison

You can use the GL Monthly Actual to Budget Comparison
 report to compare Monthly Account net activity to a specific Budget Code based on Accounts
 and Months entered by selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL Monthly Actual to Budget
 Comparison.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Budget Code (Required)
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select budget code.

Beginning Account
Enter or press F4 to select a beginning GL account.

Ending Account
Enter or press F4 to select an ending GL account.

Beginning Month
Enter beginning month (MM/YY) or leave blank for all.

Ending Month
Enter ending month (MM/YY) or leave blank for all.

Include Memo Accounts?
checkbox to include memo accounts. Leave unchecked to exclude memo accounts.
