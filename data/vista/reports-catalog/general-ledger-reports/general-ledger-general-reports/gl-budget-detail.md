# GL Budget Detail

You can use the GL Budget Detail report for the GL Budget
 amount by account for each month with a budget by selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL Budget Detail.
This report provides GL Budget amount by account for each month with a budget.
 Totals by Account, Budget Code, and Company.
