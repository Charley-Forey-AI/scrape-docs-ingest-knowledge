# GL Chart of Accounts – Summary

You can use the GL Chart of Accounts – Summary report for
 a list of accounts, account description, type, subtype, and detail flag by selecting
 General Ledger > Reports > GL Chart of Accounts – Summary.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning GL Account
Enter or press F4 to select a beginning GL account.

Ending GL Account
Enter or press F4 to select an ending GL account.

Include Inactive Accounts?
Check this box to include inactive accounts only, leave unchecked to exclude inactive accounts.

Print Notes?
checkbox to include notes attached to bank account. Leave unchecked to exclude notes.
