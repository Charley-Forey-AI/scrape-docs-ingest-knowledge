# GL Account Parts

You can use the GL Account Parts report for Multipart
 General Ledger Codes by selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL Account Parts.
This report provides Multipart General Ledger Codes. Prints the Part# of the
 Account, Part Description, Instance of Account, and Instance's Description.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.
