# GL Budget Comparison Drilldown

You can use the GL Budget Comparison Drilldown to print
 the Budgeted Amount, Actual Amount and a percent of Actual to Budget amount for a range of
 accounts during a specified Fiscal Year by selecting General Ledger > Reports > GL Budget Comparison Drilldown.
This report prints the Budgeted Amount, Actual Amount and a percent of Actual
 to Budget amount for a range of accounts during a specified Fiscal Year. Further
 drilldown can be accessed to see Budgeted amounts per month for each account.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Account
Enter or press F4 to select a beginning GL account.

Ending Account
Enter or press F4 to select an ending GL account.

Beginning Month
Enter or select the applicable beginning month.

Ending Month
Enter or select the applicable ending month.

Budget Code
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select budget code. This field is
 required.
