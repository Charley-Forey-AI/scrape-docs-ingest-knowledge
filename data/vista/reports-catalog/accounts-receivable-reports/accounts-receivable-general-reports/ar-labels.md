# AR Labels

You can use the AR Labels report for labels for customer
 names and billing addresses. Inputs are available for Beginning and Ending Customer and
 Beginning and Ending State with the option of pulling the Mailing or Billing address from
 the Customer Master. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Labels.
