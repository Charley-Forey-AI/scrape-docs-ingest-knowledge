# AR Sales Tax Received Report

You can use the AR Sales Tax Received Report report for a
 list of sales tax invoices and the corresponding sales tax from receipts. This report is
 using the AR Transaction Line (ARTL) table. The transactions are summarized at the Apply to
 AR Trans level. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Sales Tax Received Report.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning tax code

Click the Field Lookup
