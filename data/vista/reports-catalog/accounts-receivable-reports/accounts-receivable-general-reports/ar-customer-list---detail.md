# AR Customer List - Detail

You can use the AR Customer List - Detail report for a
 list of customers, which includes Customer Group, Customer Number, Name, Contact, Phone,
 Fax, Email address, default Miscellaneous Distribution Code, Credit Limit, Status, Terms,
 and Tax Code. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Customer List - Detail.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Customer
Press F4 to select the beginning customer for sorting.
