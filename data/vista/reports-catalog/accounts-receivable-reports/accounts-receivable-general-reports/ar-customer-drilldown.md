# AR Customer Drilldown

You can use the AR Customer Drilldown report to obtain
 invoice information for a given customer. You can access this report by selecting
 Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Customer Drilldown.
Level 1 of the report will present a list of Customers or Contracts, depending
 on the value specified in the "Sort By Customer (S)ortName or (C)ontract?"
 parameter.
Level 2 presents a list of invoices for the selected Customer or
 Contract.
Level 3 lists a summary of the transactions for the selected invoice. The amount column includes the original invoice amount and any adjustments, but excludes applied payments. However, the Retainage and Current columns include applied payments and therefore reflect the open amounts as of the through month.
Level 4 displays the line level details.
Level 5 gives additional line level data.
