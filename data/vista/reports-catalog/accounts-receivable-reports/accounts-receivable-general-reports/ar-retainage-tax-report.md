# AR Retainage Tax Report

You can use the AR Retainage Tax Report report to detail
 the Retainage Tax information for AR Transactions. You can access this report by selecting
 Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Retainage Tax Report.
This report details the Retainage Tax information for AR Transactions. It lists the invoice line amount, retainage percent, retainage amount, and the corresponding retainage tax amount. User options for this report include specifying customer (one or all), contract (one or all), the beginning and ending months, the transaction type (finance charge, write off, payment, miscellaneous, return, invoice, credit, adjustment) and whether or not to print a separate page for each contract.
