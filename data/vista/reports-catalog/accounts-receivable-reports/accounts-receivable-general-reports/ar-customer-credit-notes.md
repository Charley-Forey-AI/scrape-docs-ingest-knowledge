# AR Customer Credit Notes

You can use the AR Customer Credit Notes report to list
 the customer notes by Customer. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Customer Credit Notes.
This report lists the history of customer contacts in reference to invoices.
 It can be ran for only unresolved issues, or all issues.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Customer
Press F4 to select the beginning customer for sorting.

Ending Customer
Press F4 to select the ending customer for sorting.

(N)ote Date or (F)ollowup Date
Enter N or F

Click the Calendar button

Ending Date
Click the Calendar button
