# AR Credit Memo

You can use the AR Credit Memo report to print the
 information for credit memos. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Credit Memo.
Note: This report is a modified version of the AR Invoice, except it prints the
 information for Credit Memos.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Invoice
Enter or select the applicable beginning invoice date.

Ending Invoice
Enter or select the applicable ending invoice date.

Customer
Press F4 to select the Customer
