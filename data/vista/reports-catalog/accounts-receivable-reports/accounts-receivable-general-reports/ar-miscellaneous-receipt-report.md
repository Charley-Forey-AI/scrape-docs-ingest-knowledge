# AR Miscellaneous Receipt Report

You can use the AR Miscellaneous Receipt Report to print
 Miscellaneous Receipts only. When printing for a certain type use the corresponding
 Beginning and Ending parameters. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Miscellaneous Receipt
 Report.
