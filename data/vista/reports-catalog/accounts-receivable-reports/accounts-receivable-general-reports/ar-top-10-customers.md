# AR Top 10 Customers

You can use the AR Top 10 Customers report to print a line
 for each of the 10 top customers and group the rest into a single line. You can access this
 report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Top 10 Customers.
This report prints a line for each of the 10 top customers and groups the rest into a single line. If you drill down on a customer you will see the monthly details for each customer so that you can also see what customers are combined in the "Others" line.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Beginning Month

Ending Month
Select the date-range arrows to select the Ending Month

Print Totals Only?
Check the box to Print Totals Only
