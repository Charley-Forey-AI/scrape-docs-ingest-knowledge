# AR Monthly Revenue by GL Account

You can use the AR Monthly Revenue by GL Account report to
 sort by General Ledger account and includes transaction level detail and GL account Totals
 for Invoices, Adjustments, Credit Memos, and Finance Charges. You can access this report by
 selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Monthly Revenue by GL
 Account.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Month
