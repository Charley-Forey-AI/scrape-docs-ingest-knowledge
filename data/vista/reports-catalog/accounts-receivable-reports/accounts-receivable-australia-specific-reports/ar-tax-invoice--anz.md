# AR Tax Invoice – A/NZ

You can use the AR Tax Invoice – A/NZ report to print a
 basic Invoice from Accounts Receivable. You can access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Tax Invoice – A/NZ.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Invoice
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the Beginning Invoice

Ending Invoice
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the Ending Invoice

Customer (Blank for All)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the Customer or leave blank for All

Include Adjustments?
Check the box to Include Adjustments

Break Out GST?
checkbox to Break Out GST

Print Summary Only?
Check the box to print summary invoice, leave
 unchecked to print detail invoice
