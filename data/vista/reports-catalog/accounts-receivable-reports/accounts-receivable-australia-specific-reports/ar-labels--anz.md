# AR Labels – A/NZ

This Accounts Receivable Labels report was designed to
 print on Avery L7159 63.5 x 33.9 mm laser labels. The report prints labels across and then
 down. Inputs are available for Beginning and Ending Customer and Beginning and Ending States
 with the option of pulling the Mailing or Billing address from the Customer Master. You can
 access this report by selecting Accounts Receivable > Reports > AR Labels – A/NZ.

Description
