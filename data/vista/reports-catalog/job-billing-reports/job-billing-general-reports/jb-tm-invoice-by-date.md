# JB T&M Invoice By Date

You can use the JB T&M Invoice By Date report to print
 the time and material invoices in date and template sequence order by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB T&M Invoice By Date.
The JB T&M Invoice By Date prints the time and material invoices in date and template sequence order. The report may be printed in Summary - one line per date and template sequence, or detail - one line according to the Summary Option specified on source lines in the template (See F1 Help on the Sum Opt column in JB T&M Template Setup for documentation on all possible summarization options). Detail Addons and Amount type sequences print in the body of the invoice whereas Total Addons are summarized at the end of the invoice.
The Sort Order on the template used for the contract during initialization must be set to either Actual Date or Posted Date for printing and sorting by Date. Otherwise, the Date field will be blank.
Because JB T&M Line Details contain many different fields of information, the standard T&M invoice reports will print selected information. All the other tables with the possible description fields, such as craft name, material description, etc., are linked to the JB T&M Detail (JBID) table, making this additional data easily available for copied customized versions of the report.
