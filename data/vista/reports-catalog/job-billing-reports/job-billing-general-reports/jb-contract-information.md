# JB Contract Information

You can use the JB Contract Information report to list the
 JC Contract Master and Contract Item information from JCCM and JCCI tables by selecting
 Job Billing > Reports > JB Contract Information.
This report lists the JC Contract Master and Contract Item information from JCCM and JCCI tables. It lists the original contract amount by item with a total for the contract.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Processing Group (Blank for All)
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the processing group or leave blank for all.

Beginning Contract:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract or leave blank for all.

Ending Contract:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract or leave blank for all.

Print Items?
checkbox to print contract items.

Page By Contract?
checkbox for page break after each contract.
