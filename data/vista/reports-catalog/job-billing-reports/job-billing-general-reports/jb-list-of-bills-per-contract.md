# JB List of Bills Per Contract

You can use the JB List of Bills Per Contract report for a
 list of billing for each contract by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB List of Bills Per Contract.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract or leave blank for all.

Ending Contract:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract or leave blank for all.

Beginning Month
Enter beginning month or leave blank for all.

Ending Month
Enter ending month or leave blank for all.
