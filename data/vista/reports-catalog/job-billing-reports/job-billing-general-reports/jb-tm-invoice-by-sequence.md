# JB T&M Invoice By Sequence

You can use the JB T&M Invoice By Sequence report to
 print the time and material invoices in template sequence order by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB T&M Invoice By Sequence.
The JB T&M Invoice by Sequence prints the time and material invoices in template sequence order. The report may be printed in Summary - one line per template sequence, or detail - one line according to the Summary Option specified on source lines in the template (See F1 Help on the Sum Opt column in JB T&. Template Setup for documentation on all possible summarization options). Detail Addons and Amount type sequences print in the body of the invoice whereas Total Addons are summarized at the end of the invoice.
Because JB T&M Line Details contain many different fields of information, the standard T&M invoice reports will print selected information. All the other tables with the possible description fields, such as craft name, material description, etc., are linked to the JB T&M Detail (JBID) table, making this additional data easily available for copied customized versions of the report.
