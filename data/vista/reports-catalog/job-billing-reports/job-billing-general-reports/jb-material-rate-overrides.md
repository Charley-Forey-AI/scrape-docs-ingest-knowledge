# JB Material Rate Overrides

You can use the JB Material Rate Overrides report to see
 material pricing set up for T&M templates in JB Material Rate Overrides by selecting
 Job Billing > Reports > JB Material Rate Overrides.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Template:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning template or leave blank for all.

Ending Template:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending template or leave blank for all.

checkbox to print notes.
