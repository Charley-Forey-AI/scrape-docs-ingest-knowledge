# JB Equipment Rates

You can use the JB Equipment Rates report for equipment
 rates set up for T&M templates in JB Template Equip Rates by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB Equipment Rates.
This report shows equipment rates set up for T&M templates in JB Template Equip Rates. Only templates set up to use rates will be included. The report may be sorted by template or category.
