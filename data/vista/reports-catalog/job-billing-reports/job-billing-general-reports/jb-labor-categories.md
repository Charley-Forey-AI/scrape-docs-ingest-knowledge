# JB Labor Categories

You can use the JB Labor Categories report for a list of
 the Job Billing Labor Categories setup by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB Labor Categories.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Labor Category:
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning labor category or
 leave blank for all.

Ending Labor Category:
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending labor category or leave
 blank for all.
