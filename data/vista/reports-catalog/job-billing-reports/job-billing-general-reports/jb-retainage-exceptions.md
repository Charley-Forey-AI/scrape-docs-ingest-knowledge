# JB Retainage Exceptions

You can use the JB Retainage Exceptions report to display
 any discrepancies between the retainage latest bill on a Contract and the sum of all the
 retainage on previous bills by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB Retainage Exceptions.
This report displays any discrepancies between the retainage latest bill on a
 Contract and the sum of all the retainage on previous bills.
Report Parameters
Description
Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Contract:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning contract or leave blank for all.

Ending Contract:
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending contract or leave blank for all.
