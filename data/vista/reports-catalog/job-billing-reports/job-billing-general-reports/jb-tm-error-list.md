# JB T&M Error List

You can use the JB T&M Error List report to print
 print the errors generated when initializing the JB T&M Bill by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB T&M Error List.
This report will print the errors generated when initializing the JB T&M Bill. This will allow correction of the errors and the bill can be initialized into the Bill Edit form.
