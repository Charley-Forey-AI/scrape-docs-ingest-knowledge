# JB T&M Invoice By Cont Item With Dates

You can use the JB T&M Invoice By Cont Item With Dates
 report (a copy of the T&M Contract Item with an exception that a little more detail was
 added to the different source lines) by selecting Job Billing > Reports > JB T&M Invoice By Cont Item With
 Dates.
The report is a copy of the T&M Contract Item with an exception that a little more detail was added to the different source lines. In order for the JC Date to print, the Sum Opt for the source lines on the template must be set to 1- Full Detail.
AP Material- Vendor Name/Matl Desc/ AP Ref/JC Date
AP Not Matl- Location/Matl Desc/ JC Date
PR Labor- Craft/Class Desc / JC Date
AP Not Matl- Vendor Name/ AP Ref/JC Date
AP Subcontract- Vendor NAme/ SL Item Desc/ JC Date
EM Equipment- Equip Desc/ Rev Code/ JC Date
Access this report by clicking Job Billing > Reports > JB T&M Invoice By Cont Item With Dates.
