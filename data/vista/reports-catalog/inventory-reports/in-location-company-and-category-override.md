# IN Location Company and Category Override

You can use the IN Location Company and Category Override
 report to see GL Account overrides to locations by company and category. To access this report, select Inventory > Reports > IN Location Company And Category
 Override.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Location
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning location.

Ending Location
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending location.
