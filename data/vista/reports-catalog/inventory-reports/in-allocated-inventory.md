# IN Allocated Inventory

You can use the IN Allocated Inventory report to list the
 details from IN Material Orders and MS Quote Detail with status 1 by selecting Inventory > Reports > IN Allocated Inventory.
This report lists details from IN Material Orders and MS Quote Detail with status 1 and inventory materials that have been ordered and not confirmed in IN Material Orders or Sold in MS Ticket Entry. The report checks the transaction unit of measure against the HQ stocked unit of measure and calculates the UM conversion.
