# IN Bill of Materials Overrides

You can use the IN Bill of Materials Overrides report for
 a list by location and finished material, with a listing by the override location of the
 alternate material, units or weight used to create the finished material for this location. To access this report, select Inventory > Reports > IN Bill of Materials Overrides.

Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Location
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the beginning location.

Ending Location
Select the Field Lookup button or press F4 to select the ending location.

Beginning Finished Material
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning finished
 material.

Ending Finished Material
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending finished material.
