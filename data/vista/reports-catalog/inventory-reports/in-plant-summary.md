# IN Plant Summary

You can use the IN Plant Summary report to report
 inventory sales, cost of sales, and production Costs by plant location. To access this report, select
 Inventory > Reports > IN Plant Summary.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

As of Month
Enter month.

Beginning Plant
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning plant.

Ending Plant
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending plant.
