# IN Bill of Materials

You can use the IN Bill of Materials report for a list by
 location of finished material and the component materials, UM, Units or % of weight used to
 create the finished material by selecting Inventory > Reports > IN Bill of Materials.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Location Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning location group.

Ending Location Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending location group.

Beginning Finished Material
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the beginning finished
 material.

Ending Finished Material
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select the ending finished material.
