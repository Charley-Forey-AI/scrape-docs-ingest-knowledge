# IN Inventory Totals By Matl And Location

You can use the IN Inventory Totals By Matl And Location
 report for a listing of Inventory facts by either Location, Material order or by Material,
 Location order by selecting Inventory > Reports > IN Inventory Totals By Matl And
 Location.
Report detail consists of purchases, production,
 sales, adjustments and transfers. Both units and amounts are displayed with totals
 based on the grouping.
Report Parameters

Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.
