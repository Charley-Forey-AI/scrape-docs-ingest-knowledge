# HQ Compliance Groups

You can use the HQ Compliance Groups report for a list of
 compliance groups from the HQ Compliance Groups form. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Compliance Groups.
Report Parameters
Description

Beginning Compliance Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning compliance group or
 leave blank for all.

Ending Compliance Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending compliance group or leave
 blank for all.

Print Notes?
checkbox to include notes attached to bank account. Leave unchecked to exclude notes.
