# HQ Tax Groups

You can use the HQ Tax Groups report to see tax groups set up in the HQ Tax Groups form. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Tax Groups.
Report Parameters
Description

Beginning Tax Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning tax group or leave blank
 for all.

Ending Tax Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending tax group or leave blank
 for all.
