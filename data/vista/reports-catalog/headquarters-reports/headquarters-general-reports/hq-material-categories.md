# HQ Material Categories

You can use the HQ Material Categories report to see setup data for material categories. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Material Categories.
You can use material
 categories to group similar types of material together for reports.
Parameters

Beginning Material Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning material group or leave
 blank for all.

Ending Material Group
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select ending material group or leave
 blank for all.
