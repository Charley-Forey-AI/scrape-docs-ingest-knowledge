# HQ Reviewer Groups

You can use the HQ Reviewer Groups report to see a list
 of reviewer groups and associated details, including the status of each
 group. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Reviewer Groups.
Additional details include the list of reviewers assigned to the group, as well as each reviewer's status within the group. Other details are also displayed for each reviewer, such as approval sequence and threshold amount.
