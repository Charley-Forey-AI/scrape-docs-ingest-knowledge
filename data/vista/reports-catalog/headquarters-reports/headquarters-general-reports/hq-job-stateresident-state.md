# HQ Job State/Resident State

You can use the HQ Job State/Resident State report for a
 list by states along with reciprocal assignments with other states. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Job State/Resident State.
 States without any reciprocal assignments do not display on this
 report.
