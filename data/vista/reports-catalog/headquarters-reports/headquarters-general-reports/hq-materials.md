# HQ Materials

You can use the HQ Materials report to see a list of materials from the HQ Materials form. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Materials.
 The detail
 version of the report displays every field on the HQ Materials form.
Report Parameters
Description

Company
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Beginning Category
Click the Field Lookup
 button or press F4 to select beginning material category or
 leave blank for all.

Print Notes?
checkbox to include notes attached to bank account. Leave unchecked to exclude notes.
