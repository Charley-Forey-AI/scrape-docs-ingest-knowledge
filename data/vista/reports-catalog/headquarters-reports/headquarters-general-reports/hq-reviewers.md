# HQ Reviewers

You can use the HQ Reviewers report to see a list of reviewers, along with their user names. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Reviewers.
You can use this report primarily in support of the invoice approval process (AP Unapproved Invoice forms).
Parameters

Show (A)ctive Reviewers, (I)nactive, or (B)oth
Enter A, I, or B.
