# HQ Companies

You can use the HQ Companies report to see a list of setup data for each company in the HQ Company form. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Companies.
Report Parameters
Description

Company (Blank for All)
Accept the default, or press F4 to select a company.

Print Notes?
checkbox to include notes attached to bank account. Leave unchecked to exclude notes.
