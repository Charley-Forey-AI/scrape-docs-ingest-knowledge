# HQ Compliance Groups/Codes

You can use the HQ Compliance Groups/Codes report to see a list of compliance groups along with the assigned compliance codes. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ Compliance Groups/Codes.
There are no run-time parameters for this report.
