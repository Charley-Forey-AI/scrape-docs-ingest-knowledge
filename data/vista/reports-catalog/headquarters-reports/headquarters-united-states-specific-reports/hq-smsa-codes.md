# HQ SMSA Codes

You can use the HQ SMSA Codes report to see all codes set up in the HQ SMSA Codes form. To access this report, select Headquarters > Reports > HQ SMSA Codes.
SMSA stands for Statistical Metropolitan Surrounding Area.
