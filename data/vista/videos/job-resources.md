# Job Resources

The links below provide access to a variety of short videos
 to help you learn about functionality in the Vista Job Resources modules.
Each video page includes links to detailed help pages for additional
 information.
Note: You must use a browser on your local computer to view these
 videos, and not a browser in the cloud. If you are currently using a cloud-based
 browser, for each video you want to view, copy the page URL and paste it into a web
 browser on your local computer.
