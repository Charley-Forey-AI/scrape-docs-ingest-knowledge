# Printing Documents from the Batch Scan Viewer

You can print documents from the Batch Scan Viewer form.

1. Select
 File > Print
 Images . The system displays a message asking if you
 want to stretch the document to fit page margins or
 not.

1. Click
 Yes
 to stretch the document or No to
 leave the document as is.Once you have made your
 selection, the system will print all documents in the
 thumbnail pane.If you do not want to print
 previously attached documents, click Hide
 Attached to remove the documents from
 the thumbnail pane. The system prints images in the
 thumbnail pane display order.
