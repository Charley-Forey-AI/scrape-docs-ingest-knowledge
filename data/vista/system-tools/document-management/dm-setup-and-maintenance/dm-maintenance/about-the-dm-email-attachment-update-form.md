# About the DM Email Attachment Update Form

Use this form to update email attachments.
This form allows you to take advantage of the [Microsoft Outlook integration functionality](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/document-management/dm-setup-and-maintenance/set-up-document-management/email-integration-with-microsoft-outlook) in Vista™. The grid on this form displays the emails and attachments which are not already indexed.
