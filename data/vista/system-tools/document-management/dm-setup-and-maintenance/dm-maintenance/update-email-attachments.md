# Update Email Attachments

Update email attachments using the DM Email Attachment Update
 Form.
To update email attachments:

1. Check the
 Update
 box for each attachment in the grid that you want to update. You
 can also click
 Select All
 to check the
 Update
 box for all attachments in the grid or
 Clear All
 to uncheck the
 Update
 box for all attachments.

1. Click
 Update
 .  The system will display that the update was successful for each
 attachment in the
 Status
 field, but the attachment record will stay on the grid.

1. Click
 Refresh
 to clear successfully updated attachments from the grid. Note: When updating emails with this form,
 there is the potential to increase disk usage by up to 50% per email.
