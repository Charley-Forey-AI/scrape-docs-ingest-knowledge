# About the BI Operational Target Level Form

Use this form to create and maintain target levels, which can be added to operational target revisions using the Info tab in the lower portion of the BI Operational Target form.
This allows you to maintain a standard list of target levels that can be associated with many different operational targets.
Examples of target levels that you might want to create:

- Associated General Contractors of America Gold Award

- Associated General Contractors of America Silver Award

- Associated General Contractors of America Bronze Award

- Construction Financial Management Association Gold Level

- Construction Financial Management Association Silver Level

- Construction Financial Management Association Bronze Level

- Institute of Certified Construction Industry Financial Professionals A Grade

- Institute of Certified Construction Industry Financial Professionals B Grade
