# Copy an Operational Target

You can copy an operational target that has been created using the BI Operational Target form.
This copies every field on the source target except the notes entered on the Notes tab in the both the upper and lower portion of the BI Operational Targets form.
To copy an operational target:

1. Open the BI Operational Target Copy form. It is located in the Programs folder in the Business Intelligence module.

1. Enter the target that
 you would like to copy in the Source Target field or press
 F4 to select it from a list.

1. Enter the target that you would like to create in the Destination Target field. The destination target can be up to 50 characters long.

1. Click the Copy button when complete. A message will display if the copy
 is successful.

1. Copy another operational target or click the Close button.
