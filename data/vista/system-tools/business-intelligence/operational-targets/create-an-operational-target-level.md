# Create an Operational Target Level

Use the BI Operational Target Level form to create and maintain a standard list of target levels.
Target levels can be associated with many different targets.
Here are some examples of target levels:

- Associated General Contractors of America Gold Award

- Associated General Contractors of America Silver Award

- Associated General Contractors of America Bronze Award

- Construction Financial Management Association Gold Level

- Construction Financial Management Association Silver Level

- Construction Financial Management Association Bronze Level

- Institute of Certified Construction Industry Financial Professionals A Grade

- Institute of Certified Construction Industry Financial Professionals B Grade

To create an operational target level:

1. In the BI Operational Target Level form, click the New Record icon () to create a new target.

1. Enter a name for the
 target level in the Target Level field. You can
 also press F4 for a list of existing
 target levels.

1. In the Description field, enter a description of the target level.


1. Click the Save button. Now you can add this target level to operational target revisions using the Info tab in the lower portion of the [BI Operational Target](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/business-intelligence/operational-targets/about-the-bi-operational-target-form) form.
