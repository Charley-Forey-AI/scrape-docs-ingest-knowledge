# Business Intelligence

The Business Intelligence module provides tools to help you
 analyze your company's data.
Using the Operational Target and SQL Service Reporting Services (SSRS) tools, you can create reports and/or build dashboards to share information with others in your organization. The reports and dashboards you create can help you analyze and interpret data, spot and track significant changes in your business, and track financial, production, and customer data.
With SSRS, you have access to standard reports or can create your own custom reports, which you can then add to any of the Vista work centers.
