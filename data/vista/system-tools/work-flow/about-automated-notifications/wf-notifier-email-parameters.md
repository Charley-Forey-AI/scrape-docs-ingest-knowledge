# WF Notifier Email Parameters

Use the WF Notifier Email Parameters form to add email parameters to the Email tab on the WF Notifier Job Manager form.

You can launch this form by clicking the Email Fields button on the Email tab of WF
 Notifier Job Manager.
Upon opening this form, the grid automatically displays a list of fields applicable to the query you selected for the job. You can then add one or more of the listed fields to WF Notifier
 Job Manager form by either double-clicking on the field or by selecting the field and clicking Insert Field.
