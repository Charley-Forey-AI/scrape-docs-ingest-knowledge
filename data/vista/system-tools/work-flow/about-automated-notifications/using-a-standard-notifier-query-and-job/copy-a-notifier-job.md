# Copy a Notifier Job

If you need to create a Notifier Job and a similar one already exists, you can copy the Notifier Job and then modify the copy.
Note: This will not copy the consolidation groups, or rich text email set up on the notification job.

- A job creates a rich text email if Rich
 Text is selected in the Formatfield on the Email tab of
 the WF Notifier Job Manager form.

- Consolidation groups are set up using the Consolidation
 Groups tab on the WF Notifier Job Manager form.

1. Open the [WF Notifier Job Manager ](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/work-flow/about-automated-notifications/wf-notifier-job-manager) form.

1. Open the Notifier Job that you want to copy.

1. Select Records > Copy from the menu at the top of the form.

1. Click the New Record () icon to create a new Notifier Job record.

1. Enter a name of the new job
 in the Job
 Name field and press Tab.

1. Select Records > Paste from the menu at the top of the form.

1. Click Save and then make any changes to the copied Notifier Job.
