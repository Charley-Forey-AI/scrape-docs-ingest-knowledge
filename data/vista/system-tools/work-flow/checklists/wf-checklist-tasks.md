# WF Checklist Tasks

Use this form to monitor checklist task progress.
 Using this form, you can also use this form to edit checklist tasks and steps. While you
 cannot reorder tasks or their associated steps, you can change all other settings. You can
 also use this form to complete tasks and steps. If a task or step has an associated form or
 report, you can access the form or report by clicking the Launch button.
