# Reorder Checklist Task Steps

After completing a checklist in the WF Checklist Maintenance form, you may need to modify the workflow and reorder task steps.

1. Select the Steps tab.

1. Select File > Reorder Steps. The Reorder Steps window displays. All steps associated with the task display in the Steps text box.

1. Select a step to move and click either the Up or Down button. You can also drag steps to reorder them. The step moves up or down one position in the list.

1. Rearrange steps as necessary and click OK.

1. Click Close.

The system renumbers the steps appropriately.
