# Update Your Task and Step Status for a Worfklow Checklist

You can use the WF Tasklist form to update your assigned
 checklist steps and tasks.

1. Filter for a specific task or step.

1. Select the statuses for filtering in the Checklist Item Status Type section.   The system
 automatically selects the New and In Progress checkboxes. If you clear
 all checkboxes in the Checklist Item Status section, the system
 reselects the New and In Progress checkboxes for the search.

1. Select a date
 type to search by in the Search By Date field:
 None, Due, or Assigned.

1. If you selected
 a date type of Due or Assigned in step 3, enter a date range in the
 Beginning Date and
 Ending Date fields.


1. Click Refresh. The system displays all tasks for the
 checklist that meet the filtering criteria.

1. Select the task or step
 to update.

1. Enter a new status in
 the Status field.

1. If the task or step has
 an associated form or report, click the Launch button to access the
 form or report.

1. Enter the completion
 date in the Completed Date field, if
 necessary.

1. Save the record.

1. Repeat the steps as
 necessary for any other tasks and steps you need to update.
