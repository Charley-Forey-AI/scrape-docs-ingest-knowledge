# Open Vista to a Work Center when Logging in

You can set Vista to open automatically to a Work Center
 upon login instead of to the Main Menu.
To set Vista to open to a Work Center by
 default:

1. From the main application
 window, select Options > Work
 Centers.

1. In the Work Centers form,
 double-click the Work Center you want to open when you log in to Vista.

1. In the Work Center -
 Settings form, select the Load this Work Center Tab as Default on
 Startup checkbox.

1. Click Finish.

1. In the Work Centers form,
 click OK.

1. Close and open the
 application to see the change.
