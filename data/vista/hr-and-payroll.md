# HR and Payroll

You can use the HR and Payroll modules in Vista to maintain employee information. The HR module allows you to manage an employee's history (such as benefits, salary, accidents, and training), while the PR module provides full payroll timecard entry, processing, check printing, and reporting capabilities.
