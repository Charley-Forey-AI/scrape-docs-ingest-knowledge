# Field Definitions: SL Close Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the SL
 Close form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  JC Co#

 Enter the Job Cost company in which the subcontracts you want to close exist.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-setupmaintenance/sl-maintenance/about-the-sl-close-form)SL Close

##  Job

 Specify the job whose subcontracts you want closed.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-setupmaintenance/sl-maintenance/about-the-sl-close-form)SL Close

## Include all Open and Completed Subcontracts on this Job

Check this box to close all subcontracts on this job with a status of “Open” or “Complete.”
Uncheck this box to select the subcontracts to close.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-setupmaintenance/sl-maintenance/about-the-sl-close-form)SL Close

##  Beginning Subcontract

 Enter the beginning subcontract in the range of subcontracts to close.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-setupmaintenance/sl-maintenance/about-the-sl-close-form)SL Close

##  Ending Subcontract

 Enter the ending subcontract in the range of subcontracts to close.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-setupmaintenance/sl-maintenance/about-the-sl-close-form)SL Close

## Include Open Subcontracts with Remaining Units Costs

Check this box to close ‘open’ subcontracts that have remaining units and costs.
Unheck this box to close only those ‘open’ subcontracts without remaining units and costs.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-setupmaintenance/sl-maintenance/about-the-sl-close-form)SL Close

##  Close Date

 Specify the close date for all subcontracts in this batch.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-setupmaintenance/sl-maintenance/about-the-sl-close-form)SL Close
