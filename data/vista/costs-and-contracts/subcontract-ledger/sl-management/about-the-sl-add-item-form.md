# About the SL Add Item Form

Use this form to add a new change order item to a subcontract.
This form opens when you enter a subcontract item
 number that does not already exist on the subcontract using the Item #
 field on the [SL Change Order Entry ](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/subcontract-ledger/sl-management/about-the-sl-change-order-entry-form) form.
To enter a change order item, complete the fields on
 the form and then click Add. This will close the form and
 the new item information will display in the lower portion of the SL Change Order
 Entry form.
