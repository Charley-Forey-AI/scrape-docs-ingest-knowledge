# About the SL Update to AP Form

Use this form to update subcontracts on an SL worksheet to AP Unapproved Invoice
 Entry.
Access this form from SL Worksheet by selecting File > Update to Accounts Payable > Update to AP Unapproved Invoice
 Entry.
If you send these invoices to AP Unapproved Invoice Entry, they
 will be available to edit just like any other unapproved invoice.
