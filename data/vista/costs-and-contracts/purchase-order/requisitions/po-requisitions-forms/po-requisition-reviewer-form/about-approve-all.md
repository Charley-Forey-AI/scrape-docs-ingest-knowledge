# About Approve All

Use the
 Approve
 All
 button to approve all of the items in either the RQ Lines or Quote Lines
 tab.
For example if you click the
 Approve
 All
 button when the RQ Lines tab is open, the
 Review
 Status
 field on all of the requisition lines changes to
 Approved
 .
