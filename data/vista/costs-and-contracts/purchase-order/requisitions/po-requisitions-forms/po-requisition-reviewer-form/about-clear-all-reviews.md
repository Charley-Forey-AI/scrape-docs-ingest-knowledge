# About Clear All Reviews

Use the Clear All Reviews button to clear the
 review status on all of the items in either the RQ Lines or Quote Lines tab.
For example if you open the RQ Lines tab and click
 the Clear All Reviews button, the Review Status field on all of the
 requisition lines changes to New .
Clearing lines that were previously rejected will
 not clear the rejection reason specified for each line.
