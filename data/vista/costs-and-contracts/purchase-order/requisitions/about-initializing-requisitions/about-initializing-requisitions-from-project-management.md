# About Initializing Requisitions from Project Management

Use of the PM module allows you to initialize requisitions
 based on materials that you enter for jobs in PM Material Detail.
 You must select ‘R-Requisition’ from the
 Material Type drop-down and you must check the Send box; however, you do not need to
 enter values in the Vendor or Units fields. Use PM Material Detail to initialize
 requisitions and the system automatically updates the requisition line to PO Requisition
 Entry. You can only make further edits in PM Material Detail if you delete the
 requisition line from PO Requisition Entry.
