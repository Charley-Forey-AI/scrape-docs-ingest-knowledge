# Purge Requisitions

You can purge requisitions that were closed in or prior to a given month.

Purging requisitions removes header records and all requisitions
 lines with a status of Complete. If any requisition lines are not complete, the system
 will not purge the header record. The purge process does not update PM Subcontract
 Detail. To purge requisitions:

1. Open the Requisitions
 tab on the PO Purge form.

1. Iin the

 Purge Requisitions with a Date Through
 field, enter a date. Note: This field represents the date the
 requisitions where entered into the system.

1. To purge all quote lines that are not associated with any
 requisition, select the
 Purge Orphaned Quote Lines
checkbox.

1. To purge all requisitions with a Denied status, select the
 Purge Denied Requisitions
check box.

1. Select Update. All of the requisitions that meet the criteria are listed in the Available Requisitions section.

1. In the Available Requisitions section, select requisitions to purge using one of the following methods::

- Highlight only the requisitions that you want to purge and select Add.

- Choose all listed requisitions by selecting Add All.

The selected requisitions display in the Requisitions to Purge section.Tip: You can use the
 Remove and
 Remove All
 buttons to move the requisitions from the Requisitions to
 Purge section back to the Available Requisitions section.

1.  Once all of the requisitions that you would like to purge
 display in the Requisitions to Purge section, select
 Purge.

1. In the dialog box that appears, select
 Yes.
