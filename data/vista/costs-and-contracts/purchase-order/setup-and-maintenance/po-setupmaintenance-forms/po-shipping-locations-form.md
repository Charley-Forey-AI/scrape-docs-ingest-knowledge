# PO Shipping Locations Form

Use this form to create and maintain the shipping addresses that can be assigned to purchase orders, for example yard or ship to addresses.
You do not need to set up a shipping location for each job.
When referencing a shipping location on a PO, the selected location defaults to the Shipping Address tab. You may override the default, if needed, and you are also provided a place to enter special shipping instructions. The shipping address will print on the Purchase Order Form in the Ship To section.
