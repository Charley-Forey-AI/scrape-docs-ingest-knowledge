# About the PC Bid Coverage Form

The Bid Coverage tab on PC Potential Projects is used to maintain the vendor bids on a potential project. This tab will display all of the bid packages and scopes/phases on a potential project as well as information on each vendor bid.
You can use the Group Bar feature to group the vendor bids by specific
 columns and organize the information in the grid. For example, if you group the records
 by Bid
 Package and Scope, you can see what coverage you
 have for the listed scopes/phases of work. You can modify the groupings by adding more
 columns to the group bar or by selecting different columns to group by different values.
 For more information about using the Grouping Bar feature, [click here](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/user-interface-guide/system-forms/form-grids).
