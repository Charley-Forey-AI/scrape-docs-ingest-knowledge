# About the PC Bid Package Scope Notes Form

Use this form to identify the work that is included and excluded from a particular bid package. You can use this form to enter this information or you can just use the grid on the Inclusions/Exclusions tab on PC Bid Package.
To launch PC Bid Package Scope Notes, open [PC Bid Package](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/pre-construction/pc-potential-projects/bids/about-the-pc-bid-package-form) and double click in the grid on the Inclusions/Exclusions tab. PC Bid Package is launched by double clicking in the grid on the Bid Packages tab in [PC Potential Projects](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/pre-construction/pc-potential-projects/potential-project-setup/about-the-pc-potential-projects-form).
If you use [PC Create PM Project](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/pre-construction/pc-potential-projects/potential-project-setup/about-the-pc-create-pm-project-form) to create subcontract detail, any inclusions and exclusions associated with the subcontract detail will be copied to the PM module. The copied inclusions and exclusions will display on the Inclusions/Exclusions tab of [PM Subcontracts](/en/vista/vista/project-management/subcontracts/pm-subcontracts-form).
