# About the PC Contact Types Form

Use this form to create and maintain contact types.
Contact types are used to identify the different types of contacts you want to use during the pre-qualification process.
Note: Contact types are associated with a vendor group (defined in
 HQ Company Setup, AP Vendors and PM Firm Codes field);
 therefore, PC companies pointing to the same vendor group will share contact
 types.
Examples:

- Primary

- Secondary

- Estimating

- Scheduling

- Other

Once you created your contact type, you can assign them to a subcontractor contacts using the Contacts tab on [PC Pre-Qualification Info ](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/pre-construction/pc-potential-projects/subcontractor-prequalification/about-the-pc-pre-qualification-info-form).
