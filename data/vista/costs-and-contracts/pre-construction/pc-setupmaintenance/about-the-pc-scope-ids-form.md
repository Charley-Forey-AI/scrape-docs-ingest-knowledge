# About the PC Scope IDs Form

Use this form to create and maintain scope IDs. Scope IDs identify the different areas of work performed on a job.
Note: Scope IDs are associated with a vendor group, so PC
 companies associated with the same vendor group will share scope codes. Vendor groups
 are set up using the AP Vendors and PM Firm Codes field on
 [HQ Company Setup ](/en/vista/vista/administration/headquarters/company-setup/hq-company-setup-form).
Examples of Scopes:

- Site Work

- Concrete

- Roofing

- Electrical

- Flagging
