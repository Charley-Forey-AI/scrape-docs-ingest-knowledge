# Field Definitions: JB Move Bill Month Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the JB Move
 Bill Month form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Bill Month

The Bill Month field on the JB Move Bill Month
 form.
Enter the current bill month of the bill you are moving. Month must be open in GL.

## Bill Number

The Bill Number field on the JB Move Bill Month
 form.
Enter the bill number of the bill you are moving or press F4 to select from a list of bill numbers for the specified bill month.

## Target Month

The Target Month field on the JB Move Bill Month
 form.
Enter the month to which you are moving the bill. This can be a future month or a prior month, as long as the month is open in GL.
