# JB T&M Template Material Rates Form

Use this form to set up special billing rates for materials by template.
Access this form from either the JB Programs folder or by double-clicking a record on the Matl Rate Overrides tab in JB T&M Template Setup. The material rates established here override the standard rates defined for materials in HQ Materials (non-stocked) and/or IN Location Materials (stocked).
