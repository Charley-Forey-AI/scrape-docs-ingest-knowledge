# JB T&M Template Addon Seq Form

Use this form to determine the basis for an add-on.
This form is
 accessed by clicking the Initialize Basis of Addon
 button on the JB T&M Template Setup form. The
 “Initialize...” button is only available when an
 add-on is selected in the sequence grid and the
 Basis using this Addon tab is active.
For related
 information, see [JB T&M Template Setup Form](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-billing/billing/tm-billing/jb-tm-template-setup-form).
