# JB T&M Template Equipment Rate Form

Access this form from either the JB Programs folder or by double-clicking a record on the Equipment Rates tab in JB T&M Template Setup.
Use this form to set up equipment billing rates by equipment category.
Note: If the Equipment Rate Option is set to Costs for a template, equipment rates are disregarded in the billing process.

## About Deleting Equipment Rates

To delete an equipment rate, select the rate’s line in the grid and click the Delete
 button. The system displays a message box confirming the deletion. Click
 Yes to delete the equipment rate from the grid.

For related information, see [JB T&M Template Setup Form](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-billing/billing/tm-billing/jb-tm-template-setup-form#ID-00015a9a--en__ID-00015a9a).
