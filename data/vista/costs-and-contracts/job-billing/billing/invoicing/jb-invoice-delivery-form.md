# JB Invoice Delivery Form

Use the JB Invoice Delivery form to select multiple invoices to deliver at one time.
Access this form through the Vista main menu by going to Job Billing > Programs > JB Invoice Delivery.
Whether you have invoices you need to redeliver or invoices that you are delivering for the first time, you can use this form to select a group of invoices to deliver at one time. Enter search criteria to filter the invoices shown in the grid, then select the invoices you want to deliver. When you are ready to deliver the selected invoice(s), select the Deliver button.
Note: The Deliver button is only enabled when you have selected one or more invoices to deliver.

Note: The JB invoice delivery feature does not currently include attachments. If you have attachments that you want included when delivering invoices to recipients, use the Billing Compiler in Financial Controls.

For information about how to deliver invoices, see [Deliver JB Invoices](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-billing/billing/invoicing/deliver-jb-invoices).

## Access to the Billing Compiler in Financial Controls

If you have Financial Controls and have integrated your Vista Web account (that is, you have designated the appropriate Vista Web URL in VA Site Settings), you can access the online Billing Compiler by selecting the Vista Web Billing Compiler button on this form.
You can also access the online Billing Compiler from the Job Billing Programs menu through the JB Billing Compiler link.
For more information about the Billing Compiler, see [Billing Compiler](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:FC_000012:FC:FC).
