# JB Invoice Delivery - Send Form

Use the JB Invoice Delivery - Send form to send print or email invoices to the recipients listed.
Access this form via JB Invoice Delivery by choosing invoices and selecting Deliver.
Note: The Deliver button in JB Invoice Delivery is only enabled once you select one or more invoices to deliver. Otherwise, it is disabled.

The Grid and Info tabs of this form are display only. You can make edits to the Recipients tab fields, which populate by default with the details entered in JC Contract Recipient Details.
To deliver invoices to the recipients listed, select Deliver. For more information about delivering invoices, see [Deliver JB Invoices](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-billing/billing/invoicing/deliver-jb-invoices).
