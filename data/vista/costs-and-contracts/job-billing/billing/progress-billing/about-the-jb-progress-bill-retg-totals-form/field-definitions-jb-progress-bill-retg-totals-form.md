# Field Definitions: JB Progress Bill Retg Totals Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the JB
 Progress Bill Retg Totals form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  WC Retainage: This Bills Retg %

 This field displays the percent that retainage is calculated at for this contract and billing.
 If retainage has been calculated for contract items, the field defaults a rate based on the total item retainage amount and the number of items. Otherwise, the default is 0.00. This field may be overridden. Be aware that by entering a value in this field, you will override any rates specified at the contract item level.

##  WC Retainage: This Billing

 This field displays the total amount of retainage for this contract and billing.
 If retainage has been entered for contract items, the field defaults the total amount of retainage withheld for all items. If no retainage has been entered for contract items, defaults to 0.00; may be overridden. Be aware that by entering a value here, you will override any values entered at the contract item level.

##  SM Retainage: This Bills Retg %

 This field displays the percent that stored materials retainage is calculated for this contract and billing.
 If stored material retainage has been calculated for contract items, the field defaults a rate based on the total stored materials retainage withheld for all items and the number of items. Otherwise, defaults to 0.00; may be overridden. Be aware that by entering a value in this field, you will override any rates specified at the contract item level.

##  SM Retainage: This Billing

 This field displays the total amount of stored materials retainage for this contract and billing.
 If stored material retainage has been entered for contract items, the field defaults the total amount of stored materials retainage withheld for all items. If no retainage has been entered for contract items, defaults to 0.00; may be overridden. Be aware that by entering a value in this field, you will override any values entered at the contract item level.
