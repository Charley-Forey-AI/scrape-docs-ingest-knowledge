# JB Progress Bill Initialize by Item

Use this form to initialize single billing or contract items to progress billings.
 Access this form from JB Progress Billing by selecting Tasks > Initialize Single Bill Item.
When you initialize billings in JB Bill Initialize, the system creates new billings for a process group or range of contracts, based on selection criteria. With the Initialize Single Bill Item feature, you can initialize a single bill item or contract item to an existing progress billing. This allows you to refresh the data for billing items impacted by a change or correction, without affecting other items on the billing.
 Note: This feature is only available for billings with a status of Active, Change, Interfaced, or Never Interfaced. Additionally, you can only reinitialize progress items that were initialized as "Progress" items on the selected billing.
