# Field Definitions: JB Complete Bill Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the JB
 Complete Bill form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Bill Month

 Enter the bill month for all billings generated by this form.

##  Completed

 Check this box to indicate that this contract’s work is complete.
 Leave this box unchecked if this contract’s work is not complete.
