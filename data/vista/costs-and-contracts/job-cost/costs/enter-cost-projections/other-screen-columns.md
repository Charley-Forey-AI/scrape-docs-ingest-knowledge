# Other Screen Columns

Other screen columns in the Projections grid in JC Cost
 Projections.
In addition to showing the current estimate,
 actual, percent complete, over/under, and remaining amounts, other columns that display
 on the screen are:

- Final – This column is always
 displayed and accessible. In fact, if all you want to work with is the projected
 final amounts, you can leave all three of the optional columns off the screen.


- Previous – The previous projections
 are the values prior to this update.

- Forecast – The forecasted final
 projections are the values calculated based on the standard projection formula.
 These will not change with plugged projections.
