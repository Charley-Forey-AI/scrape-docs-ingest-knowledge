# About Row Highlighting

Throughout the projection process, several conditions may
 occur that will cause the rows in the JC Cost Projections grid to be highlighted.
These conditions and their resulting row
 colors are as follows:
Condition
Row Color

Phases/cost types with
 plugged values
Light Yellow

Remaining Committed Cost
 plus Actual Cost exceeds projected cost (Actual method only)
Peach

Buyout flag is checked
 and plugged value is not equal to the buyout
Light Blue

Inactive phases/cost
 types
Gray (overrides all
 other colors)

Tip: Right clicking your mouse while in the projections
 grid displays an explanation of what the row colors represent.
