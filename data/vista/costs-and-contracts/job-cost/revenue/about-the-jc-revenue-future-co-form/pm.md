# PM

Change orders with a source of PM include:

- Pending change orders set up and approved in PM, but not interfaced to JC

- Pending change orders set up in PM, but not approved and not interfaced to JC

- Approved change orders set up in PM, but not interfaced to JC

Note: These change orders have not yet been interfaced; therefore, approval months can be less than, equal to, or greater than the revenue projection batch month.
Pending change orders will only display in this form if the 'Include in Projections Flag' is checked for the document type assigned to the pending change order.
