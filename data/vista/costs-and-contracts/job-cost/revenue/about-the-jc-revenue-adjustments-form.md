# About the JC Revenue Adjustments Form

Use this form to post adjustments to contract revenue that you
 cannot otherwise enter in Job Billing and interface to Accounts Receivable.
There are two ways to make revenue adjustments:

- using an offset account

- leaving the offset account blank

If you use an offset account and the detail record
 is modified in another batch, the offset account will be used. If you do not use an
 offset account then you are responsible for creating/editing another transaction to
 balance to.
