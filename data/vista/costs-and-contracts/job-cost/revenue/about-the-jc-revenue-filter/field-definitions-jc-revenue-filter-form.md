# Field Definitions: JC Revenue Filter Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the JC
 Revenue Filter form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Beginning/Ending Department

 Enter the beginning and ending item in a range of departments (from JC Departments) by which to restrict projection detail. Only contract items assigned departments within this range that meet the ‘bill type’ criteria (specified below) will display in the revenue projections grid.
 Leave blank if only restricting on contract item and/or bill type.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-cost/revenue/about-the-jc-revenue-filter)JC Revenue Filter

##  Beginning/Ending Item

 Enter the beginning and ending item in a range of contract items (from JC Contract Items) by which to restrict projection detail. Only contract items within this range that meet the ‘bill type’ criteria (specified below) will display in the revenue projections grid.
 Leave blank if only restricting on department and/or bill type.

[](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-cost/revenue/about-the-jc-revenue-filter)JC Revenue Filter

##  Contract Item Bill Types

 Specify the bill type by which to restrict projection detail.

- All – Select this option to populate the projections grid with all contract items (within the specified range), regardless of bill type (i.e. Progress, T&M, Both, or None).

- Progress – Select this option to populate the projections grid with only those contract items (within the specified range) having a bill type of 'Progress'.

- T&M and Both – Select this option to populate the projections grid with only those contract items (within the specified range) having a bill type of 'T&M' or 'Both'.
