# About the JC Contract Close Form

Use the JC Contract Close form to perform a soft close or a
 final close on contracts that have been completed.
Closing a contract will also close all of the
 related jobs.
You cannot close a contract if revenue or costs are
 posted to future months; for example, if you try
 to close a contract in June but there are Job Cost
 transactions posted to July.
Open purchase orders and open subcontracts are listed on the Open PO's and
 Open SL's tabs. If you allow closing a job
 including any purchase orders or subcontracts that
 have not been interfaced, select either the
 Close Purchase
 Orders when Job Hard-Closed checkbox
 or the Close
 Subcontracts when Job Hard-Closed
 checkbox on the Info tab of the JC Company
 Parameters form.
