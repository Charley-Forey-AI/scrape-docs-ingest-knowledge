# Soft Close a Job

You can soft-close a job that is tied to a multi-job contract
 so that it does not continue to accrue costs when the contrac is still open.

1. From the Vista main menu, select Job Cost > Programs > JC Jobs.The JC Jobs form
 displays.

1. In the Job field, enter the job to close or press F4 to locate the job you want.

1. From the toolbar, select Tasks > Soft Close Job.The JC Job Soft Close form
 appears, asking you to confirm soft-closing the selected job.

1. Click Soft Close Job.

The system soft-closes the job. You can no longer post costs to the job, even though the contract is still open.
 Note: If errors are encountered during the soft close process, the system displays a list of the errors. You must correct all errors before you can soft close the job
