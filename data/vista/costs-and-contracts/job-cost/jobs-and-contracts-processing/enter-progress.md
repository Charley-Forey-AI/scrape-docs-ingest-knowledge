# Enter Progress

Follow the steps below for instructions on the most basic way
 of entering progress on a phase/cost type on a job. Organizations generally enter progress
 monthly, or weekly with timesheets.
Tip: You can print out JC Progress Worksheet reports
 and distribute them to employees so that they can record the progress and return
 them with their timesheets. The JC Progress Worksheet report is in the Reports
 folder in the JC module.

1. Select JC Progress Entry
 on the Programs folder. This will open the Batch Selection form.

1. Click the Create a new
 batch box, enter the current month in the Batch
 Month field, and then click OK. Click [here](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/user-interface-guide/system-forms/batch-processing/about-the-batch-selection-form) for more information on the Batch
 Selection form.

1. Enter the date of the
 progress in the
 Actual Date
 field. This date should occur during the month selected in the

 Batch Month
 field back on the Batch Selection form. You can also leave this
 field blank [More](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-cost/jobs-and-contracts-processing/about-the-jc-progress-entry-form/field-definitions-jc-progress-entry-form#ID-00019ad5--en)

1. Enter the job/project
 that you want to enter progress on using the Job
 field. You can leave this field blank if you want to enter progress on more than
 one job in the same batch. [More](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-cost/jobs-and-contracts-processing/about-the-jc-progress-entry-form/field-definitions-jc-progress-entry-form#ID-00019ae5--en)

1. The JC Progress Filter
 form will open. This form allows you to select which phases and cost types will
 populate in the grid so that you can enter progress on them.

1. Select the phases and
 costs types you would like to include and then click the Set
 Filter button. This will close the JC Progress Filter form and
 populate the JC Progress Entry form.

1. Enter progress into the

 Newly Completed
 column. Tip: You can also enter values in the

 Total Completed
 or
 Total Percent
 columns and the system will automatically calculate the

 Newly Completed
 column.

1. Once you have entered
 the progress, select File >
 Process Batch to post the entries. This will open the JC Batch Process form.


1. Click the Validate
 button and then preview or print any audit reports.

1. Click the Post button to post the progress batch and complete.
