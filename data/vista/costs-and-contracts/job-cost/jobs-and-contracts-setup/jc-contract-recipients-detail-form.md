# JC Contract Recipients Detail Form

Use the JC Contract Recipients form to specify a delivery
 method for sending JB invoices to customers.
This form allows you to set up one or more recipients for invoice delivery. For each recipient, designate whether they will receive invoices via email or print format.
Access this form through the JC Contracts > JB Info tab. In the JB Email Delivery section, select the Recipients button to open the
 form.
For more information, see [Set Up Recipients for JB Invoice Delivery](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-cost/jobs-and-contracts-setup/set-up-recipients-for-jb-invoice-delivery).
