# JC Job Phase Status Change Form

Use this form to change the status of multiple phases on a job at one time.
You can quickly change the 'active' status of all phases on a job or change the status for a selected range of phases. For example, if you have a range of phases that are no longer needed on a job, you can quickly change the status to Inactive for those phases. This prevents posting of cost, estimates, and commitments to those phases.
