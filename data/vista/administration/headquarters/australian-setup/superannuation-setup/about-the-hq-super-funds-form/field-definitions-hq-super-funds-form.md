# Field Definitions: HQ Super Funds Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the HQ
 Super Funds form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Fund

Required.
Enter a code, up to 20 characters, that
 uniquely identifies this fund, or press F4 for a list of existing funds.

## Name

Enter a name for the superannuation fund.

## Fund ID

Required.
Enter the fund ID for this fund, up to 30 characters. This ID is generally designated by the fund.
Each Fund ID must be unique, and not have been assigned to any other superannuation fund.

## Contact / Phone

In the Contact and Phone fields,
 enter the name and the telephone phone number of the contact person at the bank or
 organisation that holds the superannuation fund.

## Address

Enter the address of the superannuation fund.

- Address

- City

- State

- Postal Code

- Country

- Add'l Address
