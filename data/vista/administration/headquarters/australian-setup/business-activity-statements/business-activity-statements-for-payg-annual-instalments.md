# Business Activity Statements for PAYG Annual Instalments

You can use the HQ BAS Process form to prepare Business Activity Statements (BAS) for reporting your annual PAYG instalments.
When preparing a BAS for your annual instalment, you have four options:

- Paying the instalment amount provided by the ATO

- Paying with a varied instalment amount

- Paying the instalment amount using income multiplied by a rate provided by the ATO

- Paying the instalment amount using income multiplied by a varied rate
