# Business Activity Statements for FBT Instalments

You can use the HQ BAS Process form to prepare a Business Activity Statement (BAS) for Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT) instalments. Typically, you will report instalment amounts on a quarterly basis to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).
When preparing a BAS for the FBT instalment, you have two options:

- [Paying the instalment amount](/en/vista/vista/administration/headquarters/australian-setup/business-activity-statements/business-activity-statements-for-fbt-instalments/paying-the-provided-fbt-instalment-amount) that was provided by the ATO

- [Paying with a varied instalment](/en/vista/vista/administration/headquarters/australian-setup/business-activity-statements/business-activity-statements-for-fbt-instalments/paying-varied-fbt-instalment-amounts) amount
