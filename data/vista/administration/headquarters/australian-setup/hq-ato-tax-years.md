# HQ ATO Tax Years

Use this form to set up company information that is used to prepare tax and financial data for reports to submit to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO) for a tax year.
Once you set up this standard information for a tax year, you can navigate to additional forms where you can create specific reports, such as Fringe Benefit Tax (FBT) returns, PAYG individual non-business payment summaries, PAYG employment termination payment (ETP) summaries, and Business Activity Statement (BAS) reports.
