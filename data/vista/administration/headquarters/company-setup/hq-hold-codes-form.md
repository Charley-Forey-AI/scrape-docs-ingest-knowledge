# HQ Hold Codes Form

Use this form to create and maintain hold codes.
Hold codes are used to withhold payments from AP transactions. Having multiple hold codes allows you to have standard descriptions or reasons for the hold, for example "Out of Business", or "Performance Problems".
Note:You cannot delete a hold code if it has been associated with a vendor using the [AP Vendor Hold Codes ](/en/vista/vista/accounting/accounts-payable/vendors/ap-vendor-forms/ap-vendor-hold-codes-form) form.
