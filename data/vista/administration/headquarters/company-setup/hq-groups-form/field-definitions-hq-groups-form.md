# Field Definitions: HQ Groups Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the HQ
 Groups form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Group

 Enter a number (0-255) that identifies this group. Groups set up here will be assigned to companies in HQ Company Setup to determine the customer, vendor/firm, material categories/materials, phases/cost types, tax codes/rates, and equipment cost/revenue codes, cost types, and shop files to use.

##  Description

 Enter a description of this group, up to 30 characters.
