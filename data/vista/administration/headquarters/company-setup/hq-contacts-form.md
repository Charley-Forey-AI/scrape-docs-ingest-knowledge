# HQ Contacts Form

Use this form to set up all of the contacts that will be used in the Service Management module.
For each contact that you set up, you can specify the organization to which they are associated. Although not validated, this allows you to associate contacts with AR customers, AP vendors, and/or PM firms. You can also associate contacts with organizations not defined in the Viewpoint system.
