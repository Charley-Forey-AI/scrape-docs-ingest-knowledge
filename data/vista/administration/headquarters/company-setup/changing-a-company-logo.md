# Changing a Company Logo

You can assign a logo to each company set up in the HQ Company Setup form.
Follow the steps below to add or change the logo associated with a company record. The logo is saved in the Vista database.

1. Open the HQ Company
 Setup form.

1. Open the Additional
 Information tab.

1. Select Browse in the Company Logo section.

1. Select the logo in the
 window that displays.Note:Logos must be in BMP, JPG,
 or GIF format and cannot exceed 2MB. The Logo Size Allowance that
 displays at the bottom of the form does not apply. The logo can have any
 dimensions because the system will automatically resize it to fit in the
 main application window.

1. Select the Upload button when complete. The new logo will display in the
 Existing Logo field.

1. Select Save.

1. Select Yes
 on the message that displays. If you log out of Vista and then
 log back in, the new logo will be associated with the selected company. If you want to remove the logo
 associated with a company record, select the Remove button.
