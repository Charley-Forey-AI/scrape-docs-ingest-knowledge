#  HQ Units of Measure Form

Use this form to set up and maintain the unit of measure codes that will be used throughout the system.
 (Master File)
When your system is installed, you are provided with a pre-defined master file of Unit of Measure codes. It is recommended that after installation, you print a list of the unit of measure codes using the HQ Unit of Measure report and review whether these codes meet your reporting needs. If not, make the necessary changes, or contact Viewpoint Support to remove the installed list so you can start an entirely new list.
