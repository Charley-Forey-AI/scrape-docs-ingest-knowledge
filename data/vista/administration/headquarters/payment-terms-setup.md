# Payment Terms Setup

You can use payment terms to determine due dates, discount dates, and discount rates for customers and vendors when posting transactions in Accounts Payable, Accounts Receivable, Job Billing, and Material Sales. To setup payment terms, use the HQ Payment Terms form.
