# Activating and Deactivating Reviewers

When setting up or editing Reviewers in the HQ Reviewers form,
 you can choose to set a reviewer as "active" (meaning they will have the ability to approve
 future unapproved invoices and/or timesheets), or deactivate them (to remove their ability
 to approve future unapproved invoices and/or timesheets).
The following instructions detail how to "deactivate" a reviewer, disabling their ability to approve future unapproved invoice and/or timesheets:
To deactivate a reviewer in HQ Reviewers:

1. Highlight the particular
 reviewer on the grid.

1. Deselect the Active checkbox next to the reviewer's name.

1. Click Save
 Record.Note:If you deactivate a
 reviewer, you may receive a message that the reviewer is currently
 assigned as a reviewer, and asks you to manually replace with another
 "active" reviewer in any listed locations.

Note:The system may also notify
 you that the particular reviewer you are attempting to deactivate is the
 Responsible Person in at least one HQ Reviewer Group and therefore
 cannot be inactivated in HQ Reviewers. In such an instances, you must
 change the Responsible Person in that particular reviewer group in order
 to deactivate the selected reviewer.
