# Field Definitions: HQ Insurance Codes Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the HQ
 Insurance Codes form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Insurance Code

 Enter an insurance code, up to 10 characters. Insurance codes set up here are assigned to states in PR State Insurance Codes to define rates for worker’s comp and general liability insurance. They can also be set up on templates (in JC Insurance Templates) to define worker’s comp insurance codes by phase.

##  Description

 Enter a description of the insurance code, up to 30 characters.
