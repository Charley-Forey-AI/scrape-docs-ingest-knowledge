# Field Definitions: HQ SMSA Codes Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the HQ SMSA
 Codes form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  SMSA Code

 Enter a code, up to 10 characters, that identifies this SMSA region.

##  Description

 Enter the description of the SMSA code, up to 30 characters.

##  Minority Goal

 Enter the minority goal percent. Enter 18% as 18.00.

##  Female Goal

 Enter the female goal percent. Enter 18% as 18.00.
