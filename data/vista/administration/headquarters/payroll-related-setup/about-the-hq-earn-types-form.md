# About the HQ Earn Types Form

Use this form to set up earn types.
Earn types are assigned to earnings codes and are
 used to determine which GL account will be
 expensed when earnings are updated to GL. You can
 also assign earn types to departments in Payroll,
 Job Cost, and Equipment Management to determine
 the GL accounts used for earnings expensed to jobs
 and equipment (PR Departments), equipment (EM
 Departments), and open and closed jobs (JC
 Departments).
