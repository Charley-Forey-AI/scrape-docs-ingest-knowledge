# Delete Custom Fields

You can use VA Custom Fields Wizard to delete existing custom fields.
You must delete each custom field separately.
You can delete a custom field even when data exists for that field. In addition, you can delete a custom field from a form even when the form is currently in use.
To delete a custom field:

1.  Specify the form and field name and click the Delete button (which appears after you specify the field name).

1. Click Finish to exit the wizard and delete the field. Note: If the form from which you are deleting the custom field has a corresponding form (e.g. JC Contracts/PM Contracts, JC Jobs/PM Projects, etc.), the system displays a message asking if you want to delete the field from the corresponding form as well. Click Yes to delete the field from both forms or No to delete the field from only the selected form.
