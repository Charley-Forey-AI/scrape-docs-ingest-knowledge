# VA Custom Form Tabs Form

You can use this form to link a query to a tab on a form and link a user-defined (UD) table to a tab on a form.
Access this form using the Programs folder in the VA module, or the Edit button on the [Form Properties](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/user-interface-guide/system-forms/about-the-form-properties-form) form.
