# Forwarding Messages from VA Messages

You can forward messages stored in VA Messages.
These are messages sent by Vista™ - for example, workflow notifications.
To forward an email message:

1. Open the VA Messages form from the VA Programs folder or by clicking the pop-up icon from the system tray of your desktop

1. Click the Forward
 Message button.

1. Forward the message

- If you have Microsoft Outlook installed on your computer, a new message displays in your Outlook client, from which you can forward the message.

- If you do not have Outlook installed, the Send Email form displays. Use the Send Email form to make any necessary modifications and send the message.
