# Test Your Vista SMTP Email Configuration

Test your SMTP email relay configuration to make sure
 everything is set up properly.
Set up SMTP
 settings as outlined in [Configure SMTP OAuth Email Settings in
 Vista](/en/vista/vista/administration/viewpoint-administration/setup-and-maintenance/get-started-in-the-va-module/set-up-vista-to-send-emails/configure-smtp-email-settings/configure-smtp-oauth-email-settings-in-vista).

1. In Vista, go to Viewpoint
 Administration > Programs > VA Site
 Settings > Email Settings
 tab.

1. Make sure your settings are configured the way you want.

1. At the bottom of the form, select Send Test
 Email.The Send Test Email window opens, summarizing your current SMTP
 settings.

1. Enter the email address that you want to receive the test email, and
 select OK.You should see a messaging stating *Test email sent successfully
 to [youraddress@email.com]. Check your inbox or spam
 folder.*

1. Check your email to verify that the message sent. If the message sent, your email relay is set up properly.
