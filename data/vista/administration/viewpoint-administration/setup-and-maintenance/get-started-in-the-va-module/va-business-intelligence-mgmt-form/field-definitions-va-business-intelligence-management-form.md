# Field Definitions: VA Business Intelligence Management Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the VA
 Business Intelligence Management form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Companies to Process

Companies to Process box on the VA Business Intelligence Mgmt form
Select the checkbox for each company that you want to include in processing the OLAP database. Check the All box to include all companies.

## SSAS Database

SSAS Database field on the VA Business Intelligence Mgmt form
 Enter the name of the Viewpoint OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) database that resides on the SSAS (SQL Server Analysis Services).

Related information

- [VA Business Intelligence Mgmt Form](/en/vista/vista/administration/viewpoint-administration/setup-and-maintenance/get-started-in-the-va-module/va-business-intelligence-mgmt-form)

## SSAS Job

SSAS Job field on the VA Business Intelligence Mgmt form
 Enter the name of the job that process the Viewpoint OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) database.

Related information

- [VA Business Intelligence Mgmt Form](/en/vista/vista/administration/viewpoint-administration/setup-and-maintenance/get-started-in-the-va-module/va-business-intelligence-mgmt-form)

## SSAS Server

SSAS Server field on the VA Business Intelligence Mgmt form
 Enter the name of the SSAS (SQL Server Analysis Services) server that hosts the Viewpoint OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) database.
