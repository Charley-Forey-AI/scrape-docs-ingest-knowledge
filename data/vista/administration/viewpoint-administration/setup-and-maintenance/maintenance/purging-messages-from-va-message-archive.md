# Purging Messages from VA Message Archive

Use this form to purge a selected range of messages from VA Message Archive.
 To purge record:

1. Open VA Message Archive Purge.

1. Enter a date in the
 Purge Records Older Than field. The system displays the number
 of records to be purged in the ‘Number of Records…’ display field. The total
 number of messages displays in the Total Records field.

1. Click Purge
 Records. The system removes all messages older than the date
 specified in the Purge Records Older Than field.
