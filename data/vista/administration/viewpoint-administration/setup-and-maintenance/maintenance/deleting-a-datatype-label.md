# Deleting a Datatype Label

Use the VA Datatype Label form to delete labels (field description) for fields associated with certain datatypes.
This is useful when standard Vista™ software terminology does not quite match your organization’s terminology.
 To delete a datatype label:

1. In the VA Datatype label
 form, in the Datatype field, press
 F4 to select from a list of datatypes.

1. Select the text in the
 Label field and click the Backspace key on your keyboard. Note: The Delete option has been removed from
 the toolbar and the Edit menu to prevent datatypes from being accidentally
 deleted.
