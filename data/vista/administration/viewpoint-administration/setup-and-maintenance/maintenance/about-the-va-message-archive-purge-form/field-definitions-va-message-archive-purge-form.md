# Field Definitions: VA Message Archive Purge Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the VA
 Message Archive Purge form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Purge Records Older Than

 Enter a date, or select one using the Calendar button. The system deletes all messages/records older than this date from VA Message Archive.
