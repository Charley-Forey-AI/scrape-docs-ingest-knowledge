# Field Definitions: VA Datatype Label Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the VA
 Datatype Label form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Datatype

Datatype field on the VA Datatype Label form
Enter the datatype that requires a data
 change. Use F4 to lookup all defined datatypes.
