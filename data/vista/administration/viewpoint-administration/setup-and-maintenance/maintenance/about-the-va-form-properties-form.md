# About the VA Form Properties Form

Use this form to find and open the Form Properties for most forms in Vista™.
 On any record in the VA Form Properties form, double-click to select that form to then view or modify its properties.
For more information on viewing or editing a form's properties, see [Form Properties](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/user-interface-guide/system-forms/about-the-form-properties-form).
