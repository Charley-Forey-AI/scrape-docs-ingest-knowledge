# Set Up Identity Federation Through Trimble ID

To set up federated logins for your organization, you can
 integrate Trimble ID with your federation
 provider to simplify user management and access across your applications.
To get started, complete the [Trimble ID Federation form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/13b6OmHB8vxe3aJEtHtviCAQWr7Zqta1PXQJsStui6j4/viewform), which collects information such as your identity provider (IdP) and the domains of your users' email addresses in their existing IdP.
Using the information you provide, our Trimble ID team will initiate an identity federation for your organization and will assist your IT team with the federation.
