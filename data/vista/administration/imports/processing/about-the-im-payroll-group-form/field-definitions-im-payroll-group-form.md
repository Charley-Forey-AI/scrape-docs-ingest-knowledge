# Field Definitions: IM Payroll Group Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the IM
 Payroll Group form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Payroll Ending Date

 Enter the payroll ending date that corresponds with the specified Payroll Company and Payroll Group. Press F4 for a list of valid pay periods.
