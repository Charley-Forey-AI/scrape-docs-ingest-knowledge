# SMServiceSite

Service sites being imported for upload to SM Service Site must be assigned a site type of C - Customer or J - Job.
Although many of the fields are the same, there are additional fields available based on the site type. Additionally, records with a site type of C - Customer must specify a valid SM Customer. Records with site type of J - Job, must specify a valid JC Co and Job.
