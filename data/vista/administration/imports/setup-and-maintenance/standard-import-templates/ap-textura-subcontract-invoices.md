# AP Textura Subcontract Invoices

If you import from/export to Textura, you will need to limit your subcontract numbers to 20 characters.
Textura allows a maximum of 20 characters for subcontract numbers; subcontract numbers that exceed 20 characters will be truncated during the export (via SL Textura Subcontract Export and SL Textura Subcontract Compliance Export reports).
