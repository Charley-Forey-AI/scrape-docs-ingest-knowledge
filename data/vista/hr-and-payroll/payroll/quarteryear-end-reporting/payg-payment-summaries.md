# PAYG Payment Summaries

Find information about setting up and generating PAYG payment summaries for year-end reporting.
Note: The Australian Taxation Office (ATO) has functionally replaced PAYG with Single Touch Payroll (STP). STP allows you to report the same info in every pay period that you did with PAYG. However, to preserve historical data, the PAYG forms are still available for your convenience. See the ATO website for more information about [PAYG withholding](https://www.ato.gov.au/businesses-and-organisations/hiring-and-paying-your-workers/single-touch-payroll/ato-payg-withholding-pre-fill-for-activity-statements). For information about STP Processing in Vista, see [Single Touch Payroll (STP)](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/payroll-processing/single-touch-payroll-stp).
