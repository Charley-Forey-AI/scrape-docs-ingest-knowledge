# PR Employee T4 Slips Form

(Canada only) Use the PR Employee T4 Slips form to set up employees for T4 reporting.
This form displays when you double-click an employee's
 name on the T4 Employees tab of the PR Canada T4 form.
This form is also for recording multiple T4 slips for an employee who has worked in more than one
 province and/or worked under more than one payroll program account for the year selected
 (displayed in the Tax Year field).
Tip: The Tax Year field displays the tax
 year in which you are working. The Selected T4 Slip section displays the Province
 and CBN Payroll Program Account information for the specific line number selected in
 the grid below. Press F1 in each of the other fields on
 this form for specific and additional field information.
