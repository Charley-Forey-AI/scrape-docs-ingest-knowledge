# PR Aatrix - ACA Print and eFile Form

Use this form to process 1094 and 1095 returns.
Access this form via PR ACA Processing by selecting Tasks > Launch Aatrix Print and eFile.

With this form, you can process the following returns:

- 1094-C and 1095-C Employer Provided Health Insurance Offer and Coverage Information

- 1094-B and 1095-B Health Coverage Information

 Once you complete processing 1094s (B & C) and 1095s (B & C), you can make corrections, if needed, and reprint or process additional copies. For more information, see [Make Corrections to Regulatory Filings via Aatrix](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/quarteryear-end-reporting/aca-reporting/make-corrections-to-regulatory-filings-via-aatrix).
