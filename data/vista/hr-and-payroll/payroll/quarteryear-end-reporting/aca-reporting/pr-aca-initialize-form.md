# PR ACA Initialize Form

Use this form to initialize the data tracked in the PR ACA Process form for a tax year for full-time or all employees.
This form allows you to initialize ACA data from
 Payroll or Human Resources into the PR ACA Process and PR ACA 1095-C Employee forms for
 Affordable Care Act (ACA) compliance. This automates the data-entry process for a reporting
 year.
Access this form by selecting Initialize
 ACA from the Tasks drop-down on the PR ACA Process form.
