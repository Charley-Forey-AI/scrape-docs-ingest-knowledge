# About Unused Annual Leave Payments

(Australia only) There are two types of unused annual leave payments that you may need to make to your employees.
These are:

- normal termination and

- termination due to redundancy, invalidity, or early retirement.

You must create earnings codes for both types of annual leave, along with corresponding leave loading earnings codes, and deduction codes for taxation purposes.
