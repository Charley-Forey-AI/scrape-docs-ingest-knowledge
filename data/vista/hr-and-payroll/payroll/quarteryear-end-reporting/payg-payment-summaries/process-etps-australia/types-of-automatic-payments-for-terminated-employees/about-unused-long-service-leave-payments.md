# About Unused Long Service Leave Payments

There are three types of unused long service
 leave payments that you may need to make to your employees.
These are:

- Normal termination, payment for leave dates prior to 18 Aug 1993,

- normal termination, payment for leave accrual dates after 17 Aug 1993,
 and

- termination because of genuine redundancy, invalidity, or early
 retirement.

You must create earnings codes for all three types, along with corresponding
 deduction codes for taxation purposes:
