# Track Rostered Days Off Accruals: Australia

You can use the system to track Rostered Days Off (RDO)
 accruals. This topic applies to Australia users only.
 The dollar amount accrued is based on the employee's normal rate of pay and is
 withheld from the employee's paycheck until the day is taken off. Accruals can include
 holidays, leave, and any other time posted that is paid at the normal rate of pay.
The system allows you to track either weekly or daily RDO
 accruals.
