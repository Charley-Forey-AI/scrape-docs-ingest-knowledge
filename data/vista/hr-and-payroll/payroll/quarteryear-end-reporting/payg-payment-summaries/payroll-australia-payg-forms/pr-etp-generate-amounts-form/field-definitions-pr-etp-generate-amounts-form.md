# Field Definitions: PR ETP Generate Amounts Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the PR ETP
 Generate Amounts form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Employee #

 To generate information for an ETP payment summary or summaries for all terminated employees, leave this field blank.
 To generate information for an ETP payment
 summary or summaries for a specific terminated employee, enter the identification number
 for the employee, or press F4 to see a list of employee numbers.
For more information, see [PR ETP Generate Amounts](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/quarteryear-end-reporting/payg-payment-summaries/payroll-australia-payg-forms/pr-etp-generate-amounts-form).

## Date of Payment

 To generate information for ETP payment summaries for all dates on which you made a termination payment within a tax year, leave this field blank.
 To generate information for an ETP payment summary for a specific payment date within a tax year, enter the date of payment here.
For more information, see [PR ETP Generate Amounts](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/quarteryear-end-reporting/payg-payment-summaries/payroll-australia-payg-forms/pr-etp-generate-amounts-form).
