# PR PAYG Summary Process Form

Use the PR PAYG Summary Process form to prepare PAYG individual non-business payment summaries.
You can prepare payment summaries once for the entire tax year or you can create partial payment summaries throughout the year as necessary. The system uses the information on this form to complete the fields on the PR PAYG Payment Summary - Individual Non-Business report. Once you are done preparing payment summaries for the current tax year, you can generate and submit an electronic file to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).
