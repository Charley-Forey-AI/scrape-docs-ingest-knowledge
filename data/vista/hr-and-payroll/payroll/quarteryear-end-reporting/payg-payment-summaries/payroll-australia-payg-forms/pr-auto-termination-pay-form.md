# PR Auto Termination Pay Form

(Australia only) Use the PR Auto Termination Pay form to generate employment termination payments (ETPs) for employees.
First, you will bring employees into the form and review their records.
Once you have determined which employees require a ETP, you can have the system automatically generate timecard records for each applicable ETP type.
