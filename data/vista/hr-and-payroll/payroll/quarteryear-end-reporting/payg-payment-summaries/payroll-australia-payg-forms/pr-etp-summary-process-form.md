# PR ETP Summary Process Form

Use the PR ETP Summary Process form to prepare PAYG payment summaries for Employment Termination Payments (ETPs).
From this form you can prepare ETP information for the specified tax year and preview/print the PR PAYG Payment Summary - Employee Termination Payment. You can then generate an electronic file to submit to the Australian Taxation Office (ATO).
