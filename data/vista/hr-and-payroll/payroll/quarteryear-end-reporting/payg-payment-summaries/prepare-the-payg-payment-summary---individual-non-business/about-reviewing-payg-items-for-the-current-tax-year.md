# About Reviewing PAYG Items for the Current Tax Year

For ease of review, the PR PAYG Summary Process form (ATO / Super Items and Misc Items tabs) displays the PAYG values for the earnings, deduction, and liability codes according to your settings.
Once you have saved general PAYG information for the current
 tax year in the PR PAYG Summary Process form,
 the system defaults the values for PAYG items based on the ATO Category you selected for
 the related earnings codes (in the PR Earnings Codes form) or deduction/liability codes (in the PR
 Deductions/Liabilities form).
