# Entire Payment by Check

Below are steps to follow if you need to produce a physical
 check for an employee that is routinely paid by direct deposit.

1. Find the employee in PR
 Employee Pay Seq Control.

1. Select C-Check from the Payment Method drop-down
 field.

1. Select
 C-Computer
 from the
 Check Type
 drop-down field to enable the system to generate a computer
 check, or select
 M-Manual
 if you are going to manually write a check.

1. Click Process.

1. Proceed with the rest of
 the payroll process.
