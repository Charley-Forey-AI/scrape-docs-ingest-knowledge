# Post Automatic Earnings Based on Add-On Earnings

Some automatic earnings may be based on regular earnings plus add-on earnings. Since the system does not calculate add-on earnings until you run the PR Payroll Process form, it may be necessary to use the following procedures.

1. Enter and post regular timecards in PR Timecard Entry.

1. [Process the payroll period](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/payroll-processing/payroll/processing-payroll/processing-a-payroll-period) to calculate add-on earnings.

1. Post automatic earnings.

1. Process the payroll period again to calculate deductions and liabilities and complete the process.
