# Post Automatic Earnings Based on a Rate of Subject Gross

This section applies only to Australian and Canadian users who are calculating an earnings using a rate of subject gross; that is the system is using the RateofGros routine to calculate the earnings. For more information, see [Determining Earnings Code Calculation Methods](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/general/earnings-code-calculation-methods).
The following details how to post automatic earnings that are based on a rate of subject gross.

1. Enter and post regular timecards in PR Timecard Entry.

1. Post automatic earnings for salary time.

1. Process payroll to calculate any add-on earnings. For more information, see [Processing a Payroll Period](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/payroll-processing/payroll/processing-payroll/processing-a-payroll-period).

1. Post automatic earnings to calculate earnings based on add-on earnings or rate of subject gross.

1. Process the payroll period again to calculate deductions and liabilities and complete the process.
