#  Fixed Rate

Instead of the actual labor details calculated by Payroll, you
 can assign a fixed rate per hour (set in the second tab of PR Employees, JC Fixed Rate) for
 selected individuals that will be used for all jobs.
This rate replaces both the actual earnings and liabilities by taking the fixed rate times the hours posted, regardless of regular or overtime, and posting this to Job Cost. This option is often used for supervisory personnel to mask their true wage details in JC reports.
