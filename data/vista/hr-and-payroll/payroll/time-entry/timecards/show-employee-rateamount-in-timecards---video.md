# Show Employee Rate/Amount in Timecards - Video

You can show employee hourly rates and amounts when entering timecards.
Due to the sensitivity of employee rates and amounts, this information defaults as hidden in the PR Timecard Entry form. However, for those employees who have permission to access timecards, you can elect to show the employee rate and amount.

Unlike other fields throughout Vista, you cannot use F3 to control the display of these fields. Instead, you must use the VA User Profile form and PR User Options form in PR Timecard Entry.

This brief video shows you how to enable the Rate and Amount fields for timecard entry.
