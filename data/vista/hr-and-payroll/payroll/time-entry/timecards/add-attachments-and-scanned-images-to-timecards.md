# Add Attachments and Scanned Images to Timecards

Using the attachment and scan features, you can add attachments or scanned images to individual timecard entries in the PR Timecard Entry grid.
You can also add attachments and scanned images to multiple timecard entries.
The following instructions detail how to add attachments to multiple timecard records.

1. Highlight a timecard and
 select Tools > Attachments  > Show
 Attachments. The Attachment form displays.

1. Click Add
 and browse for the attachment.

1. Select the attachment, click Add, and close the form.

1. Click the arrow to the
 right of the Attachments button on the toolbar and select Copy
 Attachments.  The system adds the selected attachment to all
 job, mechanic, and SM work order timecards in the batch that do not already have
 an attachment.The following instructions
 detail how to scan an image to multiple timecard records.

1. Select Scan to Multiple Records from the Scan button ()drop-down menu.

1. Scan the image and close the Single Scan Viewer. The system attaches the scanned document to all jobs and mechanics timecards in the batch that do not already have an attachment.
