# Field Definitions: PR Initialize Crew Timecards Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the PR
 Initialize Crew Timecards form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Crew

 Enter a valid Crew number to initialize timecards for each employee and piece of equipment in the crew.

##  Payment Seq#

 Enter a payment sequence for crew timecards. Typically, this is sequence 1.

##  Timecard Date

 Enter the timecard date.

##  JC Co#

 Enter a valid JC Co#. This field determines the Job Cost company that receives the labor and equipment usage costs.

##  Job

 Enter a valid Job. This field determines which Job receives the labor and equipment usage costs.

##  Phase

 Enter a valid Phase. This field determines the Phase of the Job that receives the labor and equipment usage costs.

##  Standard Hours

 Enter the standard hours. This value determines the number of labor and usage hours posted with each timecard; used along with options in PR Crew.
