# Send Crew Timesheets

You can use PR Timesheet Send to send approved crew timesheets to PR, JC, and EM batches.
You can pull in all crew timesheets for a payroll group or restrict by job. You can also specify the timecard date through which to pull crew timesheets. The timecard date automatically defaults today's date, but may be overridden.
The following instructions detail how to send crew timesheets using PR Timesheet Send.

1. Select the Crew
 Timesheets tab.

1. Optional: If you want to filter by
 PR group, enter the group number in the PR Group field.
 Press F4 to see a list of valid PR groups.

1. Optional: If you want to restrict
 the send process by job, check the Select Job box. The
 system enables the JC Co and Job
 fields.

1. Optional: If you are restricting
 by job, enter the JC company in the JC Co field and the
 job in the Job field.

1. Optional: If you want to restrict
 by a specific date, enter the date in the Include Timesheets through
 Timecard Date field. Click the calendar button () to
 select a date.

1. Click Refresh
 Grid to populate the grid once you have specified the selection
 criteria.  All timesheets with a status of "Ready to Send" that meet the
 selection criteria and were posted on or before the specified “through timecard
 date” display.

1. In the PR
 Ending Date field, enter the payroll ending date that the
 timesheets should be associated with.

1. In the PR Pay
 Seq field, specify the payroll posting pay sequence that the
 timesheets should be associated with.

1. Optional: If you want to restrict
 the PR, JC, and EM batches you are creating, check the appropriate box in the
 Restricted Access section. Note: If you check one of the boxes, you will be the only user you can access
 the batch.

1. Click
 Send. All timecards in the grid are marked as 'In
 Use', the timecard (PR), progress entry (JC), and equipment usage (EM) batches
 created, and crew timesheets updated to a status of 4 (Send Complete). Note: If a timesheet's timecard date is
 greater than the date specified in the PR Ending Date field, a
 warning displays when you click Send, but you will have
 the option to continue. A message displays indicating the batches that were
 created, along with the batch month and ID for each. Batches can be accessed
 in their respective modules and edited as normal.
Once the timesheets are sent,
 you can review the timesheet information using [PR Timecard Entry](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/time-entry/timecards/about-the-pr-timecard-entry-form) or you can use PR Batch
 Process to [process the batch](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/maintenance/pr-batch-process-form).
