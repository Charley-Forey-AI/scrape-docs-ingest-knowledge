# Add/Delete Attachments on Sent Crew Timesheets

You can add and delete attachments for crew timesheets that you have already sent to a timecard batch.
You can add and delete attachments for crew timesheets that you have already sent to a timecard batch.
Adding or deleting attachments on a 'sent' timesheet affects only the timesheet; the system does not update the related timecard record.

1. From the main menu, select
 Payroll > Programs  > PR Crew
 Timesheet Entry.

1. Select Records > Show Send
 Completed.The screen refreshes to show only
 sent timesheets.

1. In the Crew
 field, enter the crew or press F4 to select from a list of crews.


1. If you know the timesheet
 date, enter it in the Date field.If you do not know the timesheet
 date, press F4, select All to
 show both Open and Sent timesheet dates, and then select the desired 'sent'
 date.
Tip: Click the Status column to sort the list of
 posting dates by status. This will group Sent dates together for easier
 selection.

1. If you know the timesheet
 number, enter it in the Sheet # field.If you do not know the timesheet
 number, press F4, select All to
 show both Open and Sent timesheets, and then select the desired 'sent' sheet
 number.
Tip: Click the Status column to sort the list of
 sheet numbers by status. This will group Sent sheets together for easier
 selection.

1. Add or Delete attachments as needed. For more information, see [Attaching Documents to Data Records](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/document-management/dm-workflow/manage-documents/about-adding-documents/about-attaching-documents-to-data-records) and [Deleting Specific Documents](/en/vista/vista/system-tools/document-management/dm-workflow/manage-documents/about-deleting-specific-documents).
