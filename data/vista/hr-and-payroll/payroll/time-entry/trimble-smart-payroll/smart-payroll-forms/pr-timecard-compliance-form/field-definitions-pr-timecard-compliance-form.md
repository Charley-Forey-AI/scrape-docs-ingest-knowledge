# Field Definitions: PR Timecard Compliance Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the PR Timecard Compliance form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Accept?

The Accept? checkbox on the PR TimeCard Compliance form.
Entry in this field is only allowed for rule violations flagged as Allow With Warning (those highlighted in yellow).
Select this checkbox to accept the violated rule without correcting it.
If you choose to correct the violation, leave this checkbox unselected and make the applicable corrections.
 Once you select Submit, the timecard batch is updated accordingly. If no further discrepancies are found, you can validate and post the batch. All lines that you accepted without correction are displayed on the Compliance tab in PR Pay Period Control.
