# Rollup Employee Accumulations

The Rollup Employee Accumulations checkbox on the PR Purge form, Accumulations tab.
Rolling up accumulations is a way to reduce the volume of
 historical data while retaining potentially important payroll data. This is done by
 consolidating a year's worth of data into a single line by keeping only the net sum
 for the year. For more information about how accumulations data is consolidated, see
 [About Rolling up Employee Accumulations](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/maintenance/about-rolling-up-employee-accumulations)
To roll up employee accumulations:

1. Open the PR Purge form.

1. Select the Rollup Employee Accumulations checkbox.

1. In the Through Year field, enter the year through which to rollup employee accumulations.Note: You cannot rollup data newer than 3 years old. Enter a year that is at least 3 years prior. For example, if you are running the rollup in December 2021, you would enter a year equal to or prior to 2018.

1. Click Purge.

For each combination of employee, month, type, and EDL code, the system consolidates a year's worth of data into a single line for the net sum of that type and EDL code.
