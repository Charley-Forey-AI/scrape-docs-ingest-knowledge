# Field Definitions: PR Generate ChampCM Exports Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the PR
 Generate ChampCM Exports form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  PR Group

 Specify the payroll group (PR Groups) for which to generate the unemployment electronic media export files.

##  Beginning/Ending Period Ending Date

 Enter a range of payroll period ending dates to include when generating the Employee and Work Hour export files for unemployment electronic reporting.

##  Beginning/Ending Job Company

 Enter a range of JC companies to include when generating the Employee and Work Hour export files for unemployment electronic reporting.

## Beginning/Ending Job

Enter a range of jobs to include when generating the Employee and Work Hour export files for unemployment electronic reporting.
