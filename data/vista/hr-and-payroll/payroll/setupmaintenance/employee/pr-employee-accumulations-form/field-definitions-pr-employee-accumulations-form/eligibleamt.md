# EligibleAmt

This field is available if Type is set to D-Deductions or L-Liabilities.
For United States and Australian companies
Enter the month-to-date eligible amount for this liability or deduction code.
For Canadian companies
This field is available only for adding new records.
For adding a new record, enter the month-to-date eligible amount for this liability or deduction code.
For editing an existing record, double-click the record to edit using the [PR Employee Accumulations Detail](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/employee/pr-employee-accums-detail-form-ca) form.
Eligible amounts include those earnings used in the calculation basis for the deduction or liability amount. If a limit is specified for the deduction or liability (PR Deductions/Liabilities), the eligible amount will be 0.00 once the limit is reached. If no limit is specified, the subject amount and eligible amount will always be the same.
