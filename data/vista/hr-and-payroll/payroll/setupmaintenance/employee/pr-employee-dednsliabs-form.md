# PR Employee Dedns/Liabs Form

Use this form to set up or override deductions and liabilities for an employee, as well as federal or state withholding, exemption from specific deductions or liabilities, personal insurance, and/or wage garnishments as applicable.
You can access this form from the Main Menu or from PR Employees by double-clicking on a record on the Dedns/Liabs tab.
