# About Methods of Calculation

The system uses the method of calculation for a deduction or
 liability code to calculate the deduction or liability amount.
You will determine the method of calculation when creating deduction and liability codes in PR Deductions/Liabilities. The use of each method is dependent on your country. Click the appropriate link below to view a topic discussing methods of calculation for your country.
