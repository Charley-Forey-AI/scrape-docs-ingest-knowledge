## YTD Supplemental Earnings Threshold

(U.S. only) The YTD Supplemental Earnings Threshold field on the PR Federal Info form, Info tab.
Enter the year-to-date supplemental earnings threshold. During payroll processing, if an employee's supplemental wages (those flagged for Supplemental Earnings in PR Earnings Codes) exceed this threshold, the system computes Federal tax using the rate specified in the Tax Rate field.
If the YTD supplemental earnings do not exceed this threshold, taxes are calculated as follows:

-  If the earnings are posted to a regular (non-Bonus) pay sequence, taxes are calculated using the Federal tax routine.

-  If the earnings are posted to a Bonus pay sequence, taxes are calculated using the Bonus rate defined for the Federal Tax deduction code.

## Tax Rate

(U.S. only) The Tax Rate field on the PR Federal Info form, Info tab.
Required.
Enter the Federal tax rate to use for an employee's YTD supplemental earnings when they exceed the threshold specified in the YTD Supplemental Earnings Threshold field.
