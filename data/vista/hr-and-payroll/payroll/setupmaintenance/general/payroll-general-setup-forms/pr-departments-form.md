# PR Departments Form

Payroll Department codes are used along with Job Cost and Equipment Management Departments to control General Ledger expense distributions.
If you always expense gross earnings and liabilities by job, you will still need to set up at least one department for your company to assign each employee a department code in PR Employees.
You may want to create department numbers that coincide with your General Ledger account codes. For example, a department number of 500 for Field Labor could correspond to a GL account code of 500-Gross Earnings, Field Labor.
