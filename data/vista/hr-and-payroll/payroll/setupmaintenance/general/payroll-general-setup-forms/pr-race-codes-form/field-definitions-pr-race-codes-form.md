# Field Definitions: PR Race Codes Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the PR Race
 Codes form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Race Code

Enter a number and/or letter to represent the race code. The code's EEO Category that you select will be used on Department of Labor and Department of Transportation EEO reports.
Important: If you are using the PR Certified Export - eMars, ReportID 1315 (U.S. only), use one-character values only to insure that your export file is compatible with the eMars system.

## Description

Enter a description for this race code.

## EEO Category

The EEO Category field on the PR Race Codes form.
Select the EEO category that will control data reporting on the Department of Labor EEO and Department of Transportation reports. Valid categories are:

- A-Asian - Individuals of Asian descent.

- B-Black/African American - Individuals of African descent.

-  H-Hispanic/Latino - Individuals of Hispanic or Latino descent.

- I-American Indian/Alaskan Native - Individuals with ancestry in the indigenous peoples of North America.

- M-Two or More Races - Individuals who identify with more than one race.

- N-Middle Eastern/North African - Individuals with origins in any of the original peoples of the Middle East or North Africa.

- P-Hawaiian/Pacific Islander - Individuals with ancestry in the indigenous peoples of Hawaii and other Pacific Islands.

- W-White - Individuals of European descent.
