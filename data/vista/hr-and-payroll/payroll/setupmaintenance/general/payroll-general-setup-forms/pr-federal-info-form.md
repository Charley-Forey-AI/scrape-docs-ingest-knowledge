# PR Federal Info Form

Use this form to establish federal deductions and liabilities (U.S.), fringe benefit and employment termination deductions and liabilities (AU), and Quebec QPP and QPIP deduction and liabilities (CA).
If you do not register deduction or liability codes in this form, the system will not use them in payroll calculations. To set up deduction and liability codes, use the PR Deductions/Liabilities form.
United States UsersUse the Info tab to enter the applicable deduction and/or liability codes (Federal, FUTA, FICA), as well as the YTD threshold and tax rate for supplemental earnings.Use the Add'l Federal Based Dedns/Liab tab to register additional codes not represented by the fields on the Info tab.
Australian UsersUse the Info tab to enter the applicable deduction and/or liability codes for PAYG and Fringe Benefit types. For Employment Termination Payments (ETP), use the Add'l Federal Based Dedns/Liab tab to register the applicable deduction and liability codes.
For more information about Employment Termination Payments, see [About Standard Employment Termination Payments (Australia)](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/quarteryear-end-reporting/payg-payment-summaries/process-etps-australia/types-of-automatic-payments-for-terminated-employees/about-standard-employment-termination-payments-australia).
Canadian UsersUse the Info tab to enter the applicable deduction and/or liability codes for
 Federal tax, CPP/CPP2, Employment Insurance, and Quebec QPP/QPP2 and
 QPIP contributions.
For EI rates associated with each Payroll Program Account, use the Employer EI Liability Rates tab. You can add, update, and delete records from this tab.
