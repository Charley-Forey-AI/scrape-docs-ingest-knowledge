# PR Pre-Tax Deduction Groups Form

Use the PR Pre-Tax Deduction Groups form to set up pre-tax deduction groups.
You should set up a pre-tax deduction group when you have separate pre-tax deductions that are combined under a single annual limit.
 For each group, define the following:

- Standard Contribution Limit - Annual limit amount for employee contributions (for example, standard and Roth 401(k) contributions).

- Catch-up Contribution Limit #1 - Annual contribution limit for eligible employees who wish to withhold amounts beyond the normal limit (eligible employees are those who meet the current age requirements defined by the IRS).

- Compensation Limit - Annual compensation limit for 401(k) employer match liabilities

- Catch-up Contribution Limit #2 - Annual contribution limit, as defined by Secure Act 2.0, for employees meeting the specified minimum/maximum age range.Note: The system automatically applies this higher limit when an employee reaches the minimum age and reverts to the standard catch-up limit when they reach the maximum age. Eligibility for the increased limit is based on the employee's age by December 31st of the tax year. If they reach the minimum age by that date, the higher limit applies from January 1st of that year. If they exceed the maximum age by December 31st of the tax year, the standard catch-up limit applies beginning January 1st of that year.
 For example, if the minimum age is 60 and the maximum age is 63, employees who will be 60 by December 31st of the tax year will be eligible for the higher rate beginning in January of that year. The system will revert back to the standard catch-up limit starting in January of the tax year in which an employee turns 64.

Once you set up a pre-tax deduction group, you can associate it with pre-tax deductions that share the same annual limit. For more information, see [Create Deduction and Liability Codes](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/general/about-creating-deductionliability-codes/create-deduction-and-liability-codes)
For more information on using this form, see [Set up a Pre-Tax Deduction Group](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/general/set-up-a-pre-tax-deduction-group).
