Puerto Rico*

bspPRPRTXX
PR Employees (Filing
 Status tab)
Use filing status S,
 M, B, or H.
S = Single claiming
 all exemptions, or Married filing separately claiming all
 exemptions and not living with spouse.
M = Married filing
 jointly claiming all exemptions, or Head of Household claiming
 all exemptions.
B = Married filing
 jointly, claiming half of the exemptions, or Married filing
 separately claiming all exemptions and living with spouse.
H = Single, Married,
 or Head of Household, claiming no exemptions.
Regular exemptions =
 number of dependents.
Additional
 exemptions = number of allowances.
Misc Factor = 1 if
 married, living with spouse, and filing separately. Otherwise,
 enter 0.
Exemptions for
 veterans: Check the Override Misc Amount
 #1 box and enter the veteran's annual personal
 exemption amount plus the annual special deduction amount (if
 any) in the Misc Amount
 #1 field.
