# Delete a Fund or Clearing House

(Australia only) Use the PR Super Clearing House Funds form to delete a superannuation fund or clearing house.
To delete a superannuation fund or clearing house:

1. In the Clearing House field, select the clearing house you want to
 delete.

1. On the Funds tab, delete every fund in that clearing house.

1. Place your cursor back into the Clearing House field, and
 click the Delete icon.Attention: Deleting a Clearing House in PR
 Super Clearing House Funds does not delete it from the system. To delete a
 clearing house from the system, first delete it from any clearing houses
 that it is associated with in PR Super Clearing House Funds, and then delete
 the clearing house in the HQ Super Clearing House form.
