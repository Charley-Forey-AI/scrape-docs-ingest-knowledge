# Associate Funds with a Superannuation Fund Clearing House

(Australia only) You can associate funds with a superannuation fund clearing
 house.
You need to have [set up Fund codes](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/superannuation/set-up-superannuation/set-up-and-maintain-superannuation-funds#task-5166--en__task-5166) and [Set up superannuation fund clearing houses](/en/vista/vista/hr-and-payroll/payroll/setupmaintenance/superannuation/set-up-superannuation/set-up-superannuation-fund-clearing-houses).
Use the PR Super Clearing House Funds form to associate funds with clearing houses:

1.  In the Clearing House field, enter the code for a
 valid clearing house or press F4
 to select from a list.

1. In the Employer
 ID field, enter the ID that identifies this PR company to the
 clearing house.

1. In the Contact and Phone fields, enter the name
 and the telephone phone number of the person to contact this clearing house.

1. On the Funds tab, in the
 Fund field, enter a valid Fund code, or press F4
 to select from a list. You can also add all funds that have been set up in the HQ Super Funds
 form by selecting Tasks > Add
 all Funds from HQ in the toolbar.Note: You cannot add a fund to more than one
 clearing house.

1. (Optional) Enter an
 identification code that is designated by the fund to identify this PR company.
 This field is typically used when a fund has been associated with a Payroll
 company before having been associated with a clearing house.

1. Repeat steps 4 and 5 as
 necessary to add more funds to the clearing house for this PR company.
