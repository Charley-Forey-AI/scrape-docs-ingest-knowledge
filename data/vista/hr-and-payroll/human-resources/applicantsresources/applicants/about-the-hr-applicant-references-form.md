#  About the HR Applicant References Form

The HR Applicant References form is used to enter contact
 information for an applicant's references.
 This form may be accessed from the main menu or
 from the Applicant Reference tab in HR Resources (by double-clicking the desired record
 or clicking anywhere in the line and selecting the Open Related Record in Form option
 from the Records menu). To enter additional information about the reference or the
 applicant (provided by the reference), use the Notes tab.
