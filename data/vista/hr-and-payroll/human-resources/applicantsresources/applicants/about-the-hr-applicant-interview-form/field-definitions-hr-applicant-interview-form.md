# Field Definitions: HR Applicant Interview Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the HR
 Applicant Interview form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

##  Resource #

 Disabled if this form was accessed from HR
 Resources.
 Enter the applicant’s resource number (as
 defined in HR Resources).

##  Position Applying For

 Enter the code (as defined in HR Position Codes) that identifies the position for which this applicant is applying.

##  Seq

 Enter a sequence number (1-9999) for this interview or enter N, New, or + to have the system auto-assign the next available sequential number.

##  Interviewer

 Enter the name of the person interviewing this applicant, up to 30 characters.

##  Interview Date

 Enter the date of this interview.

##  Interview Time

 Enter the time of the interview.

##  Previous Employer

 If applicable, specify who this applicant’s previous employer was, up to 30 characters.

##  Previous Position

 Specify what position this applicant held with the previous employer, up to 30 characters.

##  Date Left Previous Job

 Indicate the date on which this applicant left the previous job.

##  Reason Left Job

 Indicate the reason that this applicant left his/her previous job, up to 30 characters.

##  Job Application Status

 Indicate the status of the applicant’s job application, up to 30 characters.

##  Job Application Date

 Enter the date that the applicant applied for this position.

##  Job Offer Date

 If applicable, enter the date this position was offered to the applicant.

##  Job Accept Date

 If applicable, enter the date the applicant accepted the offered position.

##  Job Reject Date

 If applicable, enter the date the applicant declined the job offer.

## Interviewer Comments

This section is used to enter any notes, comments, or information about the interview/applicant. The space allowance in this box is virtually unlimited.
Add a Standard Note
Standard notes allow you to insert frequently used text into some fields in the application. This text is created and maintained using the [HQ Standard Note](/en/vista/vista/administration/headquarters/standard-notes/about-the-hq-standard-note-form) form.
To insert a standard note into the field,
 right click the mouse while focus is in the field and select Standard Notes
 from the shortcut menu, which opens the Standard Note Copy window. Then enter the standard
 note to copy (or select from F4 lookup) and click OK. The system inserts the selected note
 into the field.
Spelling Check
 Click the Spelling icon on the toolbar or
 select Tools > Spelling
  to spell check the text in this field.
