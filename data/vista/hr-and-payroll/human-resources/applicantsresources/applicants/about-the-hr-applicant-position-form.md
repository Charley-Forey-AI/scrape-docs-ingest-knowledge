#  About the HR Applicant Position Form

This form is used to identify the positions for which an
 applicant is applying.
 It may be accessed from the main menu or from the
 Applicant Position tab in HR Resources (by double-clicking the desired record or
 clicking anywhere in the line and selecting the Open Related Record in Form option from
 the Records menu).
 Once the position code is entered, you specify whether the applicant is applying for a full- or part-time position. If part-time, you also specify the number of hours per week the applicant is willing to work.
