#  About the HR Resource Contact Form

This form may be accessed from the main menu or from the
 Contacts tab in HR Resources (by double-clicking the desired record or clicking anywhere in
 the line and selecting the Open Related Record in Form option from the Records
 menu).
Use this program to set up and define emergency contacts for your employees. For each contact you set up, you define the relationship to the resource, the address, and the home, work, and cell phone numbers at which the contact can be reached.
