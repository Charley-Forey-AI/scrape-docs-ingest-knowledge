#  HR Accident Claim Contacts Form

Use this form to set up and maintain contact information for accident claims.
Individuals set up here are typically those people contacted regarding information about the accident claim (e.g. claims adjuster, doctor, witnesses, etc.) Space is provided to enter the address and phone number at which the contact can be reached. Additional fields are provided to enter email and Web page addresses.
