# Field Definitions: HR OSHA Summary Export File Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the HR OSHA Summary Export File form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Survey Year

The Survey Year field on the HR OSHA Summary Export File form.

Enter the survey year for this export. This is generally the year for which you are reporting OSHA case data. For example, if the current date is 1/30/24, but you are running the report for the 2023 tax year, you would enter 2023 as the survey year.

## HR Company

The HR Company field on the HR OSHA Summary Export File form.

Enter the HR company for which you are exporting OSHA case data (that is, injury and illness data).

## Establishment ID

The Establishment ID field on the HR OSHA Summary Export File form.

Enter the establishment ID provided by OSHA for your company (up to 14 characters allowed).

## Average Number of Employees

The Average Number of Employees field on the HR OSHA Summary Export File form.

Enter the average number of employees you had for the reporting (survey) year.

## Total Hours Worked by all Employees

The Total Hours Worked by all Employees field on the HR OSHA Summary Export File form.

Enter the total number of hours worked by all employees for the given (reporting) year.

## Management Comments

The Management Comments field on the HR OSHA Summary Export File form.

Enter any management comments for this summary report, up to 250 characters. Comments can include information about how many deaths were counted as injuries, skin disorders, respiratory conditions, poisonings, or hearing loss.
Note: Entry in this field is optional for Federal respondents.

## Establishment Comments

The Establishment Comments field on the HR OSHA Summary Export File form.

Use this field to enter any additional comments about this summary report, up to 250 characters.
