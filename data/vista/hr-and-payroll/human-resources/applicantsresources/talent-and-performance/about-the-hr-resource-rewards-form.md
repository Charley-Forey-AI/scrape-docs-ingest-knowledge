#  About the HR Resource Rewards Form

The HR Resource Rewards form is used to track rewards received
 by your employees.
 This form may be accessed from the main menu or
 from the Rewards tab in HR Resources (by double-clicking the desired record or clicking
 anywhere in the line and selecting the Open Related Record in Form option from the
 Records menu). Space is provided to describe the reward, enter the reward amount, and to
 specify the reason the reward was given to the employee.
Note: You can track changes to reward information by checking the Rewards option in the Employment History Event Options section of HR Company Parameters.
