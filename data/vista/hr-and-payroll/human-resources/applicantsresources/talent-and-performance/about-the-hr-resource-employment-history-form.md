#  About the HR Resource Employment History Form

The HR Resource Employment History Form is used to track
 employment history for employees.
 This form may be accessed from the main menu or
 from the Employment History tab in HR Resources (by double-clicking the desired record
 or clicking anywhere in the line and selecting the Open Related Record in Form option
 from the Records menu). It is used to track employment history events for employees.
 This includes events such as interview/hire/start dates, seasonal layoffs, termination,
 position changes, merit raises, leave of absence, accidents, grievances, and so forth.
 Each event is defined by date, code type (History, Reason, or Position), employment
 history code, and the supervisor assigned to the employee at the time of the event.
Note: You control the employment history events that are tracked by selecting the desired options in the Employment History Event Options section of HR Company Parameters.
