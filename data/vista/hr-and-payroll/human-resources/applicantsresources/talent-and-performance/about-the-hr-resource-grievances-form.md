#  About the HR Resource Grievances Form

The HR Resource Grievances form is used enter and track grievances filed by employees.
 This form may be accessed from the main menu or
 from the Grievances tab in HR Resources (by double-clicking the desired
 record or clicking anywhere in the line and selecting the Open Related
 Record in Form option from the Records menu). For each grievance
 recorded, you specify the date the grievance was filed, a description of
 the grievance, the outcome, and if applicable, the hearing and final
 appeal dates.
Note: You can track changes to discipline information by
 checking the Grievance option in the Employment History Event Options section of HR Company
 Parameters.
