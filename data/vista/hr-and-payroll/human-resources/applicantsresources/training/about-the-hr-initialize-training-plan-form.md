#  About the HR Initialize Training Plan Form

This form is accessed by selecting the Initialize Position Training Plan option from the File menu of HR Resource Training, and is used to initialize a training plan for the resource.
 Training plans are defined by position code in HR Position Codes. Each training plan consists of one or more training codes that are applicable to that position. When you initialize a training plan here, all of the training codes assigned to the specified position code are then set up for the resource in HR Resource Training, each as a separate sequence.
Note: Initializing a training plan for a resource does not automatically register the resource for the class; you will need to do that via HR Training Classes or HR Training Class Registration.
