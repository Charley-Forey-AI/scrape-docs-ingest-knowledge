# Delete an Asset

Delete an HR Asset.
An HR Asset that has history records posted to it
 cannot be deleted. However, an asset can be deleted after the history has been deleted
 first.

1. In the History tab, go
 to the top record and delete the value in the DateIn field.

1. Note the value in the
 HRRef field.

1. In HR Asset In/Out by
 Resource, identify the record whose Resource # value matches the
 value you noted in Step 2, and delete that record in HR Asset In/Out by
 Resource.

1. Repeat steps 1 – 3 for
 all records shown in the History tab of the HR Company Assets for that asset.


1. In HR Company Assets,
 select the asset and click to delete it.
