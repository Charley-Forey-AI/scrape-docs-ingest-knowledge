# Field Definitions: HR Lic RestrictEnd Copy Form

The following is a list of field descriptions for the HR Lic
 RestrictEnd form. Many of the descriptions include links to other topics that provide additional information about or related to the topic.

## Source State

Enter the state for copying license
 endorsements, restrictions, and certificates. Press F4 for a list of valid states associated
 with the country that you specified in HR License Endorsements.

## Destination State

Enter the state for copying license
 endorsements, restrictions, and certificates to. Press F4 for a list of valid states associated
 with the country that you specified in HR License Endorsements..
