# Field Definitions: HR Initialize Rating Groups Form

##  Rating Group

 Specify the rating group (defined in HR Ratings Group) to use for initializing performance rating codes. Then click the Initialize Ratings button to begin initialization. All rating codes set up for this ratings group will be assigned to the position code (in HR Position Codes) or the resource/review date (in HR Resource Review).
