# Welcome To Vista

Vista™ is a construction ERP software system that connects all the different parts of your business in a single place.

## Vista Overview

Vista, part of Trimble Construction One, is a comprehensive construction management software suite. Instead of using separate tools for different tasks, you can use Vista to:

- Handle everything from detailed job costing and accounts payable/receivable to your general ledger.

-  Manage project details, from scheduling and document control to tracking progress in real time.

-  Track your equipment's location, usage, maintenance, and costs.

-  Manage inventory and the entire procurement process, from purchasing to on-site use.

-  Handle work orders, dispatching, and service calls.

- Manage HR and payroll, from employee records to processing payroll to keeping up with necessary regulations.

Vista gives you a complete and accurate view of your operations, helping your teams work together more effectively and giving you the data you need to keep projects running smoothly. It provides tools to manage various aspects of a construction business, including accounting, project management, and field operations. Vista plays a key role in Trimble Construction One's bundle of cloud-hosted technologies, integrating construction management with other Trimble solutions.

## Vista Help

Vista Help assists users and administrators by providing information on new releases, conceptual and workflow guidance, instructions for setting up, maintaining, and processing data, and year-end / regulatory reporting. Users can also find detailed information about the various reports used throughout the Vista software in the [Vista Reports Catalog](/en/vista/vista/reports-catalog/reports-catalog).
Before you start using Vista, there are considerations for setting up and accessing your Vista environment. For more information, see [Getting Started with Vista](/en/vista/vista/getting-started/getting-started-with-vista).
