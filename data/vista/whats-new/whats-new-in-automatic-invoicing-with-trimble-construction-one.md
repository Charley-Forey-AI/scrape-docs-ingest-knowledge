# What's New in Automatic Invoicing with Trimble Construction One

With Automatic Invoicing, Trimble Construction One users can automatically enter invoice headers into Vista without performing manual data entry, saving time and avoiding potential mistakes.
Use Automatic Invoicing to enter
 validated vendor invoices.
You can take advantage of Automatic Invoicing if your company's subscription for Vista is part of our Trimble Construction One contract.
Users who access Automatic Invoicing must be using single
 sign-on in Vista. For
 details on enabling single sign-on for your users, see [Enable Single
 Sign-on](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:vista_60004:Vista:Vista).
For instructions on how to enable and use Automatic Invoicing, see
 [Automatic Invoicing (for
 Trimble Construction One Users)](https://apim.viewpointplatform.com/help/v1/contents/urn:contentId:vista_60007:Vista:Vista).
