# General Features

Vista 2021 R1 delivers on General user-requested enhancements, fixes, and other improvements.

## Expanded Email Character Allowance

To accommodate entering multiple recipients for email correspondence, all email fields throughout Vista now allow up to 255 characters. If entering multiple email addresses, you must separate them with a semi-colon (e.g. janedoe@email.com;johndoe@email.com).
