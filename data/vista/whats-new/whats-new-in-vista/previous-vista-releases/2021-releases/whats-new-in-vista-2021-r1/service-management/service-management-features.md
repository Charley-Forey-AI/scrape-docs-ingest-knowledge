# Service Management Features

Vista 2021 R1 delivers on user-requested Service Management enhancements, fixes, and other improvements.

## Ability to Record Reason for Return Trips on Work Orders

You can now record the reason and description for return trips to service sites associated with Service Management work orders. Two new fields were added to the SM Trips form: Reason and Notes.
The Reason field is used to enter a reason code (set up in HQ Reason Codes) identifying the reason why a technician needs to return to the service site associated with a work order. You can then use the Notes field to further detail the reason for the return trip.
Note: The information entered in these fields is informational only.
