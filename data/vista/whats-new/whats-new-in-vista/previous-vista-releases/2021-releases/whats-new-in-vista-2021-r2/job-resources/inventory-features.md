# Inventory Features

Vista 2021 R2 delivers on user-requested Job Billing enhancements, fixes, and other improvements.

## Associate Material Orders with Job Cost Field Tickets

You can now assign field tickets to material order items, enabling you to link related costs to specific work activity on a job, and facilitate owner billing.
 To enable this functionality, a new Ticket field was added to the IN Material Order Entry form. When you enter a material order item, you can associate it to a field ticket for the specified job/contract; however, the field ticket must be Open. You cannot post to field tickets with a Closed, Approved, Rejected, or Billed status.
Once you post the material order batch, the system automatically creates an entry on the Cost Detail tab of JC Field Ticket for each material use transaction, with a Source of IN MatlOrd.
Note: Costs associated with approved field tickets only impact T&M billings; therefore, assigning field tickets to material order items for a job is only useful if the job's contract/contract item has a bill type of T&M or Both.

For more information about associating field tickets with material orders, see [IN Material Order Entry](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/inventory/material-orders/about-the-in-material-order-entry-form#ID-00013f30--en__INMatlOrder_FieldTickets). For more information about the Field Tickets feature, see [JC Field Ticket Form](/en/vista/vista/costs-and-contracts/job-cost/jobs-and-contracts-setup/jc-field-ticket-form).
