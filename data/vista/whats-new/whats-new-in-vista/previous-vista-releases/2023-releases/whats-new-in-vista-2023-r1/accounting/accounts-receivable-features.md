# Accounts Receivable Features

Vista 2023 R1 delivers on user-requested Accounts Receivable enhancements, fixes, and other improvements.

## Assign Job Billing Invoice Formats to Customers for Invoice Delivery

In support of the JB Invoice Delivery feature added in this release, you now have the ability to assign invoice formats to customers in AR Customers.
Two new fields were added to the AR Customers form (Info tab) for designating the invoice formats to use when delivering Progress and T&M invoices:

- Progress Bills Invoice Format

- T&M Bills Invoice Format

You will only need to enter values in these fields for customers who require a different invoice format than the one you have defined for the Job Billing company in JB Company Parameters. When delivering invoices (in JB Invoice Delivery), the invoice formats defined for a customer override those defined for the JB company.
Note: You can also assign invoice formats at the contract level; however, you need only set up contract-level invoice formats for contracts that require a different format than the one specified for the customer or the Job Billing company. For more information, see the [Job Cost](/en/vista/vista/whats-new/whats-new-in-vista/previous-vista-releases/2023-releases/whats-new-in-vista-2023-r1/costs-and-contracts/job-cost-features#concept-409--en__SetContractRecipients) release notes.

 For more information about the JB Invoice Delivery feature, see the [Job Billing](/en/vista/vista/whats-new/whats-new-in-vista/previous-vista-releases/2023-releases/whats-new-in-vista-2023-r1/costs-and-contracts/job-billing-features) release notes.
