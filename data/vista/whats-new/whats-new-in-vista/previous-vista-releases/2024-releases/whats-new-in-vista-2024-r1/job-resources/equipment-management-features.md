# Equipment Management Features

Vista 2024 R1 delivers on user-requested Equipment Management enhancements, fixes, and other improvements.

## AU Only: Clarifications on How Diminishing Value is Calculated for Assets

Note: The Diminishing Value method of depreciation applies to
 Australia only.

Several fixes in this release resulted in the need to clarify how diminishing value
 is calculated and scheduled on the EM Asset Setup form. This is the formula for
 calculating diminishing value:
base
 value × (days
 held ÷ 365) × (200% ÷ asset's effective life)
For detailed explanations and examples, see [About Diminishing Value (Australia)](/en/vista/vista/job-resources/equipment-management/depreciation/about-depreciation/about-declining-balance--diminishing-value-depreciation/about-diminishing-value-australia).
