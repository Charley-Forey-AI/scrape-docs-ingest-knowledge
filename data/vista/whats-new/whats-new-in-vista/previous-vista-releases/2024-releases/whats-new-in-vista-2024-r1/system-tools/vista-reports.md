# Vista Reports

The following discusses updates made to standard reports for the 2024 R1 release.

## Updated SM Tax Report

Report ID: 1313
The SM Tax Report now displays data for cost-related Sales tax or VAT (as appropriate) for SM Work Completed Miscellaneous lines (type 3-Misc) entered directly in the Work Completed grid (in SM Work Orders) or in the SM Work Completed Misc form. Previously, the report would only show cost-related Sales and VAT tax for miscellaneous lines that had been posted via AP Transaction Entry or AP Unapproved Invoice Posting.
