# Work Centers

Vista 2022 R1 delivers on user-requested enhancements, fixes, and other improvements for Work Centers.

## Accounting Work Centers

The Accounting Work Center now requires entering a four-digit year when filtering by month range. The Begin Month and End Month fields were relabeled to Begin Month (MM/YYYY) and End Month (MM/YYYY) to indicate that you must enter the month as shown (including the slash).
You can still use the plus (+) and minus (-) keys to move the entered month forward or backward, as well as the up/down buttons to the right of each field.

## Faster, more relevant query results

SM Work Center queries now include options for entering Begin Month and End Month when querying work orders or invoice-related data. Similar to the Accounting Work Center, by limiting the number of records to just the posted month(s) you're interested in, you can get your results faster.

## Check Issue Status

To see a list of defects fixed in this version, go to the [Track Cases/Issues page](https://support.viewpoint.com/s/browse-cases-and-issues) of the Viewpoint Customer Portal. Use the filter options to select your product and module. In the Fixed in Version field, select this version, then select Apply Filters.
