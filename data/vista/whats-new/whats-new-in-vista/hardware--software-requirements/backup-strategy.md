# Backup Strategy

Backup management is an extremely important customer
 responsibility. You must understand and manage a backup and recovery plan that fits your
 needs.
The utilities provided within Windows Server and SQL Server are adequate for
 complete backups. If you choose, you may also employ other third-party software.
Viewpoint does not monitor
 customer backups but can help you determine the best strategy for your organization.
