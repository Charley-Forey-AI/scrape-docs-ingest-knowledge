# 2025 Tax Update #7 (2025.7)

This tax update applies to customers doing business in Australia.
Important:Vista tax updates are cumulative by year; therefore, 2025 Tax Update #7 includes all prior tax updates for 2025. It does not include any tax updates for 2024.

## Compatibility

These tax routines are compatible with current and earlier versions of Vista, unless specifically stated otherwise. However, they are not tested on older versions, so compatibility is not guaranteed.
