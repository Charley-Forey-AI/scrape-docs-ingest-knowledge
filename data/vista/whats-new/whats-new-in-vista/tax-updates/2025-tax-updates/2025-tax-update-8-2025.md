# 2025 Tax Update #8 (2025.8)

This tax update applies to customers doing business in the United States.
Important:Vista tax updates are cumulative by year; therefore, 2025 Tax Update #8 includes all prior tax updates for 2025. It does not include any tax updates for 2024.

## Compatibility

These tax routines are compatible with current and earlier versions of Vista, unless specifically stated otherwise. However, they are not tested on older versions, so compatibility is not guaranteed.

Tax routines are effective as of 10/01/2025 unless otherwise stated.
