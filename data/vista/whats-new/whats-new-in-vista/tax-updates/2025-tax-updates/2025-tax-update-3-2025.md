# 2025 Tax Update #3 (2025.3)

This tax update applies to customers doing business in the United States.
Important:Vista tax updates are cumulative by year; therefore, 2025 Tax Update #3 includes all prior tax updates for 2025. It does not include any tax updates for 2024. If you need updated routines for 2024, you can install 2024 Tax Update #5, which includes all tax updates for 2024. However, you should do this before installing any 2025 tax updates.

## Compatibility

These tax routines are compatible with current and last year's Vista releases (2024 R1, 2023 R2, and 2023 R1). They will also install on older versions, unless specifically stated otherwise; however they are not tested on older versions, so compatibility is not guaranteed.

Tax routines are effective as of 01/01/2025 unless otherwise stated.
