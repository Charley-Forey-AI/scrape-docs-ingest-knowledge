# 2026 Tax Update #2 (2026.2)

This tax update applies to customers doing business in the
 United States.
Important:Vista tax updates are cumulative by year; therefore, 2026
 Tax Update #2 includes all prior tax updates for 2026. It does not include any tax
 updates for 2025. If you need updated routines for 2025, you can install 2025 Tax
 Update #8, which includes all tax updates for the year of 2025. However, you should
 do this before installing any 2026 tax updates.

## Compatibility

These tax routines are compatible with current and earlier versions of Vista, unless specifically stated otherwise. However, they are not tested on older versions, so compatibility is not guaranteed.
