# 2026 Tax Update #1 (2026.1)

This tax update applies to customers doing business in the United States and Canada.
Important:Vista tax updates are cumulative by year; therefore, 2026
 Tax Update #1 does not include any tax updates for 2025. If you need updated
 routines for 2025, you can install 2025 Tax Update #8, which includes all tax
 updates for 2025. However, you should do this before installing any 2026 tax
 updates.

## Compatibility

These tax routines are compatible with current and earlier versions of Vista, unless specifically stated otherwise. However, they are not tested on older versions, so compatibility is not guaranteed.
