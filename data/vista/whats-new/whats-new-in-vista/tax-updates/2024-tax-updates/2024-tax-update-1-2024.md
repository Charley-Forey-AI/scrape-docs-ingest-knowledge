# 2024 Tax Update #1 (2024.1)

This tax update applies to customers doing business in the United States.
Important:Vista tax updates are cumulative by year; therefore, 2024 Tax Update #1 does not include any tax updates for 2023. If you need updated routines for 2023, you can install 2023 Tax Update #7, which includes all tax updates for 2023. However, you should do this before installing any 2024 tax updates.

## Compatibility

These tax routines are compatible with current and last year's Vista releases (2023 R2, 2023 R1, and 2022 R2). They will also install on older versions, unless specifically stated otherwise; however they are not tested on older versions, so compatibility is not guaranteed.

Tax routines are effective as of January 1, 2024.
