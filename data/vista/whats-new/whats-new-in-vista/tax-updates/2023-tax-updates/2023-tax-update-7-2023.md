# 2023 Tax Update #7 (2023.7)

This tax update (Tax Update #7) applies to customers doing business in the United States.
Important:Vista tax updates are cumulative by year; therefore, 2023 Tax Update #7 includes all prior tax updates for 2023. It does not include any tax updates for 2022.

## Compatibility

These tax routines are compatible with current and last year's Vista releases (2023 R2, 2023 R1, and 2022 R2). They will also install on older versions, unless specifically stated otherwise; however they are not tested on older versions, so compatibility is not guaranteed.
